Sawtooth brings the experience of your bed at home with you wherever you go. Its collection of thoughtful features go far beyond the ordinary: A built-in, ultra-soft bed sheet offers versatility on summer nights and is removable for washing. An integrated sleeve keeps the support of your sleeping pad in place (our Roamer™ pad fits perfectly!) all night. Finally, Sawtooth is topped with an insulated, featherbed-style quilted layer for ultimate plushness and warmth.

The overall design is symmetrical, with zippers on both sides to easily zip bags together, and a tapered geometry at the head and foot ends to reduce excess volume and keep the interior toasty. Our unique Blanket Fold™ draft collar is good for snuggling with and regulating body temperature, and a pillow pocket anchors your pillow in place.
