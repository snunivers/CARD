Overview






Jazz brings the experience of your bed at home with you wherever you go. Its collection of thoughtful features go far beyond the ordinary: A built-in, ultra-soft bed sheet offers versatility on summer nights and is removable for washing. An integrated sleeve keeps the support of your sleeping pad in place (our Roamer™ pad fits perfectly!) all night. Finally, Jazz is topped with an insulated, featherbed-style quilted layer for ultimate plushness and warmth.


The overall design is symmetrical, with zippers on both sides to easily zip bags together, and a tapered geometry at the head and foot ends to reduce excess volume and keep the interior toasty. Our unique Blanket Fold™ draft collar is good for snuggling with and regulating body temperature, and a pillow pocket anchors your pillow in place. Best of all, when it’s time to pack up, keep your deflated sleeping pad in place and roll it up together with Jazz in the included oversized duffle for grab-and-go convenience. If you’re looking for the comforts of home for a night under the stars, Jazz hits the perfect note.
