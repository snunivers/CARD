Benefits














 










Customized Filtration 






Configure your AX9 based on your personal and seasonal needs with our 360 filtersoptions. Your AX9 comes with the all-round Breeze filter but you can opt to switch this with a filter which specialises in the capture of pollen or even one which neutralises common viruses.. Whichever filter you choose, it will come with a smart tag that keeps track of its usage. Even if you remove a filter and re-install it the next season it will automatically recognize the remaining lifetime. 
























 










Responsive Technology 






The unique PureSense responsive technology continuously measures indoor air quality levels and, when operating in Auto mode, automatically adjusts fan speed to provide the most efficient clean depending on the air quality detected.
























 










Constantly connected 






Whether you are home or on the go, monitor and optimize your home air quality in the AEG Wellbeing app or trust your AX9 will do it for you. Review the job done on a given timeframe, get smart summaries or simply adjust your preferences. Monitor remaining filter lifetime and receive notifications when it is time to change the filter. The Wellbeing app is the perfect air quality partner. 
























 










The AirSurround system is engineered to reach all corners of the room 






AirSurround system creates a powerful yet smooth spiral movement of the indoor air that efficiently circulates and cleans the air. AX9  is recommended for large room sizes up to 92m2(based on Swedish regulations of air changes per hour) and 54m2 (US AHAM) 
























 










Quietly brilliant 






With a minimum noise level of just 17 dB(A)* your AX9 runs so quietly you will hardly notice it.  Even at maximum speed the noise level is just 49 dB(A)*, quieter than your dinner conversation at home. 
*measured 1m in front of the product.
