The AX9 combines a distinctive design with efficient 5 stage filtration and optimised air flow for larger rooms.

Customized Filtration
Configure your AX9 based on your personal and seasonal needs with our 360 filters options. Your AX9 comes with the all-round Breeze filter but you can opt to switch this with a filter which specialises in the capture of pollen or even one which neutralises common viruses.. Whichever filter you choose, it will come with a smart tag that keeps track of its usage. Even if you remove a filter and re-install it the next season it will automatically recognize the remaining lifetime.

Responsive Technology
The unique PureSense responsive technology continuously measures indoor air quality levels and, when operating in Auto mode, automatically adjusts fan speed to provide the most efficient clean depending on the air quality detected.
