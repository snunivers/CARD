Introducing the first-ever U-shaped Inverter Window Air Conditioner that allows you to open or close your window while the unit is installed. The innovative design makes installation very easy, and the ability to close the window isolates the noise outside, making it ultra-quiet.

Features

3-Step Quick & Easy Installation 
Installation can be done in 3 simple steps: install the (included) quick-snap bracket, set the unit on the bracket, and secure the sidearms.
Extremely Quiet 
The U-shaped design blocks the compressor noise outside, allowing ultra-quiet operation as low as 42dB. Even librarians would approve.

*9 Time Quieter disclaimer: testing done according to the ISO 3744. Nine Midea U-shaped units running at the same time is less than the sound level of some single traditional units.
Open the Window for Fresh Air 
The U-shaped design makes opening and closing your windows possible even with the unit installed. Let the fresh air in.
Powerful, quick cooling 
The strong airflow allows you to feel the cold air up to 20 feet away, and the full DC inverter technology helps to rapidly cool the room.
Control with your Smart Devices 
The Midea U works with Google Assistant and Amazon Alexa so that you can control it with voice commands. Or control your Smartphone from anywhere with the iOS or Android app.
Ultra efficient Inverter Technology 
Our patented inverter technology achieves more than 35% energy saving** compared to traditional AC units. It's the first window AC to be awarded Energy Star's Most Efficient 2020 certification.

**Compared to Federal Minimum Requirement.
