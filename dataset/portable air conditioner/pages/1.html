SereneLife Powerful Portable Room Air Conditioner, Compact Home A/C Cooling Unit. Chilling 8,000 BTU with Built-in Dehumidifier, Fan Modes, Remote Control, Complete Window Mount Exhaust Kit

COOLS ROOMS IN JUST MINUTES with this very powerful 8,000 btu refrigerated system. The advanced A/C has moving vents that promote better air circulation. We revolutionized the design with a built-in dehumidifier that gives you cool, dry air. It feels so comfortable and relaxing. No more hot, humid summers for you and your family! Makes quality of life so much more enjoyable and productive with optimum conditions in your home and rooms.

  • COMPACT ROOM AIR CONDITIONER: puts out a mighty 8,000 BTU of refrigerated air to perfectly cool any room. This is an affordable, economical way to enjoy hot summers and year round. Includes 2 fan settings, temperature select, and convenient 24-hour timer to save power.
  • BUILT-IN DEHUMIDIFIER: takes the moisture out of the air and makes your room much more comfortable. This is the brilliant feature that works wonders for hot humid conditions. Removes 1.2 Liters per Hour to keep you dry during the day and when sleeping. Helps improve mood while giving you more energy
  • PORTABLE AND LIGHTWEIGHT: at just 53 pounds. Comes with a complete window mount kit for exhaust. Simply set the unit on the floor in a room where needed. Plug it in and the A/C is ready to use. The unit has wheels for easy moving around the room or though your home.
  • INCLUDES HANDY REMOTE CONTROL: that lets you control fan speed and temperature from a distance. Need to adjust the A/C? No need to get up from your work to do it. Also offers a Digital Touch Button Control Panel. It's highly energy efficient with low power consumption that saves money every month. You'll love the low noise motor that is quiet and peaceful.
