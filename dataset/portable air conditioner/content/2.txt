ZoneAire
®
 Compact 


The 3-in-1 ZoneAire® Compact offers great versatility with cooling, dehumidification, and fan-only options all in a sleek innovative design NOW with Friedrich
Go™
 wi-fi built-in.

Features










3-in-1 System


Your ZoneAire Compact portable unit will provide air conditioning, dehumidification, and fan-only options.






Wi-Fi Built-In with FriedrichGO


Home or away, you're in control with our FriedrichGo app. Control your devices, change the temperature or set a schedule, and you can even do it all through voice command with Amazon Alexa or Google Home. 






Easy Storage and Portability


When the unit isn’t needed, getting it stored is not a headache. ZoneAire Compact units are easily stored with an exclusive "snap-n-store" built-in hose storage compartment underneath the unit. Easy roll casters make moving the unit around a breeze.










User-Friendly Controls


ZoneAire Compact units have a control panel on the top of the unit. Controls feature large LED display and rotary dial fan and temperature adjustment.






Breathe Easy


Maintain that breath of fresh air inside your home with easy-access washable filters.






Installation


Units are quick to start-up and begin cooling. All needed installation accessories are included with your ZoneAire Compact portable purchase. Just unbox your new unit, and you will find all needed equipment inside.
