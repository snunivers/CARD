Makes quality of life so much more enjoyable and productive with optimum conditions in your home and rooms.

BUILT-IN DEHUMIDIFIER:

PORTABLE AND LIGHTWEIGHT:

INCLUDES HANDY REMOTE CONTROL:
 that lets you control fan speed and temperature from a distance. Need to adjust the A/C? No need to get up from your work to do it. Also offers a Digital Touch Button Control Panel. It's highly energy efficient with low power consumption that saves money every month. You'll love the low noise motor that is quiet and peaceful.
