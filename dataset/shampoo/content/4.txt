Our hair goes through a lot, from changes in the weather to everyday heat styling. The secret to caring for damaged hair? Dove Intensive Repair Shampoo and Conditioner for damaged hair – specially made to target and revive damaged hair, restoring its healthy look and feel. 

When used with the Dove Intense Repair Conditioner, this reviving shampoo gives you complete care that works in harmony with your hair. Made with Dove Bio-Nourish Complex, our reparative shampoo and conditioner for damaged hair work together to precisely target damaged hair on the surface and replenish it with a blend of protein, lipid and nutrients. After just one use, these clever hair repair products help to stop 98% of daily hair damage.*

Perfect for























• Damaged hair 
Made with Bio-Nourish Complex, the system helps to repair damaged hair by targeting and repairing recurring hair damage. When used with the conditioner, this system helps prevent up to 98% of daily hair damage*.

*due to breakage vs. non-conditioning shampoo

• Intense nourishment 
When used together, our shampoo and conditioner for damaged hair target and visibly repair damage to restore your hair’s healthy look and feel.  With Dove Bio-Nourish Complex,   the rich formulas nourish damaged hair to leave it looking shiny and silky smooth.

• A caring hair care routine
Developed to work in harmony with your hair, this reviving shampoo does more than just cleanse your hair. When used with the Intensive Repair Conditioner, this shampoo   helps you to repair hair damage at home.



























 How to use 























Ready to wash your hair? Massage this shampoo for damaged hair into wet hair and rinse. For best results, use with Dove Intensive Repair Conditioner for healthy-looking hair.



























How to adapt the product for mobility or motor needs




















































Try this























For a caring hair care routine, a wide-toothed comb is your best friend when it comes to wet hair. Try to only use a brush when your hair is completely dry – it'll help to prevent hair damage.
