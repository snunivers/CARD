BladeRunner X2 10 Piece T-Shank Blade Set




The Rockwell 10pc. T-Shank Blade Set includes a variety of replacement blades for your BladeRunner X2. Cut wood, metal, tile, plastic and more! Order now.

Description




Swap these t-shank blades in and out of your BladeRunner in seconds, without the need for tools


Includes 10 blades for cutting metal, aluminum, ceramic and wood


Compatible with the BladeRunner (RK7321) and BladeRunner X2 (RK7323)


Multiple wood cutting blades allow you to choose between smooth or scroll cuts


Replacement blades may be stored directly in your BladeRunner
