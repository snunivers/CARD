HomeRight Finish Max Sprayer

$ 59.99 Add to Cart Find a Retailer
Whether you're looking for a quality paint sprayer, are an avid Do-It-Yourselfer, or simply want to paint the easy and fun way, the Finish Max is for you! The Finish Max sprays a smooth, factory-like finish with no compressor required.

The Finish Max paint sprayer lays down a fine, quality finish with minimal overspray. Applies paint or stain quickly, saving time when compared to using a brush or roller. Ideal for spraying crafts, dressers, doors, furniture and cabinets.

Features

  • Ideal for small to medium projects (furniture, crafts, dressers, cabinets, etc.)
  • Spray chalk-type paints, cabinet paints, furniture paints, stain, poly and latex
  • 2.0 mm brass spray tip and durable housing for longer life and improved performance
  • Three different spray patterns: horizontal, vertical, and circular
  • Adjust the trigger control knob to change spray pattern from 1” to 6” wide
  • 400 watts of power
  • 27 ounce container capacity
  • Sprays evenly to lay down a professional finish
