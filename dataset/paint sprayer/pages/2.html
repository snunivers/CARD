Finex FX3000

The FX3000 is a full size HVLP spray gun for larger jobs and the gun of choice when HVLP is required.

Innovations of the Finex gun include 29% fewer parts, precision lids, repair kits, optional 3M™ PPS™ adapter, optional interchangeable aluminum cups and lowest trigger pull among all guns in its class.
