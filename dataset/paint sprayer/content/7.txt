Features



                Product Brochure
                















                            DIY homeowners, including those new to paint sprayers, breeze through paint jobs with the Magnum Project Painter Plus. This sprayer has the capacity to handle most interior and exterior home improvement projects and lets you spray directly from a 1 or 5 gallon container to finish faster.
                        




Stainless Steel Piston Pump
 - Delivers high pressure power to spray unthinned paints


Adjustable Pressure Contro
l - Easy control of paint flow


Flexible Suction Tube
 - Spray directly from 1 or 5 gallon containers for small or large projects


TrueAirless Spray Tip with SoftSpray Technology
 - greater control and less overspray with a wide range of coatings


Power Flush Adapter
 - Connects to a garden hose for fast and easy cleaning


Compact Design
 - Stable, low profile with integrated pail hook so everything can be carried together


Extra Storage Compartments
 - Space to store the spray gun, extra spray tips, and power cord

Home Project Solutions
























Cabinets


Painting or staining your cabinets can completely transform the look of your kitchen or bathroom. Since many rooms with cabinets usually get a lot of traffic and activity, you’ll want to get this project done fast.




















Ceilings


Painting textured ceilings with paint rollers takes time and can cause the texture to flake off from the surface. Using an airless sprayer to paint your ceilings is easier and saves you time.




















Decks


Applying a new coat of finish to your deck will protect the wood from the sun and harsh weather and make all the difference in the appearance of your entire home.




















Doors & Trim


Painting trim and doors in a room can often be overlooked. A new or updated color like white or stain can put the finishing touch on a professional-looking interior painting project.




















Exterior Walls


Add value and curb appeal to the exterior of your home with a fresh coat of paint. Learn how to save time while still achieving proper paint coverage.
