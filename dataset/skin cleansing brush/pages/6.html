Skinvigorate Sonic™ Skin Care System

Skinvigorate Sonic™ Skin Care System removes four times more dirt, oil, impurities, makeup and pollutants – including tiny pollution particles known as PM2.5 – than hand-cleansing!* With an oscillating brush head that can move back and forth over 12,000 times per minute, this device is designed to give you a deep clean that gently lifts away pore-clogging impurities.

*Results based on a one-day independent clinical study during which 21 women used cleanser with the Skinvigorate Sonic™ Skin Care System + Skinvigorate Sonic™ Facial Cleansing Brush Head for 15 seconds

How It Works

  • Rechargeable battery with USB cord means no searching for replacement batteries.
  • Red and green LED indicators show when device needs charging and when it is fully charged.
  • Full charge allows for up to 30 days of usage (when used 4 minutes per day).
  • Safely go from sink to shower, thanks to IPX7 waterproof standard.**

**Fully submerging device in water is not recommended.

Key Benefits

Customized cleansing: Featuring three speeds – gentle, standard and power – this device allows you to control your cleansing with the touch of a button.

Consistent performance: An automatic, one-minute timer ensures a superior clean every time.

Multiple attachments: Enjoy even more skin benefits from this device with your favorite serum† and the Skinvigorate Sonic™ Facial Massage Head.†† Simply snap on and snap off to transform your device in an instant.

†The device was not designed to be paired with acne or sunscreen products.
