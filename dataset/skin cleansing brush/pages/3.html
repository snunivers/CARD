Frequently Asked Questions

Steaming your face opens clogged pores while loosening unwanted buildup (dirt, oils, makeup) for a deeper cleanse. Steaming also helps decongest and detoxify the skin, fight bacteria and inflammation, and increase circulation.
Ionic steam is made of nano-sized water molecules to hydrate and soften the skin’s surface. Smaller in size, ionic steam particles penetrate the layers of the skin for a deep, therapeutic massage and nourishing cleansing experience.
Yes, you can steam your face daily.
If you are looking for a way to decompress and relax after a long day, we would recommend steaming your face. There is no limit to how many times a week or how much you can steam. If you are needing extra hydration, you can steam twice a week or do two cycles of steam each time.
It is best to steam before exfoliating your skin to allow pores to be cleansed and remain open for deeper extraction of debris and bacteria. Steam to soften sebum and unclog pores, then proceed with chemical or mechanical exfoliation.
After steaming your face, you can either apply your favorite skincare or you can begin your makeup routine. After steaming, your pores are wide open, and deeply cleansed which is a great time to apply your skincare to ensure it gets fully absorbed and to maximize the hydration effects. If steaming is a part of your pre-makeup routine, we recommend applying your skincare then beginning your makeup routine. Applying your makeup after you steam ensures that your makeup lays on your skin smoothing and gives your skin an extra hydrated glow!
Gently turn your facial steamer around. Grip the water tank from the bottom and push it down, like a button. Your water tank will pop up and you can gently pull it out. Now, you're ready to steam!
