The silicone bristles are softer than your typical brush and more sanitary for all the germaphobes out there, but still left 87% with squeaky-clean skin.

We were particularly impressed by the soft, hygienic silicone bristles that are known for being kind to the skin barrier. Cleaning those hard-to-reach areas (think around the nose and eyes) for a through cleanse that eliminated our post-workout sweat and bacteria immediately.

HOW TO USE

Simple & easy

STEP 1

Unlock device

Start by unlocking your device and registering your warranty via the FOREO For You app. Download the app here.

STEP 2

Apply cleanser

Dampen your face and apply your regular face cleanser. For optimal results, we recommend FOREO Micro-Foam Cleanser. Wet LUNATM 3 and press the universal button to turn on your device.

STEP 3

Cleanse

Glide device in circular motions over your face until skin is cleansed, for no longer than 1 minute. Rinse cleanser and device and pat dry face.

STEP 4

Firm

Apply FOREO SERUM SÉRUM SERUM to clean, dry skin. Follow instructions in FOREO app to activate preferred firming massage routine.
