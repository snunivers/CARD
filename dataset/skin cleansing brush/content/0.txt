Facial cleansing brushes
Facial cleansing just like in a beauty salon – our facial cleansing brushes enable deep cleansing with up to 6 times more thorough cleansing action than by hand. Different brush heads are available to suit your individual skin type. They can be used to both cleanse sensitive facial skin and to remove daily make-up thoroughly. The rotating motion of the brushes also makes it easy to remove dead skin cells and dirt in the pores. The electric facial brushes are particularly suitable for thorough facial care on sensitive skin.
