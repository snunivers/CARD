  HOW TO USE




  Simple & easy











































STEP 1






Unlock device






Start by unlocking your device and registering your warranty via the FOREO For You app. 
Download
 the app here.




























STEP 2






Apply cleanser






Dampen your face and apply your regular face cleanser. For optimal results, we recommend FOREO Micro-Foam Cleanser. Wet LUNA
TM
 3 and press the universal button to turn on your device.

Cleanse






Glide device in circular motions over your face until skin is cleansed, for no longer than 1 minute. Rinse cleanser and device and pat dry face.

Firm






Apply FOREO SERUM SÉRUM SERUM to clean, dry skin. Follow instructions in FOREO app to activate preferred firming massage routine.
