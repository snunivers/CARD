The Troy-Bilt® Pony® 42 helps you tackle yardwork with ease. The Step-Thru™ frame is designed for easy on-and-off access with ample legroom. 7-speed Shift-on-the-Go™ transmission lets you quickly adjust and control the mowing speed.
