Elevate Your Grill Game

For those who crave the next level of innovation with features such as our Kontrol Tower™ Top Vent for precise temperature control and Air Lift™ hinge to easily lift the grill dome, this grill is ideal to master any dish with convenience and ease.

Kamado Joe Classic Joe Series II Features

Air Lift Hinge

Prevent your grill dome from slamming during lifting or closing with our Air Lift Hinge. This feature significantly reduces dome weight, so you can lift with ease of a finger and precisely control the opening location of the grill.

2-tier Divide & Conquer Cooking System

With its multi-level, half-moon design, The Divide & Conquer Flexible Cooking System allows you to prepare multiple foods in various temperatures and styles simultaneously.
