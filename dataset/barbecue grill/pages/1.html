Pit Boss Classic Wood Pellet Grill

SKU: 71705

Buy at Walmart
The Pit Boss Classic offers the best value per square inch in the pellet grill industry! The Classic has been perfectly designed to be as versatile as it is practical. With a 700 sq. in. porcelain coated steel cooking surface including a second tier rack, it’s perfect for a group of four to six.

This 8-in-1 grill ranges in cooking temperature from 180° to 500°F. Select your preferred cooking styles with the digital control board and standard flame broiler by sliding the plate and sear it over an open flame. No need for natural gas or propane—our wood pellet fuel produces an exceptional taste and is made of 100% Natural Hardwood Pellets.

With heavy gauge steel components, complete structural strength and durability, The Classic is meant to last. Grill, smoke, bake, roast, sear, braise, barbecue or char-grill to perfection with the Pit Boss Classic.
