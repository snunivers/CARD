Claber 8053 Oasis Automatic Drip Watering System

$125.00

The Oasis is a gravity fed watering system that has 4 independent watering programs. These programs can be selected by simply turning the dial to water a maximum of 20 plants for a maximum duration of 40 days. LED light located on the programming panel indicates when unit is in use or when battery is low. Isolated tank prevents condensation and non-slip rubber feet avoid scratching of surface or sliding of tank. Operates on one 9v battery (not included).

  • Made In Italy
  • Timer has 4 program settings: 10, 20, 30 and 40 days to water up to 20 plants

  • Includes a 6.6 gallon polypropylene plastic reservoir, 33-feet of PVC hose, 20 drippers and 20 spikes
  • Operates on a gravity feed; one 9V alkaline battery required (not included)
