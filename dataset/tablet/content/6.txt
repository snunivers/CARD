Acer Iconia Tab P10


Shop Now











                        Sit down with your family and watch movies, read books, or play games together thanks to the Acer Iconia Tab P10’s large 10.4” display and 2K (2000 X 1200) resolution. Its thin and light design and long battery life make it easy to use at home or on the road.
                    




























































Big Screen & High Resolution


Watch movies whenever and wherever you want. This 10.4-inch big screen tablet features 2K (2000 X 1200) resolution and a narrow bezel design that makes it easy to get comfortable and immerse yourself in a movie.




















Take it Anywhere


This tablet’s aluminum alloy design keeps it thin and lightweight, measuring 7.8mm thin and weighing 440 grams. In-Cell Touch Panel (iTP) technology also reduces display thickness while improving optical performance.




















Built to Perform


This Android 12 tablet lets you watch videos, save pictures, and play games with ease thanks to its MediaTek Kompanio 500 Octa-Core processor, up to 4 GB of memory, 64 GB of eMMC storage, and a MicroSD slot.




















All-day Battery


Equipped with a 6,000mAh battery and a USB Type-C port for charging, this tablet gives you up to 10.5 hours
1
 of video playback so that you can take this tablet with you anywhere without worrying about running out of power.




















Video Calls Made Easy


This tablet features dual-band Wi-Fi and an up to 5MP camera so that your video calls are always crystal clear. The 8MP Auto Focus rear camera also lets you take high-resolution pictures on-the-fly.




















Apps for Kids


Google Kids Space gives you and your child a safe place to explore quality content designed to help them learn and keep them engaged. Parents can also help manage the content, screen time, and more with the Family Link app.
