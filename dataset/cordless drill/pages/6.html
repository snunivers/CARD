There’s a SKIL System for Every Job. Choose Your Power

DL529302

Your Versatile Power Drill. Drive through a variety of surfaces with confidence and precision with the PWRCORE 20™ Brushless 1/2 Inch Drill Driver. This cordless drill’s brushless motor provides power in every drive, and the speed settings provides maximum control for every project. Choose from 17 torque settings and an added drilling mode for just about any application. This Cordless Drill Driver Kit comes with a PWRCORE 20™ 2.0Ah Lithium Battery and Automatic PWRJUMP™ Charger.

What's Included: Drill/Driver; 2.0Ah Battery with PWR ASSIST™ Mobile Charging; PWR JUMP™ Charger; Belt Clip; PH2 bit

Features

  • 20V CORDLESS DRILL DRIVER KIT—Includes a PWRCORE 20™ 2.0Ah Lithium Battery with PWRASSIST™ USB Port and PWRJUMP™ Charger.
  • HIGH/LOW SPEED SETTINGS—Low speed is ideal for high torque fastening applications, while high speed is perfect for accurate drilling applications.
  • QUICK BIT CHANGES—1/2 inch single-sleeve, keyless metal chuck allows for easy bit changes to keep your project going.
  • DIGITAL BRUSHLESS MOTOR—Provides efficient, high-performance power and extended tool life.
  • LONGER RUN TIME & BATTERY LIFE—Industry leading PWRCORE 20™ lithium battery technology wraps each cell with cooling material to keep the battery powering on for 25% longer run time and 2X battery life.
  • POWER ON THE GO—Plug in to the battery’s USB port wherever, whenever with PWRASSIST™ mobile charging.
  • CHARGES IN 5 MINUTES—Automatic PWRJUMP™ charges the battery in just 5 minutes, or 100% battery in 30 minutes.

Specs

  • Chuck capacity: 1/2 IN.
  • Chuck type: Keyless Metal Chuck
  • Clutch settings: 17+1
  • LED light: Yes, with pre-light and after-glow
  • Net weight exclu. battery: 2.23 lbs. (1.01 kg)
  • No-load speed: 0-480/0-1800 rpm
  • Variable speed switch: Yes
  • Voltage: 20V
