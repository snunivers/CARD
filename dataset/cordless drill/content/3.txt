18V EC Brushless Connected-Ready Brute Tough 1/2 In. Drill/Driver Kit with (2) CORE18V 4.0 Ah Compact Batteries




Next-gen drill/driver– engineered with Bluetooth® connectivity (when the optional Connectivity Tool Module is installed), KickBack Control, 25+1 precision clutch and EC Brushless motor


Bluetooth® connectivity – which connects to an optional Connectivity Tool Module, linking the tool to the free Bosch Toolbox app on any connected device to customize settings and provide detailed feedback for optimized tool control


Two CORE18V batteries – provide next-gen Li-ion 21700 cell technology in lightweight, high-power batteries with all-day performance


KickBack Control – reduces the risk from sudden rotational torque reaction in a bind-up scenario


EC Brushless motor – efficient motor for a tool that weighs just 3.4 Lbs. (tool only, not including battery but that delivers 755 In.-Lbs. of torque with a long lifetime


Precision clutch with 25+1 settings – provides driving accuracy for reduced fastener damage


Upgraded all-metal chuck – for more durability, along with first-rate bit retention


Brute Tough design – robust drill/driver that provides ample power for tough jobs


Outstanding control – variable drilling speeds for high-speed (0-2, 100 RPM) and high power (0 to 480 RPM)


Sure-grip side-assist handle – helps prevent slipping and increases control during use


Ergonomic grip zone – soft grip and improved design enables better handling and greater comfort


Electronic motor and cell protection – protects the tool and battery from overload for longer lifetimes longer life in tool and battery, protects from overload


Longer belt clip – for a more secure hold


Drill-area LED with feedback – illuminates dark zones; provides tool-status change feedback

GSR18V-755CB25 Professional: Further information


Product Highlights


This Bosch Drill/driver delivers next-gen innovation with Brute Tough power, in a kit with two CORE18V batteries that keep the power and lose the weight. This GSR18V-755C 18V EC Brushless Connected-Ready Brute Tough 1/2 In. Drill/Driver connects to an optional Connectivity Tool Module, linking the tool to the free Bosch Toolbox app on any connected device for enhanced tool control and detailed feedback. It has a powerful EC Brushless motor, that provides efficient power for up to 755 In.-Lbs. of torque, but it weighs only 3.5 Lbs. (tool only, not including battery). It has variable speed settings: high speed (0-2,100 rpm) and high power (0-480 rpm). The kit features two lightweight, high-power CORE18V batteries, with next-gen Li-ion 21700 cell technology for all-day performance. The drill has KickBack Control, which reduces the risk from sudden rotational torque reaction in a bind-up scenario. The 25+1 precision clutch reduces fastener damage. It has an all-metal chuck that provides durability, and it has a longer belt clip for a more secure hold.
