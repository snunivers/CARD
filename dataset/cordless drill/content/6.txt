Features 
 




20V CORDLESS DRILL DRIVER KIT
—Includes a 
PWR
CORE 20™ 2.0Ah Lithium Battery with 
PWR
ASSIST™ USB Port and 
PWR
JUMP™ Charger.


HIGH/LOW SPEED SETTINGS
—Low speed is ideal for high torque fastening applications, while high speed is perfect for accurate drilling applications.


QUICK BIT CHANGES
—1/2 inch single-sleeve, keyless metal chuck allows for easy bit changes to keep your project going.


DIGITAL BRUSHLESS MOTOR
—Provides efficient, high-performance power and extended tool life.


LONGER RUN TIME & BATTERY LIFE
—Industry leading 
PWR
CORE 20™ lithium battery technology wraps each cell with cooling material to keep the battery powering on for 25% longer run time and 2X battery life.


POWER ON THE GO
—Plug in to the battery’s USB port wherever, whenever with 
PWR
ASSIST™ mobile charging.


CHARGES IN 5 MINUTES
—Automatic 
PWR
JUMP™ charges the battery in just 5 minutes, or 100% battery in 30 minutes.
