Product Overview
 
Complete jobs in tight spots with the lightweight 20V MAX* Compact Drill/Driver. Featuring a high-performance motor and a 2-speed transmission, this drill delivers up to 300 UWO for a wide range of applications. For convenience this kit includes (2) 20V MAX* batteries, a charger, and a durable storage bag for portability.
 
 
 
 
Additional Features
 
1/2" single sleeve ratcheting chuck provides tight bit gripping strength
Ergonomic handle delivers comfort and control
New 18V XR Li-Ion compact drill driver featuring XR 1.3Ah Li-Ion battery technology


 
 
Includes
 
 
 (2) 20V MAX* Compact Li-ion battery packs 
 
 (1) Compact charger 
 
 (1) Contractor bag 
 
 (1) DCD771 1/2" Drill driver
