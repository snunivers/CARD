The Bosch machine set (tool) TE-TK 18/2 Li Kit (CD+AG) offers basic tools – always at hand. The powerful Power X-Change machine set consists of cordless drill/driver and cordless angle grinder. It allows cordless freedom and is essential for many DIY tasks in the home, workshop or garage. A lot of basic work can be done with it. The cordless drill/driver GSR18V-755CB25 is equipped with a powerful motor and high-quality 2-speed metal gearbox. The single-sleeved quick-action chuck has a quick-stop function. The torque-slip clutch allows sensitive work especially at low speeds and in soft materials. The TC-AG 18/115 Li cordless angle grinder has a robust aluminium gearbox housing. It is equipped with soft start and restart protection for increased user safety. A modified air duct provides better cooling and thus ensures longevity.
