Join us in celebrating the arrival of one of America's Hottest Styling Tools...the NEW award-winning CurlBar from our #BlackGold range.

From the makers of the #1 best-selling salon curling irons, Hot Tools introduces the CURLBAR™ Curling Iron.

The iron is designed to set the “bar” as the newest, most innovative curling iron in the professional salon industry. The CURLBAR™ is ergonomically angled and uniquely shaped like a 7 which offers “A New Angle On Curls”.

The curling irons shape helps the stylist achieve beautiful, long-lasting curls in seconds without strain on their wrist, shoulder and elbow. (Stylist…can you imagine going an entire day of styling without shoulder, elbow or wrist strain?)

With Hot Tools CURLBAR™ curling iron, this dream becomes a reality. Another distinctive feature is the first of its kind, an optional timer feature. The timer can be set according to the client’s hair type and texture which assists the stylist create a full head of curls in minutes.
