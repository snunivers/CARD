THE DETAILS

The REMINGTON® Pro 1" - 1½" Pearl Ceramic Conical Curling Wand features Professional Pearl Ceramic - ceramic infused with real crushed pearls creates fast, salon results with less damage. The ceramic provides even, consistent heat while styling, and the crushed pearl helps create smooth, frizz defying waves. The wide conical barrel (1”-1½”) creates beautiful tousled waves. Digital controls and nine heat settings (max 410°F) make it easy to select the right temperature for your hair. The temperature lock function allows you to lock the styler at your preferred temperature during styling. A heat protective glove has been included!
