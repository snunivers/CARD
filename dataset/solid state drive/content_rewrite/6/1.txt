Kingston's Kingston FURY Renegade PCIe 4.0 NVMe M.2 SSD uses the latest 3D NAND technology that features vertical, stacked expansion of storage cells. By using high-quality flash memory and enhanced firmware algorithms, the Kingston FURY Renegade PCIe 4.0 NVMe M.2 SSD delivers greater performance and reliability.

3D expansion to break through limits

Unlike planar NAND memory, 3D NAND flash features memory cells stacked vertically in multiple layers. 3D NAND overcomes density limitations of 2D planar NAND, and delivers a higher level of performance and endurance.

Performance boost for everyday computing

Taking full advantage of the PCIe 4.0 NVMe M.2 interface and built-in SLC caching technology, Kingston's Kingston FURY Renegade PCIe 4.0 NVMe M.2 SSD achieves exceptional transfer speeds of up to 560MB/s read and 520MB/s write.

Guaranteed endurance and reliability

Kingston's Kingston FURY Renegade PCIe 4.0 NVMe M.2 SSD offers great Terabytes Written (TBW) values (up to 2,240 TB) indicating the total amount of data you can write into the drive over its lifetime.
