Flexibility defines every coffee maker in the line. coffee makers allow you to brew a delicious cup of coffee using single-serve pods such as K-Cup® pods* or your favorite ground coffee.

With a coffee maker in your kitchen, you’ll enjoy maximum brewing flexibility. You can brew into a cup or travel mug, enjoy up to 10 oz. of coffee with a single-serve pod, or brew up to 14 oz. with ground coffee. With the Barista Express, you can brew a single cup or a full carafe, or even program your pot of coffee to be ready when you wake up. With all this flexibility, you’re sure to find the coffee maker that fits your needs.
