Gaggia Cadorna Prestige





                  The Gaggia Cadorna Prestige super-automatic espresso machine allows you to fully express yourself using 4 user profiles for distinct, customized drinks. A vibrant full color display offers beautifully easy navigation through menus and the Cadorna Prestige's 14 specialty drinks. An integrated milk carafe delivers coffee specialties in one touch, and can be removed for storage in the refrigerator.

Integrated Milk Carafe


The integrated milk carafe delivers frothed milk automatically for quick and easy coffee specialties and can be removed for storage in the refrigerator.












Intuitive Interface


A vibrant LCD presents each drink option colorfully, navigated by an array of tactile buttons.












Espresso Tray


A specialized tray slides forward to provide a space for an espresso cup closer to the spouts, ensuring the crema is not broken apart during extraction.












14 Specialties


The Cadorna can produce 14 personalized beverages including ristretto, espresso, espresso lungo, coffee, americano, cappuccino, cappuccino XL, café au lait, café cortado, latte macchiato, latte macchiato XL, flat white, frothed milk, hot water.
