Power meets precision with ER-SB40 Men's Trimmer. A high-performance linear motor makes 9,800 cuts per minute, powering ultra-sharp blades through even thick, dense hair with smoothness and ease. The advanced, wide-tipped blades are uniquely designed to capture more hair in one pass, lifting and cutting through even the most unruly hair.




Features










A high-performance linear motor delivers 9,800 cuts per minute while ultra-sharp wide-tipped blades capture more hair to smoothly cut through thick, dense hair quickly with no tugging
A quick-adjust dial and comb attachment provides 19 precision length settings (0.5MM to 10MM) for personalized sculpting, trimming, cutting and detailing
Ergonomic, rubberized grip provides better control while trimming
Use the mustache trimmer cordless anytime for up to 60 minutes with rechargeable battery power, or plug in and use corded for longer use
A waterproof body rinses clean under running water and allows you to trim cordlessly in the shower when unplugged from an AC outlet
