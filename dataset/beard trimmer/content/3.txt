          Description
        
 
 
 
A crowd favorite — our tried and true Bevel Trimmer covers the basics and more, earning a permanent counter spot. Up to zero gap, no tools required, and crisp fades for the rest of your days.
Packaging may vary.
 
 
 
The Basics
 
 
 

        Up to Zero-Gap
      
 
Say goodbye to fumbling fingers and screwdrivers with the Bevel Dial. Thanks to innovative design, any blade adjustment is literally a thumb-turn away.

        FOR BEST USE
      
 
To get the best cut, start by adjusting the Bevel Blade to your liking. Just take the blade off of your Trimmer and adjust the gap between the cutting blade and guide blade a few times until you find a length that gives you a shave you love without irritating your skin.

        FOR BEST CARE
      
 
The Bevel Blade was designed to repel dirt, oil and buildup that can lead to irritation. For a simple cleaning, wipe it with a damp cloth, or use a brush to more deeply clean the blade, firmly holding its base to ensure it stays in place.

        Design
      
 
The Bevel Trimmer is built to the balance specs of master barbers. When you hold it, it should feel at home in your hand. Together, the soft-touch grip and custom-faceted body give you superior control and 360° non-slip handling. It also runs cooler than other trimmers so you can cut comfortably, at your own pace.
