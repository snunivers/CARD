The Basics

Up to Zero-Gap
Say goodbye to fumbling fingers and screwdrivers with the Andis Dial. Thanks to innovative design, any blade adjustment is literally a thumb-turn away.

FOR BEST USE
To get the best cut, start by adjusting the Andis Blade to your liking. Just take the blade off of your Master Cordless Lithium Ion Trimmer and adjust the gap between the cutting blade and guide blade a few times until you find a length that gives you a shave you love without irritating your skin.

FOR BEST CARE
The Andis Blade was designed to repel dirt, oil and buildup that can lead to irritation. For a simple cleaning, wipe it with a damp cloth, or use a brush to more deeply clean the blade, firmly holding its base to ensure it stays in place.
