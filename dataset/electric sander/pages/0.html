5" RANDOM ORBIT PALM GRIP SANDER

  • HIGH-OUTPUT FOR PRO-GRADE RESULTS: Provides maximum sanding speed for smooth finishes
  • EASY OPERATION AND MINIMAL SCRAPING: Controlled Finishing System maintains controllable pad speed and minimizes gouging during start-up
  • REDUCES USER FATIGUE with dual-plane, counter-balanced fan
View more features

Product Overview

Take on demanding jobs with this high-performance 5 in. Random Orbit Sander. Get smooth, expert-level finishes with this sander’s controlled finishing system that delivers easy operability and less gouging during start-up. Cut user fatigue with its dual-plane fan and tackle routine tasks with the confidence and protection provided by a dust-sealed switch that defends against dust ingestion for longer life.

Additional Features

  • PROTECTS AGAINST DUST INGESTION: Dust-sealed switch keeps harmful particles out for long tool life
  • HIGH-ENDURANCE CONSTRUCTION: Sealed 100% ball-bearing
  • SPECIALIZED COMPATIBILITY: Accepts 5 in., 8-hole hook-and-loop paper

Includes

  • (1) Sander
  • (1) Operating Manual
