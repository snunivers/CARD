The BDCMS20C 20V MAX Lithium Ion Mouse Sander has a 3 amp motor that delivers between 8,000 to 12,000 OPM. The separate counterweight reduces vibration and along with the rubber overmold grip provides comfortable sanding. A shorter design allows users to get closer to the work surface and the one-handed locking dust bag attaches securely to the sander to aid in dust collection or the user can attach the sander directly to the BLACKDECKER dust collectors.

Rubber over-mold in all of the critical areas of the sander
Improved dust-sealed switch to protect against dust ingestion for longer switch life
Improved dust collection and one-handed locking dust bag
Dust port is designed to fit directly to the BLACKDECKER dust collectors
To attach dust port to other vacuum systems with 1-1/4 in. hoses, use BLACKDECKER Universal Quick Connector

Sanding Wood
