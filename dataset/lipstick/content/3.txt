Overview










DRAMATIC DEPTH. UNSTOPPABLE CHARACTER.



Unadulterated luxury lipstick glides on like satin across skin with single-stroke coverage in a spectrum of shades. Bold, ultra-saturated color feels hydrating and stays put—does not bleed or feather. Features an embossed NARS logo on the lipstick bullet.


DETAILS




Single-stroke, full coverage


Satin finish


Long-lasting





















Reviews

















More Reviews












Benefits












Weightless and creamy texture applies smoothly and evenly to deliver completecoverage with just one swipe.


High-impact pigments deliver intense color that lasts without bleeding or feathering.













How to use












For full coverage with one stroke, apply directly from the bullet or with the 
#30 Precision Lip Brush
.


To create a perfectly defined lip, apply Instant Line and Pore Perfector around the edge of the mouth and pat in. Apply 
Radiant Creamy Concealer
 on top and blend. Line lips with 
Velvet Lip Liner
 and fill in. Finish with Audacious Lipstick.
