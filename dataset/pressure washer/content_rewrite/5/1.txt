Kärcher's Italian Manufactured K5 Premium is the new standard in the foaming industry. Whether you are foaming down your car on the weekend or you are a professional contract cleaner foaming hog barn for hours, this tool will save you time and money. Simply attach the K5 Premium to your pressure washer using one of the included fittings, fill with your desired ratio of chemical and you are up and running.

The K5 Premium launched in 2017 as the latest and greatest foam cannon to hit the world stage. The K5 Premium offers both, adjustable fan blades, and a two stage nozzle allowing you to spray vertically and horizontally. By adjusting the fan blades (narrow or jet) Kärcher Foam cannons throw thick foam onto your vehicle, pole barn siding, house, fence, sidewalk, roof or other washable items.
