CD-1002-3MUH

This electric series is lightweight and a perfect option for effectively washing wooden surfaces, driveways, sidewalks and siding.

Pump
Forged brass manifold · Thermal and safety relief valves · Built-in low pressure detergent injector

Motor
Totally enclosed fan-cooled electric motor with magnetic circuit breaker · 35-foot, 15-amp power cord with GFCI

Frame
Powder coated steel mounting plate with center balanced lift and four rubber isolators

Unit Includes
Variable high/low pressure nozzle · 25-foot x 3⁄8-inch non-marking hose · Two-piece, 36-inch lance with insulated trigger gun
