The Duplex is a completely free-standing, 4 season backpacking tent for 1 person. Its quick pitching and large vestibule make it ideal for any time of the year.

A completely free-standing, 4 season backpacking tent for one person. It pitches quickly with the flysheet and inner together.

The Duplex takes its design inspiration from the award-winning Laser range of lightweight tents. Includes an additional pole to provide extra stability and structure. Pitching is quick and easy as the flysheet and inner are erected as one and the flysheet and groundsheet are fully taped providing complete waterproof protection. Due to its freestanding nature, pegging is kept to a minimum. There is a spacious inner and porch, the porch door can be fixed open in several ways to provide the most suitable weather protection for the current conditions.
