The NEMO® Dragonfly Osmo Backpacking Tent IX™ WGTE is a sleek angled compact two-person tent. It is a ‘Fly-First’ pitch tent that allows a quick and easy set-up with an opposing pole design, that keeps condensation buildup to a minimum. The inner tent is made of a No-See-Um Mesh Mosquito Net to keep out bugs, snakes, etc. The Dragonfly Osmo Backpacking Tent IX™ WGTE will shelter you from the worst four-season weather, it has a 5000mm hydrostatic head (HH) polyurethane (PU) coated flysheet, with taped seams. The Dragonfly Osmo Backpacking Tent IX™ WGTE has an impressive sleeping space, measuring 205cm/81” long and 110cm/43” wide, creating ample room for two people in a top-toe sleeping arrangement with an additional spacious front porch for extra storage. In an emergency, there is enough space to accommodate up to six people sitting down. The sleek angled profile of the Dragonfly Osmo Backpacking Tent IX™ WGTE tent packs down small with a light trail weight of 4.9lbs / 2.2kg.
