Description
Your new go-to home in the hills, the Limelight 2-Person Tent is a roomy tent with pre-bent poles that add space without adding weight. Extra-wide doors makes it easy to move gear in and out, and double vestibules plus side wings add tons of storage space.
Features
Zone pre-bend construction creates vertical walls for a roomier sleeping area and greater headroom; freestanding design with two D-shaped doors
Two vestibules and additional side wings for gear storage
Seam-taped bathtub floor helps keep water out
Laundry line on top of tent for hanging gear or clothing
Interior pockets; lampshade pocket securely holds your headlamp to provide ambient light
Footprint included
