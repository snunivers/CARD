Description








When you’re ready to put in the time and miles to get to the remote and beautiful places outdoors, the right tent can make all the difference. The Mountain Ultra Tent comes in different sizes to fit your needs. Choose a 1, 2, 3, or 4-person tent depending on how many adventurers you’re taking with you. It’s hassle-free so you don’t spend your time wrestling with the setup. Lightweight, waterproof, and made for all types of weather with a built-in ventilation system for added comfort. When it’s time to head back home the Mountain Ultra Tent fits easily into its storage bag. It’s camping made easy.


Lightweight backpacking tent


Easy 1 person setup


Reinforced corners, seams, and anchor points


Waterproofed rainfly has reinforced, heat-sealed seams


Built-in gear pockets


Innovative doors have an easy-rolling, two-way zipper


Two doors

Shipping Info








Please note: We do not ship to P.O. boxes. 
Please provide a physical shipping address when checking out.
We ship from our warehouse in Salt Lake City, Utah via various methods, including USPS and FedEx. If an item is available for sale, it is in stock in our warehouse and will ship as soon as the payment processes, usually within 1 business day of your order.
Once shipped, orders typically take approximately 3 to 5 business days to arrive. 
Standard shipping is free for orders $75 and above—after discounts are applied and before taxes and shipping are calculated—to destinations in the continental 48 US states.
When you place an order, you will get a confirmation email. Once your order has left our warehouse, we will email your tracking information to you. Your carrier can give you an estimated arrival date. Most carriers provide step-by-step updates if you request them.
Express shipping is available via FedEx. 
Rates are calculated by package weight and destination. These options and their rates will be available during checkout.
