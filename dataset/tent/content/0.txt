The Snugpak® Scorpion 2 IX™ WGTE is a sleek angled compact two-person tent. It is a ‘Fly-First’ pitch tent that allows a quick and easy set-up with an opposing pole design, that keeps 
condensation
 buildup to a minimum. The inner tent is made of a No-See-Um Mesh Mosquito Net to keep out bugs, snakes, etc. 
The Scorpion 2 IX™ WGTE will shelter you from the worst four-season weather, it has a 5000mm 
hydrostatic head
 (HH) polyurethane (PU) coated flysheet, with taped seams. The Scorpion 2 IX™ WGTE has an impressive sleeping space, measuring 205cm/81” long and 110cm/43” wide, creating ample room for two people in a top-toe sleeping arrangement with an additional spacious front porch for extra storage. In an emergency, there is enough space to accommodate up to six people sitting down. 
The sleek angled profile of the Scorpion 2 IX™ WGTE tent packs down small with a light trail weight of 4.9lbs / 2.2kg. Using tried and tested lightweight aluminium poles, it does not take up valuable space in your pack, making it an ideal choice when light weight and small pack size are essential. 
The Scorpion 2 IX™ WGTE is a true tried and tested favourite of mountaineers, climbers, campers & military personnel that protects you all year round from severe weather conditions.
