The Snugpak® Scorpion 2 IX™ WGTE is a sleek angled compact two-person tent. It is a ‘Fly-First’ pitch tent that allows a quick and easy set-up with an opposing pole design, that keeps condensation buildup to a minimum. The inner tent is made of a No-See-Um Mesh Mosquito Net to keep out bugs, snakes, etc.

The Scorpion 2 IX™ WGTE will shelter you from the worst four-season weather, it has a 5000mm hydrostatic head (HH) polyurethane (PU) coated flysheet, with taped seams. The Scorpion 2 IX™ WGTE has an impressive sleeping space, measuring 205cm/81” long and 110cm/43” wide, creating ample room for two people in a top-toe sleeping arrangement with an additional spacious front porch for extra storage. In an emergency, there is enough space to accommodate up to six people sitting down.

The sleek angled profile of the Scorpion 2 IX™ WGTE tent packs down small with a light trail weight of 4.9lbs / 2.2kg. Using tried and tested lightweight aluminium poles, it does not take up valuable space in your pack, making it an ideal choice when light weight and small pack size are essential.

The Scorpion 2 IX™ WGTE is a true tried and tested favourite of mountaineers, climbers, campers & military personnel that protects you all year round from severe weather conditions.

  • Weight: 2.7kg / 5.95lbs
  • Pitch Type: Fly-First
  • Packsize: 43cm x 19cm / 17" x 7"
  • Origin: Imported
  • Waterproof Taped Seams
  • Doors: single-point front entry with ample vestibule
  • Vents: 3 mesh vents
  • 8 Mesh Internal Pockets for equipment and head-end hanging loop (4 on each side)
  • Supplied with 16 Lightweight Alloy Y-Stakes (14 + 2 spare stakes)
  • Flame Retardant
  • Supplied with a Compression Storage Bag
  • Supplied with a Basic Repair Kit (no glue included)
  • Flysheet: 210t 100% polyester ripstop with (5000mm HH) waterproof polyurethane coating
  • Inner Tent: 190t 100% nylon inner fabric, with a 50D polyester No-See-Um Mesh
  • No-See-Um Netting 1600 holes per square inch
  • Groundsheet: 190t 100% nylon with (5000mm HH) waterproof polyurethane coating
  • Lightweight Anodised Aluminium Poles (7001) with pressfit connectors, 100% aluminium
