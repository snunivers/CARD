We don't camp to hang out in our tent all night and day⏤that's why when it's time to zip up into your sleeping bag and lay your head down, the Mineral King™ maintains the view. You can peer out the oversized doors at your alpine oasis and gaze at the starry night thanks to an adaptable fly. So even when you're "inside," it's like you never left what you really came for.
