VT25BIG
























VT25BIG 
Hand Carry Air Compressors



            The VT25BIG boasts the highest air output of any direct drive air compressor in our line-up. The motor-pump delivers a whopping 6.5 CFM @ 90 PSI. The foot-actuated folding handle and heavy-duty flat-free tires make it easy to move the VT25BIG around the job site.

            ABOUT THE PUMP
      






ROLAIR Quality


Single Stage


Ball Bearings


Cast-Iron Cylinder


Stainless-Steel Reed Valves and Splash Lubrication Ensure Longer Life









            ABOUT THE MOTOR
      






2.5 HP


3400 RPM


Overload Protection with Manual Reset


115 Volt


60 Hz


Heavy-Duty/Industrial Grade with Ball Bearings for Greater Durability









            STANDARD EQUIPMENT
      






Heavy Duty Wheels for Greater Jobsite Mobility


Start/Stop Pressure Switch with On/Off Lever


Fold-Down Handle for Easy Storage/Low Profile


User-Friendly Control Panel Including Gauges and 2 Quick-Disconnects,


Safety Relief Valve


Intake Filter with Silencer


13.8 Amp Motor


Initial 10 oz. Oil Fill Included
