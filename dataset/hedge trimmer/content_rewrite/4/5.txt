20V MAX* Lithium Ion Hedge Trimmer (5.0Ah) petrol-driven hedge trimmer: for optimum hedge trimming

General
Trimming version of the professional hedge trimmer with DeWALT 2-MIX engine. Double-sided cutting blade. Suitable for both vertical and horizontal cutting. Low weight and low vibrational strain. Specially tuned drive with up to 5,100 strokes/minute for high blade speed and therefore a fine cut.
