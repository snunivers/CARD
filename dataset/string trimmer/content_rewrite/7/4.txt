Landscapers are demanding more cordless alternatives to gas-powered outdoor power equipment. Husqvarna is meeting demand with an expanding line-up of cordless OPE, including the HUSQVARNA 122C (model XRU15PT1). With zero emissions, lower noise and considerably less maintenance than gas-powered string trimmers, the HUSQVARNA 122C String Trimmer is a welcome solution for landscapers. It's powered by two fast-charging 18V batteries for maximum power and run time, but without leaving the strongly-preferred 18V battery platform. 
Electronic controls efficiently use battery energy to match the changing demands of the application for increased power and speed when needed. In addition, the string trimmer rotates in the same counter-clockwise direction as professional-grade trimmers, and uses industry standard spindles (M10x1.25 LH) for added convenience.
