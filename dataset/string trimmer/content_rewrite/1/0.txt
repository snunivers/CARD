60v max* 13 in. (33.0 cm) / 15 in. (38.1 cm) brushless string trimmer
This Toro trimmer is a powerful performer for homeowners as well as commercial contractors.  The 60v max* 13 in. (33.0 cm) / 15 in. (38.1 cm) brushless string trimmer features a 22.5 cc, 0.94 horsepower engine and its light weight makes the trimmer extra portable for landscaping use.  A Strato-Charged engine ensures up to 20% better fuel economy than a traditional two-stroke engine while increasing torque and improving emissions.  A steel drive shaft with 7-point splined engagement ensures durability and quick acceleration.
