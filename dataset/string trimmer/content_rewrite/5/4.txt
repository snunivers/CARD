Landscapers are demanding more cordless alternatives to gas-powered outdoor power equipment. DeWalt is meeting demand with an expanding line-up of cordless OPE, including the 20V MAX* 13 in. Cordless String Trimmer With Charger and 4.0Ah Battery (model XRU15PT1). With zero emissions, lower noise and considerably less maintenance than gas-powered string trimmers, the 20V MAX* 13 in. Cordless String Trimmer is a welcome solution for landscapers. It's powered by two fast-charging batteries for maximum power and run time, but without leaving the strongly-preferred battery platform. 
Electronic controls efficiently use battery energy to match the changing demands of the application for increased power and speed when needed. In addition, the string trimmer rotates in the same counter-clockwise direction as professional-grade trimmers, and uses industry standard spindles (M10x1.25 LH) for added convenience.
