20V MAX* 10 in. String Trimmer / Edger
This BlackDecker trimmer is a powerful performer for homeowners as well as commercial contractors.  The 20V MAX* 10 in. String Trimmer / Edger features a 22.5 cc, 0.94 horsepower engine and its light weight makes the trimmer extra portable for landscaping use.
