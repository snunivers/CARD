HAVE FUN WITH THE ULTIMATE SHADOW PALETTE FROM NYX


 


Meet the Ultimate Shadow Palette from NYX Cosmetics. A pro-level colourful eyeshadow palette packed with 16 high-pigmented colours. Inspired by the beauty of colour, this eyeshadow palette is available in eight striking colour combos that are too good to miss. 


 


HAVE FUN WITH THE COLOURFUL EYESHADOW PALETTE


 


Your eyelids are your playground, so why not get playful with the Ultimate Shadow Palette in Brights? This high-pigmented, colourful eyeshadow is a must-have, especially for all pop-colour adventurers out there!


 


If you're after that shimmery, fearless party look, the NYX Ultimate Shadow in Coachella is for you. Filled with 16 daring colours, express yourself and create that bold eye look with this glitter eyeshadow palette from NYX Cosmetics.


 


If you prefer a toned-down version, try the Utopia eyeshadow. With grounded undertones and colours, this eyeshadow has the perfect balance of mattes and shimmers, perfect for a subtle yet dreamy eye makeup look. 


 


P.S. Get extra ready with our curated list of FESTIVAL MAKEUP  must-haves. 


 


Whether it's a day or night out, a brown eyeshadow palette is an easy way to switch your eye makeup, and no one does it better than the Ultimate Queen palette. With velvety smooth mattes and metallic colours, this earth-toned shadow is perfect for creating that warm, dimensional eye look on all skin tones. You can also pair this eye look with our 
LIP LINGERIE MATTE LIQUID LIPSTICK COLLECTION
 for an extra pop of warmth.


 


Plus, don't forget to complete your eye look with NYX Cosmetics' 
EYELINER
 and 
MASCARA
 collections. 


 


What are you waiting for? Be the ultimate you with the NYX Ultimate Shadow range!
