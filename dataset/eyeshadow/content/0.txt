Why you'll love it










Eyeshadow goes one and done with your makeup bag’s soon-to-be bestie, Gilded Eyeshadow Sticks.  These pigment-rich, ultra-creamy retractable sticks can be smudged, blended, or precisely applied for an all-over shadow or liner look. The buttery formula glides on seamlessly without any tugging for an effortless shadow look that is waterproof, crease-free & lasts up to 12HRS. This low-maintenance, ultra-versatile formula can be used alone or layered with other eyeshadows.

How to apply it






Apply to lash line as a liner, all over lid as an eyeshadow, or as a base for powder shadows. Immediately blend out edges with fingertips or a brush. Snap cap closed after use to prevent product from drying out.
