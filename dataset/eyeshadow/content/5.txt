Benefits












Contains nine limited-edition shades in matte, satin, shimmer, and glitter finishes for endless artistry.

Delivers rich, high-impact color payoff in one stroke.


Velvety texture applies easily and blends effortlessly to create smooth, even color.










How to Use












Apply shadow directly on top of 
Smudge Proof Eyeshadow Base
 using 
#24 High-Pigment Eyeshadow Brush
 to create full intensity that lasts.


Versatile formula can be used as an eyeshadow or liner, and applied wet or dry.


Layer glitter shades on top of any eye look to create a light-catching finish.
