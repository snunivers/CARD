Features

 

 

Thick Pan (3 mm)


A Thick Pan (3 mm) allows heat levels to respond in perfect accordance with the control program set by the microcomputer.

 

 

Microcomputer Controlled Heating


Microcomputer control and advanced Fuzzy Logic® technology allow for fine-tuned heat regulation.

Various Local Menu Programs

Separate options are provided for cooking short and long grain rice.
