The 6-Quart West Bend Manual Slow Cooker is the perfect kitchen tool for simple delicious home cooking. Place ingredients in the large cooking vessel, set your temperature, and come home to perfectly done ready-to-serve meals. Low, Medium, and High temperature settings offer versatility while cooking and the ceramic crock and tempered glass cover are dishwasher-safe for easy cleanup after the meal is done.

Gorgeous brushed stainless-steel exterior.

Three temperature settings- Warm, Low and High for maximum cooking control.

Large 6-quart capacity serves 4-6 and is ideal for entertaining.

Attractive, removable stoneware insert doubles as a serving dish.

Dishwasher-safe crock and glass cover.

Stay cool plastic handles.

Recipes for stews, soups and meat dishes included in instruction booklet
