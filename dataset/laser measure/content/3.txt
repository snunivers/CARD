The 
DISTO D1
 is capable of more than just measuring. Speed up your workflow with the 
DISTO D1
‘s integrated 
Bluetooth
 functionality – for error-free measuring data transfer and documentation. 
Bluetooth Smart Technology
 allows you to easily document and send data wirelessly from the work site to office.


It is possible to transfer measuring results direct to any smart phone or tablet. Pair your device with the 
DISTO D1
 and, using 
Bluetooth® Smart data transfer
, link to the 
FREE DISTO Sketch App.
 This 
Measuring App
 
allows you to start to creating digital floor plans or data tables on the go. Another handy feature is that dimensions can easily be entered onto site photographs.
