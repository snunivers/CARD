Product Overview
 
The 12V MAX* Green 3x360 Laser features 12V MAX* Lithium-ion  battery and green beam laser technology. This laser is debris and water resistant with an IP65 rating and with an over-molded housing, it can withstand harsh conditions found on the worksite.
 
 
 
 
Additional Features
 
Full-time pulse mode allows use with detector, maintains full brightness for visibility, and extends range
Overmolded housing and IP65 debris/water resistance
Locking pendulum helps prevent damage to internal components
