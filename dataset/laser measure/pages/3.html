Leica DISTO D1

£85.99 £82.50 + VAT

The Leica DISTO D1 is the first in line of the DISTO Family of Bluetooth Laser Distance Meters. This simple model has been stripped back to just the basics. The DISTO D1 gives easy, quick and accurate interior measurements. It is perfectly small, light and fits easily into a pocket or handbag. Whether you are measuring for flooring, curtains or hanging pictures on a wall, the DISTO D1 has it completely covered with its impressive range of 40m.

DIMENSIONS: 11.5 x 4.35 x 2.35 cm

The DISTO D1 is capable of more than just measuring. Speed up your workflow with the DISTO D1‘s integrated Bluetooth functionality – for error-free measuring data transfer and documentation. Bluetooth Smart Technology allows you to easily document and send data wirelessly from the work site to office.

It is possible to transfer measuring results direct to any smart phone or tablet. Pair your device with the DISTO D1 and, using Bluetooth® Smart data transfer, link to the FREE DISTO Sketch App. This Measuring App allows you to start to creating digital floor plans or data tables on the go. Another handy feature is that dimensions can easily be entered onto site photographs.
