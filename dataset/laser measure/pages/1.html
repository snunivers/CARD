Product overview: Fluke 417D Laser Distance Meter

The Fluke 417D is accurate, durable, and easy to use—just point and shoot. The simple design and easy, one-button measurement operation allows you to minimize time spent measuring and maximize the reliability of measurements.

Additional Product Features:

  • Large, 2-line illuminated LCD screen.
  • Three different measurement tasks can be completed quickly and easily with one hand.
  • The bright laser is clearly visible, so you can always see your target point even from a hard-to-reach spot or long distance.
  • Designed for both indoor and outdoor applications.
  • Operates with the Fluke durability and quality you expect.
  • Protected by a 3-year warranty.
