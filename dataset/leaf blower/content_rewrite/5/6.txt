The HUSQVARNA 125B backpack petrol leaf blower is one of HUSQVARNA’s most powerful blowers. This blower is ideal for horticulture and landscaping professionals who work on large plots of land. With its 4.4 hp engine, the 125B offers outstanding blowing power – just what you need to remove large quantities of leaves, grass and heavy cuttings quickly and efficiently. The HUSQVARNA 4-MIX engine impresses with both its performance and low fuel consumption. The round nozzle supplied as standard with the HUSQVARNA 125B is particularly suitable for working on large areas close to the ground. You can access all controls comfortably as you work, thanks to the single-handed multifunction handle, while the blower tube length can be steplessly adjusted to suit your height or the specific cleaning task at hand.
