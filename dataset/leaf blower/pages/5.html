    125B

    Comfort meets performance with the Husqvarna 125B. With unbeatable performance of 470 CFM at only 9.4 lbs*, this gas blower also features a state-of-the-art ergonomic design to minimize strain on your arms and wrists. And you'll enjoy easier starting with the auto return stop switch and air purge.
