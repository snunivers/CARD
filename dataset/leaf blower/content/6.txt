The STIHL BR 800 X MAGNUM® backpack petrol leaf blower is 
one of STIHL’s most powerful blowers
. This blower is 
ideal for horticulture and landscaping professionals
 who work on large plots of land. With its 4.4 hp engine, the BR 800 X MAGNUM® offers 
outstanding blowing power
 – just what you need to 
remove large quantities of leaves, grass and heavy cuttings quickly and efficiently
. The 
STIHL 4-MIX®
 engine impresses with both its performance and low fuel consumption.
The 
round nozzle supplied as standard
 with the STIHL BR 800 X MAGNUM® is particularly suitable for working on large areas close to the ground. You can access all controls comfortably as you work, thanks to the 
single-handed multifunction handle,
 while 
the blower tube length can be steplessly adjusted to suit your height or the specific cleaning task at hand
. You can also change the handle position with no need to use tools. The 
lockable throttle lever with stop button
 lets you lock the engine power setting 
in whatever position you prefer
. The engine can be easily switched off with the push of a button for short interruptions to your work. The STIHL BR 800 X MAGNUM® is then immediately ready to start again. The 
STIHL anti-vibration
 system protects your muscles and joints and supports 
effortless, low-fatigue work
. An 
ergonomic carrying
 system with S-shaped 
shoulder straps
 distributes the weight of the professional leaf blower evenly and comfortably over the shoulders and back. The 
carrying handle and the blower tube holder make it easy to transport
 the power tool to and from the work site.
