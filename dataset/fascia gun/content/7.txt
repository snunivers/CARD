How-to Guide













Getting started




To turn on, firmly press and hold the center button on the button pad.


Press the up and down buttons on the button pad to increase or decrease speed.


To turn off, firmly press and hold the center button on the button pad until the device shuts off.






Connecting the attachments


Align the attachment with the tip of the arm and push to connect. To remove, grip the attachment by placing one finger on each side of the plastic connector. Pull off the attachment while the device is off.
