Product description for EV675 Robot Vacuum Cleaner


The EV675 Robot from BISSELL cleans your floors for you, so you don’t have to! Its Triple-Action Cleaning System utilizes dual edge brushes, a rotating Brush Roll and powerful suction to clean up pet hair and other debris on your floor. It even reaches hard-to-reach spaces like along baseboards and under low furniture. It adapts to clean multiple surfaces, like area rugs, carpet* and hard floors and has smart sensors to detect when it’s approaching stairs or other drop-off points as well as low battery levels so it can return to the dock and recharge. EV675 also has a remote controlled, programmable cleaning schedule so you can control and customize cleans from the comfort of your couch.
Triple Action Cleaning System with dual edge brushes, a rotating Brush Roll and powerful suction to clean up pet hair and other debris on your floor. 
18V Li-Ion Battery for a run time up to 100 minutes on hard floors.
Scheduled Cleaning thanks to a remote controller. It allows to control and customize the cleaning from the comfort of your couch.
Cliff Detection to detect when it’s approaching stairs or other drop-off points as well as low battery levels, so it can return to the dock and recharge
Multi-Surface Adaptability to clean multiple surfaces, like area rugs, carpet and hard floors
