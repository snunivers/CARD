      Details & Features
      











            The 9 Gal.* Wet/Dry Vac, featuring a 4.25 peak HP* motor, is great for home projects. Equipped with one of the most powerful motors compact vacs have to offer, this vac is great for general cleaning, basement cleanup from flooding or seepage, as well as removing water from clogged sinks. With an extra-large drain, liquids can be emptied quickly and easily from the tank. The filter fastening system allows for quick and easy wet dry vacuum filter changes. Store this wet dry vacuum with ease with the on-board hose storage plus accessory and power cord organizers to help eliminate unneeded hassle and reduce overall storage space.

Made in the USA with global materials
