Product Overview
 
The 9 Gallon Wet/Dry Vacuum is built for jobsite cleanups. It's easy to transport and features a powerful, heavy-duty motor for maximum performance. Strong suction delivers the performance you need for tough jobs. It features a fine-dust cartridge filter for small, dry debris and wet materials, a handle for easy carrying, large on/off switch with water-resistant design for quick access, and convenient, built-in 10 ft. power cord. Keep attachments and tools organized with the onboard accessory bag. Swiveling casters provide ease of movement in any direction while a built-in tank drain makes it easy to empty liquids.
 
 
 
 
Additional Features
 
10 ft. power cord with cord wrap helps reach almost any cleanup need
Built-in blower port powerful enough to blow sawdust and debris from most workshops
Accessories included: 1-7/8 in. x 7 ft. hose, 2 extension wands, washable & reusable cartridge filter, floor nozzle, utility nozzle, crevice nozzle, disposable filter bag & mesh accessory storage bag
Large built-in tank drain makes it easy to empty liquids
Built-in accessory storage bag conveniently attached to keep all accessories neatly organized
