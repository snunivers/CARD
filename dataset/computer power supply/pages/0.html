SP750

⦁ 80 PLUS GOLD Efficiency
⦁ Fully modular braided cables for a clean look
⦁ Japanese electrolytic capacitors for high durability
⦁ ZERO RPM mode under 40% load for silent operation
⦁ Sleek and classic exterior with brushed aluminum housing

Category: Power supply Tags: Aluminum PSU, braided cables, LIAN LI PSU, Modular cabler, O11D MINI PSU, SFF Build PSU, SFX PSU
  • Description

The SP750 is a stylish high-performance PSU that perfectly fits small-form-factor PCs while supporting power-hungry components.

Available in two color

Sleek Black Look with Aluminum Housing Completely White Designing.

80 PLUS Gold Certificate

SP750 guarantees up to 92% efficiency, ensuring less power wastage.

SP750 Composition

Cooling in Silence

The SP750 features a 92mm hydraulic bearing fan that operates at zero RPM mode until 40% load in order to achieve minimal acoustic levels.

ACOUSTIC NOISE (Ambient Temperature is 25°C)

Note : Internal components reach 60°C will also trigger fan to operate

Japanese Electrolytic Capacitors

Premium Japanese electrolytic capacitors provide great durability and long-term reliability, as well as offering the highest stability.

Fully Modular Connection and Included Braided Cables

The fully modular power supply provides all necessary braided cables right out of the box, ensuring less cable clutter and more aesthetics.

Five Years Warranty

LIAN LI offers a 5-year limited warranty for the SP750, reflecting our confidence in the quality of our performance SFX PSU.
