        Details & Features
        













            Store up to 100-lbs of tools and attachments with this stout S2000 52-in 10-Drawer Rolling Tool Cabinet. Protect your drawers’ structural integrity for long-term use with heavy-duty, no-slam drawer slides that lightly shut themselves. Get the ultimate tool organizer when you combine this high-endurance, metal tool cabinet with the matching tool chest to optimize your daily productivity.

HIGH-STABILITY DRAWER SLIDES support 100 lbs. of tools, pieces, and attachments 
 EMBOSSED TOP MAT FOR SURFACE PROTECTION with drawer liners under the lid and in every drawer 
 SAFEGUARD YOUR TOOLS with over-mold key and internal locking system 
 EXPANSIVE STOWING SPACE: 18,532 cubic inches of storage 
 HIGH DURABILITY AND STRENGTH: 1,500 lb. load rating with 5 in x 2 in casters with toe-locking brakes and reinforced mounting casters 
 BUILT TO LAST: 18-20 Ga. steel I-Frame construction and top bumpers 
 PROFESSIONAL-GRADE MOBILITY: Full-grip, tubular side handle 
 MADE IN THE USA WITH GLOBAL MATERIALS: Forged in Sedalia, Missouri

The Ultimate Storage Solution
With over 18,000 cubic in. of space and a 1,500 lb. load rating, this tool cabinet is ready to hold tools, accessories, and more. Built to last with 18-20 ga. steel I-Frame® construction and top bumpers.


































Supreme Drawer Capacity
Packed with 10 heavy-duty drawers, each capable of holding up to 100 lbs. Soft-closing sliders keep tools in place and prevent drawers from slamming shut. Overmold key and internal locking system provides extra security.
