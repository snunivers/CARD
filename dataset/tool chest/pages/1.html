26-Inch 3-Drawer Top Chest

$499.99

This Top Chest fits on top of our 26-Inch 5-Drawer Rolling Cabinet or can be used on its own
  • Cold rolled steel construction with a durable powder coated finish resists wear and abrasions to keep the color vibrant
  • Soft close, full extension, ball bearing slides glide smoothly and prevent drawers from slamming shut
  • Each drawer holds up to 100 pounds
  • Full length extruded aluminum drawer pulls are easy to grip
  • Premium PVC drawer liner cushions your items and protects inside of the drawers
