The 36 x 17 in. Steel Triangle® Toolbox allows you to organize more than 280 tools and see them all at a glance with the multi-tier design. When closed, the trademarked lid design locks tools in position, creating protection, security, and organization.

Use in your workspace or mount to a vehicle for work or play. For instructions on how to mount to your vehicle, see the instruction sheet in the Manuals section below.

Model: XL450B
Pairs with: 36" 5-DRAWER UTILITY CART (3605UC)

Actual product may differ slightly from the photos. Any tools and other props shown in the photos are not included.

 California Residents WARNING: Cancer and Reproductive Harm www.P65Warnings.ca.gov
FEATURES

- Organize 280+ tools and accessories
- Multi-tier design allows you to see tools at a glance
- Heavy-duty steel pegs help hold sockets and wrenches in place
- Use in workspace or mount to your work or recreational vehicle
- Durable, reliable and long-lasting performance
- Lid design locks tools in place when closed even under off-road conditions
- 16-gauge steel construction ensures strength, durability, and protection
- Designed to carry both SAE and Metric tools
- Weather-resistant seal on lid protects tools against dust and moisture
- Lock and latching system secures tools from unauthorized use
- Durable UV resistant powder coat paint is built for outdoor use

Specifications

Width: 36-3/4" (93.5 cm)
Depth: 17-1/2" (44.5 cm)
Height: 21-5/8" (54.9 cm)
Net Weight: 94.8 lb (43 kg)

Maximum Product Weight (including contents): 500 lb (226.8 kg)
