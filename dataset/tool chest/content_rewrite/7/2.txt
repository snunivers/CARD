A roller cabinet for larger tool collections

The Craftsman 2000 Series 52-in W x 37.5-in H 10-Drawer Steel Rolling Tool Cabinet has a rugged textured finish that makes it a popular choice for your shop. It offers additional space for your tools, and a variety of drawer sizes to store everything you need to get the job done.

When portability is important, the 2000 Series cabinet rolls smoothly on 5-inch heavy-duty casters, with a sturdy chrome side handle that can be mounted on either side.

Patented quick-release self-latching drawer pulls

You’ll appreciate the range of drawer sizes. In particular, the extra-wide top drawer with room for extra bulky tools, and extra-deep bottom drawers that fit bulky tools and equipment. Also, the toolbox design incorporates exclusive quick-release self-latching drawer pulls. These keep the drawers securely closed, even when you’re moving the tool box.
