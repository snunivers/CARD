The Platform Signature 1.75” Tourmaline Ceramic Professional Styling Iron is perfect for all hair types. With three layers of baked ceramic plates infused with tourmaline for even heat distribution, this tool allows you to create multiple styles from pin-straight to beachy waves.   


Why it's Different.....






Create style staying power with our triple layer baked ceramic plates and high speed heat recovery technology offering even heat distribution for every strand




HydraHeat™ technology seals in vital moisture, repels humidity, reduce frizz, and helps eliminate static as increased negative ions give softer, smoother results




Adjustable temperature from 140°F to 450°F for all hair type needs




Gain flexibility and mobility like a PRO with a professional 12ft swivel cord




Offers even heat distribution and high speed heat recovery




Three layers of baked ceramic infused with Tourmaline




Floating plates, 1hour auto shut-off, dual voltage, 2-year warranty




Available in 4 sizes: .5”, 1”, 1.25” and 1.75”
