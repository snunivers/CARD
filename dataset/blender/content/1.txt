Well Equipped Accessory Bundle




Designed with durability and  functionality in mind, the HBF hand blender is a 
versatile and an indispensable 
all round
 
appliance.


The convenient speed control button allows users to select desired intensity, while the 
Turbo function
 maximises the power from the 
700W motor 
and
 
exclusive 
FlowBlend 
system.


The Smeg HBF02 comes complete with the following accessories: 
BPA-free beaker,
 
chopper
 with stainless steel blades, potato 
masher
, and lastly, a 
stainless steel whisk
.
