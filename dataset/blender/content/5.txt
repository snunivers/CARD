DETAILS

Hamilton Beach
® 
Wave Crusher® Blender with Blend-in Travel Jar Deliciously smooth results on the go or at home. Blend a batch of cold, creamy milkshakes for the family or a fresh smoothie to take on the go with the Hamilton Beach Wave Crusher Blender. This 700-watt peak power blender comes with a 40 oz. glass jar and a 20 oz. travel jar – both can be used on the blender – giving you ultimate versatility in serving size and portability. With 12 blending functions including Pulse, you’re sure to find the right setting for a smooth drink. You can also whip up sauces, dressings and purees. The patented Wave-Action system works to continuously pull mixture down into the blades for consistently smooth results. Enjoy no-mess serving with the larger jar’s easy-pour spout designed to eliminate drips. The jars, lids and blades are dishwasher safe and the base comes with storage for the cord. Two Blending Jars, One Easy Process Choose Your Jar Twist the blades onto the jar of your choice. Use the 20 oz. jar to blend and go, or use the 40 oz. jar for a larger batch. Place It On The Blender Base Add your favorite ingredients like frozen fruit, juice and yogurt, along with ice, to make a delicious smoothie, shake or icy drink. Blend & Enjoy In just a press of a button, get smooth and delicious results. Snap the lid onto the 20 oz. travel jar and go. Or if you’re using the 40 oz. jar, use the lid’s spout for mess-free pouring. Smooth Results Without Stirring Ordinary blenders spin contents around the walls of the jar, which can leave whole chunks untouched by the blades, but the Wave Crusher Blender has the patented Wave-Action System, which continually forces the mixture down into the blades for consistent results. Mess-Free Lid & Pour Spout The 40 oz. glass jar features a unique spout with a hinged cap for easy, no-mess pouring. The cap also works great for adding ingredients while mixing, and it closes securely for spill-resistant storage of leftovers.
