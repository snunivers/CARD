Smart Matters


Sensor Cooking






This microwave’s sensor buttons take the guesswork out of reheating your favorite foods. The microwave senses the amount of steam produced during cooking, and automatically adjusts for optimal results.






























Easy Matters


Quick-Touch Preset Buttons






Press one of the pre-programmed buttons and the microwave automatically adjusts for different foods. Make popcorn, melt butter, cook bacon, reheat a frozen entrée, and more.






























Flexible Matters


Cook or Defrost






For reheating or cooking foods, use Time Cook and choose from Power Level one through ten. Defrost meats, vegetables, or casseroles is easy with two different options - One-pound defrost or Custom defrost.






























Fast Matters


One Touch Matters






The microwave can be turned on for one to six minutes at 100 percent power by simply touching the number of minutes you would like to cook. If additional cooking time is needed while the microwave is running, press the Start button to add an additional 30 seconds of cooking time.






























Turning Matters


Turntable for even cooking






This microwave features a 12.4-inch diameter glass turntable that rotates when the microwave is on to prevent hotspots and allows food to cook more evenly.
