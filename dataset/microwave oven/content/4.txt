The NN-SN966SR Microwave Oven with Inverter Technology and Genius Sensor makes a stylish and convenient addition to any home. This exceptional 1250-Watt, 2.2-cubic-foot capacity stainless steel microwave evenly heats and cooks using patented Inverter Technology to deliver a constant stream of cooking power at every temperature for more evenly cooked meals - edges to centers - every time.




Features










Inverter technology generates a seamless stream of microwave cooking power for evenly heated foods edges to center
Genius sensor automatically adjusts power and times to take the guesswork out of everyday cooking; includes 14 sensor cook settings; Sensor reheat warms evenly
This large, family size countertop microwave allowing you to devote less space to electronics and more to interior capacity
Advanced Inverter Turbo Defrost speeds up defrosting time
Keep Warm feature keeps foods like vegetables, soups, hors d'oeuvres, gravies, sauces and desserts warm and delicious in the oven until they're ready to serve
