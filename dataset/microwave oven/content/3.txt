1.4 cu. ft. 1100W Sharp Black Stainless Steel Microwave


The rich, elegant finish on Sharp's black stainless steel microwave ovens is an absolute show-stopper for your dream kitchen. As a complimentary appliance in a black stainless kitchen, the 1.4 cu. ft. Sharp SMC1452CH microwave oven makes a stunning design statement. The elegant, grey interior complements the black stainless steel finish and features an energy efficient, bright white, LED interior light.


The roomy, family-sized 1.4 cu. ft. black stainless steel microwave oven also boasts 1100 watts of power for rapid reheating and fast cooking. The Sharp black stainless steel microwave oven allows you to showcase your design savvy and create a premium kitchen that flows with your lifestyle.
