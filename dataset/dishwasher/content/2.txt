Overview

















                        Product Description
                    



                        Stainless steel tub dishwasher with Dual Power Filtration
                    





                            Skip the soaking and scrubbing with Dual Power Filtration, a dishwasher filtration system that disposes of any food in its path. Maytag® dishwashers with Heated Dry reduce excess moisture so dishes come out dry, while a full stainless steel tub is tough enough to handle high heat for years.
                        








































Features








































































Dual Power Filtration


Skip soaking, scrubbing and pre-rinsing with Maytag engineering that puts an end to pre-work. Industry-exclusive Dual Power Filtration combines 100% microfiltration and a 4-blade stainless steel chopper to filter out then dispose of any food in its path.




















PowerBlast® cycle


The PowerBlast® cycle scours away stubborn foods like egg yolk, peanut butter and even dried-on oatmeal. High-pressure jets, increased water temperatures and hot steam work together to clear food off dishes—so all you have to do is load them in.




















Heated Dry


Up the temperature
2
 to reduce excess moisture inside your dishwasher and get dishes nice and dry.

Fingerprint resistant stainless steel


Easy-to-clean, fingerprint resistant stainless steel resists smudges and streaks to keep your dishwasher looking as good on the outside as it works on the inside.










More Usable Capacity on Upper Rack


Put bulky pots and pans in their place with increased upper rack height that fits big dishes usually left for the bottom—so there’s no need for a second load.










Leak Detect


The Leak Detect system is a combination of multiple features that measure and monitor the flow of water at various points. If a problem is sensed, the unit can take action by draining the tub and alerting you when necessary.

Great step up from old dishwasher


I bought two of these, one to replace a 12-year old dishwasher, and another to go in a kitchen in my newly finished basement. They are a clear step up from what I had before.

Things that were important to us:

- Noise. Our old dishwasher has incredibly noisy - we had to schedule reading around our dish washing schedule. This new dishwasher is so much better! Sure, you can tell it’s on if you listen for it, but it doesn’t get in the way of any other activities.

- Cleaning. On of the frustrating things about our old dishwasher was how hard it was to clean. The older it got the more residue it added to our dishes, but to really clean out the built-in filters required disassembling much of the dishwasher, which was completely ridiculous. This new Maytag makes it much easier to get to the filters, so this can become part of our regular maintenance with ease.

Other things we love:

- The stainless steel interior is much more resilient than the plastic interior of our old dishwasher. We expect years of service with little staining or degradation.

- Space on the top rack. The top rack is much lower than in our previous dishwasher, allowing us to fit many more types of dishes on the top rack. I do wonder if having a third rack would have been more helpful, but so far this extra second rack space has been useful.

I installed these myself, one requiring wiring in a olug, and the other needing direct wiring. Both were relatively easy, and things worked as expected.

All in all we are very pleased with this purchase.
