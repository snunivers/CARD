Description

Lighten workloads with this 24" dishwasher from Avanti. The perfect dishwasher for the office, studio apartment, basement, tiny home, rental room, condo, vacation home, or anywhere you need a small dishwasher.

Features

  • 24" dishwasher
  • 12 standard place settings capacity
  • Electronic control panel with LED display and cycle status indicators
  • Delayed start and 3 additional wash options (High Temp Wash, Sanitize, Extra Dry)
  • 5 automatic cycles
  • Rinse aid dispenser
  • Eco - half load mode
  • Nylon coated upper and lower racks
  • Low noise rating: 57 decibels
  • Three-stage filtering system
  • Silverware basket with handle
  • ADA compliant
  • 1-year manufacturer limited warranty
