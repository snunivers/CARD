The Y_Brand Y_Model is a 14-cup programmable coffee maker built for larger households and frequent entertaining. It offers regular, bold, and small-batch brewing modes to improve flavor whether making two cups or a full pot. The backlit display, 24-hour timer, and freshness indicator help streamline morning preparation. A permanent filter reduces waste, while the removable water tank makes filling more convenient at the sink. The carafe lid, basket, and tank are designed for easy cleaning. The Y_Brand Y_Model combines generous capacity with practical customization for everyday brewing.
