The Black+Decker BESTO720B string trimmer is a lightweight option made for routine touch-up work in smaller outdoor spaces. It features an 18V battery, an 11-inch cutting diameter, and a fixed front guard to help improve control around decorative plants. The auto-feed spool keeps fresh line ready as you work, reducing manual adjustments. A compact body and narrow cutting head make it easy to trim around garden borders, raised beds, and play equipment. This trimmer is a good fit for users who prioritize simple storage, easy startup, and comfortable one-handed maneuverability.
