Made for efficient residential trimming, the Ryobi 18V 4Ah String Trimmer electric string trimmer uses a 10-amp motor to maintain strong cutting speed through thick grass and overgrown edges. The 15-inch cutting diameter helps reduce the number of passes needed, while the cushioned overmold handle improves comfort. A cord-retention system helps prevent accidental unplugging during use. The rotating shaft allows fast conversion to edging mode, and the guide wheel assists with straighter lines along driveways. This model is a good choice for users who want consistent power, minimal maintenance, and no concerns about battery runtime.
