The Ryobi 18V 4Ah String Trimmer commercial-style string trimmer is engineered for frequent use on larger landscapes. A 30cc 4-cycle engine delivers strong torque without requiring oil and gas mixing, making maintenance simpler than many traditional gas models. The straight shaft improves reach in tall grass, and the heavy-duty gear head is built for long service life. Its 18-inch cutting width helps crews cover more area per pass, while the anti-vibration grip supports comfort during extended sessions. This model is well suited for property maintenance professionals and homeowners managing acreage or heavily overgrown borders.
