Designed for travel, the U_Brand U_Model portable speaker combines a slim body with room-filling sound. Dual passive radiators improve low-end impact, while a 16W output maintains clarity at higher volumes. It supports Bluetooth 5.2, stereo pairing with a second compatible unit, and a 3.5 mm auxiliary input for wired devices. The rechargeable battery offers up to 14 hours of use on moderate volume. An IP67 rating means it can handle dust, sand, and brief water exposure, making it a practical choice for beaches, campsites, and poolside listening.
