With its minimalist design, the Sony SRS-XB67 portable speaker fits easily on a desk, shelf, or bedside table. It uses a full-range driver and passive bass enhancement to create warm, even sound for podcasts, playlists, and casual streaming. Bluetooth 5.1 offers reliable wireless playback, and touch controls on the top panel let you adjust volume, skip tracks, and answer calls. The battery lasts up to 9 hours per charge, while USB-C charging reduces downtime. Soft rubber feet add stability, and the compact footprint makes storage convenient when traveling.
