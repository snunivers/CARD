The Johnson 406020 laser measure is designed for fast one-handed operation in tight spaces. With a range of 50 meters and precision of ±2 mm, it is suitable for installers, decorators, and cabinet work. A built-in bubble level helps improve alignment during setup, while continuous measurement mode updates distances in real time as the user moves. The unit supports area, volume, and wall area calculation for planning materials more efficiently. Its matte finish reduces slipping, and the reinforced corners add extra protection against bumps on busy job sites.
