For renovation professionals who need more than simple distance readings, the Johnson 406020 provides a balanced mix of range, durability, and calculation features. It measures up to 65 meters and includes painter’s mode for estimating wall areas while excluding windows or doors. The fold-out end piece helps with measurements from corners and edges, improving consistency in awkward locations. A rubber overmold protects the body from minor drops, and the sealed keypad is easy to clean after dusty work. The Johnson 406020 is well suited to estimating, fitting, and finish carpentry.
