Engineered for precision, the U_Brand U_Model laser measure combines a 70-meter measuring range with advanced calculation modes for professionals. A tilt sensor expands layout options, while the screen displays current and previous values simultaneously. The U_Brand U_Model also includes a timer, reference pin, and carrying pouch, making it a versatile option for surveying and renovation tasks.
