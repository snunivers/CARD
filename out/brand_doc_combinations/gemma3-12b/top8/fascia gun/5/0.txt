The Vyper Vyper 3 fascia gun is built for daily muscle recovery with a compact body and balanced grip. It uses a high-torque brushless motor with 10 mm amplitude and five speed levels from 1,200 to 3,000 percussions per minute. A 2,500 mAh battery provides up to 5 hours of use and recharges through USB-C. Four interchangeable heads target large muscle groups, joints, and trigger points. The housing features a soft-touch finish, while the low-noise design stays under 45 dB. A simple LED panel shows speed and battery status clearly.
