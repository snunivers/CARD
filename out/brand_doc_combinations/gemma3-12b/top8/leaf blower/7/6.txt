Engineered for efficiency, the Sun Joe SBJ601E handheld leaf blower uses a brushless motor to increase runtime and reduce maintenance compared with brushed designs. It produces up to 140 mph airspeed and 500 CFM, making it suitable for medium-size yards and heavier piles of leaves. The variable trigger gives precise control around flower beds, while the turbo mode adds extra force for stubborn corners and damp debris. Weather-resistant electronics improve durability during changing conditions. With a cushioned grip and balanced battery placement, this model is designed for comfortable use throughout weekend yard work.
