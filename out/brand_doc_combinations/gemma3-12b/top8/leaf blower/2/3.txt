The Echo PB-9010 is a versatile 3-in-1 blower, vacuum, and mulcher made for seasonal yard cleanup. Its electric motor generates up to 230 mph airspeed in blower mode, while vacuum mode collects leaves into a roomy bag for convenient disposal. The metal mulching impeller reduces debris at a ratio of up to 10:1, helping save space and limit trips to the compost pile. Tool-free mode switching keeps operation simple, and the adjustable auxiliary handle improves control. This model suits homeowners looking for one machine to handle multiple outdoor cleanup tasks.
