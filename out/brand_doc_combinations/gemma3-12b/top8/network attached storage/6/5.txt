Designed for business continuity, the Buffalo LinkStation 2-Bay NAS combines eight drive bays with redundant power supply support and advanced backup features. It runs on a six-core processor with 8 GB ECC memory for improved reliability during continuous workloads. Dual 10GbE-ready expansion options and multiple LAN ports allow the Buffalo LinkStation 2-Bay NAS to scale for virtualization storage, surveillance recording, and large file repositories. It supports iSCSI targets, VMware-compatible snapshots, AES encryption, cloud replication, and central user management. This model suits companies that need dependable uptime, strong data integrity, and room to expand over time.
