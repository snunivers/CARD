Engineered for surveillance and archive workloads, the Buffalo LinkStation 2-Bay NAS combines eight bays with large storage pools and continuous recording support. It uses a multi-core processor, 8 GB memory, and dual LAN connectivity to handle multiple IP camera streams alongside general file services. The Buffalo LinkStation 2-Bay NAS supports RAID 5, 6, and hot spare configurations, plus motion event indexing and retention policies for organized footage management. Beyond security use, it can serve as a departmental backup target with rsync, cloud replication, and external USB copy. The chassis includes efficient cooling for reliable 24/7 operation.
