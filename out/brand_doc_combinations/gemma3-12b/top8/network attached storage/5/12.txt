The Seagate IronWolf Pro 4-Bay NAS targets photographers and small studios with a 4-bay design, two 2.5GbE ports, and support for large-capacity drives. It features 4 GB RAM, expandable memory, and optional SSD cache to speed thumbnail generation and catalog access. The Seagate IronWolf Pro 4-Bay NAS offers RAID 1, 5, and 10, plus snapshot recovery and offsite replication for better protection of client work. USB front copy lets users import from external drives quickly after a shoot. With organized sharing links, album access controls, and broad operating system compatibility, it fits collaborative creative workflows well.
