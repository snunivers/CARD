Engineered for damp climates, the Kelty Cosmic 20 uses moisture-resistant synthetic insulation that dries quickly and retains loft better than traditional fill when exposed to condensation. This three-season bag is rated for 28°F (-2°C) and features a durable water-repellent shell with reinforced foot and hood panels. The tapered mummy shape conserves warmth, while the interior lining remains smooth and breathable for all-night comfort. A draft tube along the zipper and adjustable hood opening help seal in heat. The Cosmic 20 packs to a moderate size, making it a dependable option for canoe camping and unpredictable weather.
