The Big Agnes Torchforce 20 is a cold-weather sleeping bag intended for winter camping and late-season hunting trips. Its mummy profile and 0°F (-18°C) rating are supported by high-loft down-alternative insulation, an insulated hood, and a sculpted footbox that keeps toes warmer. The water-resistant shell helps shed condensation inside tents, while the brushed interior feels smooth against base layers. A left-side zipper includes anti-snag webbing and a wide draft tube. At roughly 4.1 lb, the Torchforce 20 prioritizes warmth over minimal weight and comes with both storage and compression sacks.
