For alpine treks and minimalist packing, the Sea to Summit Spark Sp III delivers an efficient mummy design with a 15°F (-9°C) rating and a streamlined cut. The shell uses ultralight ripstop nylon, while the synthetic microfiber insulation is mapped more heavily around the torso and feet for balanced warmth. A close-fitting hood with drawcord adjustment reduces heat loss in windy campsites. The footbox is anatomically shaped to improve comfort when sleeping on your back or side. The Sea to Summit Spark Sp III also features a short center zipper to reduce bulk and total trail weight.
