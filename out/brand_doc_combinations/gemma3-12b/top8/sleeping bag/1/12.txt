Ideal for bikepacking and fast-moving adventures, the Sea to Summit Spark Sp III emphasizes a small packed size and efficient warmth. This mummy sleeping bag weighs approximately 1.9 lb and is rated to 35°F (2°C), making it a strong fit for spring and summer overnights. Compression-friendly synthetic insulation reduces bulk and remains easier to care for than natural down. The ripstop shell has a smooth finish that slides easily into compact tent spaces. An insulated draft collar, adjustable hood, and snag-resistant zipper improve overnight comfort. The Spark Sp III also includes loops for hanging and long-term airing.
