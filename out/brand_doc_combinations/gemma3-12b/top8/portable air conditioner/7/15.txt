Compact yet capable, the AIRFOX AF-141AC is a portable air conditioner tailored to condo bedrooms and small living spaces up to 230 square feet. Its 7,500 BTU cooling system quickly lowers room temperature, and the built-in dehumidifier helps improve comfort during muggy weather. The unit supports sleep mode, two fan speeds, and a 24-hour programmable timer for personalized operation. An easy-access filter can be rinsed clean and reinstalled in minutes. With a slim profile, integrated caster wheels, remote control, and included window kit, the AF-141AC is convenient to set up and move.
