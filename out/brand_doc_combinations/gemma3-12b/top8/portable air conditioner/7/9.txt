For year-round flexibility, the AIRFOX AF-141AC works as a portable air conditioner, heater, fan, and dehumidifier in one appliance. Its 13,000 BTU cooling capacity handles rooms around 500 square feet, while the heating function provides useful support during cooler months. The design includes smooth caster wheels, recessed side grips, and a compact footprint for easier storage between seasons. Touch controls and a remote make temperature adjustments simple, and the 24-hour timer supports scheduled operation. The AF-141AC also features sleep mode, automatic restart, and a washable air filter.
