Built for demanding renovation work, the Black+Decker BDM120S variable-speed belt sander balances aggressive sanding with user control. A 1,010-watt motor drives a 76 x 457 mm belt through six speed settings, allowing the tool to adapt from rapid stripping to finer surface leveling. The front roller reaches close to vertical edges, making it useful for flooring, doors, and beams. A wide dust extraction port improves chip removal and can be paired with a vacuum for cleaner sanding. Soft-start electronics, an overmold rear handle, and tool-free belt changes enhance both comfort and productivity.
