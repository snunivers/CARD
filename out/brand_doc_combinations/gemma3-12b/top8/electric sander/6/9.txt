The Craftsman CMCS515A detail mouse sander is intended for hobbyists and home users who need access to tight, awkward spaces. A 130-watt motor provides enough power for sanding shutters, skirting boards, furniture corners, and small craft projects. The pointed pad design improves reach, and the replaceable tip extends pad life when working repeatedly in confined areas. This model includes a dust-sealed on/off switch, a compact collection canister, and a cable strain relief for durability. Weighing just over one kilogram, it remains easy to maneuver for overhead touch-ups and prolonged detailed sanding tasks.
