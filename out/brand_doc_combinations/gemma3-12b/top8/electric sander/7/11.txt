The WEN 6201 inline sander is made for bodywork, panel leveling, and sanding broad flat sections with a straight stroke pattern. Powered by a 350-watt motor, it delivers up to 3,000 strokes per minute across a long sanding shoe, helping create uniform finishes on doors, body panels, and composite sheets. Its elongated base reduces the risk of uneven sanding on larger surfaces, while the dual-handle layout improves control during horizontal and vertical use. This unit features external dust extraction compatibility, quick paper clamps, and a reinforced housing intended for repeated professional shop use.
