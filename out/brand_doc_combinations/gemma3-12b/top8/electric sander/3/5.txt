Compact but capable, the Ryobi P2520A palm sander is a practical choice for light finishing, fine sanding, and touch-up work. It runs on a 160-watt motor and offers a fixed speed of 13,500 orbits per minute, ideal for preparing wood before staining or painting. The ergonomic one-hand grip and balanced weight help reduce fatigue on small projects such as drawers, chair legs, and decorative panels. Hook-and-loop pad compatibility allows quick abrasive changes, while the onboard dust collector helps keep surfaces visible. A sealed switch design adds durability when working in dusty workshop conditions.
