Designed for workshop versatility, the Bosch GSS18V-5Q oscillating spindle and belt sander handles curved edges, contours, and straight surfaces in one machine. Its 450-watt induction motor supports both vertical spindle sanding and horizontal belt sanding for shaping shelves, toy parts, and custom joinery components. The table is cast for stability and includes inserts for different drum diameters to improve dust control around the work area. Multiple spindle sleeves and a sanding belt are supplied, allowing fast setup for varied tasks. A built-in storage compartment keeps accessories organized between jobs.
