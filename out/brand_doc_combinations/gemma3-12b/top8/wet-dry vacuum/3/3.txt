Designed for quieter operation, the Ridgid XD4500 4.5 Peak HP 14-Gallon Wet/Dry Vac wet-dry vacuum is ideal for shared spaces, indoor remodeling, and frequent household use. The 5-gallon tank is matched with a low-noise 8-amp motor that helps reduce sound without sacrificing useful suction. A flat powerhead design allows small item storage on top, and the wide wheelbase adds stability when pulling the unit over thresholds. Accessories include a combination floor tool, crevice nozzle, squeegee insert, and dusting brush. The washable filter system supports quick changeover between wet and dry jobs with minimal downtime.
