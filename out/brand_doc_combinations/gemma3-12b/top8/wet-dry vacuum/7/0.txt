The Bissell 3626A 2-Gallon Wet/Dry Vacuum is a compact wet-dry vacuum designed for apartments, cars, and small workshops. Its 2.5-gallon tank and 3 peak HP motor deliver solid pickup for dust, wood chips, and minor liquid spills without taking much storage space. A lightweight body, top carry handle, and 10-foot power cord make it easy to move room to room. The unit includes a reusable cartridge filter, foam sleeve for wet pickup, crevice tool, utility nozzle, and 4-foot hose. Integrated cord wrap and onboard accessory storage help keep everything organized after cleanup.
