The Armor All AA255 2.5 Gallon 2 Peak HP Wet/Dry Vacuum is a stainless steel wet-dry vacuum built for users who want a more durable tank and a polished workshop look. Its 10-gallon corrosion-resistant drum is paired with a 5.5 peak HP motor for collecting metal shavings, drywall dust, and accidental liquid spills. A front drain simplifies disposal, while the large lid latches help create a secure seal during operation. This model ships with a locking hose, claw utility nozzle, wet floor nozzle, and washable cartridge filter. Onboard accessory slots help keep attachments close at hand during busy cleanup tasks.
