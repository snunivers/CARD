The BaByliss PRO 3000 Nickel Hair Straightener is a wet-to-dry hair straightener created for users who want to style freshly washed hair with fewer steps. Special vented ceramic plates help release moisture as steam while the tool glides through damp strands. Adjustable settings from 160°C to 230°C let you tailor the heat to your hair’s condition, and the digital interface keeps operation straightforward. The floating plate system improves contact for smoother results, while anti-frizz ionic care helps leave hair more polished. It also includes dual voltage, a swivel cord, and a 60-minute safety shut-off.
