Designed for efficient styling on coarse or curly hair, the CHI G2 Ceramic and Titanium Straightener uses titanium floating plates and high-output heaters for strong, steady performance. It offers a temperature range of 170°C to 240°C, with an LCD screen that clearly shows the selected setting. Infrared heat technology helps distribute warmth more evenly through the hair shaft, while ionic output helps tame static. The body is slightly wider for better control on larger sections, and the extra-long 2.7-meter cord supports easier movement. A heat mat and storage lock are included in the box.
