The Polaris 9550iQ Sport is a pressure-side pool cleaner made for homeowners who need powerful debris pickup during heavy leaf season. Compatible with a dedicated booster pump, it sweeps the floor and lower walls while collecting debris in a large external filter bag. This design helps reduce strain on the pool’s primary filtration system. Durable wheels and a backup valve improve coverage by preventing stalls in tight areas. The Polaris 9550iQ Sport is suitable for in-ground pools with concrete, tile, fiberglass, or vinyl finishes and includes feed hose sections for custom installation.
