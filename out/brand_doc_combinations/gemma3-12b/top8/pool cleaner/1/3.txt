For quick cleanups between full maintenance cycles, the Polaris 9550iQ Sport offers cordless convenience in a compact handheld pool cleaner. The rechargeable lithium battery provides up to 60 minutes of runtime, making it ideal for steps, spas, tanning ledges, and small above-ground pools. Its transparent debris chamber lets you monitor collection, while the reusable stainless and mesh filter captures leaves, grit, and hair. A telescoping pole attachment expands reach without requiring you to enter the water. The Polaris 9550iQ Sport is easy to charge, lightweight to carry, and simple to rinse after use.
