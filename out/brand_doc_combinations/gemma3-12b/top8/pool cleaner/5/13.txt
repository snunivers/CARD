For everyday cleaning in family pools, the Intex Automatic Pool Cleaner provides dependable pressure-side performance with a straightforward setup. The cleaner sweeps debris into an attached mesh bag and circulates in a broad pattern to cover larger floor areas over time. A wear-resistant hose and durable chassis are built for regular exposure to sun and pool chemicals. The backup jet helps free the unit from ladders and corners, reducing interruptions during operation. The Intex Automatic Pool Cleaner is suited to in-ground pools with moderate to heavy debris and helps extend intervals between manual vacuuming sessions.
