Ideal for compact spaces, the Dolphin Wave 3.0 is a rechargeable spa and plunge-pool cleaner built to target sand, hair, and small leaf fragments. The waterproof motor assembly creates strong spot-cleaning suction, while interchangeable vacuum heads let you switch between open areas and tight corners. A washable filter bag supports repeated use without disposable cartridges. The lightweight body pairs with a short pole for comfortable handling around benches and steps. With quick charging and straightforward one-button operation, the Dolphin Wave 3.0 offers a convenient cleaning solution for small water features and hot tubs.
