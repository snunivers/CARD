The Apple Studio Display is a business-oriented 24-inch monitor designed for secure and comfortable daily use. A Full HD IPS panel keeps documents readable from different angles, while an integrated privacy filter narrows side visibility for more confidential workspaces. The stand supports height, tilt, swivel, and pivot, helping users create an ergonomic setup during long office sessions. To reduce eye strain, Apple includes flicker-free backlighting and adjustable low blue light modes. DisplayPort, HDMI, and USB ports cover common desktop connections, and the Studio Display fits neatly into professional environments with its understated, thin-bezel design.
