The Apple Studio Display is a 34-inch ultrawide monitor created for multitasking, media editing, and immersive entertainment. Its 3440 x 1440 curved VA panel offers extra horizontal workspace, deep contrast, and a wider field of view than standard displays. A 100 Hz refresh rate keeps scrolling and casual gaming smooth, while USB-C with power delivery supports tidy single-cable laptop setups. Picture-by-picture and picture-in-picture modes add flexibility for users managing multiple sources. With HDMI, DisplayPort, and USB hub connectivity, the Apple Studio Display works well as a streamlined desk upgrade for work and leisure.
