Charlotte Tilbury Pillow Talk Lipstick Transfer-Resistant Lipstick combines strong pigment with a flexible finish intended to stay put longer than standard cream formulas. The texture starts smooth and slightly creamy, then settles into a lightweight veil that resists smudging and fading. Added ceramides and synthetic beeswax help maintain comfort, making it practical for extended wear. The bullet has a tapered tip for clean application around the lip contours. Suitable for office days, events, and commutes, this lipstick offers dependable color with less need for frequent touch-ups. It removes best with an oil-based cleanser or makeup remover balm.
