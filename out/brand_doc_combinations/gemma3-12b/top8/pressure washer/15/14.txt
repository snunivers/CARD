The Z_Brand Z_Model trailer-mounted pressure washer is intended for mobile cleaning services, farms, and maintenance crews covering multiple sites. Powered by a commercial-grade engine, it delivers 3500 PSI and 3.5 GPM, with a direct-drive pump designed for reliable field use. The setup includes a welded trailer frame, hose reel, 100-gallon water tank, and 50-foot pressure hose for independent operation when a water source is unavailable. A chemical injection system supports detergent and degreaser use. LED trailer lights, safety chains, and lockable storage boxes make the Z_Brand Z_Model practical for transport and daily deployment.
