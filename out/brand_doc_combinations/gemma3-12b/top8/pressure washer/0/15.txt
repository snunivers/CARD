The Ryobi 2300 PSI 1.2 GPM Electric Pressure Washer pressure washer is a compact hybrid design that can run from AC power or an optional battery pack for added flexibility. In electric mode, it delivers up to 1500 PSI and 1.1 GPM, while battery operation provides lower-pressure rinsing for tools, bicycles, and outdoor mats. The lightweight body features a carry handle, onboard nozzle storage, and a 15-foot hose suited to quick cleanup jobs. An adjustable 5-in-1 nozzle reduces accessory changes, and the included siphon hose can draw water from a bucket. The Ryobi 2300 PSI 1.2 GPM Electric Pressure Washer suits small homes and portable cleaning needs.
