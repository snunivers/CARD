The Levoit Core 300S offers reliable whole-room purification for spaces up to 600 square feet. Its cylindrical 360-degree intake pulls air from all sides, sending it through a pre-filter, medical-grade HEPA layer, and pellet carbon filter. This model is well suited for households with pets thanks to strong odor reduction and efficient fur and dander capture. Wi-Fi connectivity allows app-based control, scheduling, and filter life tracking from anywhere. With turbo mode for rapid cleaning and a dimmable display for nighttime use, it balances convenience with strong daily performance.
