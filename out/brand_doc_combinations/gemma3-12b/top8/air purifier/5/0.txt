The GermGuardian AC4100 air purifier is designed for bedrooms, home offices, and other small spaces up to 250 square feet. Its 3-stage filtration system combines a washable pre-filter, true HEPA filter, and activated carbon layer to capture dust, pollen, pet dander, and everyday odors. A low-noise sleep mode runs as quietly as 24 dB, while three fan speeds let you adjust performance throughout the day. The compact tower design fits easily on a nightstand or shelf. Filter replacement alerts and a simple touch control panel make daily use straightforward.
