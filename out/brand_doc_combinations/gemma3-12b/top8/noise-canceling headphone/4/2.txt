The Technics EAH-A800 is a wireless noise-canceling headphone aimed at listeners who want strong performance without extra bulk. Its adaptive ANC adjusts automatically to changing environments, while ambient sound mode keeps announcements and nearby voices audible. Custom 38 mm drivers deliver detailed mids and controlled low-end response. Bluetooth 5.2 with low-latency mode improves video and gaming sync on compatible devices. A beamforming microphone array enhances call clarity in busy spaces. The battery provides up to 35 hours of use, and the adjustable headband with breathable cushions helps reduce pressure over extended wear.
