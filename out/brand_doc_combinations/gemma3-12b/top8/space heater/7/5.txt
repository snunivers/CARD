Designed for bathroom and laundry room use, the COSTWAY 1500W Electric Space Heater moisture-resistant space heater delivers quick warmth in damp environments when placed according to instructions. This compact unit provides up to 1500 watts of ceramic heat, along with low and fan-only modes for year-round versatility. Its sealed control panel and splash-resistant housing support dependable daily use, while the adjustable thermostat helps maintain comfort during cold mornings. A wall-plug or freestanding setup adds flexibility depending on the room. The COSTWAY 1500W Electric Space Heater includes automatic overheat shutoff, a tip-over sensor, and a timer for added safety.
