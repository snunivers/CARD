Ideal for travel and portion control, the Ninja Professional Plus Kitchen System with Auto-iQ cordless blender uses a rechargeable battery to prepare shakes and soft-fruit smoothies without a wall outlet. The 450 ml blending cup is lightweight and doubles as a drinking bottle with a carry loop. Stainless steel blades and a compact motor are optimized for yogurt, banana, berries, and protein powder, though not heavy ice crushing. USB-C charging adds convenience, and indicator lights show battery level and lock status. The leak-resistant lid and easy-rinse cup make it suitable for desks, cars, and short trips.
