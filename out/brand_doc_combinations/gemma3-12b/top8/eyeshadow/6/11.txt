With a smooth gel texture and precise applicator, ColourPop Super Shock Shadows offers liquid matte eyeshadow that dries to a flat, transfer-resistant finish. The lightweight formula spreads evenly across the lid, allowing enough playtime to blend before setting. It is ideal for monochromatic makeup, cut-crease bases, or quick one-color looks that stay in place through long wear. Once dry, the shadow feels flexible rather than tight and layers well under powder accents. The slim tube is compact and easy to carry, while the shade range includes everyday beige, terracotta, cocoa, and muted berry.
