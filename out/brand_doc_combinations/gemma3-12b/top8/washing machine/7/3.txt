With a generous 10 kg capacity, the Speed Queen TC500WN is made for large households and bulky items such as duvets, towels, and winter clothing. This front-load washer includes smart sensors that detect load size and optimize water usage automatically. Steam hygiene and allergy care settings are available for deeper cleaning of heavily used garments. The machine also offers a pause-and-add function, useful when items are missed after the cycle starts. Its brushless motor runs quietly, making the Speed Queen TC500WN suitable for open-plan homes where low noise matters.
