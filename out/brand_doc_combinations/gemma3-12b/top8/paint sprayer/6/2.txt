The cordless Neutec BR-775 paint sprayer is designed for quick touch-ups and portable use where outlets are limited. Powered by a 20V lithium-ion battery platform, it delivers consistent spraying for trim, garden furniture, shutters, and craft projects. The unit features a 900 ml cup, variable speed trigger, and two nozzle sizes to accommodate stains, varnishes, and thin paints. Weighing under 3 pounds without battery, it is easy to maneuver overhead or in narrow spaces. The Neutec BR-775 also includes a removable filter and simple rinse-clean components for faster maintenance between colors.
