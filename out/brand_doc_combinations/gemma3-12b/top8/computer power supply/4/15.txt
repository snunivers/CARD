Built for efficient mainstream and enthusiast systems, the Corsair HX850 is a 750W ATX 3.0 power supply prepared for modern transient load demands. It carries an 80 PLUS Gold certification and includes a native 12VHPWR cable in addition to traditional PCIe connectors, offering flexibility for current and previous-generation graphics cards. The fully modular design simplifies custom cable routing and improves case airflow. A 135 mm fan supports quiet operation with a zero-RPM mode at low loads. Inside, high-grade capacitors and multiple protection systems help deliver stable, long-lasting performance for gaming or workstation builds.
