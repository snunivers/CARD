The be quiet! Straight Power 11 750W is a 300W TFX power supply intended for slim desktops, home office PCs, and replacement upgrades in compact enclosures. Its narrow form factor fits systems where standard ATX units cannot be installed. With 80 PLUS efficiency, it helps lower power consumption during routine tasks such as browsing, document work, and media playback. A built-in 80 mm fan provides steady airflow while keeping noise at a practical level for everyday use. The unit includes a 24-pin motherboard connector, CPU power lead, SATA connectors, and basic peripheral support with essential safety protections.
