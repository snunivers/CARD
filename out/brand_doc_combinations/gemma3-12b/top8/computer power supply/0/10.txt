With a compact 125 mm depth, the Corsair RM850x (2021) is a 650W ATX power supply well suited for smaller mid-tower and micro-ATX cases. It combines an 80 PLUS Gold efficiency level with fully modular flat black cables, making cable management easier in tighter spaces. The LLC and DC-to-DC platform helps maintain stable voltage under rapidly changing loads from modern CPUs and GPUs. A 120 mm fan offers a quiet profile at low to moderate demand. The package includes dual 4+4 CPU connectors, three PCIe 6+2 leads, SATA power chains, and complete electrical protection circuitry.
