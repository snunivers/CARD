For airy, natural waves, the Bio Ionic Bio Ionic 10X Pro Styling Iron uses a 1.25-inch clamp iron with a vented barrel design that supports faster heat distribution. The adjustable range from 240°F to 400°F works well for touchable styles without excessive stiffness.
