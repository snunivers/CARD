The Manscaped Lawn Mower 4.0 combines a beard trimmer with a built-in vacuum chamber that captures a portion of clipped hair for cleaner sink-side grooming. Its precision wheel offers 18 settings between 0.7 and 12 mm, allowing easy adjustment for stubble, goatees, and fuller beard shaping. Titanium-coated blades are removable for washing, and the transparent collection chamber empties in seconds. A rechargeable lithium battery delivers up to 75 minutes of use per charge. For users who want less mess and consistent results, the Lawn Mower 4.0 is a convenient choice.
