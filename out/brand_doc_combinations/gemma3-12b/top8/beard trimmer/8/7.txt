Designed as a grooming kit, the S_Brand S_Model includes a primary beard trimmer, a detail head, and multiple comb attachments for mustache, neckline, and general beard upkeep. The main blade uses corrosion-resistant stainless steel and cuts evenly at lengths from 1 to 14 mm. A soft-touch handle improves comfort, while the washable heads make cleanup quick after each use. The lithium battery provides up to 65 minutes of runtime and recharges through a compact docking base. The S_Brand S_Model works well for users who want one tool for several beard care tasks.
