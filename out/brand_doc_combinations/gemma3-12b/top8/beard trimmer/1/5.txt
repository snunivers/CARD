For coarse or fast-growing facial hair, the Braun Beard Trimmer Series 9 uses a high-speed motor and wide cutting head to reduce the number of passes needed. Self-sharpening steel blades maintain clean performance, and the adjustable comb spans 0.8 to 18 mm for everything from heavy stubble to a full beard trim. An LED screen displays battery level, charging status, and travel lock activation. The trimmer works corded or cordless, offering up to 100 minutes of use when unplugged. The Braun Beard Trimmer Series 9 is suited to users who value power and flexibility.
