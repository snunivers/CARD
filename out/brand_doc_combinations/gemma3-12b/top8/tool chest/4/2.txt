The Seville Classics 54" W x 24" D x 35.5" H Mobile Tool Chest portable tool chest is a rugged steel box intended for service calls, mobile trades, and household maintenance. A reinforced carry handle makes transport easy, while the cantilever tray design opens to reveal multiple tiers for fast access to screws, sockets, and hand tools. The latch system closes tightly to prevent accidental opening in transit. Finished in durable powder coat, the box stands up well to chips and everyday wear. Its compact footprint fits neatly in a truck bed or utility closet, making it a dependable option for organized, grab-and-go storage.
