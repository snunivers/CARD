The Triton TCT2020 20" Tool Chest Set workstation-style tool chest is designed for users who want storage and a usable bench area in the same unit. A thick hardwood top provides a stable surface for assembly, tuning, or repair work, while the cabinet below stores tools in seven drawers and one tall side compartment. Pegboard-ready side panels offer additional organization options for hanging accessories. The steel body is finished in powder coat to resist shop wear, and the locking drawers keep contents protected. Smooth-rolling casters with foot brakes allow the chest to move when needed and stay put during work.
