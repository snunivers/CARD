The Triton TCT2020 20" Tool Chest Set service cart style tool chest offers open access and mobility for repair jobs that move around the shop. It combines three lockable drawers with a spacious top tray and lower shelf, allowing users to keep frequently needed tools within reach. The steel frame is compact but sturdy, and the raised side rails help prevent tools from sliding off while in motion. A powder-coated surface resists common shop chemicals and wear. Swivel casters with brakes improve control, making this chest a useful option for maintenance crews, hobbyists, and small workshops.
