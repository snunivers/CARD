The V_Brand V_Model modular tool chest is designed to grow with changing storage needs. Its base cabinet includes six drawers and a full-width lower compartment, while the side panel is pre-drilled for optional accessories such as paper towel holders, shelves, or peg hooks. The steel frame uses reinforced corners for added durability, and the drawers ride on smooth full-extension slides. An internal locking bar keeps contents secure during transport, and the caster set includes two locking wheels for stability. This model works well for garages that need flexible, organized, and mobile tool storage.
