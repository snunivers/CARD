The Big Green Egg Large Big Green Egg barbecue grill is an offset charcoal smoker and grill built for cooks who want traditional barbecue flavor. The main chamber provides room for ribs, pork shoulders, and chicken pieces, while the side firebox generates indirect heat and smoke for longer sessions. Adjustable dampers on the firebox and chimney help regulate airflow and temperature. Porcelain-coated grates offer durability, and a front shelf creates extra space for trays and tools. A lid thermometer assists with monitoring heat levels without opening the chamber too often. Large wheels make this heavier unit easier to reposition outdoors.
