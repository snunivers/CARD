The X_Brand X_Model barbecue grill is a ceramic kamado-style cooker built for grilling, smoking, roasting, and baking. Its thick insulated shell holds heat efficiently and supports a wide temperature range, from low-and-slow barbecue to high-heat searing. The stainless steel cooking grate offers generous space, while the multi-level fire bowl system allows precise charcoal management. An adjustable top vent and bottom draft door give fine airflow control. Side shelves provide prep room, and the wheeled stand keeps the unit stable on a patio. A built-in thermometer helps monitor cooking without lifting the lid repeatedly.
