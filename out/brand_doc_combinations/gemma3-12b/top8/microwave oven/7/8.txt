Smart features set the Black+Decker EM720CB microwave oven apart for connected households. Wi-Fi capability allows users to monitor cooking time, receive cycle notifications, and access preset programs through a compatible app. The oven provides 1.3 cu. ft. capacity, 1100 watts of power, and sensor-based settings for reheating and cooking common dishes. Voice control compatibility adds hands-free convenience when multitasking in the kitchen. Despite its technology focus, the Black+Decker EM720CB remains practical, with a clear display, child lock, mute option, and wipe-clean interior surfaces.
