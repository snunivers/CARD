The California Air Tools CAT-6410 is a 2-gallon portable air compressor packaged for everyday maintenance around the home, car, and workshop. Its oil-free motor reaches 135 PSI and provides 1.5 SCFM at 90 PSI for inflating tires, sports equipment, and rafts, while also powering light-duty nailers. The unit includes an accessory kit with hose, inflation nozzle, tire chuck, and needle adapter for added convenience. A compact body and top handle make storage simple, and the quick-pressure controls are easy for occasional users to understand. Rubber feet help minimize vibration on hard surfaces.
