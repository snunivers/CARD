The Dewalt DW20HV twin-stack air compressor is built for contractors who need portable performance on demanding job sites. With two 4-gallon tanks, a 155 PSI maximum pressure, and 4.0 SCFM at 90 PSI, it can handle framing nailers, roofing tools, and repeated fastening applications. The oil-free pump supports fast recovery without routine pump maintenance. Oversized wheels and a telescoping handle improve mobility across rough surfaces. A reinforced control panel, high-flow regulator, and thermal overload protection help maintain consistent output while extending service life in daily professional use.
