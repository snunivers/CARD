The Dewalt DW20HV ultra-quiet air compressor is ideal for studios, service counters, and indoor work areas where noise matters. Using a dual-piston, oil-free pump, it runs at roughly 60 dB while delivering 2.8 SCFM at 90 PSI from a 10-gallon tank. The 125 PSI maximum pressure suits airbrushing, benchtop tools, inflation, and light fastening. Slow-speed motor operation helps reduce wear and heat buildup over longer sessions. A compact cabinet-style layout, vibration-dampening feet, and oversized cooling fan improve comfort and reliability, while dual outlets let two low-demand tools share the system.
