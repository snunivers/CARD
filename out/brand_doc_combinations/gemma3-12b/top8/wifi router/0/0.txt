The TP-Link Archer AX55 is a dual-band WiFi 6 router designed for small homes and apartments. It delivers combined speeds up to 1800 Mbps across 2.4 GHz and 5 GHz bands, making it suitable for streaming, video calls, and everyday gaming. Four external antennas help improve signal stability, while beamforming targets connected devices more efficiently. The router includes one Gigabit WAN port and four Gigabit LAN ports for wired connections. WPA3 security, parental controls, guest network support, and app-based setup make the TP-Link Archer AX55 easy to manage for daily home use.
