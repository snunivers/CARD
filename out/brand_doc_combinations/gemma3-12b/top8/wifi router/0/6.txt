For users needing both internet backup and wireless coverage, the TP-Link Archer AX55 combines standard routing with 4G LTE failover. It supports dual-band WiFi up to AC1200 speeds and automatically switches to a cellular connection if the main wired line goes down. This makes it useful for home offices, small shops, or remote locations. The router includes a SIM slot, Gigabit WAN port, and two LAN ports for essential wired devices. The TP-Link Archer AX55 also offers guest WiFi, firewall controls, SMS management through the interface, and basic antenna connectors for better mobile reception.
