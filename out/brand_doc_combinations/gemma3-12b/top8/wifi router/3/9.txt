The Google Nest Wifi Pro is a business-oriented router intended for small offices and retail spaces. It supports dual-band WiFi 6 with stable performance for point-of-sale systems, laptops, security cameras, and guest access. VLAN capability and multi-SSID broadcasting help separate employee traffic from customer networks, while WPA3 and firewall settings add another layer of protection. The unit provides one Gigabit WAN port, four Gigabit LAN ports, and USB backup connectivity. With scheduled reboots, remote administration, and bandwidth control, the Google Nest Wifi Pro is well suited for workplaces that need straightforward network management and reliable uptime.
