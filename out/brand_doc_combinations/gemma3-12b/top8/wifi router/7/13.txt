Created for smart homes, the Amazon eero 6 is optimized to support a large number of connected sensors, cameras, speakers, and appliances. Its WiFi 6 design improves efficiency with OFDMA, allowing many low-bandwidth devices to communicate without overwhelming the network. Combined wireless speeds reach up to 2400 Mbps, which is more than enough for normal family streaming and automation tasks. The router has one Gigabit WAN port, four Gigabit LAN ports, and straightforward app setup with device naming tools. The Amazon eero 6 also includes guest access, WPA3 security, automatic updates, and scheduled internet rules.
