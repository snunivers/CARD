The TP-Link Archer AX21 is an affordable AC1200 router created for basic home networking needs. It combines 300 Mbps on 2.4 GHz with 867 Mbps on 5 GHz, providing enough bandwidth for browsing, HD streaming, and smart home devices. Two adjustable antennas extend wireless reach, and Fast Ethernet ports support simple wired connections for desktops or media boxes. The router includes WPS pairing, parental controls, and a separate guest network. With a compact design and straightforward setup wizard, the TP-Link Archer AX21 is a practical choice for students, renters, or smaller living spaces.
