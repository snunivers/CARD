The Netgear Nighthawk RAX150 is a feature-rich router for advanced users who want flexible network control at home. It supports tri-band WiFi 6E, adding a 6 GHz band for newer devices that need cleaner spectrum and reduced interference. A powerful processor and generous memory help maintain stable performance during heavy downloads, local backups, and multiple streams. The rear panel includes one 2.5 Gigabit WAN/LAN port, four Gigabit LAN ports, and USB 3.0. Software features on the Netgear Nighthawk RAX150 include VPN server support, link aggregation, detailed QoS settings, WPA3, and custom scheduling for devices and services.
