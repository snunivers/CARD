Made for families, the Netgear Nighthawk RAX45 focuses on easy setup and dependable daily performance. This dual-band router delivers up to 1500 Mbps combined speed, enough for streaming, browsing, online classes, and smart speakers. Intelligent traffic management helps distribute bandwidth more evenly when several devices are active. Three external antennas assist with consistent coverage in small to medium homes, and the compact enclosure fits neatly on a shelf or desk. The Netgear Nighthawk RAX45 includes one Gigabit WAN port, three Gigabit LAN ports, guest network support, parental controls, WPA3 security, and one-touch setup through a mobile app.
