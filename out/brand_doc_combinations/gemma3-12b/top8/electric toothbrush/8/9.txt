Offering a more premium brushing experience, the S_Brand S_Model electric toothbrush features a slim aluminum-style handle, strong sonic output, and a quiet drive system. Five brushing modes support whitening, gum massage, sensitive cleaning, standard care, and intensive cleaning. The brush stores up to 2 preferred settings for shared household use. A built-in timer helps users divide brushing evenly across the mouth, while the pressure notification helps reduce gum irritation. The battery charges in about 4 hours and lasts up to 30 days. Package contents include 4 brush heads and a travel case.
