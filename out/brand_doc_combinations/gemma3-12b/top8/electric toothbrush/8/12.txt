The S_Brand S_Model combines oral care performance with convenient smart features in a clean, modern design. An integrated brushing reminder tracks elapsed time, and the ring light changes color when pressure becomes excessive. Sonic cleaning helps disrupt plaque along the gumline, while the tapered bristles improve reach between teeth. Four programs—daily, whitening, sensitive, and gum care—allow simple adjustment for different needs. The rechargeable battery lasts up to 32 days, and the waterproof body is easy to clean. Included accessories are 2 brush heads, a magnetic charger, and a travel sleeve.
