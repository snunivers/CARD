Designed for professionals on the move, the S_Brand S_Model portable SSD combines 2TB of storage with USB 3.2 Gen 2 connectivity. It delivers transfer speeds up to 1,050MB/s read and 1,000MB/s write, making it suitable for backing up photos, 4K video, and large project files. The lightweight enclosure includes a rubberized exterior for improved grip and everyday durability. Password protection, AES hardware encryption, and cross-platform compatibility with Windows, macOS, and select mobile devices add convenience. The bus-powered design eliminates the need for an external adapter when traveling or working remotely.
