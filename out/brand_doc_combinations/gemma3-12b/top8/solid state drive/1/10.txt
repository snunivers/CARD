The Western Digital Black SN850X is a rugged portable SSD with 1TB capacity and a focus on travel-friendly durability. Through USB 3.2 Gen 2x2, it can reach transfer rates up to 2,000MB/s, enabling quick movement of large media folders and project backups. The reinforced casing is rated for everyday drops and includes an IP-rated design for added dust and splash resistance. USB-C connectivity, included cables, and broad compatibility with PCs, tablets, and game consoles make setup easy. Password protection and hardware encryption add security for photographers, field teams, and mobile professionals.
