The Amacool Double Camping Hammock deluxe backyard hammock is built for comfort-focused outdoor use with a plush pillow, padded quilted bed, and durable hardwood spreader bars. The polyester shell is treated for fade resistance, and the interior fill adds loft without becoming overly heavy. This hammock supports up to 475 pounds and fits many common 15-foot stands. Decorative tassel accents give it a relaxed, resort-inspired appearance, while steel hanging chains allow minor length adjustments during installation. It is well suited for patios, pergolas, and larger deck spaces where users want a more finished, furniture-like look.
