Designed for intensive cleaning, the Bosch 800 Series SHX88Z75N dishwasher is well suited to large households and frequent cookware use. It features a durable stainless steel interior, 15 place-setting capacity, and a powerful triple filtration system that helps keep wash water cleaner throughout the cycle. Programs include Pots, Auto, Normal, Glass, and a 60-minute quick wash for everyday loads. The lower rack has foldable tines to create space for mixing bowls and stock pots, while the upper rack adjusts smoothly for tall stemware. Running at 48 dBA, it combines strong cleaning power with manageable noise levels.
