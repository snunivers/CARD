Compact yet versatile, the LG LDP6797ST apartment-size dishwasher offers an 18-inch profile and a surprisingly flexible interior. It accommodates eight to ten place settings depending on configuration and includes a height-adjustable upper rack for taller cookware below. The control panel features intuitive cycle selections such as Normal, Eco, Quick, and Delicate, along with a rinse aid indicator and delay timer. Its stainless steel tub promotes better heat retention for improved drying. At 47 dBA, it operates quietly enough for smaller living spaces. This model is a practical option for couples, small families, or downsized kitchens.
