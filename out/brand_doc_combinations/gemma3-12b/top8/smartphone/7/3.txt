Built for gaming and heavy multitasking, the Nothing Phone (2a) delivers high-speed performance through a flagship-grade processor, 16GB RAM, and 512GB storage. The large 6.78-inch AMOLED display supports a 144Hz refresh rate and fast touch sampling for responsive play. An advanced cooling system helps maintain stable frame rates during long sessions. The 6,000mAh battery is paired with ultra-fast charging to minimize downtime. Rear cameras include a 64MP primary lens, while front-facing stereo speakers and a vibration motor enhance immersion. The phone also supports 5G, Wi-Fi 6, and customizable game controls.
