The OnePlus 12 is a practical entry-level smartphone for users who want dependable core features at an accessible price. It offers a 6.6-inch HD+ display, a clean interface, and a straightforward design with a textured back for better grip. Equipped with 4GB RAM and 64GB storage, it handles calls, browsing, social apps, and light entertainment comfortably. The 13MP rear camera and 5MP selfie camera are suitable for casual photos and video chat. A 5,000mAh battery supports long standby times, and expandable storage, dual SIM capability, and a headphone jack increase its everyday versatility.
