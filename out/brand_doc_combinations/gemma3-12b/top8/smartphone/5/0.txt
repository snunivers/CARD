The Samsung Galaxy S24 is a balanced smartphone built for everyday use. It features a 6.5-inch FHD+ display with a smooth 120Hz refresh rate, making scrolling and video playback feel fluid. Powered by an octa-core processor with 8GB RAM and 128GB storage, it handles messaging, streaming, and multitasking with ease. A 50MP main camera captures clear daytime shots, while the 8MP front camera supports sharp selfies and video calls. The 5,000mAh battery is designed for all-day use and supports fast charging through USB-C. Dual SIM, 5G, and fingerprint unlock complete the package.
