For users who spend long hours away from a charger, the Xiaomi Xiaomi 14 Pro focuses on battery life above all else. Its massive 7,000mAh battery is built to last through extended travel, work shifts, or streaming sessions, and fast charging helps refill quickly. The 6.72-inch FHD+ display provides a roomy viewing area for maps, productivity, and media. An octa-core processor, 8GB RAM, and 256GB storage keep daily tasks moving smoothly. Camera hardware includes a 50MP main lens and depth sensor. USB-C, side fingerprint scanning, 5G support, and stereo speakers round out the feature set.
