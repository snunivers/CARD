Designed for simple operation, the Roborock Q7 Max robot vacuum provides dependable everyday cleaning without complicated setup. One-touch controls on the top panel let users start, pause, or send the robot back to charge, while the included remote adds convenience. Randomized and edge-cleaning modes help it collect debris along walls, corners, and open areas. The low-profile body fits beneath many pieces of furniture, and infrared sensors help avoid stairs. With a 90-minute runtime, a reusable dust filter, and a modest charging time, the Roborock Q7 Max suits smaller homes and dorm rooms.
