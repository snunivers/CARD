Offering dependable performance on everyday messes, the V_Brand V_Model robot vacuum features dual side brushes, a central roller, and adjustable suction to handle dust, hair, and debris. It is especially useful in entryways, kitchens, and living rooms where dirt accumulates quickly. The machine can be controlled through onboard buttons, a remote, or a smartphone app, depending on user preference. Sensor-based navigation helps it trace room edges and avoid drop-offs. The V_Brand V_Model includes a charging dock, reusable filter components, and a battery designed to cover most medium-sized homes in one cycle.
