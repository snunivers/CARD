Built for busy households, the Samsung Jet Bot AI+ offers vacuuming and light mopping in one compact machine. It uses electronic water flow control to damp-mop sealed hard floors without oversaturating them. Three suction levels make it suitable for fine dust, cereal, and tracked-in dirt, while automatic carpet boost increases power when rugs are detected. The robot connects through a mobile app for scheduling, cleaning history, and no-go zones. With anti-tangle brushes and a HEPA-style filter, the Samsung Jet Bot AI+ is a practical option for homes with pets and allergy concerns.
