The BaByliss Pro Ionic Hair Dryer hair dryer is designed for quick everyday styling with a balanced mix of power and control. Its 1875-watt motor helps reduce drying time, while ionic technology smooths frizz and boosts shine. Three heat settings and two airflow speeds let users tailor performance for fine, thick, or normal hair. A cool-shot button helps set finished styles in place. The lightweight body reduces arm fatigue during longer sessions, and the removable rear filter supports easy cleaning. It also includes a concentrator nozzle and a hanging loop for convenient storage.
