The Olay Sonic Facial Cleansing Brush skin cleansing brush combines cleansing and massage functions in one handheld device. Fine silicone touchpoints on the front work with sonic vibrations to help remove oil, dirt, and cleanser residue, while the textured reverse side can be used to massage skincare products into the skin. With 9 power levels, users can choose a softer or more invigorating routine. Its IPX7 waterproof rating allows easy rinsing and shower use. The rechargeable battery offers extended runtime, and the compact shape fits comfortably in the palm for controlled movement.
