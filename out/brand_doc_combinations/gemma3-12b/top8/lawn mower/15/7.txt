The Z_Brand Z_Model electric corded lawn mower is a practical choice for homeowners who want unlimited runtime near a power source. Its 13-amp motor provides steady cutting performance for small lawns without fuel mixing or battery charging. The 17-inch deck keeps the machine light enough for easy turning in tighter spaces. A simple lever offers multiple height positions, and the mower supports both mulching and rear bag collection. The handle folds for compact storage, while carry handles help with transport. This model works best for neatly shaped yards where extension cord management is manageable.
