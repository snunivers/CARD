Created for heavy seasonal growth, the EGO LM2100E is a 23-inch gas mower with a high-output 190cc engine and front-wheel self-propelled drive. The extra cutting width reduces passes on open lawns, while the deep deck helps improve airflow for stronger bagging performance. Large front controls make height changes quick, even with gloves on. A removable grass bag includes a fill indicator so users can empty it at the right time. This mower also supports mulching and side discharge. It suits homeowners who want broad cutting coverage and reliable power during fast-growing months.
