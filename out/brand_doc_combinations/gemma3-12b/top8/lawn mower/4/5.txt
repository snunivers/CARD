For demanding yard work, the Craftsman M105 zero-turn lawn mower delivers fast coverage and tight maneuvering around landscaping. It uses a 24 HP V-twin engine and a 54-inch fabricated steel deck for durable performance over larger residential properties. Dual hydrostatic transmissions provide responsive steering, while the high-back seat and armrests improve comfort during extended mowing. The deck height can be changed quickly to match seasonal growth. Large rear tires help maintain traction, and the frame is built for repeated use. This model is ideal for users who want speed, precision, and fewer trimming passes.
