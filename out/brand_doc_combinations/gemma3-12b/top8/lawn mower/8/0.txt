The S_Brand S_Model is a compact 20-inch gas push mower built for small to medium lawns. Its 140cc four-stroke engine delivers steady cutting power, while the steel deck helps resist dents from regular yard work. Choose between side discharge, mulching, or rear bag collection depending on grass conditions. A six-position height adjustment lets you trim from 1.25 to 3.75 inches. The fold-down handle saves garage space, and the large rear wheels improve movement over uneven ground. This mower suits homeowners who want simple operation and dependable seasonal performance.
