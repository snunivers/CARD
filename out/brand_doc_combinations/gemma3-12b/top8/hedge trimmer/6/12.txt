The Craftsman CMH2000 is a compact cordless hedge trimmer package aimed at casual users who want convenience and fast setup. It includes a 2.0Ah lithium-ion battery, charger, and a 19-inch dual-action blade that trims cleanly through stems up to 9/16 inch thick. The front bale handle supports multiple grip positions for shaping rounded shrubs and low hedges. A lightweight body and low-noise motor make it comfortable in residential areas where quieter operation is preferred. This trimmer is well suited for touch-ups, seasonal shaping, and general garden care around patios and walkways.
