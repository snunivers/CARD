With an emphasis on extended reach, the Craftsman CMH2000 combines a telescopic shaft and 180-degree rotating head for efficient trimming of tall boundary hedges. The electric motor is powered by a 40V battery platform that delivers steady cutting force to the 20-inch hardened blade. A 3/4 inch cut capacity allows it to handle thicker annual growth, while the adjustable shaft length helps users adapt to hedge height and working angle. The unit also includes a blade sheath, shoulder harness, and lock-off control. It is a practical option for homeowners managing long hedge lines.
