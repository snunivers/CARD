The WORX WG163GT.9 is a telescoping pole hedge trimmer created for reaching tall hedges without using a ladder. It combines an extendable shaft with an articulating cutting head that adjusts through multiple positions for trimming the tops and sides of dense growth. Powered by a rechargeable 40V battery, it offers strong cutting performance with a 18-inch blade and 5/8 inch tooth gap. The padded shoulder strap and balanced motor placement improve comfort during overhead use. This model is ideal for larger properties where reach and flexibility matter most.
