Built for entertainment, the Apple iPad Air (5th Generation) features a large 12.4-inch display with rich color and a smooth 120Hz refresh rate. Quad speakers tuned for immersive audio make movies, games, and music sound fuller and more detailed. Inside, a high-performance processor works with 8GB RAM and 128GB storage, expandable via microSD. The generous 10,000mAh battery is designed for long playback sessions, while fast charging gets you back up quickly. The Apple iPad Air (5th Generation) includes a sleek metal chassis, facial unlock, and reliable wireless connectivity for a premium home media experience.
