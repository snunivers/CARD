The Alps Mountaineering Lynx 2-Person Tent is an instant cabin tent designed for campers who want maximum interior space with minimal setup effort. Pre-attached poles allow the frame to unfold quickly, so the tent can be ready in about two minutes. This 8-person shelter offers standing height through most of the interior, making it easier to change clothes, organize gear, or wait out bad weather. Large windows, a mesh roof, and ground vents support airflow in warmer months. The rainfly covers the roof and upper walls for balanced ventilation and weather protection. The Alps Mountaineering Lynx 2-Person Tent is ideal for convenient car camping.
