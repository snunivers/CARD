For cyclists and adventure travelers, the Alps Mountaineering Lynx 2-Person Tent is a streamlined 2-person bikepacking tent with short segmented poles that fit neatly into panniers or frame bags. The tapered profile helps reduce packed size while preserving usable sleeping space for two slim pads. A ripstop polyester fly with a 2500 mm rating handles wet weather, and reflective guylines improve visibility around camp at night. The tent includes one side door, a modest vestibule, and several interior loops for hanging lights or drying small items. Compact, stable, and easy to transport, the Alps Mountaineering Lynx 2-Person Tent suits multi-day touring routes.
