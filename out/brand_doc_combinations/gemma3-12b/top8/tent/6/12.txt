The Alps Mountaineering Lynx 2-Person Tent is a rugged safari-style canvas tent built for extended stays where durability matters more than low weight. Heavy-duty cotton canvas regulates temperature well, staying cooler in sun and helping reduce internal condensation overnight. The tall A-frame structure creates an open interior suitable for cots, storage trunks, and camping furniture. Large screened windows and a high roof vent improve airflow, while the PVC groundsheet protects against rough terrain and moisture. Reinforced guy points and steel poles add strength in windy conditions. The Alps Mountaineering Lynx 2-Person Tent is well suited to base camps, glamping setups, and seasonal use.
