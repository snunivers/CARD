Built for harsh alpine conditions, the Marmot Tungsten UL 2P is a 4-season mountaineering tent engineered for stability in wind, snow, and freezing temperatures. The geodesic pole structure uses durable aluminum poles that create multiple crossing points for added strength. A low-profile rainfly, snow skirts, and reinforced guylines help the tent stay secure in exposed terrain. Inside, the 2-person layout balances storm protection with enough space for cold-weather sleeping gear. Two vestibules allow protected equipment storage and sheltered entry. With a 5000 mm waterproof fly and robust floor, the Marmot Tungsten UL 2P is ready for demanding expeditions.
