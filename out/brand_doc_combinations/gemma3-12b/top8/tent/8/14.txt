Made for backyard sleepovers and beginner campers, the S_Brand S_Model is an affordable 4-person tent with a simple dome structure and user-friendly setup. Shock-corded fiberglass poles connect to the tent body with clips and sleeves, helping new users pitch it without much effort. The polyester shell includes a basic rainfly for light showers, while mesh roof panels support nighttime ventilation and stargazing when the fly is removed. A single front door, internal pocket, and small vestibule keep the layout uncomplicated. Lightweight and easy to store, the S_Brand S_Model offers a dependable introduction to tent camping.
