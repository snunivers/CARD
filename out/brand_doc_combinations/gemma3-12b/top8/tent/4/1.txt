Designed for family camping, the North Face Stormpeak 2 offers a roomy 6-person cabin layout with near-vertical walls for better headroom and easier movement inside. The tent includes a removable room divider, large mesh windows, and a sewn-in groundsheet to improve comfort during longer stays. Its fiberglass and steel pole system creates a stable structure, while the rainfly and taped seams help protect against moderate rain. Interior storage pockets and an overhead gear loft keep small items organized. With a center height of 198 cm, the North Face Stormpeak 2 feels spacious and practical at the campsite.
