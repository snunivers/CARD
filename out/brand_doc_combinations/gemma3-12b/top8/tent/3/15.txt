The Kelty Salida 2 is a premium 3-person all-around tent that balances low weight, interior comfort, and reliable three-season performance. Its semi-freestanding design uses DAC-style aluminum poles, creating strong headroom at both ends and stable pitching on mixed terrain. The double-wall construction pairs a breathable inner canopy with a silicone-coated rainfly rated to 3000 mm. Two doors and oversized vestibules allow easy access and protected storage for packs, boots, and cooking gear. Color-coded attachment points speed up setup, and multiple vents help control condensation. The Kelty Salida 2 is well suited for backpacking, paddling trips, and general camping.
