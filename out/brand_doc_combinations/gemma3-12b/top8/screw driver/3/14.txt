Compact and versatile, the Bosch GSR18V-50 Professional 18V Brushless Cordless Screwdriver offset screwdriver is designed for low-clearance areas where a straight driver cannot turn. The double-ended tool features Phillips and slotted tips positioned at right angles, allowing access behind equipment, inside tight enclosures, and near obstructions. Made from forged alloy steel, it offers solid strength in a small format. The textured finish improves grip during hand turning, and the low-profile design stores easily in a pocket or small tool pouch. It is especially useful for automotive trim work, machinery covers, and compact household repairs.
