Built for hobbyists and serious DIY users, the Porter-Cable 690LR fixed-base router offers straightforward control and reliable cutting power. Its 11-amp motor produces up to 27,000 RPM, making it suitable for chamfers, rabbets, dados, and decorative edging. The aluminum motor housing adds durability without excessive weight, and the coarse plus fine depth adjustment system helps dial in exact bit projection. A flat-top design lets the router rest upside down during bit changes, and the spindle lock simplifies maintenance. The Porter-Cable 690LR comes with a 1/4-inch collet, parallel guide, and removable dust hood.
