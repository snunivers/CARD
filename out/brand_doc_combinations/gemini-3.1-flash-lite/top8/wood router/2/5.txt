Ideal for finish carpentry and light shop tasks, the Makita RT0701C palm router delivers dependable trimming performance in a compact body. Its 5.8-amp motor spins from 12,000 to 28,000 RPM, giving users flexibility for laminate, veneer, and round-over work. The square fixed base improves balance on narrow edges, while the transparent base plate helps track the cut line. A rack-and-pinion depth system provides simple, accurate adjustment without tools. The Makita RT0701C uses 1/4-inch bits only and includes a guide fence, template bushing adapter, and dust extraction port for cleaner operation.
