For woodworkers who want flexibility, the Ridgid R24012 router combo kit includes both fixed and plunge bases with one 2 HP motor. Variable speed ranges from 9,000 to 24,000 RPM, allowing better performance with small trimming bits or larger panel-raising cutters. The fixed base features micro-adjust depth control and a low center of gravity for smooth edge routing. The plunge base provides 2 inches of travel, cushioned handles, and a turret stop for repeated cuts. LED work lights, soft start, and interchangeable 1/4-inch and 1/2-inch collets round out this versatile package.
