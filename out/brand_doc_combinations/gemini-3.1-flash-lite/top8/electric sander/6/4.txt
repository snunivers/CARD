The Ryobi RS290G drywall sander is engineered for sanding plaster, joint compound, and large wall areas with less strain. A 750-watt motor powers the 225 mm disc head, with variable speed from 800 to 1,800 rpm for controlled finishing. Its extendable handle reaches ceilings and tall partitions, while the pivoting head follows surface contours for more even contact. Built-in LED work lights help reveal scratches and high spots in low-light conditions. This model supports direct vacuum connection, includes several sanding discs, and folds down for easier transport and storage between job sites.
