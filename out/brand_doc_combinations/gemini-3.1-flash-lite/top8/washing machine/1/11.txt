Created for energy-conscious buyers, the Bosch 800 Series WAT28400UC combines a 7 kg capacity with water-saving sensors and low-temperature wash performance. Its eco program cleans everyday cotton loads effectively while using less electricity, and a short refresh cycle is available for lightly worn clothes. The anti-allergy option adds extra rinsing to help remove detergent residue from fabrics. A compact control panel keeps operation clear and simple, even for first-time users. The Bosch 800 Series WAT28400UC also includes a child lock, automatic fault diagnosis, and a durable tempered glass door for everyday reliability.
