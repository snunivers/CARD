The Samsung WF45R6100AW is a compact top-load washing machine designed for dorms, small apartments, and secondary laundry areas. Its 5.5 kg capacity suits lighter weekly use, and intuitive knob controls make operation straightforward for any user. The machine includes gentle, standard, intensive, and rinse-plus settings, giving flexibility for different fabric types. A transparent lid allows cycle monitoring without opening the machine. The Samsung WF45R6100AW also features auto restart after power interruption, a self-clean function, and low water consumption, making it a practical option where space and efficiency are priorities.
