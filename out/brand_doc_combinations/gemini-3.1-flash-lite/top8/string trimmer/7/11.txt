The Worx GT Revolution WG170.1 curved-shaft string trimmer offers simple control and a familiar feel for everyday yard maintenance. Powered by a 22cc 2-cycle engine, it is suited to trimming grass around flower beds, walkways, and low fences. The curved shaft helps users see the cutting area more clearly, making precision work easier in tighter spaces. A dual-line bump feed head releases line as needed, and the lightweight frame helps reduce fatigue. This model is a practical entry-level gas trimmer for homeowners who want mobility, straightforward operation, and good reach without extra complexity.
