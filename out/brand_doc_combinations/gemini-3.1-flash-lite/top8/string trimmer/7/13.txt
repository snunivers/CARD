The Worx GT Revolution WG170.1 compact string trimmer is tailored for lighter yard care and users who prefer a highly manageable tool. It runs on a 12V battery platform and features a 10-inch cutting width that works well in small lawns, patios, and narrow garden paths. The lightweight body is easy to lift and store, while the adjustable front handle improves control around delicate landscaping. A simple feed system keeps the line ready without much effort. This unit is especially useful for touch-ups after mowing and for trimming areas that are difficult to reach with larger equipment.
