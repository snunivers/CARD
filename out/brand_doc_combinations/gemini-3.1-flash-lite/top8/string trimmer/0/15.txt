The EGO Power+ ST1511T professional-duty string trimmer is intended for users who need extended runtime, rugged construction, and strong cutting performance. It operates on a high-capacity 60V battery and includes a shoulder harness for improved support during longer jobs. The straight shaft and high-torque drive system help tackle tall grass, ditch edges, and stubborn weeds with confidence. A variable trigger provides precise speed control, and the weather-sealed electronics are built for outdoor conditions. This trimmer is a solid option for demanding residential properties, estate maintenance, and frequent use where gas-free operation is preferred.
