For demanding residential use, the EGO Power+ ST1511T brushless string trimmer delivers a balance of runtime and cutting strength. It operates on a 56V battery and offers a variable-speed trigger so users can switch from light grass trimming to heavier weeds with better control. The 15-inch swath covers wider sections efficiently, and the rapid-load head speeds up line replacement. A weather-resistant housing adds durability for outdoor storage conditions, while the balanced weight distribution helps reduce strain. This model is well suited for medium to large lawns requiring frequent trimming along fences, walls, and hardscapes.
