The X_Brand X_Model is a low-noise dishwasher built for households that prioritize a calm kitchen environment. Rated at only 40 dBA, it runs quietly enough for nighttime cleaning or open-concept spaces. The 24-inch interior accommodates 14 place settings and includes a third rack, adjustable middle rack, and stemware supports. Wash options include Auto, Eco, Intensive, Quick, and Machine Care, giving flexibility for both daily dishes and deeper cleaning tasks. A leak detection system helps protect cabinetry and floors, while the condensation drying process reduces energy use. Hidden controls and a minimalist front panel complete the refined look.
