The Bosch 800 Series SHP78CM5N is a 24-inch built-in dishwasher designed for busy households needing dependable daily performance. It holds up to 14 place settings and uses a stainless steel tub to improve drying and reduce odors. Six wash programs, including Heavy, Normal, Eco, Quick, Glass, and Rinse, help handle everything from pots to delicate stemware. A third rack adds room for utensils and small items, while adjustable upper racking improves loading flexibility. With a quiet 46 dBA operation, leak protection, and a delay-start timer, this model balances convenience, efficiency, and practical cleaning power.
