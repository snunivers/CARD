Built for smaller kitchens, the KitchenAid KDTM404KPS slimline dishwasher offers 18-inch width without sacrificing everyday usability. It accommodates 10 place settings and features fold-down tines for bowls, pans, and taller glasses. The appliance includes five wash cycles, a half-load option, and a sanitize setting for added hygiene. Its stainless interior supports consistent drying, while electronic touch controls provide a clean, modern look. Operating at 49 dBA, it stays relatively quiet during evening use. Energy-saving Eco mode and a child lock make this dishwasher a smart fit for apartments, condos, and compact family homes.
