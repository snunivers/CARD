Engineered for detail cleaning, the DeWalt DXV10P wet-dry vacuum features a compact body with strong airflow, making it a smart choice for vehicle owners and hobby workspaces. The 3-gallon tank keeps the machine easy to carry, while the 4 peak HP motor provides enough suction for sand, crumbs, and small liquid messes. A flexible hose, precision crevice tool, dashboard brush, and upholstery nozzle support interior cleaning in tight spaces. The unit can also be used as a blower for drying mats or clearing debris. A washable filter helps reduce replacement costs over time.
