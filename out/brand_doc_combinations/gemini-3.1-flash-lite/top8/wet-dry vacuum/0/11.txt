Compact but capable, the Ridgid WD1450 wet-dry vacuum is intended for quick cleanups in laundry rooms, utility spaces, and compact workshops. Its 4-gallon tank and 3.5 peak HP motor offer dependable performance for dust, pet food, mud, and sink overflows. The ergonomic top handle allows one-handed carrying, and the short, stable base helps prevent tipping when the hose is pulled around furniture. Included accessories include a gulper nozzle, crevice tool, and reusable filter set for wet and dry pickup. Simple snap latches and a smooth interior tank make emptying and rinse-out easy.
