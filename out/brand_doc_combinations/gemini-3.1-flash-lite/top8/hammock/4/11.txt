Featuring a handwoven look, the Wise Owl Outfitters SingleOwl rope hammock brings classic style to porches and shaded gardens. The thick-spun polyester rope is engineered to resist rot, moisture, and UV damage better than traditional untreated cotton. This model measures 13 feet overall with a lying area of 76 x 55 inches and a 375-pound weight capacity. Varnished hardwood spreader bars provide support and maintain a visually open shape. The open rope construction encourages airflow, helping it stay cool in hot weather. It is a comfortable option for afternoon naps and relaxed outdoor reading.
