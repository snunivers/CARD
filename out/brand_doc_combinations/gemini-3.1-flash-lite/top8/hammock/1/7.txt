Ideal for smaller patios, the Kammok Roo Double single hammock is sized for one person yet built with sturdy materials for everyday outdoor use. The polyester-cotton blend fabric offers a softer feel than all-synthetic options while improving resistance to moisture and stretching. This hammock measures 110 inches in total length, with a bed width of 47 inches and a maximum capacity of 250 pounds. Hardwood spreader bars create a neat, tailored appearance, and braided ropes connect securely to metal O-rings. Its compact footprint makes it a smart choice for balconies, porches, or limited garden areas.
