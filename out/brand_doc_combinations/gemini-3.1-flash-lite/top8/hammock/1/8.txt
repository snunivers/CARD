The Kammok Roo Double freestanding hammock set includes both the hammock and a powder-coated steel stand for easy placement anywhere in the yard. The adjustable frame fits users who want a no-tree setup and assembles with simple push-pin connections. The hammock bed uses quilted polyester fabric with internal padding for extra comfort, and it supports up to 420 pounds. Non-slip feet help protect patio surfaces and improve stability on level ground. With an assembled footprint of approximately 124 x 47 x 45 inches, this all-in-one option is convenient for seasonal outdoor lounging.
