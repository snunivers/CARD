The Ekrin Athletics B37 is a mid-size fascia gun that balances reach, stability, and comfort for regular use at home. It features an offset handle to help users target calves, glutes, and upper back areas with less awkward wrist movement. The motor delivers 1,600 to 3,300 percussions per minute across eight levels, supported by an 11 mm amplitude. Four durable silicone heads reduce drag on the skin and are easy to wipe clean. Battery life is rated around 5 hours. The device also includes auto memory, preserving the last used setting after power-off.
