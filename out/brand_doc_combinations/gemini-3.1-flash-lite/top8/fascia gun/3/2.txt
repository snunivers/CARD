The Hyperice Hypervolt Go 2 fascia gun combines portable size with strong performance for use at the gym, office, or while traveling. Its brushless motor supports four preset modes plus a manual mode, covering gentle warm-up to deep tissue recovery. Speed ranges from 1,800 to 3,200 rpm, and the 9 mm amplitude helps loosen tight calves, shoulders, and back muscles. The gun includes five attachment heads made from easy-clean EVA and ABS materials. A 2,000 mAh battery runs up to 3.5 hours, and the quiet operating system helps keep sessions discreet.
