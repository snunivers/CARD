The T_Brand T_Model is a slim laser measure intended for quick and accurate home improvement projects. It measures distances from 0.2 to 30 meters and features ±3 mm accuracy for common indoor applications. Users can switch between front and rear reference points to match different measuring situations. The device calculates area and volume automatically, helping with paint estimates, flooring plans, and material checks. A simple two-button interface keeps operation straightforward, while the durable ABS housing protects the tool from daily wear in toolboxes or drawers.
