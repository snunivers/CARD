Built for users who need documentation as well as measurement, the Stanley TLM99s combines an 85-meter laser range with app connectivity for saving project data. Measurements can be transferred wirelessly to a mobile device for annotation, room sketches, or estimate preparation. The tool supports distance, area, volume, trapezoid, and indirect measurements, giving it flexibility across many tasks. A rechargeable battery reduces operating cost, and the USB charging port is protected by a sealed cover. The Stanley TLM99s also features a high-visibility display and robust casing for frequent transport.
