The Bosch Blaze GLM 20 offers a lightweight alternative to tape measures for interior projects and property maintenance. It provides measurement functions for distance, area, volume, and continuous scanning, with a range of up to 25 meters. Accuracy is rated at ±2.5 mm, which is well suited to general household tasks. The compact body fits easily in a pocket, and the anti-scratch display window helps preserve visibility over time. Automatic reference switching, memory recall, and energy-saving sleep mode give the Bosch Blaze GLM 20 everyday convenience without unnecessary complexity.
