The Tacklife HD60 brings practical measurement functions together in an easy-to-learn format. It covers distances from 0.05 to 45 meters and supports direct, continuous, area, and volume modes. A clear monochrome display shows measured values in large digits, reducing reading errors during quick tasks. The device includes automatic laser shutoff, auto power-off, and last-value memory to conserve energy and streamline repeat measurements. With its light weight and rounded edges, the Tacklife HD60 is comfortable to carry in a pocket, apron, or small technician’s bag.
