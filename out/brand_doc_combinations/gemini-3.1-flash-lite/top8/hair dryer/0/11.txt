Engineered for thick hair and faster styling, the Dyson Supersonic hair dryer uses a powerful 2200-watt motor to create strong, consistent airflow. Ionic conditioning helps smooth the hair cuticle for a sleeker finish, while ceramic components help distribute heat evenly. Users can choose from three temperature levels and two speed settings depending on desired drying time and styling precision. The included narrow concentrator is ideal for blowouts, and the diffuser supports body and natural texture. A soft-touch handle improves comfort, and the detachable rear filter makes cleaning quick and convenient.
