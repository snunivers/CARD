Designed for versatility, the BaBylissPRO Nano Titanium Spring Curling Iron includes three interchangeable barrels: 0.75-inch, 1-inch, and tapered. This allows users to switch between ringlets, classic curls, and relaxed waves without buying multiple tools. The ceramic tourmaline coating helps reduce static and leaves hair looking smoother. Heat can be adjusted from 250°F to 430°F using a digital LCD panel, and the lock-in attachments click securely into place. A heat glove, storage pouch, dual voltage, and automatic shut-off are included, making the set useful for both home styling and travel.
