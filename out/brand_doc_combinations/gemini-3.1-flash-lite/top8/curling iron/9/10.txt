The T_Brand T_Model is built for tighter curls and spiral styles, featuring a 0.5-inch barrel with a smooth ceramic finish. This slim shape is useful for short hair, layered cuts, and adding definition to specific sections around the face. Temperature can be adjusted between 230°F and 420°F, allowing more flexibility for different textures. The compact handle offers steady control, while the cool tip helps during precise styling close to the scalp. With an LED temperature display, automatic shut-off, and swivel cord, it balances detailed styling performance with everyday convenience.
