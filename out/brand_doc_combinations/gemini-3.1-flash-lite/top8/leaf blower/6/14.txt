The Worx Turbine 600 WG520 professional handheld leaf blower is built with durability and responsiveness in mind. Its 30cc gas engine delivers up to 190 mph airspeed and 500 CFM, giving it enough force to handle leaves, hedge trimmings, and debris on residential or light commercial sites. An easy-access air filter simplifies routine maintenance, and the translucent tank helps monitor fuel during use. The throttle lock allows steady output without constant finger pressure. Combined with a balanced chassis and anti-vibration handle, this blower is suitable for frequent cleanup in demanding conditions.
