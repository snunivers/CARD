The Makita XBU03SM1 cordless leaf blower is designed for quick yard cleanup around patios, driveways, and garden beds. Its lightweight handheld body helps reduce fatigue, while the brushless motor delivers air speeds up to 125 mph and airflow of 420 CFM. A variable-speed trigger and turbo button let you balance runtime and power as needed. The unit uses a 40V lithium-ion battery system and includes a soft-grip handle for comfort. Low-noise operation makes it suitable for residential neighborhoods, and the detachable tube allows easier storage in sheds or garages.
