Quiet operation is a standout feature of the Black+Decker BEBL7000 electric leaf blower, making it a smart option for early morning or neighborhood-friendly yard care. The motor delivers up to 130 mph airspeed and 390 CFM, enough for dry leaves, dust, and light grass clippings. A concentrator nozzle is included for directing airflow along edges, under shrubs, or between patio pavers. The ergonomic handle and low overall weight help reduce wrist strain during longer use. With no fuel emissions and minimal maintenance, this blower fits homeowners seeking cleaner, simpler outdoor equipment.
