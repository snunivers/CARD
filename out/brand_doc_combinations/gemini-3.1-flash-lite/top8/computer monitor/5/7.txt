Creative professionals will appreciate the Gigabyte M28U, a 27-inch QHD monitor with an IPS panel tuned for dependable color performance. Covering a wide color gamut and supporting hardware-friendly calibration workflows, it is suitable for photo editing, illustration, and design work where consistency matters. The matte finish controls glare without dulling detail, and the higher resolution provides extra room for toolbars and timelines. USB-C, HDMI, and DisplayPort inputs support flexible studio setups, while a USB hub keeps accessories close at hand. Gigabyte completes the M28U with ergonomic adjustments and multiple preset color modes.
