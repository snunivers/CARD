Engineered for speed, the Keurig K-Elite Coffee Maker uses a high-output heating system to bring water to ideal brewing temperature quickly and maintain consistent extraction throughout the cycle. This 8-cup coffee maker includes a stainless steel carafe, pre-infusion setting, and manual drip-stop for cleaner serving. Its wide showerhead evenly disperses water over the grounds, helping produce balanced flavor with less bitterness. The low-profile design fits under many cabinets, and the removable brew basket supports easy cleanup. The Keurig K-Elite Coffee Maker is well suited for users who want fast brewing without giving up cup quality.
