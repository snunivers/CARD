The Keurig K-Elite Coffee Maker cold brew coffee maker is designed to create smooth, low-acid coffee concentrate with minimal effort. Its tall glass brewing pitcher holds up to 1.5 liters and includes a fine-mesh stainless steel infuser that keeps grounds contained during steeping. An airtight lid helps preserve freshness in the refrigerator, and the pour handle allows easy serving. The durable construction is suitable for daily batch brewing, iced coffee, or tea infusions. With a clean profile and simple setup, the Keurig K-Elite Coffee Maker is a practical option for households that enjoy chilled coffee drinks year-round.
