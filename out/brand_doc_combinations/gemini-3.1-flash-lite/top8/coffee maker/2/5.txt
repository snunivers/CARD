Designed for simplicity, the Bonavita Connoisseur One-Touch Coffee Brewer is an 8-cup pour-over style automatic coffee maker with a precision showerhead and bloom function to improve extraction. Its borosilicate carafe, wide brewing basket, and measured reservoir markings support more controlled brewing with standard paper filters. A single dial allows users to choose classic or richer brew settings, while the heated base can maintain temperature briefly after brewing. The modern design pairs matte finishes with stainless steel accents for a clean countertop look. The Bonavita Connoisseur One-Touch Coffee Brewer is ideal for coffee enthusiasts seeking manual-inspired flavor with automatic convenience.
