Made for travel trailers, offices, and compact living spaces, the Bonavita Connoisseur One-Touch Coffee Brewer is a lightweight 4-cup coffee maker with a durable plastic body and simple on/off switch. The swing-out filter basket provides quick access for filling and cleaning, while the glass carafe includes a drip-free spout and comfortable grip. Non-slip feet help keep the machine stable on smaller surfaces. It uses standard paper filters and includes an auto pause feature for pouring mid-brew. The Bonavita Connoisseur One-Touch Coffee Brewer offers straightforward performance for users who want a basic, dependable coffee maker without extra programming.
