The W_Brand W_Model is a smart coffee maker with Wi-Fi connectivity and app-based scheduling for added flexibility. Users can start brewing remotely, adjust strength settings, and receive maintenance reminders through a compatible mobile device. This 12-cup model also includes onboard controls for manual operation, a reusable filter, and a warming plate with adjustable hold time. The removable reservoir features measurement guides for precise filling, and the brew basket is dishwasher safe. Blending modern convenience with familiar drip performance, the W_Brand W_Model fits connected homes and busy weekday routines.
