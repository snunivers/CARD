Designed for eco-conscious gardeners, the Melnor Bluetooth AquaTimer automatic garden watering system pairs solar charging with a rechargeable battery for off-grid operation. It draws water from a barrel, tank, or reservoir, making it ideal where no outdoor tap is available. The controller supports several preset programs and can deliver measured watering to up to 20 pots or raised beds. A low-water alert and anti-siphon design improve reliability during daily use. With UV-resistant tubing and stake-mounted drippers, this system supports efficient irrigation while helping users make practical use of collected rainwater.
