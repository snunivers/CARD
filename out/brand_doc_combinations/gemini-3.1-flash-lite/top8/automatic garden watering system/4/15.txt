Compact yet capable, the RainPoint WiFi Sprinkler Timer automatic garden watering system is designed for urban gardens where space and water use both matter. It combines a simple electronic timer with slim tubing, micro-sprayers, and root-zone drippers that work well in narrow beds, vertical planters, and courtyard layouts. The system can run at selected times each day and includes a manual boost option for particularly hot afternoons. Corrosion-resistant fittings and UV-treated materials support season-long use outdoors. Easy to install and unobtrusive once in place, it offers dependable automated watering for small but intensively planted spaces.
