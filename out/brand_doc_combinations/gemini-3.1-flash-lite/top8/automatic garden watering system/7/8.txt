The Orbit 62061Z Single-Outlet Programmable Hose Faucet Timer automatic garden watering system is a smart Wi‑Fi enabled solution for users who prefer app-based control. Through the mobile interface, watering times, zone schedules, and rain-delay settings can be adjusted remotely without returning to the tap. The kit includes a connected controller, leak-resistant fittings, drip emitters, and a soil-moisture accessory port for optional sensor integration. It is designed for beds, containers, and small lawns that benefit from regular scheduling. With usage tracking and customizable routines, this system helps simplify garden maintenance while supporting more informed water management throughout the season.
