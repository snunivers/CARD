The Ninja Professional Plus Kitchen System (BN801) multifunction blender set combines countertop power with useful attachments for everyday prep. Its 800-watt base includes a 1.6-liter main pitcher, a grinding cup for coffee beans and spices, and a smaller blending jar for sauces and dressings. Three speeds and pulse support a range of textures, from chunky salsa to smoother smoothies. The stainless blade assemblies detach for easier washing, and the jars use twist-lock mounting for secure placement. This package suits users who want one appliance for beverages, dry grinding, and simple food preparation.
