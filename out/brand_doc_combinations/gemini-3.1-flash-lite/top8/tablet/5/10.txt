The Lenovo Tab P12 is a gaming-oriented tablet featuring a 12.1-inch display with a fast refresh rate for smoother motion and lower input delay. A high-end chipset, 12GB RAM, and 256GB storage help demanding titles load quickly and run consistently. An advanced cooling system is built in to maintain performance during longer sessions. Four speakers create spacious audio, and customizable game tools can block alerts or boost network priority. The battery supports extended play and can be topped up quickly through USB-C. The Lenovo Tab P12 also works well for streaming and multitasking beyond gaming.
