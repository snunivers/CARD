For users who prefer a compact skincare tool, the Clarisonic Mia Smart offers powerful sonic cleansing in a palm-sized design. The flexible silicone surface adapts to facial contours to help remove excess oil and leftover makeup from around the nose, chin, and hairline. It has 12 intensity options, memory function for your last setting, and a quiet motor suitable for morning or nighttime routines. USB charging makes it easy to power up anywhere, and one full charge supports several weeks of use. The sealed design is hygienic and easy to rinse clean.
