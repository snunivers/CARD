The PMD Beauty Clean cleansing brush is designed to support clearer-looking skin with a combination of rotating action and soft-touch bristles. Two brush speeds allow either gentle daily cleansing or a more thorough wash after long wear makeup or outdoor activity. The kit includes a sensitive brush head and a silicone massage attachment to help apply cleanser evenly. Its slim handle fits comfortably in the hand, and the standing base helps keep the device dry between uses. A simple power button and charging indicator keep operation straightforward for everyday skincare routines.
