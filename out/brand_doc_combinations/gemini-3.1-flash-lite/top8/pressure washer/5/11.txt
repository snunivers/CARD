Designed for demanding residential and light commercial use, the Greenworks GPW2000 features a brushless induction motor paired with a brass-headed pump for durability. Output reaches 2800 PSI and 1.8 GPM, making it suitable for masonry, pool surrounds, siding, and utility trailers. The reinforced 30-foot hose resists abrasion, and the included turbo nozzle speeds up work on stubborn stains. Users can switch to the fan tip for more delicate surfaces. The Greenworks GPW2000 also includes onboard GFCI protection, a removable detergent tank, and a fold-down handle to simplify transport and winter storage.
