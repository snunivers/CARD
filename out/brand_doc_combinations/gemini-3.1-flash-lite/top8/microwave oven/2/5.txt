The Breville BMO850BSS over-the-range microwave oven helps save counter space while adding ventilation above the cooktop. It offers 1.7 cu. ft. capacity, 1000 watts of cooking power, and a two-speed exhaust fan to help manage steam and cooking odors. Sensor reheat and cook options adjust settings automatically for more consistent results. The bottom-mounted light improves visibility over the range, and the wide door opening supports larger cookware. With touch controls, a hidden vent design, and a sleek finish, the Breville BMO850BSS complements modern kitchens well.
