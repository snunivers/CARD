The Breville BMO850BSS combination microwave oven is made for home cooks who want one appliance for reheating, grilling, and convection baking. It delivers 1000 watts of microwave power, 1.1 cu. ft. capacity, and several automatic programs for meats, vegetables, baked items, and frozen snacks. Combination modes can crisp the outside of foods while keeping the inside moist, making it useful for pizzas, casseroles, and chicken portions. A square interior design improves usable space, and the included rack expands cooking flexibility. The control panel remains intuitive despite its broad feature set.
