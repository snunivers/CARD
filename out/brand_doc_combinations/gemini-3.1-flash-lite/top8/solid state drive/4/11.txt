Aimed at workstation builders, the Samsung 980 Pro is a 2TB PCIe 5.0 NVMe SSD with very high throughput for advanced computing tasks. In the M.2 2280 form factor, it offers sequential read speeds up to 10,000MB/s and write speeds up to 9,500MB/s, ideal for large asset libraries, simulation data, and multi-stream editing. An integrated heatsink helps handle sustained workloads, while ECC, wear-leveling algorithms, and thermal safeguards improve long-term consistency. The drive supports DirectStorage-ready environments and modern motherboard platforms. It is best suited for enthusiasts seeking top-tier responsiveness and next-generation bandwidth.
