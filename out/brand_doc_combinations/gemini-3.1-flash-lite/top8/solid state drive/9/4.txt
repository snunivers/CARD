The T_Brand T_Model is an enterprise-focused U.2 NVMe solid state drive with 3.84TB capacity for servers, virtualization, and database workloads. Built on PCIe 4.0, it provides high random IOPS, low latency, and consistent sustained throughput under mixed read/write conditions. Power loss protection, end-to-end data path protection, and advanced ECC help safeguard critical information in always-on environments. The drive is optimized for 24/7 operation and supports extensive monitoring through standard management tools. With a robust endurance rating and efficient thermal behavior, it is suited for data centers and high-demand storage arrays.
