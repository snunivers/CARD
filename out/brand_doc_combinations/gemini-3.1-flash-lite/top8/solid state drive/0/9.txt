Engineered for compact systems, the Samsung 990 Pro is a DRAM-equipped 2TB M.2 NVMe SSD that fits ultrabooks and mini PCs without sacrificing speed. Running on PCIe 4.0, it can deliver up to 5,000MB/s read and 4,400MB/s write performance, helping reduce delays in heavy multitasking and creative applications. The efficient controller is tuned for lower power consumption, which can benefit battery-powered devices. Additional features include AES encryption support, advanced thermal management, and NAND health monitoring. Its roomy capacity makes it ideal for users who need both system speed and substantial local storage.
