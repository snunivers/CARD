Maybelline SuperStay Matte Ink Sheer Balm Lipstick combines a tinted lipstick and nourishing lip balm in one easy product. Designed for quick, mirror-free application, it gives lips a wash of buildable color with a fresh, hydrated finish. The formula includes coconut oil, mango butter, and antioxidants to help lips feel soft throughout the day. Its rounded bullet glides over dry areas and smooths the appearance of fine lip lines. Ideal for casual wear, this lipstick can be worn alone for a natural look or layered over liner to add comfortable color and light shine.
