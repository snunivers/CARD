NARS Powermatte Lipstick Plumping Shine Lipstick is designed to add visible color, glossy shine, and a fuller-looking effect in one step. The cushiony formula contains hydrating oils, peptides, and a mild warming complex that creates a temporary plumping sensation. Color payoff is medium and buildable, making it suitable for both daytime and evening use. The finish reflects light well, helping lips appear smoother and more voluminous. Packaged in a sleek click-close tube, this lipstick is easy to carry and reapply. It works particularly well on dry lips that need comfort with a polished, luminous look.
