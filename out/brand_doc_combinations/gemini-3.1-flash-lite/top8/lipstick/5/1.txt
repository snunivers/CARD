Pat McGrath Labs MatteTrance Lipstick Satin Finish Lipstick is made for users who want color with a touch of moisture and a subtle sheen. Its emollient formula contains shea butter and jojoba oil to soften lips while depositing medium-to-full pigment in one or two swipes. The finish looks smooth and luminous rather than glossy, making it easy to wear every day. A classic bullet shape helps define the cupid’s bow and corners of the mouth. The cap closes securely for travel, and the color wears evenly as it fades without looking patchy.
