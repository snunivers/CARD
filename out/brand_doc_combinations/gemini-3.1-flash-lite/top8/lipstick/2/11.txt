MAC Cosmetics Retro Matte Lipstick Soft Stain Lipstick gives the effect of a lip stain in a convenient stick format. The lightweight formula deposits translucent-to-medium color that can be layered gradually, making it easy to customize intensity. Once applied, it leaves behind a diffused finish that looks natural and wears down gracefully. Hyaluronic acid and lightweight oils help prevent lips from feeling overly dry, while the slim bullet supports quick, accurate application. This product is ideal for users who prefer blurred lip looks over sharp edges. It pairs well with minimal makeup and is easy to refresh throughout the day.
