MAC Cosmetics Retro Matte Lipstick Metallic Finish Lipstick brings reflective color to the lips with fine shimmer and a smooth cream base. Rather than chunky glitter, the formula uses pearl pigments to create a luminous effect that catches light evenly. It offers medium-to-full coverage and can be worn alone for a statement lip or dabbed over another shade for dimension. The texture remains surprisingly comfortable thanks to added emollients and waxes. Best suited for evening makeup, parties, or editorial looks, this lipstick adds depth without feeling gritty. The slim tube allows precise placement, especially on the center of the lips.
