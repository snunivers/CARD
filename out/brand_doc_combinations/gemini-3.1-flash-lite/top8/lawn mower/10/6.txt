The U_Brand U_Model robotic lawn mower automates routine grass cutting for smaller, well-defined yards. It uses boundary wire guidance and returns to its charging station automatically when the battery runs low. A weather-resistant housing allows operation in light rain, and the quiet motor makes it suitable for early or evening schedules. Through a companion app, users can set mowing times, monitor status, and adjust cutting height within a limited range. Built-in lift and tilt sensors stop the blades for safety. This product helps maintain a consistently trimmed lawn with minimal hands-on effort.
