The Craftsman M215 159cc 21-Inch FWD Self-Propelled Mower hybrid lawn mower offers the flexibility of gas-like performance with electric convenience by pairing a compact battery system with a high-torque motor and extended-run power module. Its 20-inch deck works well for medium yards, and the variable-speed self-propel drive reduces effort during longer cuts. A digital control panel displays battery status, selected drive speed, and maintenance reminders. The mower supports mulching, bagging, and side discharge, with a quick-lock height system for fast adjustment. This model appeals to homeowners who want modern controls, reduced noise, and strong everyday mowing capability.
