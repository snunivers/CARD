Built for electrical work, the Milwaukee 11-in-1 Multi-Tip Screwdriver insulated screwdriver is tested for use up to 1000 volts and meets common safety requirements for professional service environments. The slim insulated blade reaches recessed fasteners while helping protect the user during live circuit tasks performed by qualified personnel. Its precision-machined Phillips tip improves contact and reduces cam-out. The ergonomic handle is color coded for quick identification and shaped to resist rolling on flat surfaces. Durable, dependable, and easy to control, this driver is well suited for panel work, outlets, and breaker maintenance.
