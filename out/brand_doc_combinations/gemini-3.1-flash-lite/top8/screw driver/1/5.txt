The compact Wiha SoftFinish Multi-Driver stubby screwdriver is designed for tight spaces where standard tools cannot reach. Measuring just over 1.5 inches in blade length, it fits easily behind appliances, inside cabinets, and around engine components. The slotted tip is formed from hardened alloy steel and finished to resist rust. A broad, palm-friendly handle provides strong grip and better turning power despite the shorter design. Lightweight yet sturdy, this screwdriver is a practical addition to a household or automotive kit, especially for maintenance jobs in cramped or awkward working positions.
