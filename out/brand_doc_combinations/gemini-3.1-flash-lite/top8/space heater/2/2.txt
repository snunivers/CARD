The Dreo Solaris Slim H3 infrared quartz space heater combines radiant warmth with fan-assisted circulation for fast comfort in living rooms, dens, and home offices. Rated at 1500 watts, it offers high, low, and energy-saving modes plus a programmable thermostat with an easy-read display. The infrared elements are designed to warm people and objects directly while helping preserve indoor humidity. A cabinet-style body with side handles makes placement simple, and the included remote adds convenience from across the room. The Dreo Solaris Slim H3 features child lock, overheat shutoff, and a washable intake filter.
