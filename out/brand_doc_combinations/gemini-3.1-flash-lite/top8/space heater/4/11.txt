Small enough for travel yet powerful enough for everyday use, the Honeywell UberHeat Ceramic Heater HCE200B mini plug-in space heater is designed for hotel rooms, offices, and personal corners that need quick supplemental warmth. It plugs directly into a standard wall outlet and delivers up to 500 watts, making it suitable for smaller spaces and spot heating rather than whole-room coverage. An onboard digital thermostat and simple timer help manage comfort without cluttering the floor with cords. The Honeywell UberHeat Ceramic Heater HCE200B rotates to fit different outlet orientations and includes ceramic heating, overheat protection, and a cool-touch outer shell.
