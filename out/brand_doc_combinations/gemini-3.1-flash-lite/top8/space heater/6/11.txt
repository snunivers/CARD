Small enough for travel yet powerful enough for everyday use, the Pelonis Ceramic Tower Heater PHTPU1501 mini plug-in space heater is designed for hotel rooms, offices, and personal corners that need quick supplemental warmth. It plugs directly into a standard wall outlet and delivers up to 500 watts, making it suitable for smaller spaces and spot heating rather than whole-room coverage. An onboard digital thermostat and simple timer help manage comfort without cluttering the floor with cords. The Pelonis Ceramic Tower Heater PHTPU1501 rotates to fit different outlet orientations and includes ceramic heating, overheat protection, and a cool-touch outer shell.
