Designed for car camping and cabin trips, the Western Mountaineering UltraLite offers a roomy rectangular shape that allows plenty of movement during sleep. The bag features a 40°F (4°C) comfort rating, hollow-fiber insulation, and a cotton-blend lining with a soft, familiar feel. A two-way zipper opens fully so the bag can double as a blanket around camp. Measuring 84 x 34 inches, it suits most adults and folds easily into the supplied stuff sack. Reinforced stitching and machine-washable construction make the Western Mountaineering UltraLite practical for frequent weekend use.
