The Marmot Tungsten 2P is an ultralight 1-person trekking tent intended for solo backpackers who count every gram. It uses a hybrid design that pitches with trekking poles or included lightweight supports, keeping packed weight under 1.2 kg. The silnylon fly and bathtub floor resist rain, while mesh inner panels promote ventilation on humid nights. A side-entry door and compact vestibule provide enough room for shoes, a stove bag, and a small pack. Despite its minimal footprint, the sleeping area fits a standard pad comfortably. The Marmot Tungsten 2P is efficient, packable, and trail-focused.
