The W_Brand W_Model is a compact pop-up tent made for festivals, parks, and short camping trips where speed matters most. It opens in seconds with a pre-attached flexible frame and folds into a flat carry case for simple transport. This 3-person design features a polyester shell with UV-resistant treatment, mesh ventilation panels, and a zippered front door with a privacy flap. The floor is water-resistant for light dew and occasional damp ground, though it is best suited for fair-weather use. Lightweight and uncomplicated, the W_Brand W_Model is ideal for casual outdoor outings.
