Ideal for beach days and sunny parks, the The North Face Wawona 6 is a portable sun shelter tent designed for quick comfort outdoors. The open-front shape creates shade for two to three people, while side windows and mesh vents help hot air escape. Sand pockets and ground stakes improve stability in breezy conditions, and the water-resistant floor offers a clean place to sit. The coated fabric provides UPF 50+ protection to help reduce sun exposure during long afternoons. Weighing just 1.8 kg and folding into a shoulder bag, the The North Face Wawona 6 is easy to carry and fast to deploy.
