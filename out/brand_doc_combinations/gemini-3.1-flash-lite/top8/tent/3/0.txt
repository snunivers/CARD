The REI Co-op Half Dome SL 2+ is a lightweight 2-person backpacking tent built for hikers who need dependable weather protection without extra bulk. Its aluminum pole frame sets up quickly and supports a full-coverage rainfly with a 3000 mm waterproof coating. The inner tent uses breathable mesh panels to reduce condensation, while the bathtub floor helps keep gear dry on wet ground. Two doors and dual vestibules provide convenient entry and separate storage space for boots and packs. Packed weight is 2.3 kg, making this tent a practical choice for weekend treks and overnight camping.
