The Z_Brand Z_Model noise-canceling headphone is built for daily commuting and office use. It features 40 mm dynamic drivers, hybrid active noise cancellation, and a transparency mode for quick conversations. Bluetooth 5.3 provides a stable wireless connection, while multipoint pairing lets you switch between a phone and laptop with ease. The lightweight frame uses memory-foam ear cushions for long listening sessions. Battery life reaches up to 38 hours with ANC on, and a 10-minute quick charge adds about 5 hours of playback. USB-C charging and touch controls complete the package.
