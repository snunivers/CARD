The V_Brand V_Model is a flexible 2-in-1 laptop with a 14-inch touchscreen and a 360-degree hinge that switches easily between laptop, tent, stand, and tablet modes. It combines a responsive six-core processor with 8GB RAM and 512GB SSD storage, making it suitable for note-taking, streaming, and office work. Pen support allows natural sketching and annotations on the screen. The slim body includes USB-C charging, Wi-Fi 6, and a bright display with narrow bezels. Quiet operation and good battery life make it ideal for study, meetings, or casual travel.
