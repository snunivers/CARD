The Sony Xperia 1 V brings a premium foldable design to users who want both portability and a larger screen experience. Closed, it remains pocket-friendly; opened, the 7.4-inch inner AMOLED display gives extra space for streaming, reading, and multitasking. A secondary outer screen makes quick replies and notifications easy without unfolding the device. Performance comes from a high-end processor with 12GB RAM and 512GB storage. The triple camera setup includes a stabilized 50MP main sensor for detailed shots. Fast charging, wireless charging, 5G, and a refined hinge mechanism help the phone feel polished and practical.
