The Google Pixel 8 is a rugged smartphone made for demanding conditions. It features a reinforced body with dust and water resistance, drop protection, and a textured frame that is easy to hold with gloves. The 6.58-inch display uses strengthened glass and offers increased brightness for outdoor visibility. Inside, 8GB RAM and 128GB storage support navigation, field reporting, and work apps. A 50MP rear camera captures jobsite or travel images clearly, while the large 5,500mAh battery is designed for dependable all-day power. GPS, NFC, dual SIM support, and programmable shortcut keys add utility.
