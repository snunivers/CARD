For users who want salon-inspired control at home, the T_Brand T_Model combines floating nano-ceramic plates with an easy-to-read LED display. Temperature can be adjusted in 10-degree increments from 130°C to 230°C, supporting a wide range of hair types and styling needs. The rounded barrel edges allow both straightening and loose curling, adding versatility to one tool. Ionic technology helps leave hair looking smoother and shinier, while the memory function recalls the last used setting. It also includes a 3-meter salon-length swivel cord and auto shut-off after one hour.
