Ideal for precision styling, the CHI G2 Ceramic and Titanium features ultra-slim floating plates that can get close to the roots and edges for a smooth finish. The ceramic-coated surface promotes even glide and helps minimize friction on short styles, layers, and fringes. It offers 12 temperature settings from 130°C to 230°C, displayed on a bright digital screen for easy control. Rounded outer edges make it useful for curling ends inward or outward as well. A fast heating element, travel lock, dual voltage support, and 60-minute automatic shut-off add everyday practicality.
