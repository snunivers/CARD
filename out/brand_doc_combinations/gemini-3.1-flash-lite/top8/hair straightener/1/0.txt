The GHD Platinum+ hair straightener is designed for fast daily styling with 1-inch floating ceramic plates that glide smoothly through fine to medium hair. It heats from 140°C to 230°C with six adjustable settings, making it suitable for sleek straight looks or soft bends. A 30-second heat-up time helps save time during busy mornings, while ionic conditioning helps reduce visible frizz and static. The lightweight body includes a 2.5-meter swivel cord, plate lock for storage, and automatic shut-off after 60 minutes for added peace of mind.
