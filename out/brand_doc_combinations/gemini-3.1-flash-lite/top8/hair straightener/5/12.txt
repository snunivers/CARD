The Remington S9500 Pearl Pro hair straightener combines steam-assisted styling with ceramic plates to help smooth stubborn hair while adding a softer-looking finish. A small integrated water chamber produces a controlled steam output, helping the plates glide more easily through dry or frizz-prone sections. Variable temperature control from 150°C to 230°C allows customization based on hair thickness and condition. The device heats rapidly and includes floating plates for better contact during each pass. Its package also includes a filling bottle, heat-resistant pouch, swivel cord, and automatic shut-off for added safety.
