The Remington S9500 Pearl Pro hair straightener is made for gentle styling, using ceramic-coated plates infused with argan and keratin-inspired conditioning elements. The result is even heat distribution and smoother passes that help reduce snagging on dry or color-treated hair. Five temperature settings from 150°C to 220°C provide flexibility without overcomplicating use. It heats quickly, features anti-slip side grips, and has a curved outer housing for straightening or creating soft waves. Safety details include a closing latch, cool-tip ends, and automatic shut-off after 60 minutes of inactivity.
