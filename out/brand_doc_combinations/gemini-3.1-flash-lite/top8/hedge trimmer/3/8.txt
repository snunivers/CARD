The Ryobi RY40610A articulating pole hedge trimmer is designed for versatile trimming across tall, wide, and awkwardly shaped hedges. Its pivoting head locks into several angles, allowing clean cuts along roofline shrubs and hard-to-reach corners. A 20V battery powers the 17-inch blade, which is capable of cutting branches up to 5/8 inch in diameter. The split shaft design makes storage simpler, while the cushioned grip and shoulder strap improve control during extended jobs. Homeowners maintaining tall privacy hedges will appreciate the reach and reduced need for climbing equipment.
