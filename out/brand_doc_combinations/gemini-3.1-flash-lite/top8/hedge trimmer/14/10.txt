The Y_Brand Y_Model offers a straightforward solution for everyday trimming with an emphasis on comfort and control. Powered by a compact 18V battery, it features a 20-inch blade and a balanced center of gravity that reduces strain on the arms. The saw-function teeth near the blade base help handle slightly thicker stems, while the electronic brake stops blade motion quickly after release. A battery charge indicator makes planning jobs easier, and the included blade cover protects the cutting edge in storage. This trimmer works well for routine maintenance around medium-sized residential landscapes.
