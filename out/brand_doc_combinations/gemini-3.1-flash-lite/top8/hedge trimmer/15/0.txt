The Z_Brand Z_Model hedge trimmer is a lightweight corded option designed for routine garden maintenance. It features a 20-inch dual-action steel blade that helps reduce vibration while delivering clean cuts on hedges and shrubs up to 5/8 inch thick. A 3.2-amp motor provides steady cutting power without the need to recharge batteries. The wraparound front handle supports vertical and horizontal trimming, and the built-in cord retention hook helps prevent accidental unplugging. With a slim profile and balanced weight, this model is well suited for homeowners maintaining small to medium yards.
