The Coway Airmega 400 is built for users who want strong purification with minimal maintenance. It covers up to 700 square feet and features a long-life composite filter combining pre-filtration, HEPA capture, and odor adsorption in one cartridge. A dust sensor manages fan speed automatically, while manual settings include silent, normal, and high-performance modes. The large display can be dimmed completely for dark rooms, and the top grille is designed to distribute clean air evenly across wider spaces. The unit’s smooth corners and stable base make it suitable for family environments.
