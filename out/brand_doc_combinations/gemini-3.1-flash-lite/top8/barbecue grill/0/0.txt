The Weber Genesis E-325s barbecue grill is a four-burner gas unit designed for everyday outdoor cooking. It delivers 48,000 BTU across porcelain-coated cast-iron grates, with a 12,000 BTU side burner for sauces or side dishes. A double-layer lid helps retain heat, while the built-in thermometer supports accurate temperature control. Two fold-down side shelves add prep space without taking up too much patio room. Electronic ignition offers quick starts, and the enclosed cart includes storage for tools and a standard propane tank. Locking casters make it easy to move and secure.
