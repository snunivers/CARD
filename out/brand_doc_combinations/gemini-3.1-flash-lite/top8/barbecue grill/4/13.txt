The Weber Original Kettle Premium 22" barbecue grill is a premium gas unit equipped with a rotisserie system for whole chickens, roasts, and larger cuts of meat. It features three stainless steel burners plus a rear rotisserie burner, delivering balanced heat for direct and indirect cooking. The motorized spit kit is included, along with a warming rack and porcelain-enameled cast-iron grates. A double-door cabinet offers concealed storage for accessories and the propane tank. Side shelves provide prep space, with one shelf housing a flush side burner. Ignition is electronic, and the hood thermometer supports better control during longer cooks.
