The Char-Broil Performance 4-Burner Cart Gas Grill barbecue grill brings smart features to pellet cooking with Wi-Fi connectivity and app-based monitoring. Users can adjust temperatures remotely, track probe readings, and receive alerts when pellets run low or food reaches target doneness. The grill offers a range of 165°F to 500°F, suitable for smoking ribs, roasting poultry, or finishing steaks with direct-flame access. Its powder-coated steel body is paired with porcelain-coated grates for durability and easier cleaning. A 22-pound hopper supports long cooks, and the hopper cleanout chute simplifies changing pellet flavors between recipes.
