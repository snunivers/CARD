The Oral-B iO Series 9 electric toothbrush is designed for everyday plaque removal with a slim, lightweight handle and simple one-button control. It offers 3 brushing modes—clean, sensitive, and massage—to suit different preferences. The soft DuPont-style bristles are gentle on enamel while helping sweep along the gumline. A built-in 2-minute timer with 30-second pacing reminders encourages more even brushing. The rechargeable battery delivers up to 25 days of use per charge, and the IPX7 waterproof body can be safely rinsed after use. Includes 2 brush heads and a USB charging cable.
