The Philips Sonicare 4100 Series is a compact electric toothbrush made for commuters, students, and frequent travelers. Its fold-friendly charging stand and lightweight body take up minimal space, while the quiet motor provides consistent sonic cleaning performance. Users can choose from clean, soft, and pulse modes depending on their routine. The built-in timer gives 30-second reminders and ends at the recommended 2-minute mark. This model includes a hard-shell travel case, 2 brush heads, and a USB cable. The battery is rated for up to 35 days, reducing the need for frequent charging.
