The Netgear Nighthawk RS700S is a simple single-unit mesh router designed to expand later as network needs grow. On its own, it covers an apartment or modest home with dual-band WiFi 6 speeds up to AX1800 class. When paired with additional compatible nodes, it creates seamless roaming under one network name. Two internal antennas keep the appearance clean, while beamforming improves device connection quality. The Netgear Nighthawk RS700S includes one Gigabit WAN port and two Gigabit LAN ports, plus guest WiFi, basic parental controls, WPA3 encryption, and app-based diagnostics for signal strength and device activity.
