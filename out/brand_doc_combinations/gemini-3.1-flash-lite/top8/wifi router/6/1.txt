Built for larger households, the Linksys Velop Pro 7 is a tri-band router with mesh-ready support and strong whole-home coverage. It offers one 2.4 GHz band and two 5 GHz bands, helping reduce congestion when many devices are active at once. The unit supports MU-MIMO, OFDMA, and automatic band steering for smoother performance during 4K streaming and online gaming. Connectivity includes a 2.5 Gigabit WAN port, four Gigabit LAN ports, and USB 3.0 for network storage. The Linksys Velop Pro 7 also features guest access, advanced QoS, WPA3 encryption, and web or app management.
