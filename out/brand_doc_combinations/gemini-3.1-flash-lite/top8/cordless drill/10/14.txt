The U_Brand U_Model is a slim cordless drill/driver designed for precision work, maintenance calls, and spaces where a full-size tool is unnecessary. Powered by a 12V battery, it reaches up to 1,500 RPM and includes 21 clutch settings for controlled driving into softwood, plastic anchors, and delicate fixtures. The 1/4-inch hex chuck allows quick changes between drilling and fastening bits, saving time during repetitive jobs. A magnetic screw tray built into the base keeps small hardware nearby, while the compact charger and storage bag make the set easy to carry between rooms.
