Versatile and robust, the Bosch GSB18V-535C cordless drill is suited to both workshop projects and regular jobsite use. Its 20V platform supports a high-output motor, two mechanical speeds, and a maximum torque rating suitable for drilling through timber, metal studs, and dense panel materials. The 1/2-inch metal chuck, LED work light, and trigger-controlled speed adjustment provide dependable everyday usability. A reinforced nylon housing helps resist impacts, and the battery pack includes onboard charge indicators. This package comes with two batteries, charger, carrying case, and a starter set of common drill and driver bits.
