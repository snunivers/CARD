Built for renovation and contractor punch-list tasks, the Milwaukee M12 FUEL 3404-22 is a heavy-duty cordless hammer drill with a durable brushless drive system. It offers three modes, two speed settings, and a high-torque output suitable for drilling large diameter holes and driving structural screws. The auxiliary handle improves leverage, and the all-metal chuck resists slipping under load. A smart control module helps protect the battery from overheating, over-discharge, and overload. This package includes two 5.0Ah batteries, rapid charger, and hard case, making it a reliable option for demanding daily use.
