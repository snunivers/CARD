With a brushed metal finish and premium feel, the Wahl Stainless Steel Lithium Ion 2.0+ beard trimmer is aimed at users who want both style and performance. Titanium-plated blades resist wear and provide smooth cutting across dense facial hair, while four attachment combs support lengths from 0.5 to 16 mm. The digital display shows remaining battery time in minutes, helping prevent mid-trim interruptions. Fast charging delivers 120 minutes of cordless runtime after a full cycle. Combined with a travel pouch and automatic voltage support, the Wahl Stainless Steel Lithium Ion 2.0+ is well suited to frequent travelers.
