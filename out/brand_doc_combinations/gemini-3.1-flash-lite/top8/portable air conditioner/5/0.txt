The Honeywell HL10CESWK portable air conditioner is designed for bedrooms, apartments, and small offices up to 250 square feet. Its 8,000 BTU cooling capacity delivers quick relief on hot days, while three fan speeds and a 24-hour timer help tailor comfort throughout the day. A built-in dehumidifier removes excess moisture, and the washable mesh filter supports easier maintenance. The unit includes a remote control, LED top panel, smooth-rolling caster wheels, and a standard window venting kit. Sleep mode reduces noise at night for more comfortable rest.
