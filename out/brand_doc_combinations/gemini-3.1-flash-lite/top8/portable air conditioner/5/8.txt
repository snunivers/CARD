The Honeywell HL10CESWK portable air conditioner is designed for users who want stronger moisture control alongside reliable cooling. Suitable for rooms up to 400 square feet, it produces 12,000 BTU and can remove a substantial amount of water per day in dehumidifier mode. An onboard bucketless system evaporates much of the collected moisture automatically, reducing the need for frequent draining. The front display is easy to read across the room, and the remote lets you switch between high and low fan settings. Honeywell includes washable filters and a timer.
