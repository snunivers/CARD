Built for medium rooms up to 350 square feet, the SereneLife SLPAC10 combines 10,000 BTU cooling with a dedicated dry mode for humid climates. The compact cabinet fits neatly beside a desk or sofa, and side handles make repositioning simple. Users can choose auto, cool, fan, or dehumidify settings from the touch panel or included remote. This portable air conditioner features automatic restart after power interruption, washable filters, and an adjustable thermostat. A self-evaporative design helps reduce manual draining during typical summer operation.
