With a 10-gallon horizontal tank, the Craftsman CMEC6150K balances portability and usable reserve air for garage projects. It develops 150 PSI and 3.8 SCFM at 90 PSI, making it suitable for finish nailing, stapling, blowing debris, and intermittent wrench use. The oil-free pump removes routine lubrication tasks, and the enclosed shroud helps protect the motor during transport. Large rear wheels and a front pull handle improve maneuverability, while the horizontal design stores neatly under workbenches. Dual gauges, a tank drain, and thermal overload protection support straightforward operation and maintenance.
