For higher-demand applications, the Craftsman CMEC6150K offers an 80-gallon, two-stage configuration designed for automotive, woodworking, and fabrication environments. It produces up to 175 PSI and 15.8 SCFM at 90 PSI, providing enough air for paint booths, plasma support equipment, and multiple pneumatic tools. The cast-iron pump uses an oil-lubricated design for heavy-duty durability, and the finned intercooler helps manage operating temperature. A magnetic starter and heavy-duty motor support consistent starts under load. The vertical tank, manual drain, and protected control assembly make this compressor suitable for permanent shop installation.
