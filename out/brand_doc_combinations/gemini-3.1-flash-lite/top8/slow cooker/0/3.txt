Designed for versatility, the Crock-Pot SCCPVFC600-SS slow cooker combines a 5-quart stoneware crock with a stovetop-safe insert that lets you sear ingredients before slow cooking. This feature helps deepen flavor in beef stews, braises, and sauces without needing extra pans. Adjustable temperature settings and a programmable timer provide flexibility for busy schedules, and the automatic warm mode keeps food ready until serving. The tempered glass lid includes a sturdy handle and snug fit to retain moisture. With durable construction and simple touch controls, this model suits both everyday dinners and weekend cooking projects.
