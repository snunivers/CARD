The Buffalo TeraStation 3420DN is a premium 4-bay NAS for users who want private cloud convenience with advanced data protection. It pairs a quad-core processor with 8 GB RAM, dual 2.5GbE networking, and M.2 slots for SSD cache acceleration. The Buffalo TeraStation 3420DN supports RAID 1, 5, and 10, immutable snapshots, encrypted folders, and one-touch USB backup. It also includes app support for notes, mail, file synchronization, and virtual machine storage, making it versatile for both home labs and professional use. The polished management software simplifies deployment while offering detailed control over storage and services.
