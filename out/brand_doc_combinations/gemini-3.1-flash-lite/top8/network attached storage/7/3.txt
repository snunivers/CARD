For creative professionals, the Buffalo TeraStation 3420DN delivers fast network storage with six bays, SSD cache support, and dual 2.5GbE ports for higher throughput. A powerful quad-core platform with 8 GB of expandable memory helps handle large image libraries, video projects, and multi-user editing workflows. The Buffalo TeraStation 3420DN supports RAID 5, RAID 6, RAID 10, snapshots, and real-time folder synchronization to protect active work. It also includes HDMI output for local playback, multiple USB ports for direct imports, and permission management for studios that need secure shared access across teams.
