The Asustor Flashstor 6 FS6706T is a 4-bay desktop NAS built for family media storage and personal cloud access. It supports up to four SATA drives, hot swap installation, and RAID 1, 5, and JBOD configurations. With a low-noise cooling design, it fits easily in a living room or home office. The Asustor Flashstor 6 FS6706T offers multimedia indexing, smart TV streaming, automatic PC backup, and secure remote login through web and mobile apps. Two USB 3.0 ports simplify external archiving, while user accounts and folder quotas help separate personal files from shared household content.
