Natasha Denona I Need A Nude Eyeshadow Palette is a liquid eyeshadow made for luminous, light-catching color with minimal fallout. The mousse-like formula applies with a flat doe-foot applicator and dries down smoothly to a long-wearing finish that resists creasing. Fine pearl pigments give dimension without feeling gritty, making it ideal for accenting the center of the lid or creating a polished all-over sheen. Wear one layer for a soft sparkle or build for stronger payoff. The compact tube is easy to store in a makeup bag and works well for both day and evening looks.
