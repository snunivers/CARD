Designed for budget-conscious pool owners, the Dolphin Nautilus CC Plus is a simple suction pool cleaner that delivers steady floor coverage with minimal moving parts. It connects to a skimmer or dedicated suction line and uses a diaphragm-driven mechanism to create a tapping motion that helps maneuver around corners. The included regulator valve supports stable operation with variable-speed pumps, and the long hose sections fit many residential pool layouts. Best suited for small to mid-size in-ground pools, the Dolphin Nautilus CC Plus helps remove sand, dirt, and light debris without requiring electricity at the cleaner itself.
