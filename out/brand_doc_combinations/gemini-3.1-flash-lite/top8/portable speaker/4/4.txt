The Marshall Emberton III portable speaker is made for social gatherings, delivering a lively 24W output with dynamic bass boost. Its built-in LED light ring can pulse with the music or stay off for a cleaner look. Bluetooth 5.3 supports quick pairing, and true wireless stereo mode lets two speakers play left and right channels together. The durable enclosure is rated IPX6 against splashes, making it suitable for patios, kitchens, and poolside use. A rechargeable battery provides up to 15 hours of playback, and onboard buttons offer straightforward control without an app.
