The Ultimate Ears Wonderboom 4 is a pocket-friendly portable speaker made for personal listening and light travel. Despite its small size, the 6W driver produces clear, focused audio for podcasts, video calls, and background music. Bluetooth 5.0 handles wireless playback, and a built-in microphone lets you answer calls without picking up your phone. The speaker runs for up to 8 hours on a charge and recharges through USB-C. A wrist strap adds portability, while the soft-touch housing helps protect against everyday scuffs. This model is best suited to bedrooms, offices, and small outdoor settings.
