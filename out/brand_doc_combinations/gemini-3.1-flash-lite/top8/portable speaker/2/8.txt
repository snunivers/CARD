The Ultimate Ears Wonderboom 4 is a versatile portable speaker with features aimed at everyday convenience. Along with Bluetooth 5.0 wireless streaming, it includes a microSD card slot and USB playback for users who prefer offline music libraries. Its 15W output delivers clean, direct sound for bedrooms, kitchens, and small gatherings. The speaker also includes a built-in FM radio and microphone for calls. Battery life reaches about 12 hours on a full charge, and a clear front grille gives it a practical, understated appearance. Rubberized edges improve grip and help prevent slips.
