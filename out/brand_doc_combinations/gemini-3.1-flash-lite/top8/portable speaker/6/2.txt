The Bang & Olufsen Beosound A1 (2nd Gen) is a rugged portable speaker aimed at users who need dependable audio on the move. A shock-resistant shell, reinforced corners, and IP66 protection help it stand up to outdoor conditions. Inside, a 20W speaker system produces energetic sound with punchy bass and crisp treble. Bluetooth 5.0 keeps pairing simple, and the integrated strap allows easy attachment to a backpack or bike. Battery life reaches up to 18 hours, and the speaker can also function as a small emergency power bank through its USB output.
