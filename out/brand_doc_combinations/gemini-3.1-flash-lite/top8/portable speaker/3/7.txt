For listeners who want a stylish speaker for indoor and outdoor use, the Bose SoundLink Flex offers balanced sound in a rounded, easy-to-carry design. The speaker uses a 10W acoustic system tuned for vocals, pop, and light bass-heavy tracks. Bluetooth 5.1 supports quick reconnection to previously paired devices, and multi-function buttons manage playback and calls. The battery provides up to 13 hours of listening at moderate volume. A durable silicone exterior helps absorb bumps, while the IPX7 rating protects against accidental drops in water, making it suitable for pool days and travel.
