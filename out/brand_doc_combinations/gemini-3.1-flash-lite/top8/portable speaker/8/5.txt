Small enough to fit in one hand, the S_Brand S_Model focuses on portability without sacrificing sound quality. A custom 8W driver tuned for speech and acoustic music produces clear output, ideal for personal listening in hotel rooms, offices, or small outdoor spaces. Bluetooth 5.2 offers low-latency pairing, and a built-in microphone allows simple conference calls. The integrated carabiner clip attaches securely to bags and belts. Battery life is rated for 11 hours, and the speaker charges through USB-C. Its IPX4 splash resistance adds everyday durability for travel and commuting.
