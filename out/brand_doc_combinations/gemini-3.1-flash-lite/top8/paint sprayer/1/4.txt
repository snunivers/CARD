The Graco Magnum Project Painter Plus paint sprayer is tailored for fence, deck, and shed maintenance, combining strong coverage with easy operation. Its 700W turbine produces even flow for stains, wood preservatives, and exterior paints, helping users finish weathered surfaces quickly. A 1.3-liter tank reduces refill interruptions during outdoor work.
