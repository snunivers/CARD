Engineered for precision work, the Graco Ultra Cordless Handheld detail paint sprayer suits hobbyists, decorators, and small finishing applications. This mini HVLP system uses a fine atomizing nozzle to apply enamels, primers, sealers, and craft coatings with minimal texture. Adjustable air cap settings support narrow fan, wide fan, and circular spray patterns for better control on shelves, frames, and decorative panels. The 400W motor balances output and noise, while the 800 ml reservoir keeps the unit compact. With a soft-grip handle and tool-free needle access, the Graco Ultra Cordless Handheld offers comfortable handling and straightforward cleaning.
