Designed for cabinet refinishing and smooth indoor surfaces, the HomeRight Super Finish Max turbine paint sprayer emphasizes fine finish quality. Its enclosed turbine system generates warm, dry air that helps coatings level evenly on woodwork, trim, and interior doors. The 5-stage power unit pairs with a lightweight metal spray gun featuring fluid, fan width, and air control adjustments. A 1-quart cup supports enamels, lacquers, urethanes, and properly reduced paints.
