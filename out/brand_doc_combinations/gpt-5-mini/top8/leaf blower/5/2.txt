The DEWALT DCBL720P1 corded electric leaf blower offers dependable performance for routine lawn and driveway maintenance. Powered by a 12-amp motor, it reaches air speeds up to 150 mph and moves 350 CFM, making it effective for dry leaves and dust. Because it plugs into a standard household outlet, you get continuous power without charging delays or fuel mixing. The slim design weighs under 8 pounds and features a lock-on button for easier extended use. An integrated cord retention hook helps prevent accidental unplugging while you work.
