Built for frequent garage use, the DEWALT DXV10P wet-dry vacuum combines a 16-gallon tank with a 6.5 peak HP motor for extended cleanup sessions. It is well suited for collecting wood dust, drywall debris, and water after appliance leaks or storm tracking. The extra-long hose and 18-foot cord provide broad working range, while the balanced dolly base helps the unit roll smoothly around obstacles. Included are a broad floor nozzle, wet squeegee insert, crevice tool, and extension wands. A quick-release drain and integrated blower port add convenience for multi-purpose cleanup tasks.
