The DEWALT DXV10P is a heavy-duty wet-dry vacuum aimed at contractors and serious workshop users. Its 12-gallon tank and 6.5 peak HP motor are suited for collecting sawdust, insulation scraps, nails, and water from demanding cleanup jobs. A built-in drain cap allows quick emptying, while the long 20-foot power cord extends reach across larger work areas. This model includes a rugged hose with positive locking connection, extension wands, floor nozzle, and car nozzle. Large rear wheels and front casters improve mobility over uneven surfaces such as garage floors and jobsite debris.
