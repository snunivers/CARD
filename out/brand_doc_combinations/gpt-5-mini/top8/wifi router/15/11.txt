The energy-efficient Z_Brand Z_Model is a compact router built for users who want stable wireless performance without extra complexity. It offers AC1750 dual-band speeds, making it suitable for HD streaming, web browsing, and light multiplayer gaming. Four internal amplifiers help maintain good signal quality, and smart connect automatically places devices on the most appropriate band. The rear panel includes one Gigabit WAN port, four Gigabit LAN ports, and a USB 2.0 port for printer or file sharing. The Z_Brand Z_Model supports guest access, IPv6, parental filters, and a browser-based setup interface with clear status indicators.
