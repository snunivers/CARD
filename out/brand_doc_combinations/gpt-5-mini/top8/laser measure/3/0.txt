The Bosch GLM 120 C laser measure is a compact distance tool designed for everyday indoor measuring. It offers a measuring range of up to 40 meters with accuracy of ±2 mm, making it suitable for furniture layout, room planning, and renovation work. A backlit LCD displays single, continuous, area, and volume measurements clearly. The device supports meter, foot, and inch units, and stores the last 20 readings for quick reference. Its soft-grip housing improves handling, while automatic shutoff helps extend battery life during routine use.
