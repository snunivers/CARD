Featuring a rugged build and practical measuring functions, the Bosch GLM 120 C is made for warehouse, workshop, and site use. It measures up to 80 meters and includes a color display that adjusts brightness for easier reading. The laser measure can save multiple project files, useful for repeat jobs or room-by-room documentation. A threaded mount allows attachment to a tripod for improved stability during longer measurements. Bosch equips the GLM 120 C with audible key feedback, unit conversion, and a low-battery indicator to keep operation simple and dependable throughout the day.
