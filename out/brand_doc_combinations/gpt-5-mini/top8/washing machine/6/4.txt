The GE GFW650SPNRS washer-dryer combo is a space-saving solution for homes that need both washing and drying in one appliance. It provides an 8 kg wash capacity and 5 kg drying capacity, making it suitable for daily mixed loads. Wash programs include cotton, easy care, delicates, jeans, and quick wash, while sensor drying helps prevent overdrying. A clear LED panel shows cycle status and remaining time at a glance. The GE GFW650SPNRS also features an eco mode, delay timer, and drum clean cycle for easier long-term maintenance.
