For households that value convenience, the Miele WXR860 WCS smart washing machine features Wi-Fi connectivity, voice assistant compatibility, and downloadable wash programs. Its 8 kg front-load design supports a full range of laundry needs, from delicate blouses to heavily used sportswear. The automatic load sensor tailors cycle length, water level, and drum motion for better efficiency. Users can monitor energy consumption through the companion app and receive maintenance notifications when service is needed. The Miele WXR860 WCS also includes steam care, a 20-minute quick cycle, and an antibacterial door seal.
