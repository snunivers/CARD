Built with younger campers in mind, the MontBell Down Hugger 800 #1 youth sleeping bag balances warmth, simple operation, and an easy-to-carry design. Sized at 66 inches long, it fits children and smaller teens without excessive empty space that can reduce heat retention. The synthetic fill is rated for 45°F (7°C), making it suitable for summer campouts, indoor sleepovers, and mild spring nights. A cheerful printed lining, glow-in-the-dark zipper pull, and interior name tag add kid-friendly details. The MontBell Down Hugger 800 #1 also includes a stuff sack and wipes clean easily after travel.
