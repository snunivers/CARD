The Feathered Friends Swallow UL 20 is a spacious semi-rectangular sleeping bag for campers who want extra room without giving up too much thermal efficiency. It carries a 32°F (0°C) rating and combines spiral-hollow insulation with a quilted construction that reduces cold spots. The shell fabric is tough enough for repeated use at campgrounds, while the brushed polyester lining adds a softer feel than standard nylon interiors. A two-way zipper allows foot ventilation on warmer nights, and zipper baffles help block drafts when temperatures fall. The Feathered Friends Swallow UL 20 comes with a carry bag featuring grab handles.
