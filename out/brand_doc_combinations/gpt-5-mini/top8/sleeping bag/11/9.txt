The V_Brand V_Model sleeping bag focuses on comfort for side sleepers who dislike the restriction of narrow mummy bags. A wider hip and knee area allows natural movement, while still maintaining a tapered profile for improved warmth. Rated to 25°F (-4°C), it uses recycled synthetic insulation and a soft-touch inner fabric that feels gentle against skin. The oversized hood accommodates a compact pillow, and the anti-snag zipper curves slightly around the shoulder for easier access. The V_Brand V_Model includes an internal stash pocket, hang loops, and a compression sack for storage.
