The Therm-a-Rest Hyperion 20 is an everyday sleeping bag created for scouts, occasional campers, and emergency preparedness kits. Its practical 45°F (7°C) temperature rating and polyester fill make it useful for mild outdoor nights, guest accommodations, or vehicle storage. The rectangular design is easy to roll and forgiving for restless sleepers. A full-length zipper opens flat for blanket-style use, and the rugged shell resists abrasion from tent floors and camp cots. The included stuff sack has compression straps for tidier packing. The Hyperion 20 offers straightforward comfort without unnecessary complexity.
