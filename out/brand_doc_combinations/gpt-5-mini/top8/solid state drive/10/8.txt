The U_Brand U_Model combines PCIe 3.0 x4 NVMe performance with an affordable 500GB capacity, making it a balanced choice for mainstream desktops and notebooks. In the M.2 2280 format, it reaches up to 3,400MB/s read and 2,300MB/s write speeds for fast booting, software loading, and file movement. 3D NAND, SLC cache acceleration, and an optimized controller help maintain smooth everyday performance. The SSD supports TRIM, S.M.A.R.T., and thermal monitoring to aid reliability. It is well suited for student workstations, home gaming setups, and users moving from SATA storage.
