The S_Brand S_Model is an automatic pool cleaner tailored for flat-bottom above-ground pools where simple operation matters most. This compact robotic unit plugs into a low-voltage power supply and runs a straightforward cleaning cycle that focuses on the floor. Fine filter screens capture dust, grit, and small leaves, while the lightweight body makes lifting and storage easy. No connection to the pool pump is required, reducing setup time for seasonal users. The S_Brand S_Model is especially useful for vinyl-lined pools and helps maintain clearer water with regular scheduled cleanings.
