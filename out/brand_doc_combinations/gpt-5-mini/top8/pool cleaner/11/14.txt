The V_Brand V_Model is a wall-climbing robotic cleaner developed for pools that collect fine dust and stubborn biofilm. Using dual scrubbing brushes and a high-flow pump, it vacuums debris while polishing floor and wall surfaces in a single cycle. Selectable cleaning programs allow quick runs for touch-ups or extended sessions for deeper maintenance. The cartridge filter is easy to remove and rinse, and the handle is shaped for balanced lifting from the water. The V_Brand V_Model works on concrete, fiberglass, tile, and vinyl surfaces in many standard residential pools.
