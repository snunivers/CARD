The AR Blue Clean AR390SS cordless pressure washer offers flexible cleaning where outlets and garden hoses are limited. Powered by a 40V battery platform, it draws from a bucket, tank, or hose connection for light washing around campsites, balconies, cars, and outdoor furniture. Pressure reaches up to 650 PSI with a 0.8 GPM flow, making it suitable for gentle maintenance rather than deep stripping. The multi-pattern nozzle switches quickly between mist, fan, and jet modes. Lightweight construction, a removable battery, and a compact storage case make the AR Blue Clean AR390SS easy to transport.
