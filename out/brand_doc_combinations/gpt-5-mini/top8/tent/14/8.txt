The Y_Brand Y_Model is a spacious tunnel tent made for groups needing efficient use of interior space. Sleeping up to 8 people, it features multiple bedrooms, a central living area, and large windows that brighten the inside during the day. The extended tunnel construction creates excellent floor space for cots, tables, and family gear, while the steel pole sections improve stability once fully guyed out. A full rainfly, sewn-in floor, and taped seams help keep out drafts and moisture. With several access points and adjustable vents, the Y_Brand Y_Model works well for longer holidays at established campgrounds.
