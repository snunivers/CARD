The ASUS ROG Zephyrus G14 is a student-friendly 14-inch laptop that keeps setup simple and performance consistent. A responsive quad-core processor works with 8GB of RAM and 512GB SSD storage, allowing quick access to class materials, cloud documents, and streaming platforms. The narrow-bezel display provides a modern look without making the chassis bulky. Dual microphones and a clear webcam support online lessons and group calls, while the lightweight frame is easy to carry between classes. USB-C, USB-A, HDMI, and wireless connectivity provide enough flexibility for presentations and everyday accessories.
