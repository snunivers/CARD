Charlotte Tilbury Matte Revolution Lipstick Velvet Cream Lipstick provides a plush texture and highly blendable color that settles into a comfortable semi-matte finish. Created for those who prefer flexibility, the formula allows soft diffusion at the edges or full opacity at the center of the lips. A mix of plant-derived oils and lightweight silicones helps the lipstick feel creamy on application while maintaining decent wear time. The standard twist mechanism is easy to use, and the case is sturdy enough for daily storage in a makeup bag. Shades range from muted mauves to warm terracotta and deep plum.
