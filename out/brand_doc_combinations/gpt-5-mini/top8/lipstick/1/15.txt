MAC Retro Matte Lipstick Powder Matte Lipstick is designed to create a blurred, modern lip with a lightweight, almost airy feel. The formula uses soft-focus powders and flexible waxes to deliver smooth, diffused color that can be worn sheer or built up for stronger impact. Unlike traditional dry mattes, it includes nourishing oils to improve glide and reduce cracking. The result is a velvety finish that minimizes the appearance of lip lines. Housed in a slim, portable case, this lipstick is easy to apply directly or tap on with fingers for a softer effect. Shades favor muted, trend-driven tones.
