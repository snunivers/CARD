The Samsung ME21M706BAS convection microwave oven is built for users who want microwave speed with oven-style flexibility. It features 1.5 cu. ft. capacity, 1000-watt microwave power, and convection baking performance for roasting, baking, and reheating in one appliance. Preprogrammed settings support common meals, while combination cooking uses both heat sources to shorten overall cook times. Multi-stage programming lets the oven move automatically between defrosting and cooking. A large turntable accommodates casserole dishes and dinner plates, and the bright interior lighting makes it easier to monitor progress.
