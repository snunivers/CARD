The Panasonic NN-SN686S microwave oven is a budget-friendly choice for basic cooking needs in small homes, guest rooms, or office break areas. It features 0.8 cu. ft. capacity, 800 watts of power, and six quick-cook menus for popular items such as popcorn, pizza, and beverages. Ten power levels provide flexibility for reheating leftovers or softening ingredients. The digital timer and express cook buttons simplify operation, while the removable turntable can be cleaned easily after spills. Its understated black exterior gives the Panasonic NN-SN686S a versatile, unobtrusive appearance.
