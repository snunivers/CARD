Made for communication-heavy setups, the Apple Studio Display combines a 27-inch QHD IPS display with collaboration features that reduce desktop clutter. An integrated webcam, microphone array, and stereo speakers support video meetings without requiring separate accessories, making it a practical choice for remote workers and shared offices. The higher resolution keeps faces, text, and interface elements looking sharp, and the matte coating helps control reflections in bright rooms. USB-C connectivity enables single-cable laptop docking with charging, while additional HDMI and USB ports expand compatibility. The Apple Studio Display also offers ergonomic adjustments and eye-care viewing modes.
