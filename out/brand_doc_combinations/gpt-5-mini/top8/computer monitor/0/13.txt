The LG UltraFine OLED Pro 32EP950 is a 34-inch curved productivity monitor built to simplify modern desk setups. Its 3440 x 1440 resolution gives users ample space for side-by-side applications, timelines, and large spreadsheets, reducing the need for a second display. A built-in USB-C dock supports video, data transfer, Ethernet, and laptop charging through one cable, helping keep the workspace neat. The IPS panel offers stable viewing angles, while the 120 Hz refresh rate makes scrolling and window movement look especially smooth. LG equips the UltraFine OLED Pro 32EP950 with picture-by-picture modes, a KVM switch, and an adjustable stand.
