The Samsung Odyssey Neo G9 (G95NA) is a 24-inch touch monitor intended for kiosks, front desks, classrooms, and interactive workstations. Its 10-point capacitive touchscreen responds quickly to taps, swipes, and pinch gestures, making it useful for software that benefits from direct input. The Full HD display offers sharp visuals, and the edge-to-edge glass surface is easy to clean in shared environments. Users can position the screen at different angles thanks to the flexible stand, which supports a near-flat layout. HDMI, DisplayPort, and USB touch connectivity allow the Samsung Odyssey Neo G9 (G95NA) to integrate smoothly into many systems.
