Built for a deeper clean than hand washing alone, the S_Brand S_Model uses a rotating brush head with dual-speed settings to remove dirt from pores and gently smooth rough areas. The set includes three interchangeable heads: a daily cleansing brush, a soft sensitive-skin brush, and an exfoliating head for occasional use. Its ergonomic handle provides a secure grip, even when wet, and the splash-resistant housing makes bathroom use simple. The brush runs on a rechargeable battery and comes with a charging stand for neat countertop storage.
