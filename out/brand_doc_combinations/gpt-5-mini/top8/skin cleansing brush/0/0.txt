The FOREO LUNA 3 skin cleansing brush is designed for daily facial care with soft silicone touchpoints that help lift oil, sunscreen, and makeup residue without scratching sensitive skin. It offers 8 adjustable intensity levels, a one-button interface, and a curved cleansing surface that reaches around the nose and chin easily. The waterproof body is suitable for sink or shower use, and the rechargeable battery supports up to 30 days of regular use per charge. Compact and lightweight, this brush includes a USB charging cable and protective travel cap for convenient storage.
