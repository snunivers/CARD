The Greenworks 2600502 corded electric string trimmer offers steady performance without charging or fuel mixing. Powered by a 6.5-amp motor, it delivers reliable speed for routine lawn maintenance and light weed control. The 14-inch cutting path helps finish edging and trimming jobs quickly, and the automatic line-feed system minimizes interruptions. An ergonomic front handle and lightweight design make it comfortable for extended use around the yard. This model is especially useful for homeowners who want low-maintenance operation, reduced noise, and consistent power for trimming near patios, walkways, and fences.
