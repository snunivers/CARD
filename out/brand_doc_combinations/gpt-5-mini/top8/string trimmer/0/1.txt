Built for tougher overgrowth, the EGO Power+ ST1521S gas string trimmer features a 25cc 2-cycle engine and a straight shaft for extended reach under shrubs and along property lines. Its 17-inch cutting width helps cover ground efficiently, while the dual-line bump head supports thicker trimmer line for more aggressive cutting. The handle is balanced to reduce fatigue during longer sessions, and the translucent fuel tank makes refills easy to monitor. This trimmer is a practical choice for larger yards that need more power than a standard battery unit can deliver.
