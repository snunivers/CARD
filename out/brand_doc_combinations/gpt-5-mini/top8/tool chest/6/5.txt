For users with limited space, the Mac Tools 63 in. 9-Drawer Pro Series Roller Cabinet compact tool chest delivers efficient storage in a smaller 26-inch format. Six drawers provide room for common household and automotive tools, and the flat top surface can serve as a temporary work area. The chest is made from formed steel with a chip-resistant finish, making it suitable for garages, sheds, and utility rooms. Ball-bearing drawer slides allow smooth opening, and the included liners help reduce noise and movement. With two fixed and two swivel casters, this unit is easy to maneuver while remaining stable during use.
