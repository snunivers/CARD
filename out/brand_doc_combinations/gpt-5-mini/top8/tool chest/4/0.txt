The Craftsman 26 in. 6-Drawer Top Tool Chest tool chest is a 41-inch rolling cabinet built for home garages and light professional use. Its powder-coated steel body resists scratches and corrosion, while seven ball-bearing drawers open smoothly under load. A solid wood work surface adds space for repairs, and the keyed locking system secures hand tools, sockets, and small power tools. Four swivel casters, including two with brakes, make repositioning simple. Drawer liners help prevent shifting, and the lower cabinet offers deep storage for bulkier equipment. This chest combines mobility, organization, and everyday durability.
