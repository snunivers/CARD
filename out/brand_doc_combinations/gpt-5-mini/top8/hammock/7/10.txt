The Grand Trunk Double Parachute Hammock portable hammock is designed for spontaneous relaxation at the beach, park, or campsite. Made from lightweight ripstop nylon, it folds into a compact attached pouch and weighs under two pounds. The fabric supports up to 300 pounds and measures 108 x 54 inches, offering a comfortable single-person fit. Included daisy-chain straps allow multiple suspension points for fast setup between trees. The material is breathable, fast drying, and easy to shake free of sand or dirt. This simple, packable design is practical for travel, day trips, and emergency backup use.
