For minimalist campers, the Grand Trunk Double Parachute Hammock ultralight hammock focuses on low weight and efficient packing without sacrificing strength. The 20D nylon construction weighs about 16 ounces yet supports up to 250 pounds. It measures 106 x 52 inches and compresses into a palm-sized stuff sack that can clip to a pack. Bar-tacked stress points and narrow but strong suspension straps help reduce bulk for thru-hikers and bikepackers. The smooth fabric feels soft against the skin and dries quickly after morning dew. This streamlined hammock is best for solo users prioritizing portability above extra space.
