Built for camping and travel, the Kammok Roo Double Hammock parachute nylon hammock packs into an integrated stuff sack for easy storage in a backpack or glove box. The lightweight fabric dries quickly, resists tearing, and supports up to 400 pounds. Measuring 118 x 78 inches, it provides enough space for comfortable overnight use or daytime lounging at the campsite. Triple-stitched seams add strength, and included tree straps with multiple loop points simplify setup without tying knots. This compact hammock is a practical choice for hikers, festival-goers, and weekend adventurers.
