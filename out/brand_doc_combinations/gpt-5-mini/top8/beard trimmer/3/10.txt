The Braun BT7240 beard trimmer emphasizes skin comfort with rounded blade tips and a guard design intended to reduce irritation on sensitive areas. It offers 15 selectable settings between 0.4 and 12 mm, making it easy to maintain anything from light stubble to a neatly shaped beard. The waterproof head detaches for thorough cleaning, and the quiet motor keeps noise levels lower during early morning use. A single charge gives around 80 minutes of runtime. The BT7240 is a strong pick for users prioritizing gentle trimming and easy upkeep.
