Made for wet or dry use, the Panasonic ER-GB80-S beard trimmer features a fully waterproof body that can be used with foam, gel, or in the shower. Ceramic blades help reduce heat buildup during longer sessions and are paired with 12 guide settings from 1 to 15 mm. The anti-slip rubberized handle stays secure even with wet hands, and the pop-up detail trimmer refines mustache edges. A charging stand stores the unit neatly and provides 80 minutes of cordless runtime. The Panasonic ER-GB80-S suits users who prefer flexible grooming routines.
