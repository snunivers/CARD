Engineered for detailed beard sculpting, the V_Brand V_Model features a dual-edge cutting head that trims in either direction for better control around curves and corners. This design helps when refining goatees, outlining mustaches, or defining a fade along the cheek. The trimmer includes three precision combs and an open blade option for close edging. Its compact body is balanced for fingertip control, and the rechargeable battery supports 55 minutes of use. The V_Brand V_Model is especially useful for users who regularly maintain intricate beard styles rather than simple all-over trimming.
