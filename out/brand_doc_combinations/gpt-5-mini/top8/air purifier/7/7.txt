Designed for allergy season and everyday dust control, the Molekule Air Pro cleans rooms up to 500 square feet using a 3-stage filtration cartridge. The system removes pollen, mold spores, lint, and smoke particles while the carbon layer helps freshen stale indoor air. A top-mounted digital display shows fan speed, timer setting, and filter status at a glance. This purifier includes an intelligent sleep program that learns preferred nighttime settings over time. Energy-efficient performance and certified low standby consumption make it suitable for long hours of use in occupied rooms.
