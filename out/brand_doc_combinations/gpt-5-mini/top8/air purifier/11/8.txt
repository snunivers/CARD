The V_Brand V_Model is engineered for homes with pets, offering enhanced odor control and powerful circulation in spaces up to 450 square feet. Its extra-large pre-filter captures hair and larger debris before air reaches the HEPA stage, which helps extend filter life. The activated carbon section is optimized for litter box, wet dog, and cooking odors. Users can switch between quiet, standard, and boost modes depending on room conditions. A replaceable filter set, carry handles, and a clean, easy-to-wipe exterior make this model especially practical for busy households.
