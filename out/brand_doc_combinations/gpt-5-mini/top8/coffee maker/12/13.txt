The W_Brand W_Model is a commercial-style coffee maker intended for meeting rooms, cafés, or high-demand home use. Its large-capacity urn brews up to 30 cups and uses a twist-lock lid, dual side handles, and a front dispensing tap for efficient self-service. A water level indicator and ready-light simplify monitoring during events, while the stainless steel housing stands up to repeated use. Internal heating helps maintain serving temperature after brewing. Designed for durability and straightforward operation, the W_Brand W_Model supports gatherings, catered functions, and workplaces that need coffee available for many people.
