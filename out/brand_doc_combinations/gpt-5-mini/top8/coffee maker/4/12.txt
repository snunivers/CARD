With a retro-inspired exterior and modern brewing functions, the Nespresso VertuoPlus adds visual character to the kitchen while delivering dependable performance. This 6-cup coffee maker features a compact footprint, tempered glass carafe, and simple programmable clock with auto-start. A reusable filter basket helps reduce paper waste, and the anti-drip valve allows users to pour a cup before brewing ends. Indicator lights show power and warming status at a glance. The Nespresso VertuoPlus is a good match for smaller households seeking practical daily coffee service in a stylish countertop appliance.
