Pat McGrath Labs Mothership VIII: Divine Rose II is a pressed powder eyeshadow palette designed for everyday wear with six coordinated shades in matte, satin, and soft shimmer finishes. The smooth, talc-based formula blends evenly with a brush or fingertip and builds from a sheer wash to medium coverage without looking patchy. A lightweight compact with mirror makes touch-ups easy during travel. Shades are curated for neutral looks, contouring the crease, and adding subtle highlight to the lid or brow bone. Suitable for most skin tones, it offers up to eight hours of comfortable wear.
