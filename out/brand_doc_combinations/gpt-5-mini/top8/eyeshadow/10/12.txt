U_Brand U_Model is a duo-chrome eyeshadow single made for multidimensional eye looks with noticeable shift between two complementary tones. Depending on lighting and angle, the finish may move from pink to gold, teal to bronze, or lavender to blue, adding visual depth with little effort.
