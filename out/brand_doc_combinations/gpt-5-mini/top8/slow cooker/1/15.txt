Built for substantial portions, the Instant Pot Duo 7-in-1 Electric Pressure Cooker (Slow Cook Function) extra-large slow cooker features a 10-quart capacity for gatherings, meal prep, and cooking in bulk. Its oversized oval crock can hold large roasts, whole poultry, and generous quantities of soup or chili. Despite the size, operation remains simple with basic High, Low, and Warm settings on an easy-access front dial. The removable stoneware insert helps with serving and cleanup, and the glass lid allows visibility during long cooking cycles. Strong side handles and a stable base support safer movement when the cooker is full.
