The Instant Pot Duo 7-in-1 Electric Pressure Cooker (Slow Cook Function) multi-use slow cooker is built with a 5.5-quart capacity and flexible cooking modes for slow cook, brown, and steam functions. Browning ingredients directly in the nonstick cooking pot saves time and adds flavor before switching to a lower simmer. The digital interface offers adjustable temperature control and timer settings for a range of recipes, from stews to rice dishes. A vented glass lid helps manage moisture, and the removable insert supports easy serving and washing. This model is a good fit for users who want more capability from one countertop appliance.
