The Z_Brand Z_Model is an 850W power supply created for high-end gaming rigs with power-hungry graphics hardware. It features 80 PLUS Gold certification, a fully modular layout, and a strong single +12V rail for stable delivery under heavy load. Native support for current-generation graphics cards is provided through a 12VHPWR connector alongside traditional PCIe 6+2 cables. The 140 mm fan uses a quiet bearing design and a zero-RPM mode at low temperatures. Premium capacitors, active PFC, and complete safety protections help ensure efficient, dependable operation in demanding systems.
