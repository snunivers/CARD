The S_Brand S_Model is a 550W ATX power supply designed for dependable everyday gaming and productivity use. It meets 80 PLUS Bronze standards and uses a semi-modular layout that leaves only the essential motherboard and CPU cables permanently attached. This setup reduces clutter while keeping installation straightforward for first-time builders. A 120 mm fan with optimized blade geometry helps move air efficiently and stay relatively quiet under moderate load. The unit includes two PCIe 6+2 connectors, several SATA plugs for storage, and standard protections to guard against voltage irregularities, overload, and short circuits.
