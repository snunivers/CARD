The Seasonic PRIME PX-1000 Platinum provides 500W of dependable power for mainstream PCs that need a straightforward, cost-conscious upgrade. This non-modular ATX unit is easy to install and comes with fixed flat cables that simplify routing compared with older sleeved designs. An 80 PLUS White efficiency rating supports stable day-to-day operation for family desktops, student systems, and casual gaming builds. The 120 mm fan uses automatic speed control to reduce unnecessary noise. Standard connectors include 24-pin ATX, 4+4 CPU, one 6+2 PCIe lead, SATA power for multiple drives, and classic peripheral plugs.
