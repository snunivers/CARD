The X_Brand X_Model offers 650W of reliable output for mid-range gaming systems and content creation desktops. This semi-modular ATX design keeps essential cables attached while allowing optional leads to be added only when needed, improving internal airflow. Certified 80 PLUS Bronze, it balances efficiency and value for builders seeking dependable everyday performance. A temperature-controlled 140 mm fan helps limit noise during normal use. Connectivity includes a 24-pin ATX cable, 8-pin EPS, two 6+2 PCIe connectors, and multiple SATA and peripheral plugs for storage drives, fans, and accessories.
