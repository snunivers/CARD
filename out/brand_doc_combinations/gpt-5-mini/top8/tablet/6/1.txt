Designed for portability, the Apple iPad mini (6th generation) offers an 8-inch display in a lightweight body that fits easily into a small bag. It runs on a quad-core chipset with 3GB RAM and 64GB internal storage, making it suitable for reading, casual apps, and web access. The battery is optimized for extended standby and up to 10 hours of mixed usage. Front and rear cameras support basic video chats and document capture. The Apple iPad mini (6th generation) also includes Wi‑Fi, GPS, a headphone jack, and expandable storage for users who want a simple, travel-friendly tablet.
