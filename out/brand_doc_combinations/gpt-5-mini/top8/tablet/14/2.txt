The Y_Brand Y_Model is a productivity-focused 11-inch tablet with a 2K display and support for an optional keyboard cover and active stylus. Powered by a fast octa-core platform, it includes 8GB RAM and 256GB storage for multitasking, note-taking, and document editing. Split-screen mode and desktop-style software tools help manage work on the go. A 13MP rear camera, 8MP ultra-wide front camera, and dual microphones improve video conferencing quality. The Y_Brand Y_Model also supports Wi‑Fi 6, Bluetooth 5.2, and quick charging through USB-C for efficient daily use.
