The Apple iPad Pro 12.9-inch (M2) is a slim 10.1-inch tablet built for everyday streaming, browsing, and light work. It features a bright Full HD display, an octa-core processor, 4GB RAM, and 128GB of storage with microSD expansion. Dual speakers deliver clear sound for video calls and movies, while the 8MP rear camera and 5MP front camera handle quick photos and meetings. A 7,000mAh battery provides all-day use, and fast charging helps reduce downtime. With Wi‑Fi, Bluetooth, and USB-C, the Apple iPad Pro 12.9-inch (M2) covers essential connectivity in a practical package.
