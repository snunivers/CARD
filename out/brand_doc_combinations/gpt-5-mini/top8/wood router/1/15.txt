The Bosch 1617EVSPK compact plunge router is a versatile choice for users who need controlled entry cuts in a smaller format. Its 8.5-amp motor runs from 10,000 to 25,000 RPM and is suitable for inlay recesses, sign making, and trim carpentry. Spring-loaded plunge action feels smooth and predictable, while the depth rod and turret stop allow repeatable cuts without overcomplicating setup. A clear chip guard improves visibility near the bit, and the soft-grip handles increase comfort. The router uses 1/4-inch bits, includes a circle guide, and connects to standard dust extraction hoses.
