The DeWalt DWP611PK dual-handle fixed-base router offers steady control for long edge passes and pattern routing. A 2 HP, 12-amp motor provides 10,000 to 24,000 RPM, with soft-start behavior that reduces initial twist when the tool powers on. Precision depth settings are managed through a coarse release and fine-thread adjuster, useful when matching profiles or setting exact groove depth. The wide base opening accepts larger guide bushings, and the balanced housing reduces chatter. This DeWalt DWP611PK includes both 1/4-inch and 1/2-inch collets, a straight edge guide, and a vacuum-compatible dust port.
