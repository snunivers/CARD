The DeWalt DWP611PK heavy-duty wood router is intended for demanding joinery and raised-panel applications. A 15-amp, 3.25 HP motor supplies strong torque and a variable speed range of 10,000 to 21,000 RPM. Constant-speed circuitry helps maintain cut quality through dense oak, maple, and hardwood composites. The oversized handles provide better control, and the precision depth adjustment allows repeatable settings when making grooves or dovetail jig passes. This model accepts 1/2-inch and 1/4-inch bits, includes a wrench set and edge guide, and is compatible with many router tables for dedicated shop use.
