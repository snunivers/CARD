Compact but capable, the Bosch PR20EVS woodworking router is designed for homeowners upgrading from basic tools. The 9.5-amp motor reaches 11,000 to 26,000 RPM, making it versatile enough for shelf dados, corner round-overs, and small decorative profiles. A simple cam-lock system lets users adjust the motor depth quickly, while the cast base plate keeps the router steady on flat stock. The switch is sealed against dust, and the cord exits high on the housing to stay clear of the work surface. Included are a 1/4-inch collet, guide fence, and template guide adapter.
