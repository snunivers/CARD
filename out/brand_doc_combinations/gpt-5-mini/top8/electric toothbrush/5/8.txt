The Burst Sonic Electric Toothbrush electric toothbrush is designed for simple, reliable performance without unnecessary complexity. It uses oscillating and pulsating movements to help remove plaque more effectively than a manual brush. Two cleaning modes cover daily use and sensitive care, making it suitable for a wide range of users. The non-slip rubberized grip improves handling, and the replaceable brush head clicks securely into place. A full battery provides about 21 days of use, and the charging indicator shows when power is running low. Includes one spare head and a compact charging base.
