Balanced between gentle care and strong cleaning action, the Colgate Hum Smart Electric Toothbrush electric toothbrush is suitable for daily plaque control, stain maintenance, and gumline cleaning. It uses sonic vibration technology and a curved brush profile to better follow the natural shape of teeth. There are 4 modes, including soft, clean, white, and massage, plus a memory feature that recalls the last setting used. The handle charges quickly and delivers up to 38 days of battery life. Additional features include a quadrant timer, low-noise operation, and 2 replaceable brush heads with hygienic covers.
