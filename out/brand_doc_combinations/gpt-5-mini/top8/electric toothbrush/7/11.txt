Created for deep everyday cleaning, the Fairywill FW-507 Sonic Electric Toothbrush electric toothbrush delivers up to 40,000 strokes per minute for thorough plaque removal and fresher breath. Its W-shaped bristle layout helps sweep across uneven tooth surfaces, while the back of the head includes a tongue-cleaning texture. Users can select from 4 modes and 3 intensity levels for a more customized routine. The long-lasting battery supports around 45 days of use on a full charge. The package includes a charging cable, 3 color-coded brush heads, and a compact stand for bathroom storage.
