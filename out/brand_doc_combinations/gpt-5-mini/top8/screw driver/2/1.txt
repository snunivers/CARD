Designed for controlled performance, the Klein Tools Cushion-Grip Phillips #2 6-Inch (603-6) flathead screwdriver combines a precision-ground 6 mm slotted tip with a durable steel shank for consistent fit. The blade is hardened to handle repeated use without twisting under normal torque. Its acetate handle offers a classic feel and includes fluted sides for a secure grip, even with oily hands. A hanging hole at the end allows convenient storage on pegboards. This screwdriver suits electrical cover plates, cabinet hardware, and a wide range of standard household fastening applications.
