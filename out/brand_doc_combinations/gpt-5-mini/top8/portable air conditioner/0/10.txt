The Whynter ARC-14S is a straightforward portable air conditioner for everyday household use in spaces up to 275 square feet. With 8,500 BTU cooling, it suits bedrooms, small dens, and home offices that need targeted comfort without permanent installation. The unit offers cool, dry, and fan settings, plus an adjustable thermostat and two fan speeds. A removable, washable filter helps maintain airflow, and the rear drain outlet supports continuous drainage when needed. The ARC-14S ships with a remote control, exhaust hose, and an expandable window bracket for quick setup.
