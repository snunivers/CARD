The Midea MAP12S1CWT portable air conditioner focuses on quiet operation for nurseries, studies, and bedrooms up to 300 square feet. Its 9,000 BTU cooling system runs at a lower sound level in sleep mode, helping minimize disruptions during work or rest. Users can adjust between two fan speeds, set a 24-hour timer, and choose dry mode when humidity rises. A washable rear filter captures dust, and the filter alert reminds you when cleaning is needed. With integrated wheels and side handles, the MAP12S1CWT moves easily from room to room.
