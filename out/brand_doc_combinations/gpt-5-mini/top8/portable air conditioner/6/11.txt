Made for larger bedrooms and living rooms, the Midea MAP12S1CWT delivers 12,500 BTU cooling with an energy-conscious auto mode that adjusts performance to the room temperature. This portable air conditioner includes horizontal swing airflow, helping spread cool air beyond a single direction. A digital panel on the top of the cabinet keeps controls accessible, and the remote mirrors the main functions for convenience. Dry mode reduces humidity during rainy weather, while sleep mode softens operation overnight. Midea finishes the package with rolling casters, washable filter access, and a window venting kit.
