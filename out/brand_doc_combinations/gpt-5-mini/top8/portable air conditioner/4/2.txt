The BLACK+DECKER BPACT14WT is a dual-hose portable air conditioner aimed at faster, more efficient cooling in rooms up to 450 square feet. With 12,000 BTU performance, it cools large living areas while maintaining better air circulation than many single-hose designs. Features include electronic controls, a clear digital display, and a programmable timer for scheduled use. Dehumidification mode helps manage sticky indoor air, and the swing louvers spread airflow evenly. BLACK+DECKER includes a window kit, exhaust hoses, caster wheels, and a reusable filter for practical everyday convenience.
