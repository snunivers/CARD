The Greenworks GD60HT hedge trimmer focuses on compact handling for smaller gardens, courtyards, and routine touch-up work. It features a short 16-inch blade paired with a 2.8-amp electric motor, making it easy to guide around decorative hedges and detailed shrub lines. Despite its smaller size, the dual-action blade can cut stems up to 1/2 inch thick with minimal chatter. An ergonomic D-shaped front handle improves grip in different angles, and a lock-off switch enhances safety during storage and transport. This model suits users who value simplicity and low overall weight.
