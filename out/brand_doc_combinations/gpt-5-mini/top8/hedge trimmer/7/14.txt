The Greenworks GD60HT hedge trimmer is designed for users who want dependable corded performance with added durability. Its 5-amp motor drives a 24-inch blade, providing enough reach for broad shrubs and established hedges without sacrificing control. The dual-action cutting system helps limit vibration and supports smoother operation through branches up to 3/4 inch thick. A full-length front guard, rear trigger lock, and cord retention system improve safety during use. Because it runs on household power, this model is ideal for uninterrupted trimming sessions where an outlet and extension cord are readily available.
