The Synology DiskStation DS923+ is a compact single-bay NAS aimed at apartment users, students, or anyone needing simple network storage without complex setup. It supports one 3.5-inch or 2.5-inch SATA drive and offers file sharing over a home network with low power consumption and quiet operation. Useful features include automatic photo backup from phones, DLNA media serving, USB copy, and scheduled sync to cloud services. The Synology DiskStation DS923+ is ideal for storing documents, music, and video libraries, and its intuitive management software helps first-time NAS buyers get started quickly.
