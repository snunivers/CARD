The TerraMaster F4-422 is a rackmount NAS with 12 bays, created for IT rooms handling centralized backup, shared storage, and surveillance archives. It supports high-capacity SATA or nearline drives, RAID 5, 6, 50, and hot spare options for stronger fault tolerance. Equipped with a multi-core processor, 16 GB memory, and dual 10GbE networking, the TerraMaster F4-422 is built for high concurrent access. Administrators can manage snapshots, replication jobs, Active Directory integration, and alert notifications from a unified dashboard. Tool-less trays and front-access maintenance simplify deployment in professional environments.
