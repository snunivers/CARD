The Remington S9500PP Pearl Pro Ceramic 1-inch Flat Iron is a wide-plate hair straightener designed to speed up styling for long, thick, or textured hair. Its 45 mm ceramic plates cover more hair in each pass, helping reduce total styling time. Heat can be selected between 160°C and 230°C, and the advanced heater maintains consistent temperature across the full plate surface. The housing stays comfortable to hold, even during extended use, thanks to insulated outer panels. This model also supports quick heat-up, includes a salon-length rotating cord, and features an auto shut-off system for safer everyday storage.
