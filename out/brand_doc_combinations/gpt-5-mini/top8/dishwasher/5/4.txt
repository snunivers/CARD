The Samsung DW80R9950UT panel-ready dishwasher is designed to blend seamlessly into custom kitchens while offering premium cleaning features. This fully integrated 24-inch model supports 15 place settings and includes a third-level cutlery tray for improved organization. Eight wash cycles and targeted spray zones help tackle baked-on food, delicate glassware, and everyday dishes with equal care. At just 42 dBA, it runs quietly enough for open-concept living spaces. The stainless tub enhances durability, and the auto-open door feature improves drying at the end of the cycle. Adjustable racks and interior lighting complete its upscale, functional design.
