Ideal for apartments, offices, and small households, the T_Brand T_Model countertop dishwasher provides full cleaning functionality in a compact footprint. It connects easily to a standard kitchen faucet and holds up to six place settings. Seven programs, including Quick, Intensive, Glass, Eco, and Baby Care, offer flexibility for different load types. The clear digital display shows cycle progress and remaining time, while a delayed start feature adds convenience. Its stainless steel interior resists staining and helps maintain cleanliness. With low water consumption and simple push-button controls, this model suits spaces where a full-size dishwasher is impractical.
