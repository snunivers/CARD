For kitchens needing flexible installation, the Whirlpool WDT970SAHZ portable dishwasher combines full-size cleaning with mobility. Mounted on casters, this 24-inch unit can be rolled into place and connected to a compatible faucet when needed, then stored away afterward. It fits 12 place settings and offers standard cycles such as Heavy, Normal, Light, and Quick. A laminated wood-look top provides useful extra workspace in compact kitchens. The interior includes a silverware basket and durable racks designed for daily use. With front controls, a simple display, and 53 dBA operation, it offers convenience for spaces without built-in cabinetry.
