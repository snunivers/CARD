The Bio Ionic 10X Ultralight Speed Dryer brings together portability and polished styling performance in a slim, modern design. Its foldable handle and lightweight housing make it easy to pack, yet the 1800-watt output still provides strong everyday drying power. Ionic technology helps reduce static, while two heat settings and two speeds allow simple adjustments. A cool-shot function adds finishing control for waves, bangs, or smoother ends. The set includes a compact concentrator nozzle for directed airflow. With dual voltage support and a storage pouch, this dryer is especially practical for frequent travelers.
