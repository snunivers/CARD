Built for travel and small-space convenience, the Bio Ionic 10X Ultralight Speed Dryer hair dryer features a compact folding handle and dual voltage compatibility. The 1600-watt output delivers dependable drying performance without adding bulk to luggage. Two heat settings and two speed options provide simple control, while the included concentrator helps direct airflow for touch-ups and sleek finishes. Its ergonomic grip makes it comfortable to hold, and the lightweight construction suits frequent use on the go. A detachable filter simplifies maintenance, and the long cord offers flexibility in hotel bathrooms or shared spaces.
