The S_Brand S_Model barbecue grill is a built-in gas model created for outdoor kitchen installations. It includes five stainless steel burners producing a combined 60,000 BTU, plus a rear infrared burner designed for rotisserie cooking. Heavy-duty stainless steel cooking grates provide strong heat retention and a professional look. Interior halogen lights improve visibility for evening grilling, and the double-walled hood helps maintain stable temperatures. Flame tamers reduce flare-ups and protect the burners. The control knobs feature LED illumination, while electronic ignition supports quick startup. This grill suits homeowners planning a permanent, premium backyard setup.
