The Big Green Egg Large barbecue grill is designed for high-heat performance with an infrared main burner that quickly reaches searing temperatures. This gas-powered model includes stainless steel grates, a 430-grade steel hood, and a 15,000 BTU side burner for additional cooking flexibility. Infrared heat helps lock in juices while reducing flare-ups from dripping fat. The compact cart frame fits patios with limited room but still offers a lower shelf for storing utensils and grill covers. A built-in temperature gauge and battery-free ignition support straightforward operation, making it a practical choice for frequent weeknight grilling.
