For users who want a straightforward drill for home maintenance, the Metabo HPT DV18DBFL2 offers dependable performance in a lightweight 14.4V platform. The tool includes a 3/8-inch keyless chuck, variable-speed trigger, forward and reverse switch, and 16 clutch settings to reduce stripped screws. Its slim battery pack fits into a compact handle, making the drill easy to maneuver in closets, shelves, and under sinks. A battery fuel gauge shows remaining charge at a glance. This model is especially suited to hanging fixtures, assembling flat-pack furniture, and simple weekend repair projects.
