The Bosch GSR18V-190B is a practical cordless drill created for apartment living, student housing, and first-time tool buyers. Running on a 10.8V lithium-ion battery, it offers enough power for picture hanging, cabinet hardware installation, and basic repair tasks without feeling bulky. The drill has a 1/4-inch hex quick-change holder, making it especially convenient for screwdriver bits and small drill accessories. Eleven clutch settings help prevent overtightening, and the built-in LED improves visibility behind appliances or inside drawers. A USB-C charging cable adds simple, modern charging without the need for a larger dock.
