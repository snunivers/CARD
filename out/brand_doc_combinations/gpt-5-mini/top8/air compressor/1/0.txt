The DEWALT DWFP55126 is a 6-gallon pancake air compressor designed for light household and garage tasks. Its oil-free pump reduces maintenance, while the 150 PSI maximum pressure helps deliver longer tool run time. Rated at 2.6 SCFM at 90 PSI, it supports brad nailers, staplers, tire inflation, and cleaning jobs. A compact frame, integrated handle, and rubber feet improve portability and stability. The unit features dual quick-connect couplers, a cord wrap, and an easy-read control panel with regulator and tank pressure gauges for convenient day-to-day operation.
