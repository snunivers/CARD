The Quincy QT-54 26-gallon vertical compressor is aimed at DIY users who want more air capacity without moving into a full shop machine. Its 1.9 HP motor supplies 150 PSI and 4.2 SCFM at 90 PSI, enough for trim tools, light sanding, inflation, and occasional automotive tasks. An oil-free pump keeps service requirements low, and the upright tank occupies less floor area in crowded garages. The compressor features a reinforced handle, durable wheels, and a wide base for stable positioning. Clearly marked controls and a fast-recovery pump make it easy to use.
