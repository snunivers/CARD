The Bowers & Wilkins PX7 S2 is engineered for frequent flyers who need dependable comfort and consistent sound. Its multi-mic ANC system reduces engine noise and background chatter, while a transparency setting helps during boarding announcements. The over-ear design uses lightweight aluminum accents and replaceable ear pads for long-term wear. Tuned 42 mm drivers deliver warm bass, natural vocals, and crisp treble without sounding harsh. Bluetooth supports quick device switching, and the included airplane adapter expands travel compatibility. Expect up to 34 hours of wireless playback with ANC enabled, plus compact folding hinges and a protective hard case.
