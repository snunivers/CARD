The Sony WH-1000XM5 is designed for listeners who want a lightweight headphone with effective active noise cancellation. Its slim over-ear profile fits comfortably under a hood or travel pillow, and the cushioned pads create a secure seal without excessive clamping. Inside, 40 mm neodymium drivers deliver energetic bass, open mids, and clean treble for balanced everyday sound. Wireless connectivity includes Bluetooth 5.2 and multipoint support for two devices at once. Battery life reaches 31 hours with ANC on, and a 15-minute charge provides roughly 4 hours of playback. A detachable cable is included.
