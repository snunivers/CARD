The Shure AONIC 50 combines studio-inspired tuning with travel-friendly noise cancellation. Its carefully tuned 40 mm drivers aim for detail and stereo separation, while adjustable ANC lets listeners choose between mild and maximum suppression. An ambient pass-through feature is useful for commuting or hearing colleagues. The headphone supports wired high-resolution playback through the included cable and wireless operation through Bluetooth with AAC and SBC codecs. A four-microphone array improves voice quality during meetings. With up to 33 hours of battery life, quick charging, and a padded headband, it fits both work and leisure use.
