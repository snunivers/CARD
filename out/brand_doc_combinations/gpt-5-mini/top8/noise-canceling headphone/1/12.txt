The Bose QuietComfort Ultra Headphones delivers a rich listening experience with practical travel features. Adaptive noise cancellation responds to surrounding sound levels, reducing distractions automatically whether you are in a café or on a flight. Large 45 mm drivers provide full-bodied bass and spacious presentation for music, movies, and podcasts. The headphone supports wired listening for in-flight entertainment systems and wireless playback for phones and tablets. Touch controls on the ear cup manage volume, track changes, and calls. A full battery lasts up to 30 hours with ANC enabled, and the included case protects the foldable frame.
