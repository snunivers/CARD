Compact and modern, the Bang & Olufsen Beoplay H95 brings premium-style features to everyday listening. This noise-canceling headphone uses hybrid ANC and passive isolation from thick over-ear cushions to reduce distractions at home or on the move. A dynamic 40 mm driver setup emphasizes clean vocals and controlled bass, making it suitable for pop, podcasts, and calls. It supports Bluetooth 5.3, multipoint connection, and a low-power mode for longer trips. Battery life reaches 42 hours without ANC and 29 hours with it active. Touch gestures, USB-C charging, and foldable ear cups add practical convenience.
