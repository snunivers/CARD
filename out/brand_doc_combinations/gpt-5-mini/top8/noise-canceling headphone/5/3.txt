Built for long workdays, the Bang & Olufsen Beoplay H95 offers comfortable over-ear fit and dependable active noise cancellation. It uses 40 mm composite drivers for clear speech, accurate instruments, and punchy bass that does not overwhelm podcasts or meetings. The integrated mic system includes ENC support for cleaner voice pickup during calls. Bluetooth multipoint, simple button controls, and voice assistant compatibility make operation straightforward. A full charge delivers up to 40 hours of playback with ANC off, or about 28 hours with it on. The ear cups fold inward for easier storage in a desk drawer or bag.
