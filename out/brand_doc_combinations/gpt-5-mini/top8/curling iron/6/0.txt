The CHI Spin N Curl curling iron is designed for smooth, defined curls with less frizz. Its 1-inch ceramic barrel distributes heat evenly, helping reduce hot spots that can damage hair. Adjustable temperatures from 250°F to 430°F make it suitable for fine, normal, and thicker hair types. A cool tip and spring clamp improve control while styling, and the 60-minute auto shut-off adds peace of mind. This model also includes a swivel cord, dual voltage for travel, and a simple LED indicator for quick temperature checks.
