The Beachwaver Co. S1 Rotating Curling Iron is a clipless curling iron made for natural-looking curls and beachy texture. Its tapered barrel ranges from 0.75 to 1.25 inches, letting you create tighter ends or relaxed waves with one tool. A ceramic coating supports even heat, while adjustable settings from 270°F to 450°F suit a range of hair textures. This model heats in about 30 seconds and includes a heat-resistant glove for safer styling. Dual voltage, a locking temperature function, and a long swivel cord make it practical for travel or daily use.
