For steady whole-room comfort, the Dyson Purifier Hot+Cool HP07 convection space heater uses natural airflow to gently raise room temperature without the pronounced fan noise of compact blowers. Suitable for spaces up to 250 square feet, it delivers 750 and 1500 watt output with an electronic thermostat for more precise control. The long, low body fits well under windows or along interior walls, helping offset drafts in apartments and bedrooms. Recessed side handles make repositioning simple when needed. The Dyson Purifier Hot+Cool HP07 is equipped with overheat protection, a tip-over sensor, and a timer for scheduled operation.
