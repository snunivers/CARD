The Mr. Heater Big Buddy (F232000) low-profile under-desk heater is tailored for personal comfort in home offices and study spaces. With 800 and 1200 watt settings, it provides directed warmth to legs and feet without overwhelming a small workspace. The front-facing controls are intentionally simple, including a thermostat dial and power selector, and the compact horizontal form slides neatly beneath many desks or side tables. Quiet fan-assisted ceramic heating helps reduce cold spots during long work sessions. For added peace of mind, the Mr. Heater Big Buddy (F232000) is equipped with overheat shutoff, a tip-over switch, and a cool-touch cabinet.
