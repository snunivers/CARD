The Honeywell HCE200 ceramic tower space heater is designed for bedrooms, offices, and small living areas up to 200 square feet. It delivers up to 1500 watts of heat with low, high, and eco settings for flexible comfort. A digital thermostat lets you choose a target temperature between 60 and 85°F, while widespread oscillation helps distribute warmth evenly. The slim profile fits easily beside a desk or nightstand. Safety features include overheat protection, a tip-over switch, cool-touch housing, and a 12-hour timer with automatic shutoff for worry-free daily use.
