Built for steady, silent heating, the Pelonis NY1507-14A oil-filled radiator warms medium-sized rooms without a fan, making it ideal for bedrooms and reading spaces. The unit uses 700, 900, and 1500 watt settings and a simple adjustable thermostat to maintain a comfortable temperature. Seven permanently sealed fins retain heat well, helping reduce temperature swings after the heater cycles off. Front controls are easy to reach, and integrated caster wheels allow smooth movement from room to room. The Pelonis NY1507-14A also includes overheat protection, a tip-over cutoff, and cord storage.
