Compact yet capable, the Pelonis NY1507-14A personal space heater is a good fit for desktops, dorm rooms, and small workspaces up to 120 square feet. This 750/1500 watt ceramic unit uses fan-forced heat to quickly take the chill out of the air. A manual thermostat dial keeps operation straightforward, while the lightweight body makes it easy to move between rooms. The heater’s recessed carry handle and stable wide base support everyday use. Built-in safety protections on the Pelonis NY1507-14A include tip-over shutoff, overheat control, and a flame-resistant housing.
