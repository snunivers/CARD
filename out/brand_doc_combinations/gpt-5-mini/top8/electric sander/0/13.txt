The Festool Rotex RO 150 FEQ heavy-duty orbital floor sander is designed for restoring timber floors, decking boards, and large wooden surfaces. Its 1,200-watt motor delivers strong, consistent performance, while the wide sanding base helps cover more area with each pass. A sturdy wheeled chassis and foldable handle simplify transport and setup in residential or commercial spaces. The sander features a high-capacity dust bag, adjustable handle height, and a durable aluminum housing for long service life. Suitable for finish removal and surface preparation, it offers a balanced design that helps reduce operator fatigue over extended sessions.
