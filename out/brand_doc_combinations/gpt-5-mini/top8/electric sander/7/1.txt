Designed for corners and narrow edges, the Festool ETS 125 REQ-Plus detail sander offers precise control in restoration and decorating projects. It uses a 180-watt motor and oscillates at up to 14,000 cycles per minute for efficient paint removal and surface prep. The triangular sanding pad reaches into tight spots around window frames, stair rails, and furniture profiles. A micro-filtration dust box captures fine particles, while the slim rubberized handle supports one-handed use. The package includes assorted sanding sheets, a finger attachment for confined areas, and a quick-clamp system for fast paper replacement.
