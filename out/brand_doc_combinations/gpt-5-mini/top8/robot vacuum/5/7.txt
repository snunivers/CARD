Made for mixed flooring, the Dreame Bot L10s Ultra transitions smoothly from hardwood to area rugs and low thresholds. Adaptive suction regulation improves pickup without draining the battery unnecessarily, extending runtime to about 110 minutes in standard mode. The robot creates a visual cleaning map in the companion app, where users can define cleaning zones or skip delicate areas. A pair of side brushes gathers debris from edges into the main intake path. The Dreame Bot L10s Ultra includes anti-drop protection, auto-docking, and a washable mop cloth for quick touch-up cleaning between deeper sessions.
