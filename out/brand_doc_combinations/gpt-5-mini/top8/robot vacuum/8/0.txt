The S_Brand S_Model robot vacuum is designed for daily floor care in apartments and family homes. It combines strong suction with a dual side-brush system to lift dust, crumbs, and pet hair from hard floors and low-pile rugs. Smart path navigation helps it clean in neat rows instead of random passes, while cliff and bump sensors reduce collisions and falls. A slim profile lets it reach under beds and sofas. The washable filter and 600 ml dustbin simplify maintenance, and the rechargeable battery provides up to 120 minutes of runtime per charge.
