Made for renovation work, the Fuji Spray Semi-PRO 2 backpack paint sprayer combines mobility with high-volume output. The wearable design distributes weight across padded shoulder straps, making it easier to move through hallways, scaffolding, and outdoor spaces without dragging a cart. Its pressurized reservoir supports extended spraying sessions for fences, garages, and large room interiors. A variable pressure regulator helps tailor the finish, while the long spray wand improves reach on ceilings and tall siding. The Fuji Spray Semi-PRO 2 is compatible with many water-based coatings and includes spare seals, nozzle tips, and a quick-clean flushing attachment.
