The Wagner Control Pro 170 paint sprayer is a compact HVLP unit designed for furniture, doors, cabinets, and small renovation jobs. Its 600W motor delivers smooth, controlled atomization, while the adjustable flow dial helps reduce overspray. The 1200 ml detachable container supports water-based stains, sealers, and thinned latex paints. Users can switch between horizontal, vertical, and round spray patterns by changing the included nozzle cap. A lightweight body, 2-meter power cord, and tool-free disassembly make the Wagner Control Pro 170 practical for home users who want easy setup, quick cleaning, and consistent finishing results.
