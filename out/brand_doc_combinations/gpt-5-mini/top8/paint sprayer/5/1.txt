Built for larger projects, the Wagner FLEXiO 590 airless paint sprayer provides high output for walls, ceilings, fences, and exterior siding. Its 0.75 HP pump can spray unthinned coatings at up to 3000 PSI, reducing prep time on demanding jobs. The stainless steel piston assembly is paired with a flexible suction tube for direct drawing from 1-gallon or 5-gallon paint buckets. A 25-foot hose and reversible spray tip help maintain workflow when handling clogs or tight corners. The wheeled frame gives the Wagner FLEXiO 590 better mobility around workshops and residential job sites.
