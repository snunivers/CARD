The Ekrin B37S fascia gun offers a stronger recovery setup for users who need more depth and pressure. It runs on a high-capacity 3,200 mAh battery and produces up to 18 kg of stall force, helping maintain power against tight muscles. Nine speed levels, from low vibration to intense percussion, can be selected on the digital display. The package includes eight attachment heads and a detailed quick-start guide showing suggested uses. An aircraft-grade aluminum shell improves durability, while the angled motor mount reduces wrist strain during shoulder and back treatments.
