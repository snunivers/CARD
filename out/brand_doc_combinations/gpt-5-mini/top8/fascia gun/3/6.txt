The Therabody Theragun Elite fascia gun is tailored for active households, offering a balanced mix of power, portability, and safety features. It includes a child-lock function, 15-minute auto timer, and overheating protection. The motor operates across seven speed settings, starting at a gentle 1,400 rpm and reaching 3,200 rpm for deeper tissue work. Six attachments cover common muscle zones, and the rounded silicone head is suited to general relaxation. With its 2,600 mAh battery, the unit can deliver around 300 minutes of use. A molded carry case keeps accessories organized between sessions.
