Designed for home fitness users, the Therabody Theragun Prime delivers deep percussion massage in a lightweight 780 g frame. The device offers six intensity settings and a 12 mm stroke depth for effective post-workout relief. Included are six massage heads, including a fork head for the neck and spine area and a flat head for broader muscles. The handle uses anti-slip silicone for better control. With a removable lithium battery, runtime reaches about 4 hours per charge. A hard travel case, charging dock, and auto shut-off after 15 minutes complete the package.
