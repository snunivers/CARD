The U_Brand U_Model stands out with a metal front housing and premium feel, while remaining practical for regular fascia massage. Its 13 mm amplitude and high stall force make it suitable for dense muscle groups after strength training or long runs. Users can choose from five intensity levels using a rear control dial, which is easy to adjust one-handed. The package includes heated and standard massage heads for different recovery preferences. USB-C fast charging fills the battery in around 2.5 hours. An LED strip shows active level, temperature mode, and remaining battery.
