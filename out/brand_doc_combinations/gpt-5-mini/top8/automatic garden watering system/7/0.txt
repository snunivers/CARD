The Gardena Smart Water Control Set automatic garden watering system is designed for small to medium flower beds, vegetable patches, and border plants. It includes a programmable controller, pressure regulator, filter, 20 meters of tubing, drip emitters, and adjustable sprayers for targeted watering. Users can set multiple daily schedules with run times tailored to seasonal needs. A rain-delay function helps reduce unnecessary water use, while simple push-fit connectors speed up installation. Suitable for taps with standard hose connections, this system delivers consistent moisture to roots and helps maintain healthier growth with less manual effort.
