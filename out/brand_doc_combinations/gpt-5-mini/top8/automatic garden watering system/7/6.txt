The Gardena Smart Water Control Set automatic garden watering system is designed for raised beds and kitchen gardens where precision matters. Using low-flow drip lines spaced evenly along the tubing, it delivers water slowly to reduce runoff and encourage deeper root development. The controller supports separate morning and evening cycles, and the pressure regulator maintains consistent output across the bed. Installation is simplified with cut-to-length hose, corner joints, tees, and end caps included in the box. Suitable for vegetables, berries, and herbs, this system helps improve moisture control and cuts down the time spent watering by hand.
