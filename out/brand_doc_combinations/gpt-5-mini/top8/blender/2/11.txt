The Breville Super Q (BBL920) heavy-duty blender is made for repeated blending of dense ingredients such as nut spreads, frozen desserts, and thick vegetable purees. A 1300-watt motor drives hardened blades at high speed, while the 2-liter container has a square shape that promotes stronger ingredient circulation.
