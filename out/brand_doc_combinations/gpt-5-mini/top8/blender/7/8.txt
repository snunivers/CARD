The Ninja Professional Blender (BL610) professional-style blender is intended for demanding recipes and larger households. A 1500-watt motor works with an eight-blade system to process ice, frozen berries, leafy greens, and soaked grains into a smoother texture. The 2.2-liter jar is made from shatter-resistant BPA-free material and includes clear volume markings. Preset buttons for smoothie, soup, frozen dessert, and self-clean simplify operation, while ten manual speeds allow added control. A sound-dampening lid helps reduce blending noise, and the reinforced coupling is built for repeated high-speed use.
