Offering a feature-rich experience, the Soundcore Motion+ portable speaker combines strong output with flexible playback options. The 25W speaker system includes dedicated bass enhancement for fuller low frequencies during movies, playlists, and casual parties. In addition to Bluetooth 5.2, it supports AUX input and can pair with a second unit for wider stereo sound. The top panel includes tactile buttons and a battery indicator for quick operation. With up to 19 hours of playback, it is ready for day trips and backyard use. The enclosure is IPX6-rated and built to handle splashes.
