Built for outdoor weekends, the Marshall Emberton II portable speaker combines a sturdy cylindrical design with wide sound dispersion. Two full-range drivers and passive radiators create a 30W audio output that stays detailed even at higher volumes. Bluetooth 5.3 provides strong wireless performance, and party mode lets multiple compatible speakers connect for broader coverage. The large battery supports up to 22 hours of playback, and fast charging via USB-C shortens waiting time. An IP67 rating protects against dust and water, while the removable carrying strap makes it easier to transport between locations.
