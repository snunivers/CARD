The JBL Charge 5 portable speaker is designed for users who want dependable sound in a straightforward package. Its dual 10W drivers create a balanced presentation with enough volume for small gatherings, workouts, or outdoor meals. Bluetooth 5.3 offers efficient pairing and low power consumption, while the built-in microphone supports hands-free calling. The battery can deliver up to 21 hours of playback at moderate levels, and USB-C charging keeps refueling simple. An IP65-rated shell resists dust and water splashes, and the integrated handle makes carrying the speaker from room to room especially easy.
