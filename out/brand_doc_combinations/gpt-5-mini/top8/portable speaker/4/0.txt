The Sony SRS-XB43 is a compact portable speaker built for everyday listening at home or outdoors. Its 12W driver delivers clear vocals, balanced mids, and a surprisingly full bass response for its size. Bluetooth 5.3 provides a stable wireless connection, while the built-in microphone supports hands-free calls. A 10-hour battery keeps music playing through commutes, picnics, or work sessions, and USB-C charging makes top-ups simple. The fabric-covered housing is lightweight and easy to carry, and IPX5 water resistance helps protect it from light rain and splashes.
