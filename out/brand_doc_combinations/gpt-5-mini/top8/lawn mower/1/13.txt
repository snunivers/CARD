Engineered for basic residential use, the EGO Power+ LM2142SP is an affordable 20-inch side-discharge lawn mower with a 125cc gas engine. The straightforward design focuses on dependable cutting rather than extra features, making it easy to maintain for seasonal users. Its lightweight steel deck and smaller frame improve maneuverability around garden edges and narrow passages. Height can be adjusted through several manual positions to match different grass lengths. The recoil start system is simple and familiar, and the folding upper handle helps with off-season storage. This mower is a practical choice for modest lawns and routine trimming.
