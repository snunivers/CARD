Built for homeowners who prefer extra runtime, the Honda HRR216VKA features a dual-battery system that combines two 20V packs for 40V performance. Its 21-inch composite deck resists corrosion and reduces overall weight, helping the mower glide across medium-sized lawns. The self-propelled drive includes two speed ranges, useful for flat sections and slight inclines. An onboard charge indicator keeps battery status visible while mowing. The unit supports mulching, bagging, and side discharge, and the single-point height adjustment simplifies setup. This mower is a strong option for users moving away from gas equipment.
