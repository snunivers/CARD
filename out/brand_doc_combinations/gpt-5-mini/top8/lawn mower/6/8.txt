A good fit for first-time buyers, the Ryobi RY401120-Y is a 19-inch manual reel lawn mower made for small, flat lawns. It uses five heat-treated steel blades to produce a clean scissor-like cut without gas, electricity, or batteries. The cutting height can be adjusted for a tidy, close finish, and the lightweight frame makes it easy to push and store. Because it runs quietly, it is convenient for early morning yard work in denser neighborhoods. This mower is best on regularly maintained grass and appeals to users seeking a simple, low-maintenance alternative.
