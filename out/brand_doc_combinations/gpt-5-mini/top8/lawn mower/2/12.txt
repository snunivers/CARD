The Toro Recycler 20333 is a premium cordless self-propelled mower with a 21-inch aluminum deck and a high-efficiency brushless motor. It uses a 56V battery platform to deliver strong torque and reduced vibration during operation. Intelligent power management increases blade speed when grass gets thick, helping maintain a more even cut. This model offers single-lever height adjustment, LED headlights, and a generous collection bag for longer mowing intervals. The handle can be telescoped and folded for storage. Ideal for medium to large lawns, it blends battery convenience with the feel of a more robust mower.
