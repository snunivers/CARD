The Toro Recycler 20333 is a rear-engine riding lawn mower designed for homeowners with properties up to one acre. A 30-inch cutting deck allows it to fit through wider gates while still reducing mowing time compared with a walk-behind model. Its 10.5 HP engine pairs with a simple gear drive transmission for controlled speed on open ground. The seat is adjustable, and the low step-through frame makes mounting easier. This mower includes side discharge and optional mulching capability. It is best suited to relatively level terrain and routine weekly lawn care.
