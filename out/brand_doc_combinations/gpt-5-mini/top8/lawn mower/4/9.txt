The Greenworks 25462 is a 21-inch all-wheel-drive self-propelled mower intended for yards with slopes, rough patches, and variable terrain. Its 170cc engine supplies strong cutting power, and the AWD system helps the mower maintain momentum on inclines or damp grass. A steel deck with reinforced front edges adds durability where impacts are most common. The handle adjusts to different user heights, and the washout port simplifies deck cleaning after mowing. Mulching, side discharge, and bagging modes are all included. This mower is designed for homeowners who need traction and flexibility more than minimal weight.
