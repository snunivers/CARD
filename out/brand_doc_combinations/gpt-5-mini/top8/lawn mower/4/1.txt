Designed for larger yards, the Greenworks 25462 is a 22-inch self-propelled lawn mower with variable speed control and rear-wheel drive. Its 163cc engine starts with a single pull and provides enough torque for thicker grass. The cutting deck supports mulching, bagging, and side discharge, making it useful through spring and summer growth changes. Seven height settings allow precise lawn maintenance, and the padded handle improves comfort during longer sessions. With an oversized grass bag and durable wheels, this mower balances cutting efficiency, traction, and straightforward maintenance for weekly use.
