Built for versatility, the Maytag MVWB835DW 5.3 cu. ft. 6th Sense Smart Front Control Washer compact front-load washing machine is ideal for smaller homes that still require premium features. The machine’s inverter motor reduces noise and helps extend appliance life. A self-cleaning drum cycle and removable detergent drawer simplify upkeep. The Maytag MVWB835DW 5.3 cu. ft. 6th Sense Smart Front Control Washer also has leak protection, a child safety lock, and customizable spin speeds, making it a smart fit for practical, space-conscious laundry setups.
