Built for versatility, the Bosch 800 Series 24" Front Control 2-in-1 Steam Washer Dryer compact front-load washing machine is ideal for smaller homes that still require premium features. It offers a 6.5 kg capacity, variable temperature settings, and a selection of 12 programs including eco, bedding, delicates, and fast wash. The machine’s inverter motor reduces noise and helps extend appliance life. A self-cleaning drum cycle and removable detergent drawer simplify upkeep. The Bosch 800 Series 24" Front Control 2-in-1 Steam Washer Dryer also has leak protection, a child safety lock, and customizable spin speeds, making it a smart fit for practical, space-conscious laundry setups.
