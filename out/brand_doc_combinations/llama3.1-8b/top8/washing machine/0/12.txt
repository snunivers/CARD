The Bosch 800 Series 24" Front Control 2-in-1 Steam Washer Dryer is a high-capacity top-load washer with an 8.5 kg drum and turbo wash action for efficient removal of dirt and detergent buildup. It offers dedicated cycles for blankets, mixed loads, delicate garments, and heavily soiled clothing. Soft-close hinges, rear control placement, and a wide opening improve everyday usability. The machine automatically adjusts water intake based on load size, helping reduce waste. With a corrosion-resistant exterior, lint filtration, and tub-clean reminder, the Bosch 800 Series 24" Front Control 2-in-1 Steam Washer Dryer is designed to deliver easy maintenance and dependable performance over time.
