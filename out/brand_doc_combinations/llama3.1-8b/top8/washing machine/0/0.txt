The Bosch 800 Series 24" Front Control 2-in-1 Steam Washer Dryer front-load washing machine offers a 9 kg capacity suited to medium and large households. Its inverter motor helps reduce vibration and energy use, while 14 wash programs handle cotton, synthetics, delicates, wool, and quick daily loads. A steam refresh option helps loosen odors and light wrinkles between full washes. The digital display makes cycle selection simple, and the delay start function adds scheduling flexibility. With a high spin speed of 1400 rpm and an A-rated efficiency design, the Bosch 800 Series 24" Front Control 2-in-1 Steam Washer Dryer balances strong cleaning with gentle fabric care.
