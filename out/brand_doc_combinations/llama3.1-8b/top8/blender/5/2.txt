The Blendtec Stealth 885 high-performance blender is suited to home cooks who want smoother results from tougher ingredients. A 1400-watt motor and hardened steel blade system handle nuts, seeds, frozen fruit, and dense vegetables with less strain. The large 2-liter pitcher supports batch blending for soups, nut butter, or party drinks. Variable speed control and preset programs for smoothie, ice crush, and puree make operation more flexible. A tamper is included for thick mixtures, and the thermal protection system helps prevent overheating during longer blending cycles.
