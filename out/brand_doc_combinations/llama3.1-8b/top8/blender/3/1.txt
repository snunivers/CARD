Built for compact kitchens and single servings, the Breville BBL920 personal blender features a 300-watt base and two 600 ml travel cups with sip lids. It blends shakes, protein drinks, and soft fruit quickly, then converts the cup into a portable bottle for commuting or gym sessions. One-touch operation keeps use simple, and the blade unit twists off for rinsing. The cups are BPA-free and sized to fit most car holders. A safety lock prevents operation unless the cup is correctly attached to the motor base.
