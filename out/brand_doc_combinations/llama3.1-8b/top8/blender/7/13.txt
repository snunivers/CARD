The Ninja BL660 hand blender set is designed for flexible meal prep in smaller kitchens. It features a 700-watt motor housed in an ergonomic soft-touch handle, plus variable speed trigger control for more responsive blending. The detachable stainless shaft purees soups directly in cookware, while the whisk attachment handles cream, eggs, and batters. A 500 ml chopper bowl helps with onions, nuts, and herbs. The included beaker is useful for smoothies and sauces. Quiet operation, a long power cord, and dishwasher-safe accessories make the set convenient for frequent use.
