The Linksys EA9300 is a mid-range WiFi 6 router that balances speed, coverage, and simple management. With AX3000-class performance, it handles multiple phones, laptops, TVs, and smart devices without frequent slowdowns. Four high-gain antennas and beamforming help extend coverage across medium-sized homes, while OFDMA improves efficiency when many devices connect at once. Ports include one Gigabit WAN and four Gigabit LAN connections for wired stability. The Linksys EA9300 supports WPA3, parental scheduling, device prioritization, and automatic firmware updates. Setup is guided through a companion app with clear network monitoring tools.
