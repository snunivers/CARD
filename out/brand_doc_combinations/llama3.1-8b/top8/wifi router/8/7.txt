The S_Brand S_Model is a premium WiFi 7 router aimed at demanding networks with many connected devices. It supports operation across 2.4 GHz, 5 GHz, and 6 GHz bands, delivering extremely high throughput for 8K streaming, cloud gaming, and fast local transfers. Multi-Link Operation helps compatible devices use multiple bands for lower latency and improved reliability. The hardware includes a 10 Gigabit WAN/LAN port, one 2.5 Gigabit port, and four Gigabit LAN ports. The S_Brand S_Model provides advanced security, VLAN support, parental controls, and performance tuning through an app or browser dashboard.
