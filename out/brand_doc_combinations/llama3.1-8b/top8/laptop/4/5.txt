Created for designers and media users, the Asus ZenBook 14X OLED offers a 16-inch high-resolution display with strong brightness and accurate color reproduction. A high-performance processor, 32GB of memory, and dedicated graphics allow smooth editing of large photos, layered projects, and high-bitrate video. Fast 1TB SSD storage helps move big files quickly, while advanced cooling supports sustained workloads. The laptop also includes a full selection of ports for external monitors and storage devices. Its solid aluminum build, spacious keyboard, and large touchpad provide a premium experience for demanding creative tasks.
