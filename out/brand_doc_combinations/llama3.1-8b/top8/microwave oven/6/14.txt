The Maytag MMV1165FW microwave oven is engineered for low-noise performance, making it a good fit for open-plan kitchens and late-night use. It includes 1.0 cu. ft. capacity, 900 watts of cooking power, and a sound-reduction design that softens beeps and door noise. Auto cook functions handle everyday foods, while plus-30-second and quick defrost buttons keep operation efficient. The LED cavity light improves visibility, and the eco mode helps reduce standby power consumption. With its clean-lined exterior and practical controls, the Maytag MMV1165FW balances comfort and convenience.
