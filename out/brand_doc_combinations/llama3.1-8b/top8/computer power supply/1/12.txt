Engineered for value-oriented builders, the Corsair RM750, 80+ Gold 750W is a 400W micro-ATX power supply for budget desktops and light-duty home systems. Its compact dimensions suit smaller cases, while the fixed cable design keeps setup simple for straightforward builds. The unit uses active PFC and delivers dependable output for integrated graphics platforms, basic office work, and entry-level components. A temperature-sensitive fan helps maintain reasonable cooling without excessive noise during regular use. Included connectors cover 24-pin ATX, CPU power, SATA, and peripheral devices, with essential protections against over-voltage, overload, and short circuit.
