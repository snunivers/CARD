Created for silent PC enthusiasts, the Corsair RM750, 80+ Gold 750W is a 600W fanless power supply that cools passively through a ventilated aluminum housing. It is ideal for noise-sensitive workstations, audio production setups, and living-room PCs where acoustic output matters. The 80 PLUS Platinum certification helps minimize heat generation by improving conversion efficiency. Fully modular cabling keeps installation clean and reduces clutter in compact or airflow-focused builds. Despite its silent design, the unit delivers stable power through a robust +12V rail and includes comprehensive protections, dual CPU support, PCIe connectors, and multiple SATA outputs.
