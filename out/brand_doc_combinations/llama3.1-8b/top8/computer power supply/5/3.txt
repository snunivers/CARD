Designed for performance-focused users, the Be Quiet! Straight Power 11 750W, 80+ Gold is a 750W fully modular ATX power supply with 80 PLUS Gold efficiency. Its DC-to-DC design helps maintain tight voltage regulation across demanding workloads, from gaming to streaming and workstation tasks. A 135 mm low-noise fan supports semi-passive operation, staying off at low power draw for quieter idle performance. The package includes sleeved modular cables, two EPS connectors, four PCIe 6+2 connectors, and ample SATA leads for larger systems. Built-in OCP, OVP, SCP, OPP, UVP, and OTP protection adds confidence over long-term use.
