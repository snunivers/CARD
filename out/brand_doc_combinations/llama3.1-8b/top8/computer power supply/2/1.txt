Built for compact gaming PCs, the Seasonic FOCUS GX-750, 80+ Gold 750W is a 550W SFX power supply with fully modular cabling for cleaner routing in small cases. Its 80 PLUS Gold certification helps maintain efficient power conversion, while the compact housing leaves more room for airflow. A fluid dynamic bearing fan remains quiet under light loads and ramps smoothly during demanding sessions. The unit supports modern GPUs with dual 6+2 pin PCIe connectors and includes two EPS connectors for current mini-ITX and micro-ATX motherboards. Comprehensive protection circuits guard against overload, short circuit, and overheating.
