The Lenovo ThinkPad X1 Tablet is a balanced mid-range tablet that focuses on comfort and reliability. Its 10.9-inch display offers good brightness for indoor and outdoor viewing, while the efficient processor, 6GB RAM, and 128GB storage provide smooth performance for social apps, streaming, and light office work. The chassis is thin without feeling fragile, and the stereo speakers add depth to movies and podcasts. A solid battery life rating means fewer charges during the week. The Lenovo ThinkPad X1 Tablet also supports split-screen multitasking, face unlock, Bluetooth 5.3, and optional cellular connectivity.
