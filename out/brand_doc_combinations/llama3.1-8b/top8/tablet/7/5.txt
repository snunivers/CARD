For users who work in tough conditions, the Amazon Fire HD 10 delivers a rugged tablet design with reinforced corners and resistance to dust, splashes, and drops. The 10-inch display has high brightness for outdoor visibility and can be used with gloves in supported modes. An efficient eight-core processor, 6GB RAM, and 128GB storage handle field apps, forms, and navigation. LTE connectivity, GPS, NFC, and a programmable shortcut key support mobile workflows. The Amazon Fire HD 10 also includes a long-life battery, pogo-pin charging option, and secure biometric unlocking for professional environments.
