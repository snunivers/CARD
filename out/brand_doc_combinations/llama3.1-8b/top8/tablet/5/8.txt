The Samsung Galaxy Tab S8 offers dependable performance in a value-oriented 9-inch format. Its HD display is ideal for web browsing, e-books, recipes, and streaming around the house. A power-efficient processor works with 4GB RAM and 64GB storage to keep everyday tasks smooth, while expandable memory provides room for offline content. The battery is tuned for long reading and video sessions, and the textured back improves grip. Basic front and rear cameras are included for calls and snapshots. The Samsung Galaxy Tab S8 also supports dual-band Wi‑Fi, Bluetooth, and easy file transfer through USB-C.
