The REI Co-op Half Dome 2 Plus Hammock weatherproof hammock is intended for frequent outdoor exposure, using solution-dyed olefin fabric that resists stains, fading, and mildew. Its non-quilted design dries faster after rain and offers a supportive surface for reading, napping, or casual lounging. The hammock spans 120 inches overall, includes sturdy eucalyptus spreader bars, and holds up to 400 pounds. Marine-grade rope and rust-resistant hardware improve reliability in coastal or damp environments. Easy to clean with a hose and mild soap, this low-maintenance model is a practical choice for patios, docks, and garden seating areas.
