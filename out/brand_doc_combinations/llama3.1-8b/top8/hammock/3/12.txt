The REI Co-op Co-op Hammock reversible quilted hammock offers two coordinated fabric patterns, allowing users to switch the look of their outdoor space without changing furniture. Its padded polyester bed provides a plush feel, while the weather-resistant outer layer handles regular sun and moisture exposure. With a capacity of 450 pounds and a bed width of 55 inches, it comfortably accommodates one or two adults. Hardwood spreader bars, hanging chains, and reinforced loops are included. This hammock is suited for garden stands or permanent mounting points and delivers a decorative, more structured lounging experience.
