The Kammok Mantis Hammock Brazilian-style hammock offers a cocooning feel with tightly woven cotton-poly fabric that contours gently around the body. Without spreader bars, it creates a deeper, more secure seat that many users prefer for reading or napping. It measures 130 inches long with a bed width of 63 inches and supports up to 330 pounds. Decorative hand-tied fringe adds a traditional look, while reinforced end cords distribute weight evenly to reduce stress on the fabric. This hammock works well indoors or outdoors when paired with appropriate hooks or a stand.
