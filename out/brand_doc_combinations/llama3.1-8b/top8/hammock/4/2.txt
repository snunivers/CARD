The Kammok Mantis Hammock quilted fabric hammock combines comfort and style for outdoor living spaces. Its padded bed features weather-resistant polyester over a soft fiberfill core, creating a cushioned surface that stays inviting for longer lounging sessions. The hammock is 82 inches long between spreader bars and supports up to 475 pounds. Curved bamboo-look hardwood bars provide stability and help maintain a flatter resting area. Fade-resistant fabric stands up to seasonal sun exposure, and detachable chains offer simple installation. This model pairs well with a compatible stand or between sturdy trees.
