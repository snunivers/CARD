A good choice for students and general users, the Vivo X90 Pro combines solid specifications with practical features. The 6.6-inch FHD+ screen offers enough space for class notes, web browsing, and entertainment. An octa-core processor with 8GB RAM keeps multiple apps open comfortably, and the 256GB storage provides room for media, documents, and downloads. Camera hardware includes a 50MP main sensor, 2MP depth sensor, and an 8MP front camera for online meetings and selfies. The 5,000mAh battery is built for full-day use, and expandable storage, dual speakers, and 5G connectivity improve value.
