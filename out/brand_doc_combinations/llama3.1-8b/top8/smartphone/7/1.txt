Designed for mobile photography fans, the Vivo X90 Pro combines a slim profile with advanced camera hardware. Its 6.7-inch AMOLED screen offers deep contrast and vivid color, protected by durable glass. On the rear, a 108MP primary sensor is paired with ultra-wide and macro lenses for flexible shooting. The device records 4K video and includes night, portrait, and pro modes. With 12GB RAM and 256GB storage, performance stays responsive even during editing. Stereo speakers, NFC, 5G support, and a 4,800mAh battery with 67W fast charging add practical everyday convenience.
