The Vivo X90 Pro targets premium smartphone buyers with a refined build, advanced display technology, and flagship-level speed. It features a 6.82-inch QHD+ AMOLED panel with adaptive refresh rate, high peak brightness, and excellent clarity for gaming and video. A top-tier processor, 16GB RAM, and 512GB storage provide fast performance across demanding apps and multitasking. The triple rear camera system includes a 50MP main lens, periscope telephoto, and ultra-wide sensor for flexible photography. A 5,100mAh battery supports wired and wireless fast charging, while IP resistance, 5G, and Wi-Fi 7 complete the package.
