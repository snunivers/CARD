The OnePlus 11 Pro is engineered for users who prioritize charging speed and uninterrupted daily performance. It features a 6.67-inch AMOLED display with narrow bezels and a 120Hz refresh rate for a modern, fluid feel. Under the hood, a capable chipset works alongside 12GB RAM and 256GB storage to support gaming, streaming, and heavy app use. The rear camera setup is led by a 50MP sensor with optical stabilization for cleaner photos and steadier video. Its 5,000mAh battery supports 120W rapid charging, while 5G, infrared control, and an in-display fingerprint reader add convenience.
