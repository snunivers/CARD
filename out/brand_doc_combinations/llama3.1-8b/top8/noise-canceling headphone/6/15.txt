Offering a refined feature set, the Audio-Technica ATH-M50x noise-canceling headphone is suited to users who split time between entertainment and productivity. It includes adaptive ANC, transparency mode, and customizable EQ settings through a companion app. Precision 42 mm drivers provide clear layering, controlled bass response, and smooth treble for fatigue-free listening. Bluetooth 5.3 with multipoint pairing allows seamless switching between devices, while the microphone array improves call pickup in busy environments. The battery lasts up to 41 hours without ANC or roughly 30 hours with it active. Foldable ear cups and a travel case are included.
