Designed for travelers, the Y_Brand Y_Model combines deep noise reduction with a compact fold-flat design. Dual feedforward and feedback microphones help reduce airplane cabin noise, train rumble, and office chatter. The headphone uses 45 mm drivers tuned for balanced bass, clear vocals, and smooth highs. It supports wired listening through a 3.5 mm audio cable when the battery runs low. With ANC enabled, playback lasts up to 32 hours, or 50 hours in standard mode. Soft protein-leather pads, swivel ear cups, and an included carrying case make it easy to pack.
