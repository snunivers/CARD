The Y_Brand Y_Model noise-canceling headphone focuses on immersive audio for music and movies. Large 50 mm drivers create a spacious soundstage, while hybrid ANC reduces low-frequency distractions from traffic, fans, and public transport. Users can switch between Quiet, Normal, and Awareness modes depending on the setting. The headset supports both wireless and wired playback, and the detachable boom-free microphone keeps the design clean. Battery life reaches 30 hours with noise canceling active, plus fast charging for short top-ups. Plush ear cushions, a reinforced hinge, and matte finish give it a durable everyday feel.
