The T_Brand T_Model wheeled leaf blower is intended for expansive areas where handheld tools can become tiring. Powered by a 159cc four-cycle engine, it generates high-volume airflow to move heavy leaves and debris across driveways, lawns, and commercial paths. Large rear wheels improve mobility on uneven terrain, while the front swivel wheel enhances steering around landscaping. The adjustable discharge chute lets you target airflow where needed, and the steel frame adds durability for repeated use. This model is well suited for estate maintenance, schools, and facilities with broad outdoor spaces.
