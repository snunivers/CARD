For demanding cleanup jobs, the Ryobi RY40210 commercial-style backpack blower delivers high output and durable construction. Its 63cc engine produces air volume up to 720 CFM and speeds near 200 mph, allowing it to clear wet leaves, pine needles, and stubborn debris from large lawns or parking areas. The frame-mounted anti-vibration system helps improve comfort, while the hip-mounted throttle with cruise setting supports all-day use. A heavy-duty air filter and reinforced housing are designed for frequent operation. This blower is a strong choice for property managers and landscaping crews.
