Designed for control and convenience, the Husqvarna 350BT leaf blower features a compact body with a rubberized overmold grip and simple push-button operation. Its 18V battery platform powers airflow up to 100 mph and 210 CFM, which works well for clearing sawdust, dry leaves, and loose dirt from decks or garages. The short tube and lightweight frame make it easy to use in tight spaces, including around outdoor furniture and entryways. Fast charging support helps keep downtime low. This model is especially useful for quick spot cleaning between larger yard maintenance sessions.
