Created for hunters and cold-weather campers, the Black Diamond Eldorado 2 is a hot tent compatible with a compact stove system. The fire-retardant fabric includes a reinforced stove jack, allowing safe routing of a flue pipe through the roof panel when used correctly. Its tipi-style shape sheds wind efficiently, and the central pole design keeps setup simple even on uneven ground. Adjustable vents help manage airflow and condensation, while perimeter stake points create a secure pitch. The interior fits up to four sleepers without a stove, or two to three with heating equipment. The Black Diamond Eldorado 2 supports reliable shelter in remote conditions.
