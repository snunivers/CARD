The REI Co-op Quarter Dome SL 2 Plus is a compact rooftop tent designed for overland travel and quick overnight stops. It mounts to compatible vehicle roof racks and unfolds into a raised sleeping platform that keeps campers off muddy or uneven ground. The tent body uses waterproof poly-cotton fabric, with mesh windows on all sides to improve ventilation and views. A telescoping ladder supports easy access, and the high-density foam mattress adds comfort without needing extra setup inside. When closed, a protective travel cover shields the tent from road dust and rain. The REI Co-op Quarter Dome SL 2 Plus is practical for mobile camping adventures.
