The Black+Decker BDEWM020 random orbital sander is built for smooth finishing on wood, filler, and painted surfaces. A 280-watt motor delivers 7,000 to 13,000 orbits per minute, helping reduce swirl marks while maintaining steady material removal. Its 125 mm hook-and-loop pad supports quick disc changes, and the sealed dust canister keeps the workspace cleaner during extended jobs. A soft-grip top and compact body improve control on tabletops, doors, and shelving. This model also includes variable speed control, a low-vibration design, and an integrated port for connection to a workshop vacuum.
