The Makita BO5030K compact disc sander is a bench-mounted option for precise edge sanding and small part shaping. A 370-watt motor spins the 150 mm sanding disc at 1,450 rpm, offering controlled material removal for wood, plastic, and light nonferrous workpieces. The tilting work table adjusts up to 45 degrees for angled sanding, and the miter guide supports repeatable results on craft and joinery tasks. Rubber feet reduce vibration on the bench, while the guarded design improves user confidence. Dust port connectivity helps keep the machine cleaner during repeated workshop use.
