The Makita BO5030K mini electric file sander is ideal for shaping, deburring, and sanding in narrow spaces where larger tools cannot reach. Its 400-watt motor drives a slim abrasive belt that works effectively on metal edges, weld areas, pipework, and intricate wooden profiles. The adjustable sanding arm can be angled for improved access in awkward positions, and tool-free belt replacement speeds up workflow. A lock-on function supports continuous operation during repetitive tasks, while the side handle enhances stability. Compact dimensions and a durable gear housing make it a practical choice for detailed fabrication work.
