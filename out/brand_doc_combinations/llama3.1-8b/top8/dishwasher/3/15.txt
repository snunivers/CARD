The LG LDP6797ST eco-conscious dishwasher is built to lower utility use while maintaining strong everyday cleaning results. This 24-inch built-in model accommodates 13 place settings and uses intelligent water management to minimize waste on smaller or lightly soiled loads. Eco, Auto, Intensive, Glass, and Express cycles cover a wide range of dishwashing needs, and the half-load feature is useful between full family meals. The stainless interior improves heat retention for more effective drying, while the filtration system helps keep performance consistent over time. With a 46 dBA rating and sleek concealed controls, it offers efficient, modern functionality.
