The Whirlpool WDTA50SAHZ dishwasher is tailored for hygiene-focused homes with an emphasis on deep cleaning and sanitization. It holds 14 place settings and uses high-temperature rinsing, steam assist, and a certified sanitize cycle to help reduce germs on dishes, bottles, and utensils. Soil sensors optimize water use for each load, while the stainless tub supports efficient drying and long-term durability. This model also includes a dedicated glass care program, foldable lower tines, and a third rack for small tools. Touch controls, indicator lights, and a quiet 44 dBA operation make it a capable everyday appliance.
