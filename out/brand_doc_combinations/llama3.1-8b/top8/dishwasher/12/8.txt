The W_Brand W_Model smart dishwasher adds connected convenience to modern kitchen routines. This 24-inch built-in model supports Wi-Fi control through a companion app, allowing users to start cycles, receive maintenance alerts, and monitor progress remotely. Inside, it offers 14 place settings, flexible folding tines, and a third rack for flatware and serving tools. Sensor wash technology adapts water temperature and cycle duration to soil levels, while the sanitize option helps reduce bacteria on dishes. A quiet 43 dBA rating suits open-plan homes. With sleek hidden controls and efficient drying, it delivers capable performance with added technology.
