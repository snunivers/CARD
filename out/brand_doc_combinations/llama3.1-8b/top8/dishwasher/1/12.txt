If fast turnaround matters, the Miele G 7566 SCVi-1 dishwasher is designed to shorten cleaning time without compromising results on everyday loads. This freestanding model supports 12 place settings and includes a one-hour wash and dry program that works well for lightly to moderately soiled dishes. Additional settings such as Heavy, Eco, and Rinse provide flexibility for different needs. The interior features a removable cutlery tray, cup shelves, and a height-adjustable upper basket. An LED display clearly shows cycle status, and the delayed start function supports off-peak operation. With 50 dBA sound output, it offers balanced convenience and performance.
