Designed for controlled performance, the Bosch PS31-2A 12V Max Lithium-Ion 1/4" Hex Drive Screwdriver flathead screwdriver combines a precision-ground 6 mm slotted tip with a durable steel shank for consistent fit. The blade is hardened to handle repeated use without twisting under normal torque. Its acetate handle offers a classic feel and includes fluted sides for a secure grip, even with oily hands. A hanging hole at the end allows convenient storage on pegboards. This screwdriver suits electrical cover plates, cabinet hardware, and a wide range of standard household fastening applications.
