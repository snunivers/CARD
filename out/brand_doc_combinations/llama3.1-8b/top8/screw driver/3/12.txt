The Milwaukee 2401-20 12V Lithium-Ion Cordless Screwdriver demolition screwdriver is made for rough-duty jobs that go beyond standard screwdriving. It features a reinforced square shank, hardened steel blade, and heavy-duty strike cap for chiseling, prying, and loosening seized fasteners. The large acetate handle is built to absorb impact while maintaining a secure grip. A black phosphate tip improves wear resistance and helps reduce slipping under load. Whether used in renovation, carpentry, or mechanical repair, this tool provides the rugged construction needed for harsh environments where ordinary screwdrivers may fail prematurely.
