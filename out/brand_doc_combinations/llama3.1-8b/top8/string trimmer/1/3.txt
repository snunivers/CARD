Designed as a versatile 2-in-1 tool, the Husqvarna 128LR converts from a string trimmer to a wheeled edger in seconds. Its 40V battery platform provides strong cordless performance, and the brushless motor improves efficiency while reducing maintenance. The adjustable cutting width lets users choose between runtime and coverage, while the telescoping shaft supports comfortable operation for different heights. A rotating head simplifies trimming on slopes or around landscaping features. With quick-start push-button operation and low vibration, this model suits homeowners looking for clean lawn edges without the hassle of gas equipment.
