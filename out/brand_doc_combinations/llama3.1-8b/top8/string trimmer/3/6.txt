The GreenWorks GMAX 40V attachment-capable string trimmer is built for users who want one powerhead for multiple yard tasks. Its 26cc gas engine provides solid cutting performance, and the split-shaft design accepts compatible attachments such as an edger, pole saw, or cultivator. The trimmer head uses a dual 0.095-inch line setup for handling thicker grass and patchy weeds. A padded shoulder strap and bicycle-style handle improve control during longer jobs. With durable construction and broad accessory compatibility, this unit is a practical choice for maintaining larger properties with varied landscaping needs.
