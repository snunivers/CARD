Quiet, compact, and easy to use, the WORX WG160 battery string trimmer is ideal for noise-sensitive neighborhoods and quick weekly lawn care. It runs on a 24V lithium-ion battery and features a 13-inch cutting path for trimming around sidewalks, mailboxes, and garden edges. The shaft adjusts easily, and the head rotates for edging along paved surfaces. A push-button start eliminates pull cords, while the included charger supports convenient recharging between tasks. This trimmer is best for homeowners seeking a low-fuss tool that handles ordinary grass and light weeds without excessive weight.
