For offices, shared rooms, and busy living spaces, the Coway AP-1512GA delivers dependable purification with coverage up to 480 square feet. Its filtration path includes a washable outer screen, fine particle filter, and activated carbon layer to reduce dust, pollen, pet dander, and everyday indoor odors. The built-in sensor tracks air quality and adjusts performance automatically, helping maintain comfort without constant input. Additional features include a child-safe lock, timer options, and a low-light sleep mode. The neutral finish, hidden handle slots, and straightforward controls give it a clean, versatile appeal.
