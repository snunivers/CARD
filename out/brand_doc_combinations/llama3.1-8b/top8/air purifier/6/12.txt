The Austin Air HealthMate Plus is a smart air purifier with app control, remote scheduling, and filter monitoring built in. Intended for spaces up to 650 square feet, it combines a washable pre-filter, H13 HEPA layer, and granular activated carbon to address everyday allergens, smoke, and odors. The purifier can be linked to home automation routines, allowing it to start before occupants arrive or shift to sleep mode automatically at night. A brushless motor helps reduce energy consumption and operating noise, making it suitable for continuous use in active households.
