With a minimalist cube design, the Austin Air HealthMate Plus fits neatly under side tables or beside sofas while delivering efficient purification for rooms up to 350 square feet. Air enters through side vents and passes through a washable mesh pre-filter, HEPA filter, and dense carbon sheet to reduce particles and common household smells. Touch controls on the top panel provide access to auto mode, three manual speeds, and an eight-hour timer. The fan is tuned for low vibration, making the unit stable on hard floors. Replacement filters install quickly without tools.
