The Dyson Pure Hot + Cool HP02 targets smoke-prone environments such as urban apartments or homes near busy roads. Rated for areas up to 550 square feet, it uses a sealed filtration chamber with HEPA media and a thick carbon bed designed to reduce smoke, exhaust, and lingering odors. The purifier monitors fine particulate levels continuously and displays the current reading on a bright front panel. In auto mode, it reacts quickly when indoor air quality worsens. A sturdy handle, durable shell, and easy-access rear filter door add to its everyday usability.
