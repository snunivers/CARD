The Seagate Nighthawk Pro 4-Bay NAS is a compact business NAS with 2 bays and emphasis on secure file storage for remote and hybrid teams. It includes a dual-core processor, 2 GB RAM, one 2.5GbE port, and hardware encryption support for sensitive data. Despite its smaller size, the Seagate Nighthawk Pro 4-Bay NAS provides snapshot scheduling, secure VPN-friendly access, cloud backup integration, and user-level permissions. RAID 1 mirroring protects against a single drive failure, while email alerts and drive health monitoring help reduce maintenance surprises. It is a strong fit for legal offices, clinics, and small administrative departments.
