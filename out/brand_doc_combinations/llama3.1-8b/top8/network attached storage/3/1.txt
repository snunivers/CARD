Built for small offices, the Asustor AS6604T offers four drive bays, expandable storage capacity, and dependable data protection. It supports RAID 0, 1, 5, 6, 10, and single-disk modes, making it flexible for growing teams. A quad-core processor, 4 GB RAM, and dual Gigabit LAN ports provide smooth multi-user performance and link aggregation support. The Asustor AS6604T includes snapshot protection, scheduled backups, AES hardware encryption, and remote access tools. It works well for file sharing, department archives, and collaborative projects, with a straightforward web interface for setup and monitoring.
