The Braun Series 7 Trimmer is built for straightforward daily use, combining a simple two-button design with dependable trimming performance. Its stainless steel cutting system handles short and medium beards, while the click-on comb adjusts from 1 to 10 mm in even steps. The unit can be used cordlessly for up to 50 minutes and recharges overnight on the included cable. A removable blade head allows easy rinsing under water, and the lightweight body is comfortable for quick morning grooming. The Series 7 Trimmer is a practical option for users who prefer uncomplicated controls.
