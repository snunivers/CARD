The Braun Series 7 Trimmer is a cordless beard trimmer built for longer facial hair, using a wide adjustable comb that reaches up to 24 mm. This makes it suitable for shaping fuller beards without removing too much length. Hardened steel blades are designed to stay sharp over time, and the powerful motor helps cut through thick growth cleanly. A three-stage LED display shows low battery, charging progress, and full charge status. Users get about 85 minutes of runtime, plus a small detail attachment for the mustache and lip area. The Series 7 Trimmer balances range and precision.
