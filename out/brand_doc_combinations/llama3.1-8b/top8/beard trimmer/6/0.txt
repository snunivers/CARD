The Brio 3-in-1 Trimmer beard trimmer is designed for everyday grooming with stainless steel self-sharpening blades and 20 lock-in length settings from 0.5 to 10 mm. A compact rotary motor delivers even cutting through short or thick facial hair, while the detachable head rinses clean under water. It runs for up to 60 minutes on a full charge and supports quick USB charging for travel. The textured grip improves control around the jawline and mustache, making the 3-in-1 Trimmer a practical option for neat stubble or a short boxed beard.
