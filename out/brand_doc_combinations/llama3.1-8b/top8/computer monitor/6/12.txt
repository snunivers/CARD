Fast, focused, and compact, the HP EliteDisplay E243d targets esports players who want minimal delay and maximum responsiveness. Its 24.5-inch Full HD panel runs at 240 Hz and pairs with a 1 ms response time to keep motion sharp during quick flicks and rapid camera movement. The screen size makes it easy to track the whole field of play without excessive head movement. Adaptive sync support helps keep frames smooth, and selectable overdrive modes let users tune performance. The HP EliteDisplay E243d also includes DisplayPort, dual HDMI, a headphone output, and a stand with full ergonomic adjustment.
