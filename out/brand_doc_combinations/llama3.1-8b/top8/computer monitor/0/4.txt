The Dell UltraSharp U2720Q is a lightweight 15.6-inch portable monitor made for travel, remote work, and second-screen convenience. Its Full HD IPS panel provides solid clarity for documents, presentations, and streaming on the go. USB-C connectivity allows video, data, and power through a compatible device, while mini HDMI offers broad compatibility with laptops, consoles, and cameras. The slim enclosure slips easily into a bag, and the included folding cover doubles as a stand. With built-in speakers, low blue light support, and simple plug-and-play setup, the Dell UltraSharp U2720Q is ideal for mobile productivity.
