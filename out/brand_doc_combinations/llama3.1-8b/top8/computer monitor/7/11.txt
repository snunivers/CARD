The AOC CQ32G1 is a 23.8-inch eco-conscious monitor aimed at offices that value efficiency and reliable performance. Its Full HD IPS panel delivers balanced color and clear text for email, data entry, and day-to-day multitasking. An automatic brightness feature adjusts output based on ambient light to save energy and improve comfort, while the power-efficient design helps lower running costs across larger deployments. The monitor includes HDMI, DisplayPort, and a USB hub for common accessories. With VESA compatibility, recyclable packaging, and an adjustable stand, the AOC CQ32G1 is a sensible fit for modern business spaces.
