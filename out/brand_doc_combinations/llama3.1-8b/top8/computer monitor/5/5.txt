Built as an affordable everyday display, the LG 27UK850-W combines a 22-inch Full HD panel with a compact footprint that suits smaller desks. The monitor is well matched to basic work, online learning, and general household use. Its anti-glare screen helps reduce reflections under office lighting, and the 75 Hz refresh rate gives menus and videos a smoother feel. Connectivity includes HDMI and VGA, allowing easy pairing with newer and older systems. The LG 27UK850-W also features an energy-saving mode, intuitive on-screen controls, and VESA support for wall mounting or monitor arm installation.
