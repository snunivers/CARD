Made for serious DIY users, the Kobalt 54" 3-Drawer Toolbox 9-drawer tool chest balances capacity, accessibility, and value. Its drawers are arranged in mixed depths so smaller tools stay separated while larger items fit below. Each drawer uses ball-bearing slides for a smoother feel, even when fully loaded. The top lid opens to a wide storage compartment that works well for chargers, tape measures, and specialty tools. Corner bumpers help protect nearby surfaces, and the locking bar adds security when the chest is not in use. With durable casters and a sturdy steel shell, it handles everyday garage tasks confidently.
