The Kobalt 54" 3-Drawer Toolbox stainless steel tool chest offers a cleaner, more refined look for professional garages and workshops. Its corrosion-resistant exterior is easy to wipe down, making it suitable for environments where oil, dust, and moisture are common. Eight soft-close drawers provide quiet operation and protect tools from jarring movement. An aluminum drawer trim and recessed side handles give the unit a polished appearance without sacrificing strength. The top compartment opens on gas struts for convenient access to smaller items. Heavy-duty casters and a secure locking mechanism complete this versatile storage solution.
