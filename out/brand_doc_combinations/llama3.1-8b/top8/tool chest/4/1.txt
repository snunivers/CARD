Designed for mechanics who need compact storage, the Kobalt 54" 3-Drawer Toolbox top tool chest fits neatly on most roller cabinets or workbenches. It features five shallow drawers for wrenches, pliers, screwdrivers, and measuring tools, plus a lift-top compartment for frequently used items. The steel frame uses a baked enamel finish for added protection in busy workshops. Gas struts support the lid for safer opening and closing, and the central lock secures every drawer at once. With smooth ball-bearing slides and pre-cut drawer liners, this chest offers practical organization without taking up much floor space.
