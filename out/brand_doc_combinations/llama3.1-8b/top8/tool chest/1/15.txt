The DeWalt TSTAK 5-Drawer Toolbox drawer-first tool chest focuses on efficient organization for users who prefer separated storage over large open compartments. It includes eight drawers in graduated depths, making it easier to sort fasteners, hand tools, torque wrenches, and specialty kits. The welded steel body provides rigidity, and the powder-coated finish helps protect against corrosion in humid garages. Recessed handles reduce snag points, while the single-lock system secures the entire unit quickly. Mounted on durable caster wheels with front brakes, this chest offers a stable yet mobile solution for everyday maintenance and repair work.
