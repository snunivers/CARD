The KitchenAid KSC 69631 6-Quart Slow Cooker advanced slow cooker is designed for cooks who want more precision from a 6-quart appliance. It includes a temperature probe for monitoring large cuts of meat and automatically adjusts to warm once the target is reached. The digital control panel supports custom time settings and delayed start, making it easier to coordinate dinner around a full schedule. Its removable cast-style insert can be used for browning on compatible stovetops, adding convenience and flavor. A tight-fitting glass lid, durable side handles, and dishwasher-safe parts round out this versatile kitchen tool.
