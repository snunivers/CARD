With a 2TB capacity and PCIe 3.0 x4 interface, the Kingston A2000 targets value-focused gamers who want more space and faster loading than SATA can provide. This M.2 2280 SSD delivers up to 3,500MB/s read and 3,000MB/s write speeds, improving game installs, level loads, and general system responsiveness. The drive includes 3D NAND flash, LDPC error correction, TRIM support, and thermal monitoring for dependable day-to-day use. Its broad compatibility with many existing motherboards makes installation simple. This model is a strong fit for budget performance builds and expanding crowded game libraries.
