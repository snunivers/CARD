Built for gaming and high-performance workloads, the Kingston A2000 uses a PCIe 4.0 x4 NVMe interface in the M.2 2280 format. This 1TB SSD reaches read speeds up to 7,000MB/s and write speeds up to 6,500MB/s, reducing load times in large games and content creation software. The drive supports 3D NAND, dynamic SLC caching, LDPC error correction, and thermal throttling protection. Its compact single-sided design suits many modern motherboards and laptops. Hardware encryption support and endurance rated for heavy daily use make it a strong option for demanding users.
