The WD Black SN750 NVMe SSD is a 1TB external solid state drive built for users who want a slim everyday backup device. Using USB 3.2 Gen 2, it offers read speeds up to 1,000MB/s and write speeds up to 900MB/s, reducing transfer times for documents, media, and system images. The aluminum enclosure helps dissipate heat while keeping the drive lightweight and pocketable. Plug-and-play support works with most Windows and macOS systems, and the included USB-C cable simplifies setup. It is a convenient option for students, commuters, and anyone needing fast portable storage.
