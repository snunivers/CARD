Smart control is the highlight of the Y_Brand Y_Model Wi‑Fi space heater, a modern tower unit made for connected homes. It heats rooms up to 250 square feet with 1500-watt ceramic performance, wide oscillation, and an eco mode that adjusts output automatically. Users can change temperature, schedule heating periods, and monitor status from a mobile app or through voice assistant compatibility. The digital display is dimmable for nighttime use, and the remote stores neatly in the rear compartment. The Y_Brand Y_Model also provides a child lock, tip-over switch, and overheat shutoff.
