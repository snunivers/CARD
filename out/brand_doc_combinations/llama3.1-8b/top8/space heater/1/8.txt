The Dyson Hot + Cool Jet Focus AM09 bladeless space heater combines a modern look with smooth airflow for bedrooms, nurseries, and quiet living areas. It offers 900 and 1500 watt heat settings, plus a fan mode for mild weather. The bladeless outlet design helps create even circulation while making the unit easier to wipe clean than many traditional heaters. A digital thermostat, oscillation function, and sleep timer improve everyday convenience, and the slim vertical form saves floor space beside a sofa or bed. The Dyson Hot + Cool Jet Focus AM09 includes tip-over protection, overheat shutoff, and a low-noise nighttime setting.
