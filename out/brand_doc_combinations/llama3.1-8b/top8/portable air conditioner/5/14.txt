The Whynter ARC-122DS portable air conditioner is built for users who want stronger airflow and simple controls rather than extra complexity. Rated at 10,000 BTU, it cools rooms up to 330 square feet and offers three fan speeds for better comfort adjustment. A manual drain option is available for high-humidity conditions, while the self-evaporative system handles most normal cooling operation. The top panel uses large buttons and a clear digital temperature readout, making daily use straightforward. Whynter includes a washable filter, remote control, rolling casters, and a flexible exhaust hose.
