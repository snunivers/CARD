Designed for frequent repositioning, the Edgestar AP12001G pairs a lightweight cabinet with durable wheels and side handles for easy room-to-room cooling. Its 9,500 BTU output is well suited to spaces around 300 square feet, including offices, dorms, and sunrooms. The control interface includes clearly labeled buttons for cooling, fan-only use, dehumidification, and timer programming. A washable intake filter can be removed without tools, simplifying routine care. The AP12001G also features auto-swing louvers, automatic condensate evaporation, and a compact remote, while Edgestar provides a standard window vent kit.
