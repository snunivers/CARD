The Edgestar AP12001G stands out with an inverter-style compressor system that helps maintain more stable temperatures while reducing power spikes. Intended for rooms up to 450 square feet, it offers 11,000 BTU cooling, quiet nighttime performance, and smooth variable operation. Users can manage settings through the onboard display, remote, or Wi-Fi app, making this portable air conditioner easy to control from anywhere in the home. Additional features include a 24-hour timer, dehumidify mode, child lock, and washable dust filter. The AP12001G arrives with vent hose and window installation accessories.
