Engineered for open-plan spaces, the Frigidaire EFPC1022U1 delivers 14,000 BTU cooling power for rooms up to 550 square feet. This portable air conditioner uses a strong airflow system and auto-swing vents to distribute cool air more evenly across larger areas. The control panel supports cool, fan, dry, and auto modes, and Wi-Fi connectivity allows adjustments through a mobile app. A full-function remote, child lock, and programmable thermostat add convenience. The EFPC1022U1 includes a washable filter, drain hose option, and standard window vent components for straightforward installation.
