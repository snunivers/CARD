The T_Brand T_Model is a slim portable air conditioner made for studio apartments and guest rooms where floor space is limited. It provides 7,000 BTU cooling for areas up to 200 square feet and offers simple top-mounted controls with an LED display. Three operating modes include cool, fan, and dehumidify, making it useful beyond the peak summer season. Auto water evaporation reduces maintenance in normal conditions, while the reusable filter can be rinsed clean. T_Brand also equips this model with caster wheels, a remote, and an adjustable window sealing kit.
