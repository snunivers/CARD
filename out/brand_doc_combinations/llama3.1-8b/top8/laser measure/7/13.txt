Combining portability with enhanced functionality, the Leica Disto D3 is a laser measure that suits both trade professionals and detail-focused homeowners. It has a range of 90 meters and includes min/max tracking, delayed measurement, and indirect height calculation for hard-to-reach points. The multi-line display presents current measurements alongside calculated results, improving workflow during planning and installation. Its slim housing includes side grip textures and a reinforced battery compartment for durability. The Leica Disto D3 also supports silent mode, making it convenient in occupied homes, offices, and finished interior spaces.
