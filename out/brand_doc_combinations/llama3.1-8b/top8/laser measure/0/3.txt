For longer-range measuring, the Bosch GLM 50 C delivers reliable performance up to 100 meters. This laser measure includes a digital angle sensor, allowing indirect height and slope calculations when direct access is limited. Its large illuminated display shows multiple lines of data at once, reducing the need to scroll through results. Measurement history, stake-out mode, and timer operation support more advanced layout tasks. The Bosch GLM 50 C is powered by rechargeable lithium battery technology and charges through USB-C, giving users a practical option for extended field use.
