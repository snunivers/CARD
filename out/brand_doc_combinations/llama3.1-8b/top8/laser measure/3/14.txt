The Stabila 300-260 is an entry-level laser measure built for dependable daily use. With a 20-meter range, it is ideal for measuring wall lengths, window openings, furniture placement, and small remodeling tasks. Operation is intentionally simple, with direct measurement, continuous mode, and automatic unit conversion. The clear laser point and stable base help users position the tool accurately against flat surfaces. A lightweight frame, lanyard attachment, and quick-start design make the Stabila 300-260 a practical replacement for a standard tape measure in routine indoor projects.
