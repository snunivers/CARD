Compact yet capable, the Dyson 360 Eye robot vacuum is intended for condos, studios, and other smaller living spaces. Its narrow body and low height help it maneuver around chair legs and under cabinets where upright vacuums often struggle. The unit offers auto, edge, and spot modes, plus a scheduled daily run for routine upkeep. A 400 ml dust container is easy to remove and empty, and the filter can be rinsed for reuse. The Dyson 360 Eye runs quietly, charges on its dock, and uses cliff sensors to avoid stairs and ledges.
