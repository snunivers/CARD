The Dyson 360 Eye is a high-capacity robot vacuum built for larger spaces and frequent use. Its powerful motor is paired with a floating main brush that stays in contact with uneven flooring for more consistent pickup. Precision mapping creates systematic routes, reducing missed spots and repeat passes. The oversized dustbin holds more debris before emptying, and the optional self-empty base minimizes daily upkeep. App-based controls support custom schedules, restricted areas, and suction adjustments by room. The Dyson 360 Eye works well on tile, laminate, hardwood, and medium-pile carpets.
