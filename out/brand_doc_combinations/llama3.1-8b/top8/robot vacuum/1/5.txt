For homes with shedding pets, the iRobot Roomba i7+ delivers focused performance with strong suction, rubberized brush elements, and an easy-clean roller design. Fine dust and dander are trapped by a multi-stage filtration system, helping keep indoor air fresher. The robot automatically detects high-debris zones and performs extra passes where needed. Built-in sensors guide it around furniture legs, pet bowls, and stair edges. A large battery provides up to 150 minutes of cleaning, and the unit recharges automatically. The iRobot Roomba i7+ also supports scheduled cleaning to maintain floors with minimal daily effort.
