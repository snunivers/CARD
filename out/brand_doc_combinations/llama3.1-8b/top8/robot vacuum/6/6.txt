The Eufy RoboVac 11 combines a modern, slim housing with intelligent navigation for efficient cleaning under furniture and around clutter. Its front-mounted sensors identify obstacles and help the robot slow down before contact, reducing scuffs on walls and table legs. Users can select quiet, standard, or max suction modes depending on floor type and time of day. The removable mop pad is useful for light maintenance on sealed surfaces, while the separate dry dustbin keeps vacuuming performance consistent. With voice control compatibility and app scheduling, the Eufy RoboVac 11 fits easily into connected homes.
