The Hitachi M12RDMR fixed-base wood router is built for clean edge work, dado cuts, and laminate trimming. Its 1.75 HP motor delivers 10,000 to 25,000 RPM with electronic feedback to maintain speed under load. A fine-depth ring allows precise bit adjustment in 1/64-inch increments, while the die-cast aluminum base improves stability on hardwood and plywood. This router accepts 1/4-inch and 1/2-inch shank bits and includes a clear sub-base for better line visibility. Soft-start control, spindle lock, and a dust adapter make it practical for both shop and site use.
