The Bosch PR20EVSPK professional plunge router is suited to deep cuts, joinery, and workshop jig use. Its 2.5 HP motor produces 9,000 to 21,000 RPM with constant-speed electronics for smoother results in dense material. The plunge mechanism offers 2-3/8 inches of travel and locks firmly in position once depth is set. A rotating turret stop speeds up repeated passes, especially for mortises and stopped grooves. The Bosch PR20EVSPK includes 1/2-inch and 1/4-inch collets, oversized handles, a built-in chip shield, and an edge fence for improved guidance along boards and panels.
