The Technivorm Moccamaster KBG 793 is a 14-cup programmable coffee maker built for larger households and frequent entertaining. It offers regular, bold, and small-batch brewing modes to improve flavor whether making two cups or a full pot. The backlit display, 24-hour timer, and freshness indicator help streamline morning preparation. A permanent filter reduces waste, while the removable water tank makes filling more convenient at the sink. The carafe lid, basket, and tank are designed for easy cleaning. The Technivorm Moccamaster KBG 793 combines generous capacity with practical customization for everyday brewing.
