Engineered for speed, the De'Longhi Magnifica ESAM3300 uses a high-output heating system to bring water to ideal brewing temperature quickly and maintain consistent extraction throughout the cycle. This 8-cup coffee maker includes a stainless steel carafe, pre-infusion setting, and manual drip-stop for cleaner serving. Its wide showerhead evenly disperses water over the grounds, helping produce balanced flavor with less bitterness. The low-profile design fits under many cabinets, and the removable brew basket supports easy cleanup. The De'Longhi Magnifica ESAM3300 is well suited for users who want fast brewing without giving up cup quality.
