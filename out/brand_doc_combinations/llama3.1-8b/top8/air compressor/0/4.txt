The DeWalt DXCMLT606506 is a 20-gallon vertical air compressor designed for home workshops and serious DIY use. Its 1.8 HP motor and oil-lubricated pump provide improved durability for sanding, ratcheting, spray tasks, and inflation. With a maximum pressure of 175 PSI and 4.5 SCFM at 90 PSI, it offers extended tool run time compared with smaller portable units. The vertical tank saves floor space, while integrated wheels and a front handle assist with repositioning. A belt guard, tank drain valve, and automatic pressure switch add safety and day-to-day convenience.
