Compact and easy to store, the W_Brand W_Model 1-gallon trim compressor is intended for finish carpentry and quick household jobs. It delivers 135 PSI and 1.2 SCFM at 90 PSI, enough for pin nailers, brad nailers, and general inflation. The lightweight body fits easily in closets, work vans, or shelving, while the low-profile tank improves balance when carried. An oil-free pump keeps maintenance simple and helps ensure reliable starts. The compressor includes a universal quick coupler, built-in accessory storage, and non-marring feet to help protect finished floors.
