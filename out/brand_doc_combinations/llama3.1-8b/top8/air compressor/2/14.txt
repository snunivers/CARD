The Porter-Cable PANC2LMLPS 120-gallon industrial air compressor is engineered for high-duty-cycle manufacturing and service facilities. This rotary screw system delivers a steady 25 CFM at 125 PSI, making it suitable for production lines, CNC support, packaging equipment, and multi-station pneumatic networks. A variable-speed drive adjusts output to match air demand, helping reduce energy waste during partial-load operation. The enclosed cabinet lowers noise and simplifies cooling airflow management. An integrated aftercooler, automatic condensate drain, and electronic controller provide clean compressed air and easier monitoring in continuous-use environments.
