Designed for garage and light commercial service, the Porter-Cable PANC2LMLPS 30-gallon belt-drive compressor combines steady airflow with a compact footprint. It generates up to 155 PSI and 5.3 SCFM at 90 PSI, supporting impact wrenches, drills, paint preparation, and occasional spraying. The cast-iron cylinder and oil-lubricated pump are built for longer service intervals and cooler operation. A large flywheel improves heat dissipation, and the belt-drive layout helps reduce pump speed and wear. Easy-access controls, semi-pneumatic wheels, and a reinforced steel tank make this model practical for frequent use.
