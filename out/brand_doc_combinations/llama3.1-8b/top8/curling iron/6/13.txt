The Revlon 1.25" Digital Tourmaline Ceramic combines a clipless wand design with a pearl-infused ceramic surface for smooth, glossy curls. Its 1-inch to 0.5-inch tapered barrel is ideal for creating lived-in waves, tighter ends, or more dimension around the face. Adjustable heat from 250°F to 430°F gives users room to tailor styling to their hair type. The wand heats quickly and includes a protective glove to improve handling during wrap-and-release styling. A temperature lock, dual voltage, hanging loop, and long rotating cord add convenience whether you style daily or pack it for trips.
