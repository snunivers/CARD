With its extra-large 1.75-inch barrel, the Conair Infiniti Pro Spin Air Brush is designed for loose bends, volume at the roots, and smooth blowout-inspired movement. The ceramic technology helps maintain even heat across the wide barrel, making it suitable for creating soft styles on long hair. Users can choose from 270°F to 410°F through easy push-button controls and monitor settings on a compact digital display. The barrel clip is shaped to minimize marks, while the cool tip and integrated stand improve handling. Automatic shut-off and universal voltage round out the practical feature set.
