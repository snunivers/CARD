Built for larger lawns and mixed planting areas, the W_Brand W_Model automatic garden watering system combines a four-zone timer with pop-up sprinklers and drip lines in one package. Each zone can be programmed independently, making it easier to water grass, shrubs, and flower beds according to different requirements. The weather-resistant control unit supports weekly scheduling, manual override, and start-time adjustments. With durable underground tubing, anti-clog nozzles, and a backflow-resistant inlet design, this system is intended for reliable long-term use and helps maintain even coverage across complex garden layouts.
