The AR Blue Clean AR383 pressure washer is engineered for serious residential cleaning with a durable axial cam pump and 2000-watt electric motor. Delivering 2500 PSI at 1.5 GPM, it is effective on stone patios, garage floors, and weathered outdoor furniture. A 22-foot hose and 16-foot extension lance help reach higher areas such as garage doors and low rooflines. The detergent bottle connects directly to the spray gun for quicker soap application. Waterproof control switches, onboard nozzle holders, and thermal motor protection enhance safety and day-to-day convenience in changing outdoor conditions.
