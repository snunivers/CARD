Made for vehicle owners, the Karcher K5 Premium pressure washer focuses on controlled cleaning and paint-safe performance. The 1900-watt motor produces up to 1900 PSI and 1.5 GPM, enough to lift road salt, bugs, and wheel brake dust without excessive force on trim or decals. It includes a foam cannon, soft-bristle wash brush, underbody wand, and 25-foot hose. The variable-pressure trigger lets users lower intensity around emblems and mirrors. Compact vertical storage, anti-tip wheels, and a detergent shutoff valve make the Karcher K5 Premium practical for regular driveway detailing.
