The Karcher K5 Premium pressure washer is a compact electric unit designed for patios, bikes, siding, and garden tools. Its 1800-watt motor delivers up to 2000 PSI and 1.4 GPM for reliable everyday cleaning without excessive noise. A 20-foot high-pressure hose, 35-foot power cord, and integrated detergent tank improve reach and convenience. The adjustable spray wand includes 0°, 25°, and soap settings for different surfaces. Built-in wheels and an onboard accessory rack keep storage simple, while the automatic total stop system helps reduce wear and power consumption between trigger pulls.
