Designed for demanding outdoor work, the Z_Brand Z_Model gas hedge trimmer delivers high output from a 24cc two-cycle engine. Its 24-inch double-sided blade makes fast work of thick, overgrown hedges and allows sweeping cuts in either direction. A translucent fuel tank helps monitor fuel level, while the primer bulb and choke assist with easier starting. The rotating rear handle improves control when shaping vertical surfaces, and an anti-vibration system helps reduce operator fatigue. This trimmer is a practical choice for landscapers and property owners tackling heavy seasonal pruning.
