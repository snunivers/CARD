For users who want a balance of reach and precision, the Craftsman CMEST120 cordless hedge trimmer offers a 24-inch blade and brushless motor for efficient power delivery. The included 60V battery supports extended trimming sessions and consistent performance as charge levels drop. Its blade tip guard helps protect against accidental contact with fences or walls, while the rotating rear handle allows more natural wrist positions during side trimming. With a 1-inch cut capacity, this unit handles thicker branches than many standard models. It is a strong fit for established hedges and larger landscape projects.
