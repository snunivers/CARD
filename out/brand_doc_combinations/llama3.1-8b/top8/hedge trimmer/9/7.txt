Made for detailed trimming and topiary work, the T_Brand T_Model combines a slim housing with a precise 18-inch blade for close control. This cordless unit runs on a 12V lithium-ion battery, keeping overall weight low for users who prefer maximum agility over heavy-duty output. It is suitable for soft new growth, box hedges, and ornamental shrubs, with a cutting capacity of 3/8 inch. A transparent hand guard improves visibility near delicate plants, and the compact charger stores easily in a shed or garage. This model is especially useful for small urban gardens.
