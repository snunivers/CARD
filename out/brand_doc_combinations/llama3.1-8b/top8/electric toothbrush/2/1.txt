Built for users who want a deeper clean, the Oral-B Genius Pro 9000 electric toothbrush combines high-frequency sonic vibration with a compact brush head for better access to back teeth. It features 5 cleaning programs, including whitening, gum care, daily clean, polish, and sensitive. A pressure alert reduces vibration when brushing too hard, helping protect gums. The ergonomic matte handle offers a secure grip, even when wet. One full charge supports about 30 days of regular use, and the travel lock makes it convenient for packing in a suitcase or gym bag.
