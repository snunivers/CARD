The T_Brand T_Model electric toothbrush offers a practical mix of power and portability for daily oral hygiene. Its high-speed motor supports efficient plaque removal, while the rounded bristle tips are designed to be gentle on teeth and gums. There are 3 cleaning settings and a memory function that starts the brush in the last used mode. A 2-minute auto timer and interval prompts help improve brushing consistency. The package includes a USB-C charging stand, 2 brush heads, and a ventilated travel case. Battery life lasts up to 40 days per charge.
