Engineered for sensitive gums, the Philips Sonicare ProtectiveClean 4100 electric toothbrush combines moderate sonic power with flexible bristles that adapt to the contours of teeth. It includes a dedicated gum care mode alongside daily clean, deep clean, and polish settings. The handle has a clear LED display showing selected mode and battery status. A smart pressure system lowers vibration automatically to avoid aggressive brushing. The device is fully waterproof and easy to rinse under running water. In the box are 3 brush heads, a charging dock, and a storage pouch for everyday organization.
