For users who want more control over their routine, the Quip Electric Toothbrush electric toothbrush offers 5 speed settings and interchangeable heads for standard cleaning or gum-focused care. The handle is slightly wider for a stable grip, making it comfortable for adults and older users. A 2-minute timer with short interval pulses promotes complete brushing without guesswork. The brush runs quietly and produces consistent sonic vibrations to clean around crowns, bridges, and natural teeth. One charge powers the unit for about a month. Comes with a charging base and 3 replacement heads.
