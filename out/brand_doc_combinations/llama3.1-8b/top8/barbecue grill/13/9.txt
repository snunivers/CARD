Made for larger gatherings, the X_Brand X_Model barbecue grill offers six main burners and a generous cooking surface suited for family parties and neighborhood cookouts. It produces 72,000 BTU total, with porcelain-coated flame tamers that reduce flare-ups and distribute heat more evenly. The sear burner creates high temperatures for steaks, while the side burner handles sauces or side dishes. Cast-iron grates retain heat well and leave clear grill marks. The enclosed cabinet stores grilling accessories and hides the propane tank. Large wheels, a hood thermometer, and reliable electronic ignition complete this spacious outdoor setup.
