Created for users who prefer premium styling features, the Bumble and bumble Flat Iron combines titanium plates with smart temperature regulation for stable, even heat. The tool adjusts from 150°C to 235°C and is suitable for smoothing dense hair quickly while also creating glossy bends and waves. Floating plate suspension helps reduce tugging, and the ion generator supports a sleeker finish. A clear digital display, audible ready signal, and memory recall function make operation intuitive. The straightener is finished with a 360-degree swivel cord, dual voltage compatibility, and automatic shut-off after 90 minutes.
