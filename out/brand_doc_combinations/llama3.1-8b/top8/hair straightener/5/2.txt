The HSI Professional Ceramic Tourmaline Ionic Flat Iron is a compact mini hair straightener suited to short hair, fringes, touch-ups, and travel use. Its slim ceramic tourmaline plates heat evenly and help smooth flyaways without pulling. With a fixed styling temperature of 200°C, it keeps operation simple while still delivering polished results. The straightener reaches working heat in about 45 seconds and includes worldwide dual voltage for convenient packing. A 1.8-meter swivel cord, locking plates, and a protective cap make it easy to store in a drawer, gym bag, or carry-on case.
