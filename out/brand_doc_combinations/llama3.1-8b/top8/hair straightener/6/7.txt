With a slim profile and rounded edges, the Remington S9500 Air Expert 1.5" Digital Flat Iron works well for both sleek straight styles and defined curls. Its 25 mm ceramic tourmaline plates are designed to deliver smooth heat while minimizing hot spots that can affect styling consistency. The straightener offers eight heat levels between 140°C and 230°C and reaches temperature in around 20 seconds. An integrated sensor helps maintain selected heat during use, even on thicker sections. Additional practical features include a locking hinge, hanging loop, 2-meter swivel cord, and universal voltage for everyday use or travel.
