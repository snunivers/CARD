The Z_Brand Z_Model is a professional-style hair straightener engineered for high shine and consistent results. Nano-titanium plates transfer heat efficiently, helping reduce the number of passes needed on thick or resistant hair. Users can select temperatures from 140°C to 240°C through a digital control panel, while floating plates adapt to section thickness for smoother pressure. The ionic function helps reduce static and leaves hair looking more polished. For convenience, this model includes dual voltage, a 3-meter salon cord, temperature lock, plate lock, and an automatic shut-off feature.
