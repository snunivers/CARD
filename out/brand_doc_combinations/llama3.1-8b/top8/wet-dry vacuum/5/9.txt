For workshops where airborne dust control matters, the Eureka Mighty Mite 3681GZ wet-dry vacuum uses a HEPA-style filtration system that helps capture fine particles during sanding and cutting cleanup. The 9-gallon tank balances useful capacity with manageable size, and the 5 peak HP motor maintains strong pickup on dry debris and wet spills alike. A tool-triggered power outlet lets the vacuum start automatically with compatible saws or sanders, reducing mess at the source. Included accessories cover floor cleaning, crevice work, and liquid recovery. A stable drum base and locking casters keep the unit planted during use.
