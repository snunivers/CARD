The wall-mountable Miele Complete C3 Limited Edition wet-dry vacuum is made for garages where floor space matters. Its 5-gallon tank stays off the ground when mounted, while the 6-foot locking hose and extension pieces give the user a generous cleaning radius for benches, shelves, and vehicle interiors. A remote on/off control built into the handle adds convenience during detail work. This model uses a 5 peak HP bypass motor for consistent suction and includes a blower function for clearing dust from corners. Accessory hooks and hose storage keep the setup neat between projects.
