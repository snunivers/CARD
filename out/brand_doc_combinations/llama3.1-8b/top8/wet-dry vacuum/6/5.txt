For medium-duty jobsite cleanup, the Black+Decker BDH2000PL Pivot Vac combines an 8-gallon drum with a 6 peak HP motor to handle heavier debris, damp plaster dust, and pooled water. The reinforced hose resists crushing, and the oversized drain port speeds emptying after wet collection. This wet-dry vacuum includes a fine-dust cartridge filter, foam wet filter, two extension wands, utility nozzle, and a large floor brush for broad passes across concrete or tile. A built-in power cord wrap, push handle, and durable rear wheels make transport easier around workshops and renovation sites.
