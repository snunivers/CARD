The Black+Decker BDH2000PL Pivot Vac is a professional-style wet-dry vacuum made for larger cleanup demands in workshops, warehouses, and renovation sites. Its 18-gallon drum works with a 6.5 peak HP motor to deliver high airflow and longer run time between emptying. A wide, crush-resistant hose improves pickup of bulky debris, while the front drain simplifies liquid disposal. This unit includes a floor tool, crevice attachment, extension wands, and a fine-dust filter for dry work. Heavy-duty casters, a reinforced handle, and a stable base help the vacuum move confidently across rough floors.
