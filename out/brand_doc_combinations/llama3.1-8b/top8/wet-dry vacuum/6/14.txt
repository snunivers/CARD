The Black+Decker BDH2000PL Pivot Vac targets workshop users who want strong suction and improved organization. This 6-gallon wet-dry vacuum features a 5 peak HP motor, locking casters, and molded storage points for the hose, wands, and nozzles so accessories stay with the machine. Its cartridge filter supports dry debris collection, while the foam sleeve handles wet pickup when cleaning spills or extracting water from small areas. The included utility nozzle and round brush suit both floor and bench cleaning. With a compact drum shape and durable side latches, it stores easily between weekend projects.
