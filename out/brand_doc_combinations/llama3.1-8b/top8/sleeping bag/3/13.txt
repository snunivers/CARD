The Marmot Col -20°C is a plush cold-night sleeping bag made for base camps, RV travel, and late-fall campground stays. Rated to 10°F (-12°C), it uses thick layered insulation and a generously filled hood to trap warmth in low temperatures. The oversized rectangular design suits campers who prioritize space and cushioned comfort over pack weight. A fleece-like lining creates a softer sleeping surface, while the heavy-duty outer shell resists wear from repeated trips. The zipper can be opened from either end for ventilation, and the bag stores in an included duffel-style sack with compression straps.
