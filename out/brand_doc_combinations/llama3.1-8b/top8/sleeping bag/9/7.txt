For alpine treks and minimalist packing, the T_Brand T_Model delivers an efficient mummy design with a 15°F (-9°C) rating and a streamlined cut. The shell uses ultralight ripstop nylon, while the synthetic microfiber insulation is mapped more heavily around the torso and feet for balanced warmth. A close-fitting hood with drawcord adjustment reduces heat loss in windy campsites. The footbox is anatomically shaped to improve comfort when sleeping on your back or side. The T_Brand T_Model also features a short center zipper to reduce bulk and total trail weight.
