Made for warm-weather adventures, the Big Agnes Q-Core 0 is a compact sleeping bag with a 50°F (10°C) rating ideal for festivals, summer camping, and hostel travel. Its semi-rectangular shape gives more shoulder room than a standard mummy design, while lightweight synthetic insulation adds comfort on cooler evenings. The lining is breathable and quick drying, helping reduce clamminess in humid conditions. A dual-slider zipper allows easy ventilation from the top or foot area. Weighing under 2 lb, the Big Agnes Q-Core 0 packs into a small carry sack and stores neatly in limited gear space.
