The Big Agnes Q-Core 0 is an everyday sleeping bag created for scouts, occasional campers, and emergency preparedness kits. Its practical 45°F (7°C) temperature rating and polyester fill make it useful for mild outdoor nights, guest accommodations, or vehicle storage. The rectangular design is easy to roll and forgiving for restless sleepers. A full-length zipper opens flat for blanket-style use, and the rugged shell resists abrasion from tent floors and camp cots. The included stuff sack has compression straps for tidier packing. The Q-Core 0 offers straightforward comfort without unnecessary complexity.
