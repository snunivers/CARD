The W_Brand W_Model is a lightweight mummy sleeping bag built for three-season backpacking. Rated to 30°F (-1°C), it uses synthetic insulation that continues to perform in damp conditions. The shell is made from durable ripstop polyester with a soft brushed lining for added comfort. A contoured hood, draft collar, and full-length zipper draft tube help retain warmth overnight. Weighing about 2.6 lb, it packs down compactly into the included compression sack. An internal pocket keeps a phone or headlamp close, while anti-snag zipper construction makes entry and exit easier.
