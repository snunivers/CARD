The Rab Neutrino 400 offers a versatile women’s-specific fit with added insulation in the footbox and core areas where extra warmth is often appreciated. Designed for three-season use, it carries a 29°F (-2°C) rating and features a contoured hood, shaped shoulders, and a narrower torso profile to improve thermal efficiency. The shell fabric is lightweight, wind-resistant, and finished to repel light moisture. Inside, a soft liner helps reduce noise and friction while changing position. The Neutrino 400 packs into a compact compression sack and includes an interior pocket for valuables or overnight essentials.
