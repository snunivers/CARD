For pool owners seeking quiet operation, the W_Brand W_Model delivers efficient robotic cleaning with low noise and energy consumption. Suitable for most in-ground pool surfaces, the W_Brand W_Model provides dependable cleaning performance in a compact, easy-to-service package.
