The Zodiac G4 is a premium cordless robotic pool cleaner engineered for in-ground pools up to 50 feet. Its high-capacity battery supports extended runtimes, and onboard sensors optimize path planning for improved overall coverage. The cleaner scrubs the floor, walls, and waterline, then parks near the edge when the cycle is complete for easier retrieval. A large top-access basket captures both coarse debris and fine sediment. With app-based mode selection and weekly scheduling, the Zodiac G4 offers modern convenience while eliminating the clutter and drag of a floating power cable.
