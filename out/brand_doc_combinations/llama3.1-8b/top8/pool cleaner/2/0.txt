The Zodiac MX6 is a robotic pool cleaner designed for in-ground pools up to 40 feet. It uses dual drive motors, fine-mesh filtration, and smart route mapping to collect leaves, sand, and algae from the floor and walls. A top-loading filter basket makes rinsing simple, while the weekly timer supports automatic cleaning cycles. The anti-tangle swivel cable helps maintain movement around steps and corners. With low-voltage operation and a 2-hour cleaning program, the Zodiac MX6 offers efficient maintenance for vinyl, fiberglass, and concrete pool surfaces.
