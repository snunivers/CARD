The Polaris 9350 Sport combines robotic cleaning with waterline scrubbing for a polished finish in medium to large in-ground pools. It features active brushes that loosen stuck-on debris, plus dual filtration cartridges that separate fine particles from larger leaves and twigs. The cleaner climbs walls, navigates drains, and returns to the floor without losing traction. Multiple cleaning modes let you target floor-only or full-pool coverage depending on your schedule. With an easy-lift handle and quick water release, the Polaris 9350 Sport is designed for convenient removal and routine maintenance after each cycle.
