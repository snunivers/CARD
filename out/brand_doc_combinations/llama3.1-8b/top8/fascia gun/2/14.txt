Made for recovery after running, cycling, or long workdays, the Makita XCV10Z fascia gun provides strong but controlled percussion in a streamlined body. The unit includes a dual-control system with both physical buttons and a touch slider, allowing quick adjustment during use. Six heads cover broad muscles, feet, joints, and targeted trigger points. Operating noise remains low thanks to a suspended motor mount and internal damping layer. The 2,200 mAh battery supports about 4 hours of use, and the device enters standby automatically when left idle to conserve power.
