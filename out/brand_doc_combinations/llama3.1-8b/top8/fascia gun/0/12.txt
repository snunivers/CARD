Engineered for longer sessions, the DeWalt DWFP55130 combines a ventilated motor system with a 3,000 mAh battery to deliver up to 6 hours of intermittent use. This fascia gun offers five speed settings and three guided modes focused on activation, recovery, and relaxation. The body uses a textured finish for a secure hold, even with sweaty hands after training. Seven attachments are included, among them a wedge head for shoulder blades and IT bands. The OLED screen displays speed, battery level, and applied pressure. A lockable carrying case supports safer transport.
