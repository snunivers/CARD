Compact enough for a gym bag, the Bosch GSA 18V-60 fascia gun focuses on portability without sacrificing useful recovery features. The fold-flat handle design makes storage easier, and the total weight is about 650 g. Four speed settings and a 7 mm amplitude are aimed at light to moderate muscle stimulation rather than aggressive deep tissue work. This makes it a good option for office workers, travelers, and beginners. The kit includes three attachments, a charging cable, and a drawstring pouch. One charge provides up to 3 hours of operation for quick daily sessions.
