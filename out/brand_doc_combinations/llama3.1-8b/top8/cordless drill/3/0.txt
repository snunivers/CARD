The Milwaukee 2801-20 1/2 in. Cordless Drill/Driver Kit cordless drill is a compact 12V drill/driver built for everyday home repairs, furniture assembly, and light wood or metal work. It features a 3/8-inch keyless chuck, two-speed gearbox, and 18 torque settings for better control when driving screws. The included 1.5Ah lithium-ion battery charges in about 60 minutes and helps keep total weight low at just 2.4 lb. An integrated LED light improves visibility in cabinets and corners, while the soft-grip handle and variable-speed trigger make the drill comfortable to use during longer tasks.
