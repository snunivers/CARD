The Ryobi P208 18V ONE+ Cordless Drill/Driver Kit cordless drill is engineered for frequent use, pairing a high-efficiency brushless motor with a 20V lithium-ion battery system. With up to 650 in-lb of torque, a two-speed gearbox, and 24-position clutch, it handles demanding drilling and fastening jobs in hardwood, sheet metal, and composite materials. The 1/2-inch metal chuck improves bit retention, while the reinforced housing is designed to withstand jobsite wear. A bright LED with pre-glow and afterglow timing helps in dark spaces. The kit includes two 4.0Ah batteries, charger, case, and double-ended driver bit.
