The DeWalt 20V MAX Cordless Drill/Driver Kit (DCD771C2) is a reliable 16V cordless drill/driver intended for routine household jobs and occasional workshop use. It includes a 3/8-inch keyless chuck, variable speed up to 1,350 RPM, and 19 torque levels plus a drilling mode for greater flexibility across different materials. The lithium-ion battery is removable and designed for low self-discharge when stored between projects. For added comfort, the handle uses a contoured grip with textured panels. This model is a good fit for curtain rods, shelf brackets, light maintenance, and assembling desks, chairs, and storage units.
