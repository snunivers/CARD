For contractors and serious DIY users, the Campbell Hausfeld DH4000 3-in-1 Sprayer stand-mounted airless sprayer is built to deliver steady pressure and broad coating compatibility. The unit sprays primers, acrylics, latex paints, and masonry coatings directly from the can, supported by a durable motor and hardened pump components. Maximum pressure reaches 3300 PSI, and the adjustable pressure control allows better results on both trim and open wall areas. The compact frame stores easily in a van or workshop, while the 30-foot hose increases reach. The Campbell Hausfeld DH4000 3-in-1 Sprayer also features a built-in tip storage area and prime/spray selector.
