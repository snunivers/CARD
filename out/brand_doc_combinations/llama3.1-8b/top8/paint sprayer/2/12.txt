The Wagner Control Spray Max 590 fine finish paint sprayer is intended for woodworking shops, built-in cabinetry, and decorative trim applications. Using HVLP technology, it reduces bounce-back and helps achieve a softer spray pattern for detailed surfaces. The precision gun includes a metal nozzle set, adjustable fan width, and a smooth trigger pull for improved control on corners and profiles. A separate turbine base keeps hand weight low and connects through a flexible 15-foot hose. The Wagner Control Spray Max 590 works best with sealers, stains, lacquers, and properly thinned paints, and it packs into a compact storage case after use.
