The Graco Magnum X5 heavy-duty paint sprayer is built for frequent use on warehouses, commercial interiors, and extensive exterior coating jobs. Its robust airless system supports long spraying runs with a high-flow pump, pressure adjustment, and durable piston components engineered for demanding materials. The unit can handle elastomerics, primers, block fillers, and standard wall paints with reduced downtime. A metal frame with oversized wheels improves movement over rough surfaces, while onboard hose and gun storage keeps accessories organized. The Graco Magnum X5 comes with a contractor-grade spray gun, 50-foot hose, and two reversible tips.
