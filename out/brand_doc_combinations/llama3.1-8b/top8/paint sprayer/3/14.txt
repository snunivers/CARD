The Fuji 2200 Spray Gun compact stain sprayer is an easy option for patios, pergolas, playsets, and outdoor furniture. Optimized for thinner coatings, it provides efficient coverage for transparent stains, wood oils, and sealers without requiring a large machine. The 450W motor, 800 ml cup, and simple one-knob flow adjustment make operation approachable for occasional users. Its narrow front profile helps reach between balusters, slats, and lattice panels. The Fuji 2200 Spray Gun disassembles quickly for cleaning, and the included viscosity cup and nozzle cleaning pin help maintain steady spray quality across repeated seasonal wood care projects.
