The DeVilbiss Finish Pro GP8231 texture paint sprayer is intended for interior finishing, patch repair, and decorative wall treatments. With a large-capacity hopper and powerful compressor-assisted design, it can apply orange peel, knockdown, and popcorn textures with reliable consistency. Airflow and material volume are independently adjustable, allowing users to match different compounds and desired patterns. The trigger lock helps reduce fatigue during ceiling work, and the reinforced spray gun body is suited to repeated job-site use. The DeVilbiss Finish Pro GP8231 is supplied with multiple orifice sizes, a flexible air hose, and a carry handle for easier transport.
