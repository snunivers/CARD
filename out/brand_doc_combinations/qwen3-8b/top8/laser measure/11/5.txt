Featuring a rugged build and practical measuring functions, the V_Brand V_Model is made for warehouse, workshop, and site use. It measures up to 80 meters and includes a color display that adjusts brightness for easier reading. The laser measure can save multiple project files, useful for repeat jobs or room-by-room documentation. A threaded mount allows attachment to a tripod for improved stability during longer measurements. V_Brand equips the V_Model with audible key feedback, unit conversion, and a low-battery indicator to keep operation simple and dependable throughout the day.
