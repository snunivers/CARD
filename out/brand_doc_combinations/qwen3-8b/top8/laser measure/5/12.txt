The Fluke 2350 laser measure is tailored for flooring, tiling, and room planning applications. Its 55-meter range and ±2 mm accuracy allow dependable measurements for both small and medium-sized spaces. The tool offers quick calculations for square footage and cubic volume, helping users estimate materials more efficiently. A continuous mode updates readings live, useful when checking clearances or aligning fixtures. The bright backlit display remains easy to read in basements or poorly lit interiors. Fluke gives the 2350 a simple control layout that reduces setup time for repetitive jobs.
