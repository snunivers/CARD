Engineered for precision, the Fluke 2350 laser measure combines a 70-meter measuring range with advanced calculation modes for professionals. It supports indirect measurement using two-point and three-point Pythagorean functions, ideal for ceiling heights and exterior walls. The housing features sealed buttons and a textured grip for secure handling in dusty environments. A tilt sensor expands layout options, while the screen displays current and previous values simultaneously. The Fluke 2350 also includes a timer, reference pin, and carrying pouch, making it a versatile option for surveying and renovation tasks.
