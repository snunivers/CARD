Designed for demanding layout work, the Fluke 2350 laser measure offers extended range up to 120 meters and high accuracy of ±1.5 mm under typical conditions. The unit includes a camera-assisted targeting screen to improve aiming in bright outdoor light, where the laser point may be harder to see. Users can store measured values, label sessions, and export readings through Bluetooth to compatible devices. An impact-resistant shell and IP65 protection make the Fluke 2350 suitable for construction environments where reliability, speed, and digital documentation are equally important.
