The Bosch GLM 10SD is a straightforward laser measure focused on convenience and portability. Suitable for apartments, offices, and home workshops, it reaches up to 35 meters and returns results quickly with a single press. Users can choose from meters, inches, or feet, and the device automatically computes area and volume after basic input. Its compact rectangular design sits flat against walls and corners for more stable positioning. Powered by standard AAA batteries, the Bosch GLM 10SD is easy to maintain and practical for occasional measuring without specialized setup.
