The Milwaukee M12 LASER 2 laser measure is tailored for flooring, tiling, and room planning applications. Its 55-meter range and ±2 mm accuracy allow dependable measurements for both small and medium-sized spaces. The tool offers quick calculations for square footage and cubic volume, helping users estimate materials more efficiently. A continuous mode updates readings live, useful when checking clearances or aligning fixtures. The bright backlit display remains easy to read in basements or poorly lit interiors. Milwaukee gives the M12 LASER 2 a simple control layout that reduces setup time for repetitive jobs.
