Designed for smoothie enthusiasts, the Blendtec Total Classic 64 Oz blender includes a 1000-watt motor, a 1.8-liter Tritan jar, and preprogrammed settings for green smoothies, frozen drinks, and cleaning. Its six-point blade assembly circulates ingredients efficiently to reduce chunks and improve consistency. A digital control panel displays remaining cycle time, while the variable speed dial allows custom blending. The vented lid cap can be removed to add seeds, oats, or liquids during operation. Durable drive components and overload protection support regular use in busy households with varied blending needs.
