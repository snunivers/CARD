The Breville BBL6000XL countertop blender is designed for everyday smoothies, soups, and sauces. Its 700-watt motor powers six stainless steel blades to crush ice and blend frozen fruit with steady performance. A 1.5-liter BPA-free jar offers enough capacity for family use, while three speed settings and a pulse function give better texture control. The lid includes a removable measuring cap for adding ingredients mid-blend. Non-slip feet improve stability, and the jar, lid, and blade assembly are dishwasher safe for easier cleanup after breakfast or meal prep.
