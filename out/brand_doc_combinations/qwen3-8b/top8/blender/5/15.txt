The Cuisinart Precision Touch 12 Cup family blender balances capacity, control, and easy maintenance for everyday cooking. It uses a 750-watt motor with four speeds and pulse to handle fruit blends, creamy dips, marinades, and occasional ice crushing. The 1.7-liter jar features measurement markings, a locking lid, and a pouring spout to reduce drips. Stainless steel blades are removable, helping users clean hard-to-reach areas more thoroughly. An internal safety system prevents operation if the jar is not seated correctly. The sturdy base and simple button layout make this model approachable for regular household use.
