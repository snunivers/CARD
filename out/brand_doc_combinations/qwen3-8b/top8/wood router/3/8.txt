The Hitachi Hitachi 5510V cordless trim router provides mobility for punch-list jobs, onsite fitting, and quick edge detail work. Running on an 18V battery platform, it delivers a no-load speed range of 10,000 to 26,000 RPM with electronic speed selection for different materials. Brushless motor technology improves runtime and reduces maintenance, while the compact body supports easy one-hand operation. An LED light brightens the cutting area, and the depth ring offers fine adjustment for laminate and softwood tasks. The router accepts 1/4-inch bits and includes a straight base, edge guide, and vacuum adapter.
