The Stanley Stanley 39-212 mid-size wood router balances power and handling for general woodworking projects. A 1.5 HP motor with variable speed from 10,000 to 25,000 RPM works well for edge shaping, hinge mortising, and template trimming. Its clear round sub-base improves visibility, while the ergonomic handles give better control on narrow stock. Tool-free motor removal makes bit changes and base cleaning faster. A fine adjustment dial helps set exact depth for repeat passes, and the router accepts both 1/4-inch and 1/2-inch bits. The package includes an edge guide, wrench, and dust collection attachment.
