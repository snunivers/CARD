The Dewalt Dewalt D25700 plunge router is designed for precise inlays, mortises, and pattern routing. A 12-amp motor with soft start delivers 8,000 to 23,000 RPM, and the electronic control system reduces speed drop during heavier passes. Dual guide posts offer smooth, stable plunging, and the adjustable depth rod pairs with a multi-stop turret for repeatable setups. Comfortable side handles and a low-vibration housing help reduce fatigue in extended use. This router supports 1/4-inch and 1/2-inch collets, includes a chip deflector and edge guide, and features a sealed switch for improved dust resistance.
