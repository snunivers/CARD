The Dewalt Dewalt D25700 compact trim router is a lightweight option for edge profiling, flush trimming, and decorative detail work. Powered by a 6.5-amp motor, it reaches 30,000 RPM for fast, clean cuts in softwood, MDF, and laminates. Its slim body improves one-handed control, while the quick-release motor clamp makes base changes simple. An adjustable clear base helps maintain visibility around the bit, and the spindle lock speeds up accessory swaps. The Dewalt Dewalt D25700 accepts 1/4-inch shank bits and includes a straight guide, dust nozzle, and rubber overmold grip.
