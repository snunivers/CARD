Made for router table installations, the Craftsman Craftsman 311.19211 production router emphasizes power and above-table convenience. Its 3 HP motor runs from 10,000 to 22,000 RPM, with speed control optimized for larger diameter bits used in cabinet doors, cope-and-stick joints, and molding work. An above-table height crank allows easy adjustment without removing the motor, and the sealed bearing design helps withstand fine dust in busy shops. The Craftsman Craftsman 311.19211 supports 1/2-inch and 1/4-inch collets, features soft start and overload protection, and includes a motor housing designed for stable table mounting.
