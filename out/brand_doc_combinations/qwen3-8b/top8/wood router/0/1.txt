Designed for cabinetmaking and template work, the Bosch Bosch 1617EVSPK plunge wood router combines power with accurate control. The 2.25 HP motor offers variable speed from 8,000 to 22,000 RPM, making it suitable for large profile bits and slower hardwood cuts. Smooth plunge travel with a three-stop turret supports repeatable mortises, grooves, and hinge recesses. Ergonomic handles, a trigger lock, and above-table height adjustment increase convenience during longer tasks. The router includes both 1/4-inch and 1/2-inch collets, a chip shield, and an onboard fine-tune mechanism for consistent depth settings.
