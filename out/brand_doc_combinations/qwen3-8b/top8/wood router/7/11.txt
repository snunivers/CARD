For detailed furniture work, the Ryobi Ryobi 7700-0010 precision trim router focuses on accuracy and comfort. The 7-amp motor offers variable speed between 12,000 and 30,000 RPM, allowing clean cuts on hardwood edges, plywood veneer, and plastics. A micro-depth adjustment system supports delicate flush trimming and small-profile routing, while the low-profile base keeps the tool stable near corners. Rubberized grip areas improve control during one-handed use, and the transparent base keeps the bit visible. The Ryobi Ryobi 7700-0010 takes 1/4-inch shank bits and includes an offset base, guide roller, and dust nozzle.
