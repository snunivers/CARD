The John Deere X300 cordless lawn mower offers quiet operation and low maintenance for suburban lawns. Powered by a 40V lithium-ion battery, it cuts up to 35 minutes on a full charge under typical conditions. The 18-inch deck is lightweight and easy to maneuver around flower beds, fences, and trees. This model includes push-button start, a brushless motor, and a five-position height lever for quick adjustments. Users can switch between mulching and rear bagging with minimal setup. Folding handles and vertical storage make the mower especially practical for sheds with limited space.
