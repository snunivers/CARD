The John Deere X300 stand-on lawn mower is built for fast work on expansive residential or light commercial properties. Powered by a 26 HP engine, it uses a 52-inch deck and hydrostatic drive system to cover ground quickly while maintaining precise control around beds and obstacles. The operator platform includes a suspension pad to reduce fatigue during long sessions. Easy-access service points simplify daily checks, and the deck lift mechanism allows quick height changes. Strong frame construction and commercial-style tires add durability. This mower is suited to users seeking professional productivity in a compact footprint.
