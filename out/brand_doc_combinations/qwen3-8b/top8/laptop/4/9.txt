Engineered for advanced workloads, the Microsoft Surface Laptop 5 serves as a mobile workstation for engineers, developers, and analysts. It features a 17.3-inch display, a powerful multi-core processor, 32GB RAM, and professional-grade dedicated graphics for simulation, coding, and data-heavy applications. A fast 1TB SSD reduces project load times, and an additional storage bay supports future expansion. The cooling system uses multiple heat pipes to handle sustained performance. With a numeric keypad, Ethernet, several high-speed ports, and strong build quality, this laptop is designed for users who need desktop-like power on the move.
