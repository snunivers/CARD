The Ecovacs T9 AIVI is tailored for homes that need thorough cleaning with minimal interruption. Its quiet motor keeps operating noise low in standard mode, making it suitable for daytime use in work-from-home environments. Smart route planning reduces overlap and shortens cleaning time, while anti-collision sensors help it navigate crowded rooms. The robot includes a detachable water tank for light mopping and a high-capacity battery for up to two hours of runtime. When paired with the mobile app, the Ecovacs T9 AIVI supports zone cleaning, map editing, and scheduled runs throughout the week.
