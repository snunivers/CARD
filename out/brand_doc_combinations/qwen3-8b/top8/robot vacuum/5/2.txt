The iRobot Roomba 685 focuses on efficient room-to-room cleaning with laser-based mapping and reliable obstacle detection. It stores multiple floor plans, making it useful for multi-level homes or offices. Users can choose full-home cleaning, specific room cleaning, or spot cleaning for concentrated messes. A 5200 mAh battery supports extended sessions, and the unit automatically returns to its charging dock when power runs low, then resumes from where it stopped. Large wheels help it cross thresholds, and its quiet operating mode keeps noise down during evening use.
