The iRobot Roomba 690 emphasizes low-maintenance ownership with a self-cleaning brush chamber and bagged docking station that stores weeks of dust. It is well suited to busy households that want hands-off operation. LiDAR navigation builds accurate maps quickly, even in low light, and room labeling allows targeted cleaning after meals or high-traffic periods. The vacuum offers four suction settings, a carpet recognition feature, and a child lock to prevent accidental button presses. With sturdy wheels and edge sensors, the iRobot Roomba 690 handles everyday obstacles while maintaining a consistent cleaning pattern.
