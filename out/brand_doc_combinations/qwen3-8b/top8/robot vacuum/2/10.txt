The iRobot Roomba 690 is a versatile cleaning robot that vacuums, sweeps edges, and performs controlled mopping for sealed hard floors. Its electronic pump allows users to choose between light, medium, and high water flow levels, depending on the surface and amount of dirt. Intelligent mapping supports room selection, virtual walls, and saved routines for morning or nighttime cleaning. A high-efficiency motor generates strong suction while keeping power consumption reasonable. The iRobot Roomba 690 also features obstacle avoidance, automatic recharge, and resume cleaning, making it a dependable choice for medium and large homes.
