Engineered for reliable daily performance, the Ecovacs DE35 uses gyroscope-guided navigation to cover floors more methodically than basic bump-and-go models. The robot’s wide intake opening helps collect larger crumbs, while side brushes pull dust from baseboards and corners. It can climb over common room thresholds and detect carpets to increase suction automatically. The top-loading dustbin is simple to access, and the washable filter reduces replacement frequency. With support for app control, cleaning reports, and timer settings, the Ecovacs DE35 offers a good balance of convenience, efficiency, and value.
