The Hitachi 55100-0100 belt sander is suited to fast stock removal on hardwood, softwood, and heavy sanding tasks. Powered by a 900-watt motor, it drives a 75 x 533 mm belt at speeds up to 380 meters per minute, making it effective for leveling uneven boards and stripping finishes. An automatic belt tracking system helps maintain alignment, and the front auxiliary handle improves stability under load. The unit features a lock-on switch for continuous operation, a dust collection bag, and a flat-top design that allows the sander to be inverted for bench-mounted applications.
