The Black & Decker 18V2000 finishing sander is tailored for smooth final passes on furniture, trim, and cabinet work. Equipped with a 200-watt motor, it produces 11,000 orbits per minute and uses a vibration-reducing counterbalance system for better handling. Its square sanding base works well on flat panels and along edges, while the paper clamp design accepts precut sheets or roll stock. A clear dust container lets users monitor buildup and empty it quickly. The compact housing fits comfortably in the hand, and the integrated vacuum adapter makes this model easy to connect to shop extraction systems.
