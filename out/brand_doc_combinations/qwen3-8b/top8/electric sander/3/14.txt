For automotive refinishing and fine workshop applications, the Ryobi P1330 electric dual-action sander offers controlled cutting and excellent finish quality. The 300-watt motor combines orbital and rotational action to reduce visible sanding patterns on primers, fillers, and painted panels. A 150 mm backing pad supports hook-and-loop abrasives, and the speed dial allows adjustment from 4,000 to 10,000 opm depending on the stage of work. The slim barrel grip improves comfort, while the rear exhaust-style dust outlet connects to extraction equipment. Electronic load sensing helps maintain consistent speed under pressure.
