The X_Brand X_Model double sleeping bag is designed for couples or parents sharing space with a child during casual camping trips. With a broad 59-inch width and plush insulation rated to 35°F (2°C), it offers a cozy alternative to using two separate bags. The flannel-style lining adds softness, and the outer shell uses durable polyester that stands up to repeated use. Separate two-way zippers on each side make entry easier for both sleepers. The X_Model can also unzip into two quilts, giving it added versatility for home guests or camper van travel.
