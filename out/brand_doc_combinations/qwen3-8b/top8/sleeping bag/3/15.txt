Created for travelers who need flexibility, the Black Diamond Alpini 20 Sleeping Bag is a convertible sleeping bag suited to camping, road trips, and indoor guest use. Its 42°F (6°C) rating supports mild nights, while the rectangular shape allows it to zip open completely into a large blanket. The synthetic fill is evenly distributed to minimize bunching after repeated washing and storage. A durable polyester shell handles frequent packing, and the microfiber lining adds a smoother touch than basic camp bedding. The Black Diamond Alpini 20 Sleeping Bag includes a simple roll-up storage sack and color-coded zipper pulls for quick setup.
