The Kelty Solstice 20 Sleeping Bag is a premium down sleeping bag for hikers who want low weight and strong packability. Filled with 650-fill-power duck down, it provides a 20°F (-6°C) rating while compressing efficiently for backpack storage. The outer shell uses lightweight nylon ripstop with a durable water-repellent finish to resist light moisture. Box-style baffles help maintain loft, and the ergonomic mummy cut improves thermal efficiency without feeling overly restrictive. A shaped hood, neck baffle, and zipper guard complete the design. The Solstice 20 Sleeping Bag weighs about 2.1 lb and includes a mesh storage bag.
