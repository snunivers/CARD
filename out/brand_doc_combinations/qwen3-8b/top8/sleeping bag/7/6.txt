The Sea to Summit Alpaca 20 Sleeping Bag double sleeping bag is designed for couples or parents sharing space with a child during casual camping trips. With a broad 59-inch width and plush insulation rated to 35°F (2°C), it offers a cozy alternative to using two separate bags. The flannel-style lining adds softness, and the outer shell uses durable polyester that stands up to repeated use. Separate two-way zippers on each side make entry easier for both sleepers. The Alpaca 20 Sleeping Bag can also unzip into two quilts, giving it added versatility for home guests or camper van travel.
