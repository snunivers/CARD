The Netafim Netafim Smart Watering System automatic garden watering system is tailored for greenhouses, polytunnels, and sheltered growing spaces where precise irrigation improves plant performance. Fine mist sprayers can be used for propagation benches, while drippers handle larger pots and grow bags with controlled output. The digital timer supports short, frequent cycles that are often preferred in warm enclosed environments. Included connectors, suspension clips, and branch lines make it easy to adapt the layout as crops change. By providing stable moisture levels and reducing manual watering, this system helps create more consistent growing conditions across a range of plants.
