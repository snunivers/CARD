Made for decorative gardens with mixed borders, the Rain Bird ESP-6T Smart Sprinkler Controller automatic garden watering system combines fan sprayers, bubblers, and drippers to suit different plant types in one setup. The programmable timer stores custom weekly schedules and includes a manual watering mode for quick adjustments during heat waves. Flexible tubing can be routed around established landscaping, and ground stakes hold emitters securely in place. A filter and pressure control assembly support smooth operation even with frequent use. This system is well suited to gardeners who want tailored coverage for flowering plants, shrubs, and ornamental grasses.
