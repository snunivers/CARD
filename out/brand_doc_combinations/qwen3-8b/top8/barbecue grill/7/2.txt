The Napoleon Legend 24" Gas Grill barbecue grill combines pellet-fired convenience with versatile smoking and grilling performance. Its digital control panel allows temperature settings from 180°F to 500°F, while the automatic auger feeds hardwood pellets steadily for consistent heat. A 20-pound hopper supports extended cooks without frequent refills. The primary cooking area and upper warming rack provide enough space for brisket, chicken, and vegetables at the same time. Porcelain-coated grates resist sticking and clean easily. The included meat probe, grease management system, and all-terrain wheels make this grill a solid choice for backyard barbecue enthusiasts.
