Designed for apartment balconies and covered patios, the Broil King 3600 Pro Series barbecue grill is an electric option that delivers reliable cooking without charcoal or propane. Its 1,800-watt heating element reaches grilling temperatures quickly, and the nonstick cast-aluminum grate promotes even searing with less sticking. The compact footprint suits smaller spaces, while the pedestal stand can be removed for tabletop use. A variable temperature dial allows simple heat adjustment for vegetables, fish, or burgers. The lid helps trap heat, and a removable drip tray makes cleanup straightforward. Cool-touch handles add convenience during everyday cooking.
