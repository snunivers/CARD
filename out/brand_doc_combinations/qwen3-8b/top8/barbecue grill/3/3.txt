Compact and travel-friendly, the George Foreman Electric Indoor Grill barbecue grill is a portable propane unit made for camping, tailgating, and small outdoor spaces. It features a stainless steel burner rated at 10,000 BTU and a cast-iron cooking surface that heats evenly for steaks, sausages, or skewers. Push-button ignition starts quickly, and the dome lid helps circulate heat for faster cooking. Folding legs and a locking lid create a secure carrying setup when packed in a vehicle. The removable grease tray supports easier cleanup, and its lightweight design makes transport simple for weekend trips.
