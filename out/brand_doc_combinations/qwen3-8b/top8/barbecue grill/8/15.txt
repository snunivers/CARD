The S_Brand S_Model barbecue grill is a hybrid cooker that lets users choose between gas and charcoal in one unit. One side includes two stainless steel gas burners for quick weeknight meals, while the other side uses a height-adjustable charcoal tray for smoky flavor on weekends. Separate lids and thermometers allow each chamber to run at different temperatures. Cast-iron cooking grates provide strong heat retention, and the lower rack stores charcoal bags, tools, or pans. Side shelves expand prep space, while the wheeled cart design makes this multifunction grill practical for varied outdoor cooking styles.
