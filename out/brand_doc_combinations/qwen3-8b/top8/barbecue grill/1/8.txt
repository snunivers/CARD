The Char-Broil Custom 360 barbecue grill is a compact two-burner gas model for smaller households that still want dependable outdoor performance. Its stainless steel burners deliver 24,000 BTU, and the porcelain-coated cast-iron grates provide even heating across the primary cooking area. A warming rack helps keep finished food ready to serve without overcooking. The lid-mounted thermometer allows better heat monitoring, and the twist-start ignition system offers simple operation. Foldable side shelves save space when not in use. An open cart design keeps utensils within reach and accommodates a propane tank for convenient weekend grilling.
