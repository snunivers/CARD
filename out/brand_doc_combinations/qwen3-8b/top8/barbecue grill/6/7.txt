Versatile for breakfast through dinner, the Char-Broil Traeger Pro Series barbecue grill combines a traditional grill surface with a flat-top griddle. Two independently controlled burners make it possible to cook different foods at separate temperatures, with a total output of 36,000 BTU. The grill side is ideal for burgers and kebabs, while the griddle handles eggs, pancakes, onions, and fajitas. A grease channel directs runoff into a removable collection cup for easier maintenance. Side shelves add workspace, and the powder-coated cart includes lower storage. Locking wheels help position the unit on patios or driveways.
