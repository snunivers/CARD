The Sony WH-1000XM5 is a durable noise-canceling headphone intended for commuting, studying, and casual travel. Reinforced hinges and a flexible headband help it stand up to everyday use, while soft ear cushions improve comfort during longer sessions. Hybrid ANC works with passive isolation to reduce bus noise, fan hum, and nearby conversations. The 40 mm drivers produce lively bass and crisp upper frequencies without losing speech detail. Bluetooth 5.1 supports stable streaming, and the 3.5 mm input enables wired backup listening. Battery life reaches 34 hours, with fast charging available through the USB-C port.
