Built for quieter indoor use, the Makita XA091Z features a 4-gallon hot dog tank and low-noise motor rated at approximately 68 dB. It produces up to 125 PSI and 2.0 SCFM at 90 PSI, making it suitable for trim work, hobby projects, and inflation tasks. The oil-free design offers dependable cold-weather starts and minimal upkeep. A steel roll cage protects key components during transport, while the large regulator knob and dual gauges allow quick pressure adjustments. Lightweight construction and a centered carry handle make this compressor easy to move between rooms.
