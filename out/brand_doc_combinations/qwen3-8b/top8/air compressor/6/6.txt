The Hitachi K25SF1 60-gallon stationary air compressor is made for workshops that need dependable air delivery throughout the day. Powered by a 3.7 HP motor, it reaches 175 PSI and provides 11.5 SCFM at 90 PSI for grinders, sanders, spray guns, and other shop tools. The two-cylinder, oil-lubricated pump is engineered for smoother running and improved longevity. A vertical tank helps conserve floor space, while vibration-isolating mounts reduce movement during operation. This unit also includes a fully shrouded belt guard, pressure relief valve, and industrial-style gauges for clear monitoring.
