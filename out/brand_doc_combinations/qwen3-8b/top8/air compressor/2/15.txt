The Bosch GBH28-24DFR 50-gallon smart air compressor blends workshop capacity with modern controls for users who want efficient performance. It reaches 175 PSI and supplies 6.5 SCFM at 90 PSI for ratchets, nailers, blow guns, and moderate spraying tasks. A high-efficiency motor and variable-speed pump help reduce start-stop cycling, while the digital control panel displays tank pressure, service alerts, and run status. The oil-lubricated pump is designed for durability, and the vertical tank conserves space. Lockable wheels, a guarded belt system, and low-vibration mounts round out the design.
