Built for remote job sites, the DEWALT D55492 wheelbarrow air compressor pairs a gas engine with a twin-tank design for cordless operation. It delivers up to 155 PSI and approximately 5.5 SCFM at 90 PSI, supporting roofing nailers, framing tools, fencing work, and general construction tasks where power is unavailable. The tubular frame protects the pump and engine during transport, and the pneumatic tires roll easily over uneven ground. An industrial regulator, quick-start controls, and durable cast-iron components are intended to withstand regular field use in changing weather conditions.
