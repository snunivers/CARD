Designed for frequent household use, the Briggs & Stratton 450ex electric pressure washer balances strong cleaning output with simple controls. Its brushless motor provides up to 2300 PSI and 1.6 GPM, helping remove mud, mildew, and road film from vehicles, steps, and brick paths. The unit features a telescoping handle, large rear wheels, and a reel-mounted 25-foot hose for tidy storage. Included accessories are a turbo nozzle, variable fan nozzle, foam cannon, and surface cleaner attachment. The Briggs & Stratton 450ex also uses a total stop system to reduce pump strain when the trigger is released.
