The Honda HRX536VKA wall-mount pressure washer is ideal for garages, workshops, or detailing bays where floor space matters. Its electric induction motor delivers 2100 PSI and 1.3 GPM with steady, low-vibration operation. A fixed bracket keeps the unit elevated, while a swivel hose reel manages the 60-foot pressure hose neatly. This setup works well for washing cars, motorcycles, bins, and workshop floors. The Honda HRX536VKA comes with a foam bottle, quick-connect lance, and four spray nozzles. Remote power control on the trigger handle adds convenience during repetitive cleaning tasks.
