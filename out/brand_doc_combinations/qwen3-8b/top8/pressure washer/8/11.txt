Designed for demanding residential and light commercial use, the S_Brand S_Model features a brushless induction motor paired with a brass-headed pump for durability. Output reaches 2800 PSI and 1.8 GPM, making it suitable for masonry, pool surrounds, siding, and utility trailers. The reinforced 30-foot hose resists abrasion, and the included turbo nozzle speeds up work on stubborn stains. Users can switch to the fan tip for more delicate surfaces. The S_Brand S_Model also includes onboard GFCI protection, a removable detergent tank, and a fold-down handle to simplify transport and winter storage.
