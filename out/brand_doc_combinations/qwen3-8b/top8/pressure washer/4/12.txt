The Sun Joe SJ750 pressure washer is a mid-range gas model tailored for larger properties and recurring exterior cleaning. Its 224cc engine produces 3300 PSI and 2.4 GPM, giving enough power to tackle stone paths, fencing, steps, and weathered brick. A cast aluminum pump housing helps reduce weight while maintaining durability. This unit includes a 30-foot high-pressure hose, four quick-connect spray tips, and a soap nozzle for pre-treatment. The balanced frame, soft-grip handle, and never-flat wheels make the Sun Joe SJ750 easier to move around outbuildings, driveways, and uneven yard surfaces.
