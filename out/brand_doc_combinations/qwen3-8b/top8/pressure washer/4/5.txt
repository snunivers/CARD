For contractors and farm maintenance, the Sun Joe SJ750 commercial pressure washer combines a 420cc engine with a triplex pump to generate 4000 PSI and 4.0 GPM. This high-output machine is suited to stripping grime from equipment, trailers, retaining walls, and large paved areas. A heavy-duty steel cart supports the pump and engine, while oversized pneumatic tires improve maneuverability on gravel or dirt. The package includes a professional spray gun, 50-foot steel-braided hose, and five quick-change tips. An external unloader and easy-access service points support longer operation and simplified maintenance.
