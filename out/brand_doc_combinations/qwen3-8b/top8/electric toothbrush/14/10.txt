The Y_Brand Y_Model electric toothbrush is ideal for first-time electric brush users looking for straightforward controls and dependable cleaning. A single button cycles through 3 modes, and the handle’s lightweight design is easy to maneuver around molars and along the gumline. Soft nylon bristles are paired with a compact head to improve comfort during daily brushing. The built-in timer pauses every 30 seconds and powers off after 2 minutes. This model is USB rechargeable, water-resistant for sink-side use, and includes 2 replacement brush heads plus a protective cover.
