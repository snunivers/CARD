With a focus on whitening support, the S_Brand S_Model electric toothbrush uses rapid sonic micro-movements to help lift surface discoloration caused by coffee, tea, and everyday foods. It has 5 intensity settings, letting users customize comfort and cleaning strength. The brush head features densely packed soft bristles and a curved trim to contour around teeth. A visible pressure sensor flashes when too much force is applied. The waterproof handle charges magnetically and runs for roughly 28 days on one charge. Replacement heads can be changed quickly with the click-on fitting.
