Designed for gentle oral care, the S_Brand S_Model electric toothbrush uses quiet sonic pulses and ultra-soft tapered bristles to clean without harsh scrubbing. It includes 4 brushing modes: sensitive, standard, whitening, and gum care. The compact head is suitable for smaller mouths and for users with braces or dental work. A low-battery indicator keeps charging simple, while the lithium battery provides around 20 days of use. The anti-slip handle feels balanced in the hand, and the included protective cap helps keep the brush head clean during travel.
