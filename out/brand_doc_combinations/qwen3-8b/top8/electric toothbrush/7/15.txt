Balanced between gentle care and strong cleaning action, the Oral-B Pro 1000 Cordless electric toothbrush is suitable for daily plaque control, stain maintenance, and gumline cleaning. It uses sonic vibration technology and a curved brush profile to better follow the natural shape of teeth. There are 4 modes, including soft, clean, white, and massage, plus a memory feature that recalls the last setting used. The handle charges quickly and delivers up to 38 days of battery life. Additional features include a quadrant timer, low-noise operation, and 2 replaceable brush heads with hygienic covers.
