The Philips HX8730/02 is a family-friendly electric toothbrush that balances performance, comfort, and easy maintenance. Its oscillating cleaning action helps loosen surface stains and remove buildup from hard-to-reach areas. Users can switch between 2 intensity levels depending on sensitivity. The integrated smart timer signals every 30 seconds to guide quadrant brushing and automatically stops after 2 minutes. This model comes with a countertop charging base, 3 replaceable brush heads, and color rings for easy identification. The sealed waterproof design supports safe use in the shower or at the sink.
