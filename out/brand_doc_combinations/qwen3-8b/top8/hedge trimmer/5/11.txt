If overgrown hedges are the main challenge, the Makita XU08Z1 gas-powered hedge trimmer is built to handle heavier cutting. It features a 25-inch dual-sided blade with wide tooth spacing and a 1-inch cutting capacity for tackling dense woody growth. The commercial-style air filtration system supports engine durability, and the easy-access spark plug simplifies maintenance. A swiveling rear handle improves comfort when trimming vertical faces, while the anti-vibration mounts reduce hand fatigue during longer jobs. This model is intended for users who need mobility, strong cutting power, and reliable performance in larger outdoor spaces.
