For landscaping crews and serious gardeners, the Makita XU08Z1 delivers strong cordless performance with features aimed at productivity. It uses a 56V battery and efficient brushless drive system to power a 25-inch blade with high stroke speed for fast, clean cuts. The magnesium gear case adds durability without excessive weight, and the rotating rear handle supports comfortable trimming at multiple angles. With a 1-inch cutting capacity, this trimmer can manage thick hedge material and seasonal overgrowth. Weather-resistant construction and a blade tip protector make it a dependable choice for frequent outdoor use.
