Built for convenient cordless trimming, the Makita XU08Z1 uses a 20V lithium-ion battery system to offer dependable runtime and easy maneuverability. Its 22-inch laser-cut blade is suitable for shaping hedges, bushes, and ornamental shrubs, with a maximum cutting capacity of 3/4 inch. The trimmer includes a soft-grip rear handle, front guard for added hand protection, and a two-step safety start to reduce accidental activation. Quick charging support keeps downtime low, while the lightweight body makes it comfortable for longer sessions around fences, pathways, and garden borders.
