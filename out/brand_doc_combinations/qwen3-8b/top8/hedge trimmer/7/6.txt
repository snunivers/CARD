The Oregon 7250-01 is a homeowner-friendly hedge trimmer engineered for quiet operation and low maintenance. Powered by a 4-amp corded motor, it runs continuously without concerns about fuel mixing or battery runtime. The 22-inch hardened steel blade uses a dual-reciprocating design to reduce vibration and support smoother cutting through growth up to 3/4 inch. An oversized trigger, front hand shield, and integrated extension cord lock contribute to safer handling. With dependable plug-in performance and straightforward controls, this trimmer is a practical tool for regular hedge shaping and seasonal cleanup.
