Built with professional-style features, the Craftsman 315.19145 hedge trimmer uses a high-torque brushless motor and a 26-inch blade to cover more area per pass. The longer cutting bar is paired with sharp, laser-cut teeth for efficient work on mature hedges, laurel, and dense evergreen growth. A variable-speed trigger lets users match output to the task, improving both control and battery efficiency. The rear handle rotates to support horizontal, vertical, and angled trimming positions. With weather-resistant construction and a durable gearbox, this model is suited to frequent use in demanding outdoor conditions.
