The Grohe Croma 20 cotton rope hammock is designed for relaxed backyard lounging with a breathable, open-weave surface that stays comfortable on warm days. It measures 144 x 60 inches overall and supports up to 450 pounds, making it suitable for one or two adults. Hardwood spreader bars help keep the bed open for easy entry and exit, while reinforced end loops improve durability. The soft cotton blend offers a classic feel, and the neutral color works well on patios, porches, or garden stands. Hanging hardware is sold separately.
