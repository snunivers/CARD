Designed for poolside use, the Pfister 2111-1 quick-dry hammock features breathable textilene mesh that promotes airflow and sheds water after rain or splashes. The durable synthetic material resists mildew, fading, and stretching, making it suitable for humid climates. This spreader-bar hammock measures 78 x 55 inches and holds up to 350 pounds. Powder-coated steel hanging rings and reinforced seams improve long-term reliability. Its low-maintenance construction wipes clean with mild soap and water, while the supportive surface offers a balanced mix of flexibility and firmness for casual outdoor relaxation.
