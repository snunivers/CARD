For families and shared lounging, the Delta RP4100-SS double hammock provides a roomy sleeping surface with a 450-pound capacity. The bed is made from solution-dyed polyester fabric that resists UV fading and dries faster than standard cotton. Hardwood spreader bars keep the surface open, and rust-resistant hanging hardware is included for quicker setup on an existing stand. Measuring 125 inches overall, this hammock fits many standard 12 to 15-foot frames. The fabric is soft to the touch yet durable enough for regular outdoor use, making it a versatile option for decks and backyards.
