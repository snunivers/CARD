The Levoit LV-H132 is a compact desktop air purifier made for dorm rooms, studios, and personal workspaces. Despite its smaller footprint, it includes a genuine HEPA filter and a carbon screen to reduce dust, allergens, and light odors in areas up to 120 square feet. USB-C power support offers flexible placement, and the one-button control keeps operation easy. Users can choose between two speeds or activate a gentle night setting with reduced light. Its lightweight construction makes it convenient to move from a desk to a bedside table as needed.
