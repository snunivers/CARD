Built for medium-sized living areas, the U_Brand U_Model delivers fast air cleaning with a CADR rated at 210 CFM and coverage up to 420 square feet. It uses a sealed H13 HEPA system to trap fine airborne particles, supported by a deodorizing carbon filter for smoke and cooking smells. An air quality sensor automatically adjusts fan speed based on real-time conditions, and the color indicator makes room status easy to read. The unit includes a timer, child lock, and eco mode to help reduce energy use during continuous operation.
