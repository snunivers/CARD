Ideal for nurseries and quiet bedrooms, the X_Brand X_Model focuses on low-noise comfort without sacrificing filtration quality. It cleans rooms up to 180 square feet and features a 4-stage system including a fabric pre-filter, HEPA media, activated carbon, and an optional ionizer that can be turned off. The rounded body and soft indicator lights give it a calm, unobtrusive look. Sleep mode lowers sound output and darkens the display, while the fragrance pad option lets users add a light scent if desired. Setup is simple and maintenance is minimal.
