Engineered for surveillance and archive workloads, the Netgear ReadyNAS RN104 combines eight bays with large storage pools and continuous recording support. It uses a multi-core processor, 8 GB memory, and dual LAN connectivity to handle multiple IP camera streams alongside general file services. The Netgear ReadyNAS RN104 supports RAID 5, 6, and hot spare configurations, plus motion event indexing and retention policies for organized footage management. Beyond security use, it can serve as a departmental backup target with rsync, cloud replication, and external USB copy. The chassis includes efficient cooling for reliable 24/7 operation.
