Small teams needing secure collaboration will appreciate the Buffalo TeraStation TS-D880, a 4-bay NAS with built-in file synchronization and versioning. It features a quad-core processor, 4 GB RAM, two Gigabit ports, and NVMe cache slots to accelerate frequent file access. The Buffalo TeraStation TS-D880 supports RAID 0, 1, 5, 10, encrypted shared folders, and remote worker access through a secure portal. Integrated office-style collaboration apps, calendar syncing, and permission-based sharing help organize projects without relying entirely on public cloud subscriptions. It is well suited for agencies, consultants, and distributed teams.
