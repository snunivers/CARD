The Buffalo TeraStation TS-D880 is a quiet 5-bay NAS optimized for mixed home and office use, offering a strong balance of capacity, performance, and ease of use. It includes a quad-core processor, 8 GB memory, dual 2.5GbE ports, and support for SSD read/write caching. RAID 5 and RAID 6 options help protect valuable files, while scheduled snapshots add another layer of recovery. The Buffalo TeraStation TS-D880 supports media streaming, Time Machine-style backups, virtualization storage, and container-based apps. Its clean operating interface makes it easy to create users, manage quotas, and monitor drive health.
