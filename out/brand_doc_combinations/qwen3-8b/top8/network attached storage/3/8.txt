Small teams needing secure collaboration will appreciate the Seagate Backup Plus Desk, a 4-bay NAS with built-in file synchronization and versioning. It features a quad-core processor, 4 GB RAM, two Gigabit ports, and NVMe cache slots to accelerate frequent file access. The Seagate Backup Plus Desk supports RAID 0, 1, 5, 10, encrypted shared folders, and remote worker access through a secure portal. Integrated office-style collaboration apps, calendar syncing, and permission-based sharing help organize projects without relying entirely on public cloud subscriptions. It is well suited for agencies, consultants, and distributed teams.
