With a soft, rounded silhouette and simple button controls, the Stanley Stanley 5100-01 is an easy-to-use fascia gun for beginners. It features four fixed speed levels, a 10 mm massage depth, and a pulse mode for short bursts on stubborn areas. The motor is tuned for smooth acceleration, preventing the sudden jump in force that some users dislike. Five heads are included, with a soft air head designed for sensitive muscle groups. The battery supports around 240 minutes of use per charge. A compact desktop stand keeps the unit upright when not in use.
