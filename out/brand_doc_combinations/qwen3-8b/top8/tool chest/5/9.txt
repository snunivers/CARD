The Kreg 1200 8-Tool Chest slim-profile tool chest is ideal for narrow garages, service bays, or apartment workshops where space is limited. Despite its smaller footprint, it includes five drawers, a top storage tray, and a lower shelf for compact power tools or cleaning supplies. The all-steel construction gives the unit a solid feel, while the baked finish helps guard against rust and minor scratches. A side push handle and four caster wheels support easy movement between work areas. With a central keyed lock and anti-slip drawer liners, this chest delivers straightforward organization in tight spaces.
