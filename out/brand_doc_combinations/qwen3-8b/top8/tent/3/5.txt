For campers who want extra comfort, the Kelty Trail Lite 2 combines a tent and screened porch in one practical shelter. This 5-person design features a large sleeping area plus a bug-free front room for chairs, coolers, or relaxing in the evening. The polyester fly is rated to 2000 mm and extends over the porch for better rain coverage. Mesh walls improve visibility and airflow, while roll-up panels add privacy when needed. Steel and fiberglass poles create a sturdy frame, and the wide D-shaped door makes moving gear easier. The Kelty Trail Lite 2 suits family weekends and seasonal campsites.
