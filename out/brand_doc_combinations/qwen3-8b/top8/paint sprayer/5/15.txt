Versatile and user-friendly, the Graco 340 Paint Sprayer multi-surface paint sprayer is suitable for home improvement, craft work, and light remodeling. The system features a 650W motor, adjustable trigger response, and a sealed 1.2-liter tank to limit leaks when moving around the workspace. Interchangeable nozzles allow application of primers, chalk paint, varnish, and stain on furniture, interior walls, or garden items. Ventilation slots help keep the motor cooler during longer sessions, and the rotating air cap supports horizontal or vertical spray fans. The Graco 340 Paint Sprayer includes a cleaning needle, spare gasket set, and instruction guide.
