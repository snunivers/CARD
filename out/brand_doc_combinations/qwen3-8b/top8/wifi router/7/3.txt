Designed with gamers in mind, the D-Link DIR-8680L is a high-performance WiFi 6 router that prioritizes low-latency traffic. It supports combined wireless speeds up to 5400 Mbps and uses a fast multi-core processor to keep gameplay, voice chat, and streaming responsive. Dedicated QoS profiles help prioritize consoles and gaming PCs, while six antennas enhance coverage and signal consistency. The router includes a 2.5 Gigabit gaming port, four Gigabit LAN ports, and link aggregation support. The D-Link DIR-8680L also offers WPA3 security, VPN options, RGB status lighting, and detailed traffic monitoring.
