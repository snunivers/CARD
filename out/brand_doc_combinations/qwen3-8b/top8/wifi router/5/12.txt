The Netgear Nighthawk X10 targets users who need strong wired and wireless performance for media-heavy homes. It is a dual-band AX4200 router with a 2.5 Gigabit WAN port and four Gigabit LAN ports, ideal for fast broadband plans and wired entertainment systems. High-power antennas and beamforming help maintain wide coverage, while MU-MIMO improves performance for simultaneous users. The router includes adaptive QoS for streaming and gaming, plus USB 3.0 for storage sharing. On the software side, the Netgear Nighthawk X10 offers WPA3 protection, guest networks, traffic analytics, and scheduled access controls for family devices.
