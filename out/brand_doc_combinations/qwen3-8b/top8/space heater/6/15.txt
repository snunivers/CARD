The SpaceKare SK-1500 high-capacity room heater is intended for open living areas that need faster, more robust supplemental warmth. Using a powerful ceramic element and enhanced air channel design, it produces up to 1500 watts with turbo, normal, and eco modes. The digital thermostat can be set in one-degree increments, helping maintain a more stable room temperature throughout the day. A large LED display remains easy to read from across the room, and hidden wheels improve portability despite the larger size. The SpaceKare SK-1500 features automatic oscillation, child lock, overheat protection, and tip-over shutoff.
