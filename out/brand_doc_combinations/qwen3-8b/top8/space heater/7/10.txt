The WarmlyYou WY-1000 fireplace-style space heater adds visual warmth as well as practical heating to dens, bedrooms, and small living rooms. This freestanding unit provides up to 1500 watts of infrared heat and includes an adjustable flame-effect display that can operate with or without heat. A digital thermostat supports temperature selection from 62 to 82°F, and the included remote allows changes from across the room. The cabinet is compact enough for apartments while still offering a furniture-inspired appearance. With overheat protection, cool-touch housing, and a programmable timer, the WarmlyYou WY-1000 balances style and safety.
