Small enough for travel yet powerful enough for everyday use, the Black+Decker H2000 mini plug-in space heater is designed for hotel rooms, offices, and personal corners that need quick supplemental warmth. It plugs directly into a standard wall outlet and delivers up to 500 watts, making it suitable for smaller spaces and spot heating rather than whole-room coverage. An onboard digital thermostat and simple timer help manage comfort without cluttering the floor with cords. The Black+Decker H2000 rotates to fit different outlet orientations and includes ceramic heating, overheat protection, and a cool-touch outer shell.
