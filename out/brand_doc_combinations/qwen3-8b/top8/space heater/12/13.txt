Engineered for all-day comfort, the W_Brand W_Model micathermic space heater combines radiant and convection heating in a lightweight panel design. It warms up quickly and operates with less noise than many fan-based units, making it a smart choice for bedrooms, studies, and home offices. The heater offers 750 and 1500 watt settings, an adjustable thermostat, and both freestanding and wall-mount installation options. Its slim body is easy to move, yet broad enough to distribute heat efficiently. The W_Brand W_Model includes tip-over shutoff, thermal cutoff protection, and a stable stand for secure placement.
