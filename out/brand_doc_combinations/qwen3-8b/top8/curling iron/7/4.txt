The AmazonBasics 1.25 Inch Ceramic Curling Iron is a compact curling iron created for short hair, touch-ups, and travel. Its 0.75-inch ceramic barrel helps shape tight curls, defined flips, and polished bends close to the roots. With temperature settings from 220°F to 390°F, it works especially well for fine to normal hair that needs gentler heat. The slim body fits easily in a carry bag, and the folding safety stand helps protect countertops. Quick heat-up, a one-button operation, and a 6-foot swivel cord make this model simple for everyday styling.
