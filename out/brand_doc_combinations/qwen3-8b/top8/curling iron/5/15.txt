The Remington S10000 Curling Iron is a flexible styling tool that doubles as both a curling iron and wand with a removable clamp attachment. The 1-inch barrel supports classic curls when the clamp is attached, or relaxed waves when used clipless. Tourmaline ceramic construction helps reduce static and leaves a smoother finish, while heat settings from 260°F to 440°F suit many hair types. This model includes rapid heat-up, a temperature memory feature, and a safety glove. An 8-foot swivel cord, dual voltage, and one-hour auto shut-off make it convenient for regular use.
