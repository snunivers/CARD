Built for loose waves and soft volume, the ghd Air 2 Plus features a 1.25-inch tourmaline ceramic barrel that helps seal in shine. The digital control panel offers five heat settings up to 410°F, allowing flexible styling without unnecessary heat exposure. Fast heat-up shortens prep time, and the lightweight handle feels comfortable during longer sessions. The iron includes a tangle-free 8-foot cord, cool-touch stand, and automatic shut-off after one hour. With its smooth clamp and easy glide, it works well for medium to long hair.
