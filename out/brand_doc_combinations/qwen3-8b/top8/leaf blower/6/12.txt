With a focus on user comfort, the Husqvarna 455 backpack battery leaf blower delivers strong performance without fuel mixing, engine noise, or pull starts. Its 80V power system drives air speeds up to 165 mph and 620 CFM, helping clear damp leaves and debris from larger properties. The padded harness distributes weight evenly, and the throttle control is positioned for easy access while walking. A brushless motor improves efficiency and extends service life. For users who want commercial-style output in a lower-maintenance format, this model offers a practical solution.
