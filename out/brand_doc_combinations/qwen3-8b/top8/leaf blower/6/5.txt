The Husqvarna 455 compact battery-powered leaf blower is ideal for apartments, townhomes, and smaller yards where storage space matters. It features a 20V lithium-ion platform, delivering up to 110 mph airspeed and 280 CFM for clearing porches, sidewalks, and garages. The lightweight build and balanced handle make one-handed operation easier, especially for quick touch-ups. A two-speed selector lets you switch between gentle sweeping and stronger bursts of air. The included charger restores the battery efficiently, making this model a convenient option for users who want simple, low-maintenance outdoor equipment.
