The Ego EGO HD550 high-capacity cordless blower is built for users who need strong battery performance without stepping up to gas equipment. Running on a 60V lithium-ion system, it produces air output up to 160 mph and 580 CFM for demanding cleanup around larger yards. The brushless motor supports efficient power delivery, and the variable-speed dial helps manage runtime during lighter tasks. A boost mode provides extra force for wet leaves or packed debris. The blower includes a wide comfort handle and sturdy tube connection, making it reliable for frequent weekly maintenance.
