The Ego EGO HD550 blower/vacuum combo is designed for homeowners who want to tidy the yard and reduce debris volume at the same time. In blower mode, the motor produces up to 210 mph airflow for moving leaves into manageable piles. Switching to vacuum mode allows collection into a zippered bag, while the mulching function chops material for easier composting or disposal. The unit includes adjustable speed control, a shoulder strap, and a front support wheel to reduce strain during vacuuming. This all-in-one design works well for seasonal cleanup around trees and garden borders.
