The JBL Flip 6 portable speaker delivers broad, immersive sound through a dual-driver setup and passive sub-bass support. It is designed for users who want stronger audio performance without carrying a large unit. Bluetooth 5.3 ensures stable streaming from phones, tablets, and laptops, and NFC tap pairing is included for compatible devices. The battery lasts up to 20 hours, depending on volume and lighting use. A rear USB-C port handles charging, while a standard AUX input supports wired playback. The housing features a matte finish, textured controls, and IP55 water resistance.
