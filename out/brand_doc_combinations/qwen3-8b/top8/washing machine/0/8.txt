The Samsung WW80T6340DW is built for busy families that need fast turnaround without sacrificing wash quality. This 9.5 kg washing machine offers a rapid full-load cycle, pre-soak function, and stain treatment options for everyday messes. Its automatic detergent dosing helps reduce waste by using the right amount for each load. Smart connectivity allows cycle monitoring and remote start through a compatible app. The Samsung WW80T6340DW also includes a drum light, quiet night mode, and a maintenance alert system, providing convenience for homes with frequent and varied laundry demands.
