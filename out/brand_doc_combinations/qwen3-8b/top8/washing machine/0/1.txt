Designed for compact spaces, the Samsung WW80T6340DW is a slim washing machine with a 6 kg drum and reduced depth for apartments or utility closets. Despite its smaller footprint, it includes automatic load sensing, temperature control, and multiple spin settings for everyday versatility. The machine features programs for baby care, mixed fabrics, sportswear, and a 15-minute quick wash. A stainless steel drum supports durability, while the child lock and overflow protection provide added peace of mind. The Samsung WW80T6340DW is a practical choice for singles, couples, or small families.
