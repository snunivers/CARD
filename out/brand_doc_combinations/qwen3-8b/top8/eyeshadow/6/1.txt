Create high-impact eye looks with Tarte Macaron Eyeshadow Palette, a richly pigmented cream eyeshadow stick that glides on without tugging. The twist-up pencil format allows quick application along the lid, inner corner, or lower lash line, and the built-in shape makes blending simple before the formula sets. Its waterproof, smudge-resistant texture delivers a satin-metallic finish that stays comfortable for hours. Use it alone for a one-step look or as a base under powder shadow for extra intensity. Available in versatile shades from bronze and taupe to rose gold and plum.
