Designed for classic and creative looks, Urban Decay Naked Heat includes twelve powder eyeshadows in a balanced mix of warm mattes, reflective metallics, and deep liner shades. The velvety texture picks up easily on synthetic or natural brushes, allowing smooth blending through the crease and clean layering on the lid. Each pan is pressed to minimize kickback while still delivering strong color payoff. The palette packaging includes a full-size mirror and magnetic closure for convenience. Suitable for beginners and experienced users, it supports soft daytime looks and fuller smoky-eye effects.
