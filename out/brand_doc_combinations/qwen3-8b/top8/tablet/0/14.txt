Small enough for one-handed use, the Apple iPad Pro 12.9-inch (3rd Generation) is a mini tablet with a vivid 8.3-inch display and a premium, narrow-bezel design. Despite its size, it includes a capable octa-core processor, 6GB RAM, and 128GB storage for browsing, navigation, streaming, and handheld gaming. The front camera is optimized for video chats, and the rear camera can scan documents in a pinch. USB-C charging, Wi‑Fi 6, and optional stylus support make it more versatile than typical compact tablets. The Apple iPad Pro 12.9-inch (3rd Generation) is ideal for commuters or anyone who values portability.
