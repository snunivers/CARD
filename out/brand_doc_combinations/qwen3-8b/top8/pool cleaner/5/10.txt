The Jandy Jandy AquaMaster is a hybrid pool cleaner that combines suction power with mechanical brushing to improve debris removal on textured surfaces. Designed for in-ground pools up to 16 by 32 feet, it uses a geared wheel system to maintain traction and random coverage patterns across the floor and lower walls. An adjustable skirt helps the cleaner stay effective as suction conditions change. Installation takes only a few steps using the included hose and flow gauge. The Jandy Jandy AquaMaster is a practical option for routine maintenance where moderate debris loads are common.
