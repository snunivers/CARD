The Dolphin Dolphin Premier is a smart robotic pool cleaner with programmable cleaning cycles and app-based status alerts. It is designed for in-ground pools up to 45 feet and features gyroscopic navigation to reduce missed spots during floor, wall, and waterline cleaning. The extra-large basket is accessible from the top and includes fine panels for capturing pollen, dust, and algae particles. Its anti-tangle swivel helps maintain uninterrupted movement, while the energy-efficient motor keeps power consumption low. The Dolphin Dolphin Premier balances strong cleaning performance with user-friendly maintenance and storage.
