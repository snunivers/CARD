Built for above-ground pools, the Zodiac Zodiac Dolphin 2000 is a lightweight suction-side pool cleaner that connects directly to your existing filtration system. Its flexible hose sections allow quick setup, and the compact turbine head glides across the pool floor to remove dirt, insects, and small debris. Adjustable flow control helps balance suction for different pump sizes, while the streamlined body reduces hang-ups near coves. The Zodiac Zodiac Dolphin 2000 works best in round and oval pools up to 30 feet and requires no booster pump, extra wiring, or complicated programming.
