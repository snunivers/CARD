Created for pools surrounded by trees, the Zodiac Zodiac Dolphin 2000 is a heavy-duty pressure cleaner that handles acorns, seed pods, and larger leaf debris with ease. Its oversized intake throat feeds material into a reinforced collection bag, helping preserve circulation through the main pool filter. Wide tires provide stable movement over rough plaster and pebble finishes, while the tail scrubber helps loosen fine dirt from corners. The Zodiac Zodiac Dolphin 2000 is intended for in-ground pools and performs best with a properly matched booster pump, offering reliable daily cleaning during demanding seasonal conditions.
