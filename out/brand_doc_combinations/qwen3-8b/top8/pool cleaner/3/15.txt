The Pentair Pentair Dolphin 2000 is a solar-assisted surface pool cleaner designed to skim leaves, pollen, insects, and floating debris before they sink. Suitable for both in-ground and above-ground pools, it moves independently across the water using a compact motor supported by a solar charging panel and backup battery. The removable debris tray empties quickly, and obstacle sensors help redirect the unit around walls and ladders. By reducing surface buildup throughout the day, the Pentair Pentair Dolphin 2000 complements traditional floor cleaners and helps improve overall water appearance between larger maintenance sessions.
