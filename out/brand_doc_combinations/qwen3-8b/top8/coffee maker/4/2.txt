The Keurig K-Elite is a single-serve coffee maker compatible with ground coffee and reusable filter pods. It offers brew sizes from 6 to 14 ounces, allowing users to prepare a quick cup to match their routine. A removable drip tray accommodates travel mugs, and the fast-heating system starts brewing in minutes. Simple touch controls, automatic shut-off, and a detachable water tank support easy daily use and cleaning. Designed for convenience without sacrificing flavor, the Keurig K-Elite suits busy professionals, small households, and anyone who prefers fresh coffee one cup at a time.
