The Breville BES870XL is a 12-cup drip coffee maker designed for everyday home use. It features programmable auto-start, adjustable brew strength, and a keep-warm plate that maintains serving temperature for up to two hours. A clear water window, removable filter basket, and anti-drip pause function make daily brewing simple and mess-free. The glass carafe includes an ergonomic handle and easy-pour spout. With a compact footprint and straightforward button controls, the Breville BES870XL fits neatly on most countertops while delivering consistent flavor for families, offices, or frequent coffee drinkers.
