The Cuisinart CSS-01 is a grind-and-brew coffee maker that combines a built-in conical burr grinder with a 10-cup brewer for fresh coffee from whole beans. Users can choose grind size, brew strength, and cup quantity through the digital control panel. The bean hopper includes an aroma-sealing lid, and the machine can also brew from pre-ground coffee when needed. A charcoal water filter and permanent brew basket help support clean flavor and easy upkeep. With automatic start and pause-and-pour functionality, the Cuisinart CSS-01 brings freshness and flexibility to daily brewing.
