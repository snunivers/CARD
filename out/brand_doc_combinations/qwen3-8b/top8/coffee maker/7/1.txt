Built for small kitchens and quick mornings, the Smeg CMO6150 brews up to 5 cups in a slim, space-saving body. This compact coffee maker includes a permanent mesh filter, one-touch operation, and an auto shut-off feature for added convenience. The warming plate keeps coffee ready to serve without requiring complicated settings. Its lightweight glass carafe is marked for accurate filling, while the top-loading water reservoir simplifies refills. The Smeg CMO6150 is ideal for apartments, dorm rooms, or anyone who wants reliable drip coffee without taking up much counter space.
