The Smeg CMO6150 is a pod-compatible coffee maker that offers both single-cup and carafe brewing in one machine. On the single-serve side, users can choose multiple cup sizes and brew directly into a travel mug. The carafe side prepares up to 10 cups using ground coffee, with programmable auto-start and a separate water reservoir for convenience. Independent controls make switching between formats simple, and a brew strength option adds flexibility for lighter or stronger coffee preferences. The Smeg CMO6150 is ideal for mixed households where routines, serving sizes, and tastes vary.
