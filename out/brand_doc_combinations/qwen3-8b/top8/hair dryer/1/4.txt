The Babyliss Babyliss Pro-Expert 1875 hair dryer offers a practical blend of styling flexibility and hair-friendly technology. Infrared heat works with ionic airflow to dry hair from the inside out, helping maintain moisture and reduce surface frizz. With 2000 watts of power, it is suitable for busy mornings and thicker hair types. Three heat levels, two speed settings, and a separate cool-shot button provide full control. The dryer includes a diffuser for waves and curls, plus a precision nozzle for smoother finishes. A long salon-length cord improves reach during styling.
