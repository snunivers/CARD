The T3 T3 Dryer 2.0 is a salon-inspired hair dryer made for fast results and smoother styling. A high-speed AC motor provides strong airflow for efficient drying, especially on thick or long hair. Ceramic and tourmaline components distribute heat more evenly to help reduce hot spots and minimize damage. This model offers three heat settings, two speed levels, and a cool-shot function for finishing. The package includes both a concentrator nozzle and a diffuser, making it suitable for straight styles, volume at the roots, or enhancing natural curls.
