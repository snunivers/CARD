The Conair Conair 22000 Fast Dry 2 Speed Hair Dryer is made for curly, coily, and textured hair, with accessories and settings that encourage definition while limiting frizz. Its 1875-watt motor pairs with ceramic technology for even, gentle heat distribution. The large diffuser attachment helps lift roots and maintain curl pattern, while the concentrator nozzle is useful for stretching or smoothing sections. Three heat settings and two speeds allow flexible styling, and the cool-shot function helps finish the look. A comfortable handle, long cord, and lightweight design make this dryer easy to use at home.
