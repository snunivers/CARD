With an emphasis on gentle care, the U_Brand U_Model hair dryer is suited to fine, color-treated, or easily damaged hair. Far-infrared heat is designed to dry efficiently at a more controlled surface temperature, while negative ions help minimize frizz and improve softness. The dryer features three heat options, two fan speeds, and a dedicated cool-shot button. Its lightweight body is comfortable to hold, and the quiet motor makes it less disruptive in shared spaces. A diffuser and slim concentrator are included, giving users flexibility for both natural texture and smooth finishes.
