Combining modern styling with practical performance, the LG LHP1616SMA cools spaces up to 320 square feet with 10,500 BTU output. The unit features a digital thermostat, feather-touch buttons, and a compact remote with temperature display. In addition to standard cooling, it offers dry mode for moisture control and fan-only circulation for milder days. A sleep setting gradually adjusts the temperature overnight to balance comfort and energy use. The LHP1616SMA has auto restart, washable filtration, and easy-roll casters, while the included exhaust hose and window kit support fast installation.
