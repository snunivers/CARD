For renters seeking flexible climate control, the Honeywell HH100K2A offers both cooling and supplemental heating in one portable unit. Rated at 11,500 BTU for cooling, it suits spaces around 400 square feet and transitions easily between seasons. The compact body houses a dehumidifier, fan-only mode, and sleep function for nighttime use. An easy-read front display shows current temperature and settings, while the remote provides couch-to-bed convenience. The HH100K2A also includes automatic condensate evaporation, a removable air filter, and a window installation kit for quick setup.
