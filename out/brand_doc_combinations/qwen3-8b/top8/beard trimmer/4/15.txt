Compact yet versatile, the Remington 9220 beard trimmer is aimed at users who want a close trim without switching tools. The main blade can be used alone for a near-stubble finish, while the included combs extend cutting lengths up to 9 mm for everyday beard shaping. Its USB charging system and universal voltage support make it easy to pack for travel, and the snap-on protective guard helps keep the blade secure. The trimmer runs for 60 minutes per charge and includes a small storage pouch. The Remington 9220 is simple, portable, and efficient.
