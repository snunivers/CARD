For families that run frequent loads, the Maytag MDB7609AWW high-capacity dishwasher combines generous space with effective wash performance. It fits 16 place settings and uses dual spray arms plus a dedicated bottle wash system to reach hard-to-clean containers. The machine offers Heavy, Auto, Normal, Express, and Steam cycles, along with a sanitize rinse option. A fingerprint-resistant exterior keeps the appliance looking neat, and the hidden top controls create a streamlined appearance. Operating at 44 dBA, it remains quiet during mealtimes. Smart load sensing and an energy-efficient drying system help reduce water and electricity consumption.
