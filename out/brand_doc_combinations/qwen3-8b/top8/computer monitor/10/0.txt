The U_Brand U_Model is a practical 24-inch monitor built for everyday office and home use. Its Full HD IPS panel delivers clear text, accurate color, and wide viewing angles, making spreadsheets, web browsing, and video calls comfortable throughout the day. A 75 Hz refresh rate and adaptive sync support help motion look smoother than standard 60 Hz displays. The slim-bezel design fits neatly into multi-monitor setups, while HDMI and DisplayPort inputs provide easy connectivity. U_Brand also includes low blue light and flicker-reduction modes, plus a tilt-adjustable stand and VESA mounting support.
