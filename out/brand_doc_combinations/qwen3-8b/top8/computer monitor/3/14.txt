The BenQ BenQ PD3220U delivers premium visual quality through a 32-inch 4K display with high brightness, strong contrast, and advanced local dimming support. It is well suited to creators, enthusiasts, and users who want impressive HDR performance for games, films, and detailed image work. The fast refresh rate provides smoother motion than traditional 60 Hz 4K screens, while the wide-gamut panel helps colors appear vivid and nuanced. Connectivity includes HDMI 2.1, DisplayPort, and USB-C, making the BenQ BenQ PD3220U compatible with PCs, newer consoles, and laptops. A sturdy ergonomic stand completes the package.
