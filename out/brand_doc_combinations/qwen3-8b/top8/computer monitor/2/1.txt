Designed for competitive play, the ASUS ASUS ProArt PA32UCX features a 27-inch QHD display with a fast 165 Hz refresh rate and 1 ms MPRT response time. The IPS panel balances speed with rich color, giving games a sharp and vibrant look without major ghosting. Adaptive sync helps reduce tearing during fast scenes, while HDR support improves highlight detail in compatible content. The monitor includes two HDMI ports, DisplayPort, and a headphone jack. A height-adjustable stand, gamer-oriented crosshair modes, and customizable picture presets make the ASUS ASUS ProArt PA32UCX a strong all-around gaming choice.
