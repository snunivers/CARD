The Husqvarna Husqvarna 455 XP cordless string trimmer is designed for small to medium yards, combining easy handling with dependable cutting power. It uses a 20V lithium-ion battery system and features a 12-inch cutting swath for trimming around trees, fences, and flower beds. A bump-feed spool releases 0.065-inch line quickly during use, while the adjustable shaft and auxiliary handle improve comfort for different users. Weighing under 7 pounds, this model is simple to maneuver. The pivoting head also helps with edging along sidewalks and driveways for a cleaner finish.
