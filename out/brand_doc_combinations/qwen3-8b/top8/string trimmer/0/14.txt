With dual-speed control and a durable brushless motor, the EGO EGO 5100C-12 string trimmer is designed to adapt to changing yard conditions. The low setting extends battery runtime for routine grass trimming, while the high setting adds cutting power for thicker weeds. Its 16-inch swath makes it effective on larger lawns, and the load-n-go trimmer head simplifies restringing. A metal guard reinforcement improves durability in demanding areas near stone borders and chain-link fencing. This model offers a useful combination of efficiency, power, and user-friendly features for homeowners maintaining substantial outdoor spaces.
