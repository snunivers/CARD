Featuring a foldable shaft for compact storage, the Greenworks Greenworks 40V 24 in. String Trimmer 4024-2400 cordless string trimmer is designed for convenience in garages, sheds, or apartment storage spaces. It runs on a 36V battery system and uses a 14-inch cutting swath to handle routine trimming around the lawn. The quick-adjust loop handle and telescoping shaft help tailor the fit to the user, while the line-feed mechanism keeps cutting consistent with minimal downtime. A low-noise motor makes it suitable for early or late yard work. This model combines portability, clean operation, and practical everyday performance.
