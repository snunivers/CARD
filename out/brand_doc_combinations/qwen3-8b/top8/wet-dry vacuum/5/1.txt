Built for home garages and renovation cleanup, the Eureka 40T30 wet-dry vacuum pairs a 4-gallon polypropylene tank with a 5 peak HP motor for stronger suction on debris and standing water. The balanced design rolls on four swivel casters and features a large power switch that is easy to use with gloves. Included are a locking hose, extension wands, floor nozzle, and detail brush for vehicle interiors. A rear blower port converts the vacuum into a quick leaf and dust blower, while the drainless tank keeps the body simple and lightweight.
