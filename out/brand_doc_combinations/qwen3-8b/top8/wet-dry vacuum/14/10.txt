The Y_Brand Y_Model delivers extra capacity for garages, basements, and small commercial spaces that see frequent wet and dry messes. This wet-dry vacuum features a 14-gallon drum, a 6 peak HP motor, and a wide hose designed to pass larger debris with fewer clogs. Dual locking extensions provide better control during floor cleaning, and the oversized drain makes water disposal simpler. The package includes a squeegee floor head, utility nozzle, crevice tool, and reusable cartridge filter. A built-in blower port adds versatility for clearing leaves, dust, and workshop scraps from outdoor areas.
