The S_Brand S_Model offers a practical blend of power and portability for DIY users who need one machine for dry messes and wet pickup. Its 6-gallon capacity reduces emptying frequency, while the 4.5 peak HP motor handles sawdust, pet hair, and workshop scraps with steady airflow. A washable pleated filter supports fine dust collection, and the included foam filter is suited for liquid recovery. This wet-dry vacuum uses a 7-foot hose and 16-foot cord for extended reach. Side latches, caster wheels, and onboard storage make setup and maintenance straightforward.
