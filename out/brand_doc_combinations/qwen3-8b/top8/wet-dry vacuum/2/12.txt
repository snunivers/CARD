The Hoover HVO1000 is a cordless wet-dry vacuum designed for users who need portability away from outlets. Powered by a removable 20V battery platform, it handles spot cleaning in vehicles, sheds, stairwells, and small renovation zones without dragging a power cord. The 2-gallon tank is paired with a high-efficiency motor that delivers focused suction for chips, dust, and minor liquid spills. A flexible hose, crevice nozzle, utility head, and washable filter come included. This model also offers sealed onboard storage and a compact footprint for easier transport and shelf storage.
