Designed for precise heating, the Samsung ME19R8555HS microwave oven uses inverter technology to deliver consistent power instead of cycling on and off. This helps foods thaw more evenly and reduces the chance of overcooked edges. The 1.2 cu. ft. interior and 1200-watt output support quick meal preparation, while sensor programs simplify cooking vegetables, rice, and leftovers. A keep-warm function is useful when timing multiple dishes. The streamlined control panel, soft-close door, and easy-clean enamel interior give the Samsung ME19R8555HS a polished everyday feel.
