For households seeking more versatility, the Whirlpool RBC2834SQ microwave oven pairs microwave cooking with an integrated grill function. Its 1.0 cu. ft. cavity and 1000-watt microwave output are suitable for reheating, cooking, and browning foods with better texture than a standard microwave alone. Auto menus help streamline preparation for vegetables, pizza, chicken pieces, and beverages. The grill rack expands cooking options, while combination modes save time on dishes that need both fast heating and a crisp finish. A stainless-look door adds a modern touch to the kitchen.
