The Seasonic Focus GX-1000 is a 450W ATX power supply designed for everyday desktop builds and office systems. It delivers stable output on a single +12V rail and includes an 80 PLUS Bronze efficiency rating to reduce wasted energy and excess heat. A quiet 120 mm cooling fan adjusts speed automatically for balanced airflow and low noise. Standard protections include OVP, UVP, SCP, OPP, and OTP for safer operation. The unit provides a 24-pin motherboard connector, 4+4 CPU power, two SATA chains, and one PCIe connector for entry-level graphics cards.
