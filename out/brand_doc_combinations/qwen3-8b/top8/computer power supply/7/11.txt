The Antec NE850M is a 1200W power supply aimed at advanced gaming, overclocking, and professional workstation builds. Its 80 PLUS Platinum efficiency rating helps reduce energy loss even when the system is under sustained stress. Fully modular cables include multiple EPS connectors, several PCIe 6+2 leads, and native support for newer high-power graphics cards. A large 140 mm fan with semi-passive mode keeps thermal performance under control without constant noise. High-quality internal components, low ripple output, and layered protection features make this unit suitable for users planning demanding hardware configurations and future upgrades.
