The Cooler Master MWE 1000 K is a 700W ATX power supply tailored for gaming systems that need a balance of clean power, quiet operation, and practical pricing. Its 80 PLUS Gold rating improves efficiency during long sessions, while the semi-modular cable system helps reduce unused wiring inside the case. A 120 mm hydraulic bearing fan is tuned for low vibration and consistent cooling. The unit supports modern components with dual EPS connectors, four PCIe 6+2 connectors, and ample SATA power for SSDs and hard drives. Safety features include short-circuit, overload, over-voltage, and over-temperature protection.
