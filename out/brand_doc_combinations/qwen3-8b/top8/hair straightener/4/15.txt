Compact yet capable, the Remington Remington S10000 is a travel-friendly hair straightener designed for quick touch-ups and everyday styling. Its narrow ceramic plates are suitable for short to medium hair and help create straight finishes, soft bends, or polished ends. The tool features adjustable heat from 150°C to 210°C, with a quick-heating element that reaches styling temperature in under 40 seconds. A lightweight body and universal voltage make it easy to pack for trips. The design also includes a swivel cord, protective cap, ready indicator, and 60-minute auto shut-off for safer use.
