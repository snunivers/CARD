Small but capable, the Seagate BarraCuda 1TB SATA SSD is a 128GB SATA SSD intended for entry-level systems, embedded devices, and boot-drive duties. It offers read speeds up to 500MB/s and write speeds up to 320MB/s, significantly improving startup and access times compared with hard drives. The drive features a 7mm 2.5-inch housing, low active power draw, and resistance to vibration and shock. Firmware support for TRIM and wear leveling helps extend usable life. This model is best suited for lightweight operating system installations, kiosks, thin clients, and simple office applications.
