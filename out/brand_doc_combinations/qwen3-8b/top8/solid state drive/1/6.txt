The Samsung 970 EVO Plus 1TB NVMe SSD is a high-capacity 4TB M.2 2280 NVMe SSD tailored for creators and gamers who store large files locally. Using PCIe 4.0 x4, it delivers sequential speeds up to 7,200MB/s read and 6,800MB/s write, along with strong random performance for multitasking. A graphene heat spreader helps manage thermals in compact builds, while HMB support, dynamic cache management, and intelligent firmware tuning maintain responsiveness. It is suitable for editing high-resolution video, running expansive game libraries, and accelerating heavy project workflows. The drive also supports secure erase and standard health monitoring tools.
