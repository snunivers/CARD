The U_Brand U_Model is a compact 256GB M.2 SATA SSD aimed at budget-conscious system builders and office upgrades. Using the SATA 6Gb/s interface, it offers read speeds up to 550MB/s and write speeds up to 500MB/s, providing a noticeable improvement over traditional hard drives. The drive features 3D TLC NAND, garbage collection, TRIM support, and bad block management to help preserve long-term stability. Its low heat output and cable-free installation are ideal for small form factor PCs. This model works well for operating systems, essential apps, and light document storage.
