Charlotte Tilbury Pillow Talk Sensitive Comfort Lipstick is formulated for users who want color with a simpler ingredient profile. Fragrance-free and designed to minimize irritation, it uses soothing emollients and protective waxes to help lips feel soft and calm during wear. The finish is a creamy satin that does not emphasize flakes or texture, making it especially suitable for dry or delicate lips. Pigment payoff is moderate but buildable, allowing both subtle and more defined looks. The tube opens and closes securely, and the lipstick applies smoothly without tugging, even when lips are not perfectly conditioned.
