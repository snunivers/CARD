Charlotte Tilbury Pillow Talk Lip Crayon Lipstick pairs the control of a pencil with the payoff of a traditional lipstick. Its retractable design removes the need for sharpening and makes touch-ups simple when traveling. The pointed tip helps outline lips accurately, while the creamy formula fills them in with even, saturated color. A soft matte finish keeps the look modern without emphasizing dryness. Infused with vitamin E and sunflower seed oil, it feels smoother than typical lip pencils. This product is especially useful for defining shape, correcting uneven edges, and creating a polished monochrome makeup look.
