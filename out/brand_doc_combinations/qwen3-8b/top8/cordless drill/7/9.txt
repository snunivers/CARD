With its compact brushless design, the Milwaukee M18 REDLITHIUM-5.0 cordless drill targets users who need strong performance in a smaller body. The 18V motor produces high torque while keeping vibration and heat lower during repeated fastening work. It features a 1/2-inch ratcheting chuck, two-speed transmission, and precision clutch for consistent screw depth in wood framing, cabinetry, and electrical installations. A battery status display is positioned on the foot of the tool for quick checks. The rubberized handle, integrated hook, and shadow-free LED ring light make this drill especially practical on busy jobsites.
