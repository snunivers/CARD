The Black & Decker BDC120 is a versatile 18V hammer drill and driver intended for wood, steel, masonry, and routine installation work. It combines drilling, driving, and hammer modes in one tool, with a maximum impact rate of 28,000 BPM for light brick and concrete tasks. A brushless motor supports strong torque output while maintaining efficient battery use. Additional features include a 1/2-inch keyless chuck, all-metal gears, side handle, and LED work light. The balanced design and textured grip help improve control when drilling larger holes or driving long fasteners.
