Compact and easy to control, the W_Brand W_Model is a 12V cordless drill/driver made for tight spaces and detailed work. It weighs under 2.2 lb with battery and features a short head length that fits well inside cabinets and between framing members. Users can select from two speed ranges and 20 torque settings depending on whether they are pre-drilling pilot holes or driving small screws. The 3/8-inch chuck accepts common accessories without a key. An onboard bit holder, battery indicator, and rubber overmold add convenience for household tasks and light trade use.
