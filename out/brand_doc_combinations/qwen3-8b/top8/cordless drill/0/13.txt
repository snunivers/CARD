For tougher construction and remodeling work, the DeWalt D25702 provides professional-grade cordless drilling performance on an 18V battery platform. The brushless motor supports long runtime and strong torque, while the hammer setting adds capability for anchors in block and masonry. This model uses a 1/2-inch carbide-jaw chuck for improved grip on larger bits and hole saw accessories. An electronic clutch helps deliver repeatable fastening results, and the side handle increases stability. Supplied with two high-capacity batteries and a rapid charger, it is suited to framing, decking, and mechanical installation tasks.
