Made for installers and serious DIY users, the DeWalt D25702 delivers a strong mix of speed, durability, and control. The 20V cordless drill features a brushless motor, metal gear housing, and anti-kickback electronics that help reduce sudden tool rotation during bind-ups. A 1/2-inch chuck, two speed ranges, and 25 clutch settings allow clean transitions from pilot holes to heavy screwdriving. The kit includes two 3.0Ah batteries and a charger. Weather-resistant seals around the switch and battery interface help the tool handle dusty garages, unfinished basements, and outdoor project conditions.
