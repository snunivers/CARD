Designed for general DIY and workshop use, the Bosch GSR 18V-20 cordless drill delivers 20V power with a brushless motor for improved runtime and reduced maintenance. It offers a 1/2-inch ratcheting metal chuck, two transmission speeds up to 1,900 RPM, and 22 clutch positions for precise fastening. The drill comes with two 2.0Ah batteries, a fast charger, and a belt clip for convenience on the job. Built-in overload protection helps protect the motor under heavy load, and the compact body makes it easier to work between studs or overhead.
