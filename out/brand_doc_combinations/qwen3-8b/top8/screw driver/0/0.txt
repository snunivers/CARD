The Stanley 27-512 Phillips screwdriver is built for everyday fastening tasks around the home or workshop. It features a #2 tip made from heat-treated chrome vanadium steel for dependable strength and reduced wear. A magnetic point helps hold screws in place, while the satin finish resists corrosion. The tri-lobe handle is molded with a soft, non-slip grip to improve comfort during longer jobs. A clearly marked cap makes size identification easy in a toolbox. Ideal for furniture assembly, appliance maintenance, and general repair work.
